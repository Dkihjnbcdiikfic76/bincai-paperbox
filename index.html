About Guangdong Bincai

Founded in 2003, Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. is a professional paper box manufacturer with more than 20 years of deep industry experience. We own a modern production base covering 8,000 square meters in Foshan, Guangdong — the heart of China's manufacturing hub.

We specialize in one-stop customized packaging solutions for global clients, integrating independent R&D, innovative design, intelligent production, and efficient sales into a seamless supply chain.

ISO 9001:2015 FSC Certified SGS Audited 20+ Years
