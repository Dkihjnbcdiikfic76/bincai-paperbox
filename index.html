Built on 20 Years of
Packaging Expertise

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. was founded in 2003 in Foshan, Guangdong — the heart of China's manufacturing region. We've spent two decades perfecting the art of paper box manufacturing, serving clients across five continents.

Our 8,000m² facility houses advanced automated production lines capable of handling everything from small-batch custom runs to large-volume production. Every box passes through our multi-stage quality control process before it ships.

We don't just make boxes — we engineer packaging solutions that protect your product, reinforce your brand, and create an unforgettable unboxing experience.
