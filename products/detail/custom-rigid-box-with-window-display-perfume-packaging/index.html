Overview

FAQ

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity for custom window rigid boxes? A: MOQ is 500–1000 units for fully custom boxes with custom window shape and full-color printing. For stock sizes with single-color foil stamping, MOQ can be as low as 300 units.

Q: Can the window film be biodegradable? A: Yes! We offer PLA (polylactic acid) biodegradable window film as an alternative to standard PET. PLA is certified industrially compostable (EN 13432) and delivers comparable clarity (88%+ transmission). We also offer cellulose-based film for the most demanding sustainability requirements.

Q: How do you ensure the window won’t scratch the perfume bottle? A: The die-cut window is positioned with a minimum 3mm clearance gap from the bottle surface. The interior foam or velvet insert holds the bottle with precision so there’s zero contact with the window edge. For bottles with delicate surface finishes, we add a micro-velvet lining to the window frame interior.

Q: What magnet strength do I need for my box size? A: We match magnet specifications to your box dimensions and weight: N42 8mm discs for small boxes (50–100mm), N42 10mm for medium (100–200mm), and N52 12mm for large/heavy boxes (200mm+). Our engineers calculate the required pull force based on your box dimensions and recommend the optimal magnet configuration.

Q: Can you match an existing box design exactly? A: Absolutely. Send us your existing box sample or technical drawing, and we’ll reverse-engineer the construction, match the materials and finishes, and produce a pre-production sample for your approval — typically within 7 working days.

Q: How are the boxes packaged for international shipping? A: Each box is individually poly-bagged, then packed flat in export-grade double-wall corrugated master cartons on heat-treated (ISPM 15) pallets. We use corner protectors and moisture-barrier liners for ocean freight. Box-in-box nesting configurations available to maximize container utilization.

Q: What certifications does Bincai hold? A: ISO 9001:2015 (quality management), FSC Chain-of-Custody (CU-COC-XXXXXX — responsible forestry), High-Tech Enterprise (Guangdong provincial certification), and we manufacture to additional standards as required: EN 71-3 (toy safety), REACH, RoHS, and FDA 21 CFR for indirect food contact.
