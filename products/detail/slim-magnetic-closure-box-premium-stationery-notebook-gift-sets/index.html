FAQ

Q: What is the depth range for the slim profile? A: Standard slim profiles are 25 mm, 30 mm, and 35 mm. These are engineered for A5 notebooks, journals, and planner systems. We also produce standard-depth boxes (50–100 mm) for multi-item stationery gift sets.

Q: Can the magnetic closure support heavier stationery items? A: Yes. We use N52-grade neodymium magnets rated at ≥ 8 N pull force per pair. For heavier items (multi-book sets, metal accessories), we can install additional magnet pairs to increase closure strength.

Q: What customization options are available for the exterior? A: Full CMYK offset printing on art paper wrap, PU leatherette in 30+ colors, linen-textured paper, specialty stocks, debossed or embossed logos, gold/silver/rose gold hot foil, soft-touch lamination, or high-gloss UV. Interior printing is also available for branded unboxing.

Q: Is the debossed logo durable enough for retail handling? A: Yes. Our Bobst hot foil and debossing presses achieve consistent depth (± 0.1 mm) with sharp edge definition. The deboss is permanent — it won’t fade, peel, or scratch off because it’s physically impressed into the board, not printed on the surface.

Q: What is the production lead time for slim magnetic boxes? A: Standard lead time is 15–25 days. Pre-production samples ship in 5–7 business days. Rush production at 10–12 days is available for repeat orders with existing tooling and materials.
