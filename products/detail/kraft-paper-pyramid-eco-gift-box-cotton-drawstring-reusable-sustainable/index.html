Product Overview

The Kraft Paper Pyramid Gift Box with Cotton Drawstring Closure is a packaging object that consumers keep. Its architectural silhouette — a four-sided pyramid with a soft cotton drawstring emerging from the apex — transforms product packaging from disposable wrapper to reusable keepsake. Unlike conventional boxes that are discarded seconds after unboxing, this pyramid box becomes a jewelry holder, a desk organizer, a bathroom cotton-ball container, or simply a decorative object on a shelf — extending the brand’s presence in the consumer’s life for months or years.

Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. in our 18,000 sqm ISO 9001 & FSC certified facility with 23 years of paper packaging expertise and 1.7M boxes daily output capacity, the pyramid box is die-cut from 350gsm unbleached kraft board in a single piece with integrated fold lines. The drawstring is 100% natural cotton cord with a laser-cut kraft toggle, and both are fully removable — making the box itself 100% curbside recyclable and home compostable (EN 13432 certified materials).

Design Philosophy — Packaging That Lives On

The pyramid form factor serves multiple purposes simultaneously. The wide base provides stability for retail shelf display, the tapered sides create a natural billboard for brand graphics visible from above (important for market stalls and trade show tables), and the drawstring at the apex gives the consumer a tactile, ceremony-rich opening experience — pulling the string, watching the sides relax open, and revealing the product inside is an unboxing event that customers remember and share on social media.

Post-unboxing, the box reassembles in seconds. Pull the drawstring tight and the sides fold back into pyramid form. The single-piece die-cut construction means there are no glued seams to fail with repeated use, and the 350gsm board weight provides enough stiffness for dozens of open/close cycles without visible wear.

Sustainability Credentials

This product is designed for brands for whom sustainability is a core value, not a marketing afterthought:

  • FSC® Certified Board: All kraft board sourced from FSC Mix Credit certified mills, with full chain-of-custody documentation provided
  • Soy-Based Inks: Low-VOC, food-safe, and compatible with the paper recycling stream — no de-inking complications
  • Removable Cotton Drawstring: Separable for consumer recycling — the box goes in the paper bin, the cotton cord in textiles or compost
  • Home Compostable (EN 13432): The uncoated kraft board and soy ink break down in home compost conditions within 12–24 weeks
  • Single Material (After String Removal): No mixed-material lamination, no plastic windows in the default configuration, no metallic foil barriers to recycling
  • Flat-Packed for Shipping: Pyramid boxes ship flat and are assembled by the end user or at the point of filling — saves 60%+ on shipping volume
