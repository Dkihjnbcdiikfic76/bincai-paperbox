Product Overview

Why This Telescopic Lid Rigid Box vs Standard Two-Piece Box

The telescopic lid design isn’t an aesthetic choice — it’s an engineering decision that affects everything from brand perception to shipping logistics:

The telescopic advantage for luxury brands: When a customer opens a standard two-piece box, they complete the unboxing in one motion — lift the lid, see the product. The telescopic lid adds a 1-3 second sliding window where anticipation builds before the product is revealed. For prestige cosmetics, limited-edition spirits, and premium corporate gifts where packaging IS part of the product story, those extra seconds of reveal translate directly to perceived value. Plus, the seamless exterior surface eliminates the lid-to-base junction that limits foil stamping surface area on standard boxes — you get one uninterrupted canvas for brand expression.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for telescopic-lid rigid boxes? A: MOQ is 500–1,000 units, depending on size and foil complexity. Small orders (500–1K) carry slightly higher per-unit cost; volume pricing kicks in at 5,000+. We accommodate both boutique luxury brands launching small-batch collections and global brands with consistent quarterly orders.

Q: Can I use multiple foil colors on one box? A: Yes. Our Bobst SP 102 BMA hot foil stamper supports multi-pass registration — gold + silver, gold + holographic, rose gold + matte black — all at ±0.15mm position tolerance. Multi-color foil adds one production day per additional color and a modest per-box surcharge. We recommend limiting to 2–3 foil colors for best registration results.

Q: What surface materials are available for the exterior wrap? A: Our standard options include: 157–250gsm art paper (matte, gloss, soft-touch velvet, linen-textured, pearlescent, leatherette), specialty papers (metallic, holographic, recycled kraft), and fabric wraps (linen, silk, PU leather). Custom sourcing available — send us your desired material sample and we’ll match it.

Q: How long does tooling and sampling take? A: New telescopic-lid tooling: 7–10 working days for structural design + die fabrication. Pre-production samples with your custom printing and foil: 5–7 working days. 3D structural design proof delivered within 24 hours of order confirmation. Repeat orders with approved tooling: samples ship in 3–5 days.

Q: What’s the difference between telescopic lid and standard two-piece lid? A: A standard two-piece box has the lid lift completely off the base — the reveal is instant, and a visible seam runs around the lid-to-base junction. A telescopic lid extends partially over the base (30–120mm), creating a gradual sliding reveal and a seamless exterior canvas for uninterrupted foil stamping. Telescopic boxes also nest concentrically for flat shipping (30% better pallet density) and deliver a dramatically higher perceived value — ideal for luxury cosmetics, premium spirits, and corporate executive gifts.

Q: Can you handle large-volume orders with tight deadlines? A: Yes. With 6+ automatic rigid box wrapping lines and 220,000+ rigid boxes/day capacity across our dual-factory complex, we routinely handle 100K–500K unit orders. Lead time for volume production: 15–20 working days for new tooling, 10–15 days for repeats. Rush production (7–10 days) available with 20% surcharge.

Q: Do you ship assembled or flat? A: Both. Flat-pack shipping saves 30–40% on freight (telescopic pieces nest concentrically). Assembled shipping is available for retail-ready requirements — we insert tissue paper, ribbon, and any inserts before packing. FOB Shenzhen/Guangzhou with container and LCL options.
