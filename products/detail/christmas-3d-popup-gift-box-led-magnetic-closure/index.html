Spectacular Christmas 3D pop-up gift box with integrated LED illumination — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of manufacturing expertise (founded 2003). When opened, the box reveals a multi-layered 3D paper-engineered pop-up scene — a winter wonderland, Santa’s workshop, or a custom brand tableau — internally illuminated by a warm-white LED module (CR2032 battery, CE certified) that brings the laser-cut scene to life. The rigid box construction uses 1200gsm FSC-certified greyboard wrapped in matte-laminated art paper with magnetic closure for a satisfying reveal. Bobst SP 102 BMA laser die-cutting ensures precision-fit pop-up layers. Produced on KBA 1050 4-color offset and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses across Bincai’s 18,000 sqm dual factory (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory) in Foshan, Guangdong. ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certified. FAQ

Q: How long does the LED battery last? A: The CR2032 coin cell provides approximately 48 hours of continuous illumination or approximately 1,000 open/close cycles. Replacement batteries are widely available. Upgrade to USB-C rechargeable module for unlimited life.

Q: Can the pop-up scene be custom designed? A: Absolutely. Bincai’s 6-person structural design team creates custom pop-up scenes from your brand assets, product photography, or creative brief. First prototype in 5-7 working days. Minimum order 1,000 units for custom pop-up design.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity? A: Standard pop-up designs (winter scene, Santa workshop) start at 500 units. Custom pop-up scene design requires 1,000 units minimum. Bincai offers volume pricing tiers at 5,000, 10,000, and 50,000 units.

Q: Is the LED module safe for international shipping? A: Yes. CR2032 lithium coin cells are exempt from dangerous goods regulations under IATA/IMDG special provision A123. CE and RoHS certified. Bincai includes battery isolation tabs for shipping safety.

Q: What’s the lead time? A: Standard designs: 15-20 working days. Custom pop-up scene design: add 7 working days for prototyping. Rush orders available at 1.2× standard pricing.

Q: Can the box be used for non-Christmas promotions? A: Yes — the 3D pop-up concept works year-round for brand launches, product reveals, anniversary celebrations, and milestone events. Simply replace the Christmas-themed scene with your brand story.
