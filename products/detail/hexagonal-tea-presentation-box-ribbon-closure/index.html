The hexagonal tea presentation box is a premium rigid packaging solution designed for tea brands that demand geometric distinction. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003), this six-sided gift box combines the structural integrity of 2.5mm greyboard with the refined aesthetic of 157gsm C1S art paper wrapping. Each box features a hinged lid secured by a satin ribbon tie closure, creating an unboxing experience that matches the ceremony of premium tea. Bincai’s Foshan facility spans 18,000 sqm across dual factories, equipped with KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses, delivering 1.7 million boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

The hexagonal geometry solves a key market positioning challenge: it communicates craftsmanship and exclusivity before the lid is lifted. Unlike rectangular boxes that blend into retail shelves, the six-sided silhouette captures peripheral vision and signals a premium product tier. Bincai’s die-cutting precision (±0.15mm on all six panels) ensures consistent seam alignment and lid fit across production runs from 500 to 500,000 units.

Customization Options

  • Board Construction: Choose 1.5mm, 2.0mm, or 2.5mm greyboard thickness depending on weight requirements and desired tactile heft. 2.5mm recommended for tea collections exceeding 500g total weight
  • Paper Wrapping: 157gsm C1S art paper standard; upgrade to 200gsm or specialty substrates including textured linen, leatherette, or uncoated kraft with full FSC traceability
  • Printing: Full-color offset on KBA 1050 4-color press at 15,000 sheets/hour. Upgrade to Heidelberg 7+1 UV for instant-cure spot UV, inline aqueous coating, and metallic Pantone matching
  • Surface Finishes: Soft-touch matte lamination, high-gloss film lamination, spot UV varnish (selective gloss on logo/pattern), pearlescent coating, or soft-touch aqueous with velvet hand-feel
  • Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic foil hot-stamped with ±0.15mm registration accuracy on Bobst hot-foil press. Available on lid exterior, spine, and interior platform
  • Embossing/Debossing: Blind emboss, registered emboss with foil, or multi-level sculpted emboss for dimensional brand marks
  • Closure: Satin ribbon tie in custom Pantone colors; optional concealed N52 neodymium magnet closure for a cleaner, ribbon-free look
  • Interior: Die-cut EVA foam or flocked PET insert with individual tea tin/loose-leaf compartments; silk or velvet lining in custom colors
  • Compartments: Standard 4, 6, or 8-compartment layouts; fully custom die-cut configurations for mixed product formats (tins + pouches + tea tools)
  • Sizing: Fully custom — from compact 120mm-diameter single-tea boxes to 280mm-diameter multi-tier ceremony sets

Why Bincai?

Hexagonal packaging demands precision that few manufacturers can deliver at scale. Each face must be exactly 120° to its neighbor — a 0.5mm deviation at one seam cascades into lid misalignment, exposed board edges, and premium perception failure. Bincai solves this with engineered die-cutting tooling calibrated to ±0.15mm and a quality gate system that catches geometry drift before it multiplies. The ribbon closure adds another layer of manufacturing discipline: ribbon channels must be precisely positioned so the tie sits centered without torquing the lid. These are the details that separate a $2.50 commodity box from a $5.50 luxury presentation piece — and they are built into every Bincai hexagonal order.

The Bincai Difference:

  • Engineered Die-Cutting: Custom steel-rule dies with ±0.15mm panel tolerance maintain consistent hexagon geometry across 100,000+ unit runs
  • Dual-Press Flexibility: KBA 1050 for standard 4-color runs; Heidelberg 7+1 UV for premium metallic, spot UV, and specialty coating work — no need to switch suppliers mid-project
  • Vertical Integration: In-house foil stamping, embossing, die-cutting, and assembly under one roof eliminates multi-vendor coordination risk
  • FSC Traceability: Every shipment includes auditable chain-of-custody documentation — critical for eco-positioned tea brands marketing to EU and North American consumers
