FAQ

Q: How secure is the magnetic closure? Will it open during shipping?

A: The double-door magnetic closure is engineered for shipping security. We use N35-grade neodymium magnets (3–5N closure force per magnet) positioned to create balanced tension across both doors. Combined with the satin ribbon tie (which provides additional mechanical security), the box passes ISTA 1A drop testing (10 drops from 760mm) with doors remaining closed. The ribbon tie provides backup security — even if a severe impact overcomes the magnets, the tied ribbon prevents the doors from opening and exposing the contents.

Q: Can the interior tray fit different bottle shapes?

A: Yes. We create custom die-cut trays based on your specific bottle dimensions (height, base diameter, shoulder width). Each fragrance bottle gets an individually cradled cavity with 2–3mm clearance for easy insertion and removal while preventing rattling. For gift sets with 3–5 different bottle shapes (e.g., 50ml + 100ml + travel spray), the tray is designed with differently sized cavities. We require your bottle samples or exact 3D CAD dimensions to produce the tray tooling. Tray material options: black velvet flocking (most popular for fragrance), premium suede, or satin-covered EVA.

Q: Is the soft-touch finish durable, or does it scratch easily?

A: Our soft-touch matte lamination is a thermal PET-based film (not a spray coating) with excellent durability. It resists fingerprints, scuffing, and moderate abrasion. We test to ≥4N/15mm peel strength. However, like all matte finishes, heavy abrasion from metal objects (keys, rings) can leave marks. For fragrance boxes expected to be handled extensively on retail counters, we recommend adding spot UV coating on high-touch areas or upgrading to anti-scratch PET film lamination, which provides additional surface hardness while maintaining the matte visual.

Q: Can the ribbon color be matched to my brand’s Pantone?

A: Satin ribbon is available in 50+ standard colors covering the most common brand palettes. For exact Pantone matching, custom-dyed ribbon is available with MOQ 5,000 meters (approximately 2,500–5,000 boxes depending on box size). Custom dye lot lead time adds 10–14 working days. We recommend requesting our ribbon swatch book first — in most cases, one of our 50+ standard colors provides a close (90%+) match to brand colors without custom dye costs.

Q: What’s the lead time and can I get a prototype?

A: Standard production lead time is 12–16 working days (longer than standard boxes due to the double-door assembly and tray fabrication). Free pre-production samples are provided within 7–10 working days — these are hand-assembled prototypes using your artwork, foil, and ribbon selection. For fragrance launches with strict deadlines, rush production (10–12 working days) is available with a 15% surcharge. We strongly recommend ordering prototypes first to validate the door action, magnet feel, and ribbon color.
