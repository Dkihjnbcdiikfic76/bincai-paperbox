Product Overview

This eco-friendly kraft tuck-top mailer box by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing delivers sustainable packaging without compromising on print quality or structural integrity. Made from 100% recycled kraft board (350gsm–400gsm) with FSC Chain-of-Custody certification, it features soy-based ink printing for a fully biodegradable packaging solution. The tuck-top closure eliminates the need for tapes or adhesives, simplifying both assembly and recycling. With 23 years of manufacturing expertise and daily output of 1.7 million boxes from our 18,000 sqm facility, Bincai serves e-commerce brands, subscription box companies, and retailers seeking verified sustainable packaging. Unlike many “eco” boxes that sacrifice print vibrancy, Bincai’s Heidelberg 7+1 UV press with soy-based inks achieves rich, solid colors on kraft surfaces — so your brand doesn’t look washed out. The natural kraft aesthetic resonates with today’s conscious consumers, communicating environmental responsibility before they even open the box. Available with optional FSC, REACH, and RoHS documentation for your compliance records.
