Product Overview

Bincai’s magnetic closure photo album presentation box combines archival-grade protection with the tactile luxury of linen bookcloth. The 2mm rigid greyboard core is fully wrapped in textured Belgian-style linen over a hidden neodymium magnetic closure — invisible from the outside, satisfyingly firm to open. Inside, acid-free pH-neutral archival paper lines every surface, with a custom-fit album cradle that prevents shifting during transport. Designed for wedding photographers, fine art studios, corporate yearbook publishers, and luxury brands that treat their printed work as an heirloom. Manufactured on Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses with precision die-cutting in our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility. 23 years of luxury packaging expertise.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: Heidelberg 7+1 UV offset — precise color matching for linen-wrapped panels
  • Board: 2mm rigid greyboard — double-wall construction at hinge points
  • Wrap: 180gsm textured linen bookcloth — 12 standard colorways, custom Pantone available
  • Magnets: N35 neodymium disc magnets, 8mm diameter ×4 — hidden between board layers
  • Interior Lining: 120gsm acid-free archival paper, pH 7.0–8.5, lignin-free, buffered
  • Album Cradle: Custom die-cut greyboard insert wrapped in matching archival paper
  • Daily Output: 1.7 million color boxes (post-2025 smart factory expansion)
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody, PAT (Photographic Activity Test) compliant materials
