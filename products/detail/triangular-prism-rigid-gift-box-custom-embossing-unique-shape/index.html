Customization Options

  • Triangular Construction: Precision 60° internal angles with ±0.1° tolerance. Available in equilateral (all sides equal) or isosceles configurations. Rounded corners (R5–R20mm) or sharp corners. Standard side lengths: 120mm, 150mm, 180mm, 220mm. Custom dimensions available.
  • Board & Wrap: 2.5mm or 3.0mm greyboard for structural integrity. 157g or 200g C2S art paper wrap with gloss, matte, or soft-touch lamination. FSC-certified board available. Full-wrap printing across all three faces with seamless pattern continuity.
  • Printing: Full-color CMYK offset (KBA 1050) + UV spot (Heidelberg 7+1). Geometric patterns, marble textures, metallic gradients, wood grain, or custom artwork. Pantone spot color matching with ΔE ≤ 2.0.
  • Surface Finishes: Hot foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic) on lid face with ±0.15mm registration. Deep embossing/debossing for logos and patterns — up to 0.8mm relief on 2.5mm board. Spot UV for selective gloss. Soft-touch velvet-feel lamination.
  • Closure: Three N52 neodymium magnets (8mm dia.) at vertex positions for balanced closure. Hidden magnet mounting with fabric cover. Lid-to-base alignment within 0.5mm. Optional hidden hinge for flip-top opening.
  • Interior: Full velvet flocking (0.8mm pile) in black, white, grey, navy, burgundy, or custom color. Three-compartment die-cut foam insert for product trios. Single large cavity option for single-item presentation. Satin ribbon pull-tab for lid opening.
  • Sizing: Equilateral: 120/150/180/220mm side length. Height: 40–80mm. Isosceles configurations: custom aspect ratios. All dimensions customizable with one-time die setup.

Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — greyboard grammage (±5%), velvet pile density, magnet N52 grade verification (gauss meter), art paper whiteness
  2. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0, Prinect registration monitoring on Heidelberg
  3. Triangular Die-Cutting — precision angle check (60° ±0.1° for equilateral), dimensional verification ±0.5mm per side, crease depth consistency
  4. Foil Stamping & Embossing — registration ±0.15mm on triangular faces, foil adhesion tape test, embossing depth ±0.1mm
  5. Interior Assembly — velvet bond peel test per ASTM D903, foam insert cavity fit check, magnet polarity verification, vertex alignment gauge
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.5 (critical) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4, full functional test, color match under D65, lid alignment ≤ 0.5mm
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — random sample pull Level II, packaging integrity, barcode/label verification

FAQ

Q: Can the triangular box be produced in non-equilateral shapes? A: Yes — we offer isosceles and custom triangular configurations. Any triangle geometry is achievable with a one-time die setup. We recommend providing a dimensioned sketch or CAD file for custom shapes. Standard equilateral sizes (120/150/180/220mm side length) have shorter lead times and no die setup fee for first orders over 1,000 units.

Q: How does the three-magnet closure compare to rectangular box closures? A: The three vertex-mounted magnets create a balanced closure that pulls the lid into alignment from all three corners simultaneously. This self-centering design is more precise than two-magnet rectangular closures and prevents lid shift during transit. Each magnet is individually tested for ≥ 4.5N pull force.

Q: Can the interior be divided into compartments? A: Yes — the triangular footprint naturally accommodates a 3-compartment layout, with one section per vertex. We offer die-cut foam inserts with custom cavity shapes, velvet-lined fixed dividers, and removable tray options. Single-cavity and 6-compartment (3 upper + 3 lower) configurations are also available.

Q: What are the minimum order and lead times? A: MOQ is 500 units for standard equilateral sizes with existing dies. Custom triangular configurations may require 1,000 units to amortize one-time die costs. Sample turnaround: 5–7 days. Production: 12–16 days. Rush orders of 300+ units available at 7-day turnaround with surcharge.

Q: Is the triangular box suitable for products other than gifts? A: Absolutely — the triangular format is particularly effective for retail shelf differentiation. We’ve produced triangular boxes for perfume trios, tea samplers, chocolate assortments, candle sets, tech accessories, and corporate award packaging. The unique shape naturally attracts consumer attention in any retail environment.
