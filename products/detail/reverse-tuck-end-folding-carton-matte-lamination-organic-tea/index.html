Product Overview

Bincai’s reverse tuck end (RTE) folding carton is purpose-built for organic and premium tea brands that demand packaging as pure as their product. Constructed from 350gsm SBS C1S solid bleached sulfate board — renowned for its bright white surface and exceptional print fidelity — the carton features matte lamination with selective spot UV accents that catch light on shelf. The reverse tuck end closure provides secure retention without adhesives, while the interior food-grade aqueous coating ensures product safety without chemical migration. An optional window patch using PLA compostable film (certified to EN 13432) transforms the carton into a fully compostable package. Printed on KBA 1050 and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses in our ISO 15378 GMP-certified facility — the pharmaceutical-grade standard that guarantees the highest level of cleanliness and contamination control for food-contact packaging.

FAQ

Q: What makes ISO 15378 GMP different? A: ISO 15378 applies pharmaceutical Good Manufacturing Practices to packaging — including cleanroom protocols, contamination control, traceability, and batch documentation. Essential for food-contact packaging that demands medical-grade cleanliness.

Q: Is the PLA window truly compostable? A: Yes. Our PLA film is certified to EN 13432 (industrial composting) and TÜV OK Compost HOME (home composting). It breaks down within 12 weeks in industrial facilities.

Q: Can I use recycled board? A: Yes, we offer FSC recycled SBS board with up to 100% post-consumer content. Note that recycled board has slightly lower brightness (~85% ISO vs 92% for virgin).

Q: What is the minimum order for custom die-cut shapes? A: Custom die-cut cartons start at 3,000 units due to the die-making cost. Standard RTE with custom print starts at 1,000 units.

Q: How do you ensure food safety? A: All food-contact cartons are produced in our ISO 15378 GMP zone with dedicated equipment, regular migration testing, and full batch traceability documentation.

Q: Do you offer design services? A: Yes, our in-house design team can create artwork from your brief or refine existing designs for print optimization — included free with production orders.
