Product Overview

The Bincai Kraft Paper Gift Box with Natural Hemp Rope Handle represents our commitment to plastic-free, fully biodegradable luxury packaging. Every component — from the 350gsm FSC-certified uncoated kraft board to the 5mm natural hemp rope handle to the soy-based CMYK inks — is chosen for end-of-life compostability and recyclability in standard paper streams. The hemp rope handle is threaded through reinforced die-cut slots (no metal eyelets, no plastic rivets), creating a rustic-meets-premium aesthetic that resonates with today’s eco-conscious consumers. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified with 23 years of expertise — this box is a bestseller for sustainable fashion brands, organic skincare companies, and artisan food producers across 60+ export markets. Our dual factories produce 1.7M+ boxes daily, with dedicated eco-production lines using water-based adhesives and renewable energy.

FAQ

Q: Are all components of this kraft box truly compostable and plastic-free? A: Yes — every component is certified plastic-free. The 350gsm kraft board is FSC-certified and recyclable in standard paper streams. The 5mm hemp rope is natural plant fiber (tensile strength ≥50kg). Soy-based CMYK inks contain ≥20% renewable content with VOC <3%. Water-based PVA adhesive is formaldehyde-free and EN 13432 compliant. No metal eyelets, no plastic rivets, no plastic lamination.

Q: What printing quality can be achieved on uncoated kraft board? A: Our KBA Rapida 105 4-color press achieves excellent results on uncoated kraft with ΔE ≤ 2.5 color consistency. For brighter, more vibrant colors, we offer white-top kraft that provides a coated print surface while maintaining recyclability. The Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV can apply water-based varnish for additional scuff resistance without compromising compostability.

Q: How strong is the hemp rope handle — will it support heavy products? A: The 5mm 3-strand natural hemp rope has a tensile breaking strength of ≥50kg. Each handle slot is reinforced with an internal kraft patch and tested to ≥15kg pull force without tear initiation. We test at 3× the rated weight (100% inline testing) — more than sufficient for organic skincare sets, artisan food hampers, and fashion accessories.

Q: What is the MOQ and lead time for custom kraft boxes with hemp handles? A: Standard MOQ is 500 units per design with custom printing, handle assembly, and sizing. Standard sizes are available from 300 units. Lead time is 10-15 working days from artwork approval, with rush 7-day service available. Free physical samples including hemp handle assembly are provided within 7-10 working days. Our 1.7M daily capacity ensures on-time delivery even for large orders.

Q: What certifications support sustainability claims for my brand? A: Bincai holds ISO 9001:2015 quality management, FSC Chain-of-Custody (FSC-C147399) for traceable sustainable sourcing, and Sedex SMETA for ethical manufacturing practices. We provide full FSC documentation to support your corporate sustainability reporting, ESG disclosures, and consumer-facing eco claims.
