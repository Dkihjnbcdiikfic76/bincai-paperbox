Product Overview

The Bincai Kraft Paper Shoulder Box with Magnetic Snap Closure bridges the gap between uncompromising eco-credentials and luxury gift presentation — proving that sustainability and premium branding are not mutually exclusive. The box body is crafted from FSC-certified kraft liner (250gsm) wrapped over 1600gsm recycled greyboard, creating a sturdy rigid structure with the warm, organic texture of natural kraft. The magnetic snap closure (concealed NdFeB magnets) provides the premium tactile experience buyers expect from gift packaging, while soy-based ink printing (1-2 colors) keeps the entire box 100% recyclable in standard paper streams — no de-lamination, no plastic film separation, no landfill. Inside, custom kraft card dividers (die-cut from the same FSC stock) organize multiple products — tea sachets, honey jars, soap bars, candle tins — into a beautifully organized gift set. At Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certified with 23 years of manufacturing excellence — every box is produced on our dual-factory Heidelberg 7+1 UV and KBA Rapida 105 press lines, delivering 220,000+ boxes daily with full FSC chain-of-custody documentation.
