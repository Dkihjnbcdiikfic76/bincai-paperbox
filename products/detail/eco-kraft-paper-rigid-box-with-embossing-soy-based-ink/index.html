Why Soy-Based Ink Matters

  • Low VOCs: Soy inks emit less than 10% of the volatile organic compounds of petroleum-based inks — better for workers, better for the planet
  • Biodegradable: Soy ink breaks down naturally in composting environments, unlike petroleum inks that persist as micro-contaminants
  • Food-Safe: Certified for indirect food contact (FDA 21 CFR 176.170/176.180, EU 1935/2004) — ideal for organic food, tea, and confectionery packaging
  • Color Vibrancy: Soy ink produces brighter, more saturated colors on uncoated kraft paper than petroleum inks — critical for natural-surface branding
  • Recyclability: Soy inks de-ink more easily during paper recycling, yielding cleaner recycled pulp with less chemical treatment
  • Renewable: Soy-based ink uses renewable agricultural resources versus finite petroleum feedstocks
