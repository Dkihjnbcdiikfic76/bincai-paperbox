FAQ

Q: What’s the difference between BC-flute double-wall and standard single-wall corrugated for TV shipping? A: BC-flute double-wall combines B-flute (3mm, high flat-crush resistance for printing surface) with C-flute (4mm, high compression strength for stacking) to create a 6–7mm combined structure. Compared to single-wall C-flute (4mm), BC-flute provides roughly 2.5× the edge crush resistance (44+ vs 32 ECT) and 3× the puncture resistance — critical for flat-screen TVs where even minor panel flex during transit can cause LCD damage. Single-wall is suitable for small monitors under 27"; anything larger should use double-wall.

Q: Are the foam inserts custom-designed for each TV model? A: Yes, every EPS/EPE foam cushion set is custom-engineered to your exact TV or monitor dimensions — bezel width, stand attachment points, curvature, and weight distribution are all accounted for in the CAD design. We request a 3D CAD file or physical sample of your product to create the foam tooling. The foam cushions are die-cut from block EPS/EPE to within ±1mm tolerance, with corner bumpers, base cushions, and top caps that immobilize the TV in all six axes during transit. Tooling cost for custom foam molds starts at approximately $500, amortized over the production run.

Q: Can these boxes be printed with full-color graphics for retail shelf display? A: Yes, through litho-lamination — we print high-resolution offset graphics on 157gsm coated paper on our Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV press, then laminate the printed sheet onto BC-flute corrugated board. This creates a retail-ready shipping box that doubles as point-of-purchase display packaging. Alternatively, we offer 3-color flexo post-print for simpler branding at a lower cost point. Litho-laminated finish is available for MOQ 1,000+ units.

Q: How is the box tested to ensure a TV survives international shipping? A: Every design undergoes ISTA 3A testing, which includes atmospheric preconditioning (temperature/humidity), 10-drop sequence from various orientations (including corner and edge drops from 460mm for packages under 23 kg), random vibration testing (truck and air profiles), and top-load compression testing. We also offer Amazon FFP Tier 3 certification testing and ASTM D4169 distribution cycle testing. Test reports are provided with every order, and recurring batch testing is conducted every 5,000 units to ensure ongoing compliance.
