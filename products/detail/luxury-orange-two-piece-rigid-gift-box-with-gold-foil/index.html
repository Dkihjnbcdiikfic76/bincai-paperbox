Product Overview

A striking two-piece rigid gift box with bold orange art paper wrap and elegant gold foil branding. The lid-and-base construction provides a premium unboxing moment with satisfying friction-fit closure. Manufactured in our 18,000m² ISO 9001 certified factory using Heidelberg 7+1 UV press for precise color reproduction and Bobst flatbed hot stamping for crisp metallic foil detailing. The vibrant orange exterior with gold foil creates an upscale, memorable presentation ideal for luxury retail, corporate gifting, and seasonal promotions.

Applications

The orange-and-gold two-piece rigid box excels across industries where premium presentation drives brand perception:

  • Luxury Fashion: Scarves, ties, leather goods, eyewear, and accessories from brands that use orange as a signature color
  • Corporate Gifting: Year-end appreciation gifts, employee milestone awards, VIP client packages — gold foil communicates prestige
  • Cosmetics & Beauty: Fragrance gift sets, skincare collections, premium makeup palettes — orange conveys warmth and energy
  • Jewelry: Bracelets, watches, and pendant sets — soft velvet flocking interior upgrade available to protect delicate surfaces
  • Confectionery & Gourmet: Artisan chocolate collections, premium tea sets, limited-edition holiday treats — food-safe interior lining option
  • Promotional Kits: Product launch media kits, influencer PR packages, brand collaboration co-branded boxes
  • Seasonal Campaigns: Autumn/Halloween-themed launches, Lunar New Year gift sets, Diwali festival packaging — orange is culturally versatile

Features

  • Two-Piece Construction: Separate lid and base — premium friction-fit closure, no magnets or hardware needed
  • Bold Orange Finish: Vibrant Pantone-matched orange on 157gsm art paper wrap over 2.0mm greyboard core
  • Gold Foil Stamping: Hot-stamped gold foil logo/text via Bobst flatbed press — crisp edge definition with embossing option
  • Matte Lamination: Soft matte finish protects against fingerprints and scuffing while preserving color vibrancy
  • Custom Dimensions: Available from 100×100×50mm to 300×250×120mm
  • Inner Options: White art paper lining, color-matched lining, custom printed interior, or velvet flocking upgrade

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: Heidelberg Speedmaster 7+1 UV + KBA 1050 4-color — Pantone-matched orange with ±0.15mm registration
  • Board: 2.0mm rigid greyboard core (1200–1600gsm) wrapped in 157gsm art paper
  • Foil Stamping: Bobst flatbed hot stamping press, gold foil on exterior lid — embossing die available
  • Lamination: Matte OPP lamination both sides — fingerprint-resistant, scuff-protected
  • Assembly: Hand-assembled lid-to-base fitting with ±0.5mm clearance for smooth open/close
  • Daily Output: 220,000+ rigid gift boxes across all categories
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody, SGS material testing, REACH-compliant

Sustainability Commitment

  • FSC Chain-of-Custody Certified: All greyboard and art paper sourced from responsibly managed forests with full supply chain traceability
  • Recycled Content Options: Up to 100% post-consumer recycled greyboard available on request — indistinguishable performance from virgin board
  • Water-Based Adhesives: PVA cold emulsion glue with zero VOC emissions — safe for food-contact indirect packaging
  • Soy-Based Inks: Vegetable-derived offset inks replace petroleum-based alternatives — lower environmental footprint with equal print quality
  • Design for Recyclability: Mono-material construction (paper + board + minimal metal foil) — fully recyclable in standard paper waste streams (foil under 5% total surface area)
  • Waste Reduction: Production scrap baled and returned to pulping mills; cardboard offcuts repurposed into internal partitions and void fill
