Eco kraft folding carton with soy-based ink — a fully sustainable retail packaging solution manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. With 23 years of folding carton expertise, ISO 9001 & FSC certifications, and soy-based ink printing on KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses, we produce 1.7 million eco-friendly boxes daily from our 18,000 sqm factory (10,000+8,000 smart factory) in Foshan. Every carton is biodegradable, recyclable, and printed with renewable soy inks — the responsible choice for brands committed to sustainability.

Applications

  • Organic and natural skincare / cosmetic packaging
  • Artisan soap, candle, and handmade product boxes
  • Health food, supplement, and wellness product cartons
  • Eco-conscious apparel and fashion accessory packaging
  • Tea, coffee, and specialty food retail boxes
  • Sustainable gift packaging for corporate and promotional use
  • Zero-waste store and refill product packaging
  • CBD, hemp, and natural remedy product boxes

Customization Options

  • Carton Style: Straight tuck end, reverse tuck end, auto-lock bottom, seal end, sleeve-and-tray, gable-top
  • Paper: Natural brown kraft (uncoated), bleached white kraft, coated one-side kraft, recycled kraft (post-consumer waste)
  • Inks: Soy-based CMYK offset (USDA BioPreferred), water-based flexo, algae-based experimental inks
  • Finishes: Water-based matte/gloss coating (compostable), soft-touch aqueous, blind emboss/deboss
  • Window: PET, rPET (recycled), PLA (compostable), or windowless for full recyclability
  • Closure: Tuck flap, self-locking bottom, magnetic snap (sustainable magnet sourcing), cotton string tie
  • Add-Ons: Jute string handle, cotton ribbon, seed-paper hang tag, QR code for sustainability story
  • Size: 60×40×30mm mini to 400×300×150mm large format

Production Specifications

  • Presses: KBA Rapida 105 4-color + Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV
  • Paper Stock: 250–400gsm FSC kraft, chain-of-custody certified to source forest
  • Ink System: Soy-based CMYK inks (20–30% soy oil content), VOC-free, heavy-metal-free pigments
  • Coating: Water-based aqueous (no solvents) — matte, gloss, or satin finish; fully repulpable
  • Die-Cutting: Rotary and flatbed with ±0.3mm tolerance; steel-rule dies re-sharpened for clean kraft cuts
  • Gluing: Water-based biodegradable adhesive; auto-bottom gluer lines for efficient assembly
  • Daily Capacity: 1.7M folding cartons; dedicated eco-line for kraft and soy-ink production
  • Lead Time: 10–15 days new design; 7–10 days repeat orders

Quality Assurance

  • ISO 9001:2015 — documented quality gates from paper receiving through final inspection
  • FSC Chain-of-Custody — full traceability, certificate available for every order
  • Ink Adhesion: Tape test (3M #810) — zero ink lift-off on uncoated and coated kraft
  • Rub Resistance: Sutherland rub test — 50 cycles, no visible wear on printed surface
  • Color Consistency: Spectrophotometer monitoring throughout print runs (ΔE ≤2.0)
  • Structural Integrity: Box compression and drop tests for e-commerce readiness
  • Odor: VOC compliance per GB/T — zero solvent odor, suitable for food-adjacent products
  • Free pre-production samples with full eco-decoration — 5–7 working day turnaround
