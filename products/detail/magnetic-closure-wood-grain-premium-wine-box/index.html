Magnetic closure wood-grain premium wine and spirit box — the definitive packaging solution for luxury beverage brands, from Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003, 23 years). Engineered with a 3mm heavy-duty rigid greyboard structure wrapped in realistic wood-grain textured paper, this magnetic closure box delivers the gravitas of a wooden humidor at a fraction of the cost. Six wood-grain finishes are available: American white oak, French walnut, Honduran mahogany, cherry wood, African ebony, and Scandinavian pine. Each features high-definition wood-grain texturing with authentic knot and grain reproduction, finished with a matte protective laminate. The N52 neodymium magnetic closure (4 magnets) provides a premium snap-close experience while the die-cut bottle cradle insert with satin ribbon lift securely holds standard 750ml bottles, magnum 1.5L formats, and custom spirits bottle shapes. Produced on KBA Rapida 105 4-color and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV offset presses with Bobst SP 102 BMA foil stamping for brand logos and vintage markings. Our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility delivers 220,000+ rigid boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certifications.

Quality Control — 7-Stage Inspection

  1. Material Inspection: Wood-grain paper color consistency (spectrophotometer ΔE ≤ 1.5), greyboard caliper ±0.15mm, magnet pull-force testing
  2. Printing QC: Heidelberg inline spectrophotometer, Pantone ΔE ≤ 2.0, 200-line screen ruling, wood-grain pattern registration
  3. Foil Stamping QC: Bobst registration ±0.15mm, foil adhesion test, coverage inspection under 10× magnification
  4. Box Assembly QC: Corner wrapping tension (±5% tolerance), magnet alignment gauge (±0.3mm), hinge integrity test
  5. Bottle Fit Test: 100% dimensional verification with master bottle template, cradle fit check, ribbon lift pull test (50N)
  6. Final Inspection: 100% visual under 5000K daylight, surface defect detection, closure force consistency
  7. Packaging QC: Individual polybag + corner protectors, master carton drop test (1.2m ISTA 1A), barcode verification

Sustainability

Bincai’s wood-grain wine box uses FSC-certified greyboard and paper, soy-based printing inks, and water-based adhesives. The realistic wood-grain texture eliminates the need for actual wood veneer, reducing deforestation impact while delivering an authentic wood aesthetic. Available with recycled-content greyboard (up to 70% PCR) for brands with aggressive sustainability targets.

FAQ

Q: What bottle sizes does this box accommodate? Standard 750ml wine/spirit bottles, magnum 1.5L, and custom shapes. We engineer the die-cut cradle to your exact bottle dimensions — simply provide a bottle sample or CAD file.

Q: Can I get a real wood box instead? Bincai’s wood-grain textured paper delivers an authentic wood aesthetic at 40–60% lower cost than solid wood, with lighter shipping weight and no phytosanitary certificate requirements for international export.

Q: Are the wood-grain patterns realistic? Yes — our HD wood-grain texturing reproduces authentic grain patterns, knots, and color variations. Most clients cannot distinguish it from real wood veneer at arm’s length.

Q: Can I mix wood-grain styles in one order? Yes — mix up to 3 wood-grain styles in a single MOQ of 1,500 units (500 per style).

Q: Is the magnetic closure strong enough for heavy bottles? Absolutely. Four N52 neodymium magnets provide 4.8kg of combined holding force — more than sufficient for a 1.5kg full wine bottle.

Q: Do you offer samples? Yes — pre-production samples in 7–10 days with your branding. Sample fee credited against production order.
