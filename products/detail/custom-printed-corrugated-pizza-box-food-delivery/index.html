Product Overview

Our custom printed corrugated pizza boxes are engineered for food delivery brands that demand both performance and presentation. Manufactured in our 18,000m² ISO 9001 certified factory, each box combines structural integrity with vibrant brand printing — keeping pizzas hot, fresh, and perfectly presented from oven to doorstep. B-Flute, E-Flute, and BC-Flute options let you balance insulation, rigidity, and cost.

Design Features

  • Ventilation Design: Built-in vent holes prevent steam buildup, keeping crusts crispy during transport
  • Lock-Tab Closure: Secure self-locking tabs eliminate the need for tape or adhesives
  • Grease-Resistant Liner: Optional PE-coated or clay-coated interior prevents oil bleed-through
  • Stackable Construction: Uniform sizing and reinforced corners for stable stacking during delivery runs
  • Custom Window Cutouts: Optional die-cut windows showcase the pizza without opening the box

Applications

  • Restaurant and pizzeria takeout/delivery
  • Food truck and catering services
  • Frozen pizza retail packaging
  • Food delivery platforms (Uber Eats, DoorDash, Meituan)
  • Promotional and branded pizza events
  • Meal kit subscription boxes

7-Stage QC Gate

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — corrugated board ECT verification, liner grammage, food-grade ink certification (FDA/EU)
  2. In-Process Printing — CMYK color check every 500 sheets on KBA 1050, food-grade ink traceability per batch
  3. Die-Cutting & Scoring — slot/vent precision ±0.5mm, score line depth for clean fold
  4. Coating Application — grease-resistant coating coverage check, UV cure verification on Heidelberg 7+1
  5. Assembly QC — lock-tab engagement force, vent hole alignment, window seal integrity (if applicable)
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.5 (critical) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4 under D65 lighting
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — random sample pull, full functional test, food-contact compliance documentation
