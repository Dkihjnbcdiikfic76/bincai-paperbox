Product Overview

The Bincai High-Impact E-Commerce Corrugated Mailer delivers retail-grade unboxing experiences for direct-to-consumer brands. Each mailer combines full-color water-based flexographic printing with a consumer-friendly tear-strip opening and peel-and-seal closure — no tape, no knives, no frustration. Available in single-wall B-flute and double-wall BC-flute constructions, these mailers protect products through the most demanding delivery networks while presenting vibrant brand graphics. Manufactured at Bincai’s 18,000 sqm Foshan facility with 23 years of packaging expertise, 1.7 million daily output, ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified.
