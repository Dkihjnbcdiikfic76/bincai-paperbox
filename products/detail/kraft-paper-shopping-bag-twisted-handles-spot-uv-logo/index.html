FAQ

Q: Can I get my brand logo in spot UV or foil stamping?

A: Yes. Spot UV logo highlighting via the Heidelberg 7+1 UV press creates a glossy, tactile logo that stands out against the matte kraft surface — perfect for minimalist brand designs. Foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold, copper) via the Bobst SP 102 BMA adds a premium metallic finish. We recommend vector artwork (AI/EPS/PDF) for best spot UV and foil plate fabrication results. Plate fabrication takes 3–5 working days.

Q: What’s the carrying capacity of the bag?

A: The reinforced cardboard bottom insert and side gusset construction are rated to 5–8kg depending on size and kraft grade. For heavier loads (8–12kg), we recommend upgrading to 350gsm kraft with a 2.0mm greyboard base. All bags undergo handle pull testing at G6 and ISTA 1A drop testing with a 3kg payload at G7.

Q: Are the bags truly plastic-free and recyclable?

A: Yes — 100%. The bag body is FSC-certified kraft paper, the handles are twisted kraft paper (same stock), the bottom insert is greyboard cardboard, and the adhesive is water-based PVA (VOC-free, biodegradable). The entire bag can be disposed of in standard paper/cardboard recycling streams. No plastic laminations, nylon handles, or synthetic coatings are used. We provide FSC documentation for every shipment to support your sustainability marketing claims.

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity for custom sizes?

A: MOQ is 500 units for standard sizes using our existing tooling. For custom dimensions requiring new die fabrication, MOQ is 1,000 units. We can also produce mixed-size sets (e.g., 250 small + 250 medium + 500 large) within a single order — ideal for retail brands needing multiple bag sizes. Free pre-production samples are provided within 5–7 working days.

Q: How do the bags ship — are they pre-assembled or flat-packed?

A: All paper bags ship flat-packed for maximum freight efficiency — reducing shipping volume by 80%+ compared to pre-assembled bags. Each bag is scored and pre-creased for easy one-step assembly by retail staff or fulfillment centers. The flat-packed design also minimizes warehousing space. We include assembly instructions with every shipment. Assembly time is approximately 10–15 seconds per bag once staff are familiar with the process.
