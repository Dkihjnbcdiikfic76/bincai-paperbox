Product Overview

This heavy-duty corrugated industrial parts shipper is engineered for the most demanding B2B logistics: export-grade machinery components, automotive OEM parts, precision tooling, and industrial equipment that requires absolute protection during international freight. Constructed from BC-flute double-wall corrugated on Guangdong Bincai’s BHS 2.5m Corrugator line, the shipper delivers ECT 44+ lb/in edge crush strength and 275+ psi burst resistance. A custom CNC-cut PE or EPE foam partition system secures each component in its own isolated cavity, while the integrated pallet base enables direct fork-lift handling without secondary palletization. Each shipper is ISTA 3E certified for parcel delivery and LTL freight, and manufactured under ISO 9001:2015 quality management with FSC Chain-of-Custody materials. With 23 years of corrugated manufacturing expertise (founded 2003), Bincai produces 1.7 million boxes daily from an 18,000 sqm integrated facility in Foshan, Guangdong.

Quality Control — 15 Checkpoints

Every industrial shipper passes through this 15-stage QC gate system:

  1. Linerboard Inspection — Basis weight ±3%, moisture 6–8%, burst factor verification
  2. Corrugator QC — Flute height ±0.1mm, bond strength ≥60 N/m (pin adhesion test)
  3. ECT Testing — Edge crush test per TAPPI T-839, minimum 44 lb/in (BC-flute)
  4. Burst Testing — Mullen burst per TAPPI T-810, minimum 275 psi
  5. Box Compression — BCT (Box Compression Test) per TAPPI T-804, meets or exceeds design spec
  6. Print Registration — Flexo/litho label alignment ±1.0mm, color consistency
  7. Die-Cutting — Dimensions ±1.0mm, slot/score clean with zero tearing
  8. Foam CNC Accuracy — Cavity dimensions ±0.5mm per component, zero interference fit
  9. Glue Bond — Hot-melt adhesive bond ≥8 N/25mm at manufacturer joints
  10. Pallet Base — 4-way entry verified, base flatness within 2mm across span
  11. Stacking Test — 3-high loaded stacking, 24-hour dwell, zero deflection >5mm
  12. ISTA 3E Protocol — Drop test (10-drop sequence), vibration test, compression test
  13. Moisture Resistance — Cascade liner 30-min Cobb test, ≤30 g/m² water absorption
  14. AQL Sampling — ISO 2859-1, Level II, AQL 2.5 for major defects
  15. Pack-Out Audit — Final 100% visual, foam insert integrity, pallet orientation verified
