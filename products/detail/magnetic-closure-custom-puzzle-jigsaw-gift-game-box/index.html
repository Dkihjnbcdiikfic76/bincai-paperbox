Product Overview

Premium magnetic closure puzzle gift box for jigsaw puzzles, board games, and collectible toys — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a Diamond Member on Made-in-China.com. This book-style magnetic flap box features full-color CMYK exterior printing with game board artwork or custom brand graphics, a precision die-cut EVA foam insert with puzzle piece cavities, and a satin ribbon pull for elegant opening. The rigid greyboard construction (1200–1600gsm, custom-specified per box size and weight requirements) protects puzzle pieces during retail display, storage, and e-commerce shipping while the N35–N52 neodymium magnetic closure keeps contents secure. Produced on KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses across our 18,000 sqm ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certified dual-factory complex (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory, completed 2025). Daily output of 1.7 million color-printed boxes + 220,000+ rigid gift boxes. 23 years of Guangdong manufacturing excellence since 2003, serving 200+ global brands across 60+ countries.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity for custom puzzle boxes? A: Standard MOQ is 500–1,000 units for full custom boxes. Trial orders of 300 units accepted for single-color or foil-only designs on stock sizes. Volume pricing tiers at 1,000, 3,000, 5,000, and 10,000+ units.

Q: Can you match my game’s box artwork and brand colors precisely? A: Yes. We use inline spectrophotometers on Heidelberg presses maintaining ΔE ≤ 2.0 color consistency across the entire production run. We can match any Pantone Solid Coated or Uncoated color, and provide color drawdowns for approval before production.

Q: What foam density should I choose for my puzzle pieces? A: EVA 30–35 Shore A (soft) for lightweight cardboard puzzle pieces; 40–45 Shore A (medium) for wooden or thick puzzle pieces; 50–55 Shore A (firm) for heavy game components. Our engineers recommend density based on your piece weight and count.

Q: How durable is the magnetic closure over time? A: Our magnetic closure boxes are tested to 5,000+ open-close cycles without hinge separation or magnet weakening. The N42–N52 neodymium magnets maintain ≥95% hold force after 10 years of normal use. We can upgrade to reinforced hinge construction for frequently-opened game boxes.

Q: Can you add a display window so customers see the puzzle image without opening? A: Yes. We offer die-cut windows with 0.3–0.5mm PET film or PLA biodegradable film inserts. The window can show the full puzzle image, a branded teaser, or a sample of included components.

Q: What’s the typical development timeline from concept to delivery? A: Structural design: 2–3 days. 3D render: 24 hours. Pre-production sample: 5–7 days. Production: 10–15 days for new artwork, 7–10 days for repeats. Rush service available at 5–7 days with surcharge.

Q: Are your materials safe for children’s products? A: Yes. All inks are soy-based and compliant with EN 71-3 (toy safety, migration of elements), CONEG (heavy metal limits), and FDA 21 CFR for indirect food contact. Water-based adhesives are solvent-free. FSC certification ensures responsible paper sourcing. We can provide full compliance documentation.
