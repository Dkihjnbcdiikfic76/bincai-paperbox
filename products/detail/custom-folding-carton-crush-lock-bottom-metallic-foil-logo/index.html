Product Overview

Our custom crush-lock bottom folding carton combines efficient assembly with premium branding. The patented crush-lock base snaps into place in seconds — no glue, no tape, no machine setup — dramatically reducing fulfillment labor while maintaining a rigid, flat-bottom structure that stands securely on retail shelves. Manufactured on our KBA 1050 4-color press with Heidelberg 7+1 UV for vibrant CMYK reproduction, each carton features precision hot foil stamping for your metallic logo and soft-touch matte lamination for a luxurious tactile finish. With 10,000 sqm of production space and daily output capacity exceeding 1.7 million boxes, Bincai delivers consistent quality at scale.

Design Features

  • Crush-Lock Bottom: Patented interlocking base design assembles in under 3 seconds with a single push — zero adhesive required
  • Hot Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic metallic foil logo and decorative elements
  • Soft-Touch Matte Lamination: Premium velvet-like surface feel that elevates perceived product value
  • Straight Tuck Top: Clean top closure with or without dust flaps for secure product retention
  • Die-Cut Precision: CNC die-cutting ensures consistent fold lines, clean edges, and reliable crush-lock engagement
  • Full CMYK Offset: KBA 1050 4-color offset with Heidelberg 7+1 UV for photo-quality graphics
  • Flat-Bottom Shelf Stability: Rigid structural base stands upright unaided — ideal for retail display

Applications

  • Cosmetics and skincare product packaging (serums, creams, masks)
  • Premium candle and home fragrance boxes
  • Artisanal soap and bath product cartons
  • Small electronics and tech accessories packaging
  • Confectionery and gourmet food gift cartons
  • Boutique fashion accessories and jewelry packaging

Quality Assurance

  • ISO 9001:2015 — Documented QC checkpoints at printing (color density ±0.05), die-cutting (±0.2mm tolerance), foil stamping (temperature ±2°C, ±0.15mm registration), lamination, and final assembly
  • FSC Chain-of-Custody — Full traceability from certified forest to finished carton; available FSC on-product labeling
  • Foil Adhesion Test — 3M 610 tape peel test per ASTM F2252; zero transfer after 24h cure
  • Board Burst Test — Mullen burst per TAPPI T-403 (350gsm CCNB ≥ 900 kPa); assures crush-lock mechanism integrity under load
  • Scuff Resistance — Sutherland Rub Test, 500 cycles at 4 psi; lamination and foil intact
