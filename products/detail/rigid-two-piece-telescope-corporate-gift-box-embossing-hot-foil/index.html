The rigid two-piece telescope box is the definitive packaging format for corporate gifting — a structured, high-perceived-value presentation that elevates branded merchandise, executive gifts, and client welcome kits into memorable brand experiences. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a 23-year Foshan paper box specialist operating from an 18,000 sqm dual-factory, this box combines the classic telescope lid-and-base construction with custom embossing, precision hot foil stamping, and a satin ribbon pull for effortless access.

The 1,200gsm greyboard core wrapped in 157gsm art paper provides substantial heft and rigidity — the box feels heavy, solid, and expensive. Bincai’s KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press prints the wrap in high-resolution CMYK (200 LPI), while the Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press applies inline UV coating for instant gloss or matte effects. Embossing and foil stamping are executed on dedicated Bobst hot foil presses with ±0.15mm registration accuracy.

Customization Options

  • Construction: Standard two-piece telescope (lid + base). Optional magnetic lid upgrade with N52 neodymium magnets for a satisfying closure. Hinged book-style variant also available
  • Board: 1,200gsm greyboard core — standard. Upgrade to 1,600gsm for extra heft (luxury market) or 800gsm for lighter, cost-optimized versions
  • Wrap: 157gsm art paper with matte or gloss lamination. Soft-touch velvet lamination for a premium tactile finish. Specialty papers: metallic pearl, linen-textured, leatherette-pattern
  • Printing: Full-color CMYK offset (200 LPI). Optional single-color deboss for minimalist sophistication. Metallic Pantone inks for brand-color precision
  • Embossing: Blind emboss (no foil), registered emboss with foil, or multi-level sculptured emboss. Maximum emboss depth: 1.2mm on greyboard
  • Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic, matte foil. Bobst hot foil press, ±0.15mm registration. Large-format foil coverage up to 300×200mm
  • Interior: Custom EVA foam insert (laser-cut to product profiles), flocked plastic tray, satin-lined compartment, or die-cut cardboard divider. Flocked interior base wrap for luxury feel
  • Sizing: Common: 250×200×60mm (standard gift), 300×250×80mm (multi-item kit), 350×280×100mm (large corporate set)
