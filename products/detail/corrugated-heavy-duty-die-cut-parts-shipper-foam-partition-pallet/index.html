Industrial-grade corrugated heavy-duty parts shipper with die-cut foam partitions and pallet-ready base — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. This robust shipping solution features double-wall BC flute (6mm) or triple-wall ACA flute (10mm) corrugated construction, custom die-cut EPE or PU foam partitions that cradle heavy industrial parts, and a pallet-compatible base design for forklift handling. The moisture-resistant outer coating protects contents during sea freight and warehouse storage. Heavy-duty PP strapping channels and corner protectors ensure load stability during transit. ISTA 1A and 3A testing available. Produced across our 18,000 sqm factory.

Applications

  • Automotive parts and component shipping (brake calipers, engine mounts, transmission parts)
  • Heavy machinery spare parts export packaging
  • Industrial pump, valve, and fitting shipment boxes
  • Aerospace component protective shipping containers
  • Mining and construction equipment parts packaging
  • Electric motor and generator component shipping
  • Metal fabrication and CNC machined parts export boxes
  • Palletized bulk industrial goods shipping

Customization Options

  • Corrugated grade: single-wall C flute (4mm), double-wall BC flute (6mm), triple-wall ACA (10mm), or custom
  • Wall construction: regular slotted container (RSC), half-slotted (HSC), full-overlap (FOL), die-cut custom
  • Foam partitions: EPE (expanded polyethylene), PU (polyurethane), EVA, or cross-linked PE foam
  • Cavity layout: custom CNC-routed cavities for each part geometry with ±1mm tolerance
  • Pallet integration: integrated pallet skids, bolt-on pallet base, or slip-sheet compatible
  • Coating: moisture-resistant (aqua stop), oil-resistant, anti-static ESD, or VCI (volatile corrosion inhibitor)
  • Printing: 1-2 color flexo, full-color litho-laminated label, or plain kraft with shipping marks
  • Strapping: PP (polypropylene) or PET (polyester) strapping, 2-4 strap channels
  • Accessories: corner protectors (paper, plastic, or steel), edge guards, desiccant pouches

Production Specifications

  • Corrugator: In-house corrugated board production with B, C, BC, and ACA flute profiles
  • Die-cutting: Heavy-duty rotary die-cutter for corrugated up to 10mm thickness
  • Foam fabrication: CNC contour cutting for custom part cavities with digital template matching
  • Assembly: Manual or automated gluing, stitching, and strapping stations
  • Testing: ISTA 1A (integrity), ISTA 3A (parcel delivery), drop test, vibration test, compression test
  • Daily output: 1.7 million boxes across all production lines
