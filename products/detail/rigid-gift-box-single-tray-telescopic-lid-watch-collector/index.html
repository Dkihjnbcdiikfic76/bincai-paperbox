FAQ

Q: Can the tray be designed for a specific watch model with precise cavity dimensions?

A: Yes — we create custom die-cut tooling based on your watch CAD or physical sample. Provide overall dimensions (case diameter, lug-to-lug, thickness, strap width) and we engineer the tray cavity with 2mm tolerance for a snug, wiggle-free fit. Custom tray tooling adds 5-7 days to lead time and requires 1,000-unit minimum. Pre-production tray samples ship in 3-5 days for fit verification.

Q: What hot stamping foil colors are available, and how durable is the stamping?

A: We stock gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic, matte black, matte white, and clear gloss foil — special-order colors (Pantone-matched) available with 3-5 day additional lead time. All stamping passes the ASTM D3359 tape test (5B rating) and is tested for rub resistance with 50+ cycles on a Sutherland Rub Tester. For dark wrap papers, we recommend double-strike stamping for opacity and brilliance.

Q: Can the telescopic lid be branded on the inside as well as the outside?

A: Yes — the lid interior is an untapped branding surface that many luxury watch brands use for brand story text, authenticity certificates, care instructions, or a printed QR code linking to warranty registration. We can print the interior art paper in 1-color black or full CMYK, hot-stamp a logo, or line it with satin. Interior printing is done during the box assembly process and does not affect external surface quality.

Q: What is the lead time and shipping method for watch collector boxes?

A: Standard production lead time is 15-20 days for existing tooling and stock materials. Custom tray tooling adds 5-7 days. Custom foil or flocking color adds 3-5 days. Pre-production samples ship in 3-5 days. Boxes are flat-packed (lids nested) for ocean freight efficiency — approximately 30% more units per pallet versus assembled.
