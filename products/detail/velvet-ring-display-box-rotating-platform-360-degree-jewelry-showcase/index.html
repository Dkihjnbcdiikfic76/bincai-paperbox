FAQ

Q: How long do the batteries last in the rotating display box? A: The included 3× LR44 alkaline batteries provide 100+ hours of continuous rotation with LED on. For retail counter use (8 hours/day), that’s approximately 2 weeks of operation. Batteries are user-replaceable — the compartment is accessible underneath the velvet base without tools. We also offer a rechargeable lithium-polymer battery upgrade (USB-C charging, 500+ cycles) at 1,000+ unit orders.

Q: Can the rotation speed be adjusted? A: Standard rotation speed is 6 RPM (one full rotation every 10 seconds). For custom orders of 500+ units, we can factory-set speeds from 3 RPM (slow, dramatic reveal) to 12 RPM (faster, energetic display). The speed is fixed at time of manufacture — it is not end-user adjustable to prevent tampering.

Q: Is the anti-tarnish lining effective for silver rings? A: Yes. Our electrostatic velvet interior is treated with Anti-Tarnish (AT) chemistry that neutralizes hydrogen sulfide and sulfur dioxide in the enclosed box environment. Independent lab testing shows silver coupons stored in our anti-tarnish velvet boxes exhibit 80% less tarnish after 90 days vs. untreated velvet. For gold and platinum rings, the treatment prevents fingerprint etching and maintains the polished finish.

Q: What ring sizes does the satin slot accommodate? A: The standard satin ring slot fits US ring sizes 4–10 (EU 47–62, diameter 14.8–19.8mm). For larger sizes (US 10.5–14), we offer an extended slot insert at no tooling charge for orders of 500+ units. The slot is shaped to present the ring at a 15° angle for optimal visibility during rotation.

Q: Can I customize the LED color temperature? A: Standard LED color is cool white (6500K) — ideal for diamond brilliance. Alternative options include warm white (3000K) for gold jewelry warmth and amber (2200K) for vintage and antique ring presentations. All three are the same unit cost. For orders of 1,000+ units, we can source custom CRI 95+ LEDs for jewelry that demands the highest color accuracy.

Q: Is the box suitable for international shipping? A: Yes. The rigid greyboard construction with magnetic flip lid is ISTA 1A drop-test certified — it survives 760mm drops on all 6 faces. For air freight, we recommend removing batteries and shipping them separately (IATA lithium battery regulation). We provide battery removal instructions and a resealable battery compartment design. We ship to 60+ countries with full export documentation.

Q: How does Bincai’s rotating ring box differ from generic alternatives? A: Three differences: (1) Our motor is rated for 500+ hours of continuous operation vs. 50–100 hours typical of entry-level rotating boxes — tested in our in-house durability lab. (2) The anti-tarnish velvet treatment is chem-lab certified, not just marketing. (3) Our KBA 1050 printing + Heidelberg 7+1 UV foil means your logo looks sharp even under LED spotlight at 100mm viewing distance — no pixelation, no foil flaking.
