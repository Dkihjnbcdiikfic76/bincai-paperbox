Premium loose-leaf tea folding carton with soft-touch matte lamination, auto-lock bottom, and removable inner divider tray — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). The auto-lock bottom ensures rapid assembly without adhesive, while the removable inner tray with 2–6 compartments keeps tea varieties separated and organized. The exterior soft-touch velvet-like lamination creates a premium tactile experience, complemented by hot foil branding. Produced on KBA Rapida 105 + Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV presses with Bobst SP 102 BMA/E die-cutting (±0.1mm precision) in our 18,000 sqm dual-factory (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory, 2025 expansion) in Foshan, Pearl River Delta. Daily output of 1.7M boxes.

Applications

  • Premium loose-leaf tea brand retail packaging
  • Tea subscription box monthly delivery
  • Gourmet tea gift set presentation
  • Tea house and tea room retail display
  • Corporate gift tea packaging
  • Seasonal tea collection (spring flush, autumn harvest)

Customization Options

  • Inner tray compartments: 2, 3, 4, 6 compartments for individual tea varieties
  • Carton styles: tuck-top, auto-lock bottom, sleeve + tray (two-piece), book-style
  • Paper stock: art paper (210gsm, 250gsm, 300gsm, 350gsm), uncoated kraft, metallic pearl
  • Finishes: soft-touch matte lamination, gloss lamination, matte UV spot, soft-touch + spot UV combo
  • Hot foil stamping: gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic, blind deboss
  • Embossed/debossed logo with raised tactile effect on lid or side panel
  • Interior printing: tea tasting notes, brewing instructions, flavor wheel on inside lid
  • Window options: die-cut window with PVC/PET or compostable PLA film
  • Sleeve band: removable paper band with varietal information (great for gift sets)
  • Magnetic closure: hidden N35 magnet in flap for premium snap-shut feel
  • Custom sizes: compact 80×80×80mm (50g) to large 200×150×100mm (250g)
  • Ribbon pull tab: color-matched satin ribbon for easy inner tray removal
