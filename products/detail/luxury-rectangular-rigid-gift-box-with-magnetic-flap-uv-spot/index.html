FAQ

Q: How does the magnetic flap closure compare to ribbon-tie or two-piece lid boxes? A: The hidden magnetic flap provides a clean, modern aesthetic with no visible closure mechanism — the box appears seamless from the outside. When the recipient lifts the flap, N52 neodymium magnets release with a satisfying click. Unlike ribbons that can fray or two-piece lids that can separate, the magnetic flap stays attached and maintains its closure integrity through 10,000+ open/close cycles. It’s particularly valued by skincare and tech brands for the “magic” unboxing moment.

Q: What’s the visual effect of spot UV on soft-touch matte? A: The soft-touch matte lamination creates a velvety, non-reflective surface that resists fingerprints. When high-gloss spot UV is applied to specific areas (logo, pattern, border), those areas become glossy and reflective, creating dramatic contrast that shifts as the box moves under light. The effect is subtle at a distance but mesmerising up close — the tactile difference between matte and gloss invites touch and signals premium quality.

Q: Can you combine spot UV with foil stamping on the same box? A: Yes. Our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press runs CMYK + spot colors + inline UV coating in a single pass, and Bobst foil stamping is applied as a separate finishing step. We can execute spot UV on one area and foil on another, or even registered foil with spot UV overlapping for a stunning dimensional effect. Up to 3 foil colors + spot UV per box.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity and can I order a sample first? A: Standard MOQ is 500–1000 units per design. We offer free pre-production samples with 5–7 day turnaround — these include actual board construction, your print design, magnetic closure, and spot UV finishing so you can evaluate the full unboxing experience before committing to mass production.

Q: Is this box suitable for international e-commerce shipping? A: Yes. The 2.5mm greyboard construction provides robust crush resistance. We test to ISTA 1A standards (1.0m drop test). The rigid structure outperforms folding cartons for protecting glass bottles, electronics, and delicate items during international transit. We ship worldwide via DHL/FedEx/UPS/Sea from our Foshan logistics hub.
