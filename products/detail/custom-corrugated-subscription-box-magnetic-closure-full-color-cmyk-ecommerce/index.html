Product Overview

Our custom corrugated subscription box with magnetic closure redefines the subscription unboxing experience — transforming a shipping container into a brand moment. Constructed from B-flute corrugated board with a full-color litho-laminated wrap, the box arrives as a premium branded package rather than a plain brown carton. The hinged lid with hidden neodymium magnets provides a satisfying snap-close action that subscribers will open again and again. A perforated tear-strip along the opening edge doubles as tamper-evident security while enabling easy access. The self-locking bottom eliminates tape and speeds fulfillment center assembly. Manufactured on our KBA 1050 4-color offset press with inline litho-lamination at Bincai’s integrated 18,000 sqm factory. Every box passes 7-stage QC verification from ECT compression testing to final AQL sampling. With 23 years of corrugated expertise and 1.7M daily output capacity, we power subscription brands that demand both protection and presentation.

Design Features

  • B-Flute Corrugated Construction: 2.5mm B-flute profile provides optimal balance of crush resistance and print quality — ECT rated ≥4.5 kN/m, BCT varies by box dimensions
  • Full-Color Litho-Laminated Wrap: 200gsm art paper litho-printed in CMYK then laminated to B-flute corrugated — delivers offset print quality on corrugated with 175 LPI screen resolution
  • Hidden Neodymium Magnetic Closure: N42-grade neodymium magnets embedded between liner and flute provide invisible, tool-free closure mechanism — tested to 10,000+ open/close cycles
  • Perforated Tear-Strip Opening: Micro-perforated tear strip (2mm cut/2mm tie) along lid edge enables clean, frustration-free opening while providing tamper-evident security
  • Self-Locking Bottom: Pre-glued crash-lock bottom pops open in under 2 seconds — no tape, no assembly tools required, ideal for high-volume fulfillment centers
  • Custom Interior Dividers: Die-cut corrugated partitions, foam inserts, or paperboard trays custom-configured to your product assortment

FAQ

Q: What’s the difference between B-flute and E-flute? A: B-flute (2.5mm) offers better crush resistance and is ideal for heavier products (1–8kg). E-flute (1.5mm) is thinner with better print surface and suits lighter products (0–3kg). We can advise based on your product weight and desired print quality.

Q: Can the magnetic closure handle repeated opening? A: Yes! Our N42 neodymium magnets are tested to 10,000+ open/close cycles. Many subscription brands report the box being reused by subscribers for months after delivery.

Q: How is the box shipped to our fulfillment center? A: Boxes ship flat with the self-locking bottom pre-glued. Your fulfillment team simply pops the bottom open — no tape required. This saves storage space and assembly time.

Q: Can you do seasonal or limited-edition print changes? A: Absolutely. We support frequent artwork changes for seasonal boxes, limited editions, and monthly themes. New printing plates are created for each design iteration.

Q: What’s the largest box size you can produce? A: Our rotary die-cutter handles up to 1,200mm × 900mm flat sheet size. Custom larger sizes may require flatbed die-cutting — contact us for feasibility.

Q: Can the interior be customized for different product assortments each month? A: Yes! We design modular insert systems — fixed outer partitions with swappable inner trays — so you can change product configurations without redesigning the entire box.
