Premium rigid watch gift box with velvet cushion insert and magnetic closure lid — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. Designed for luxury watch brands, timepiece retailers, and corporate watch gifting, this 2mm rigid board box delivers the weight, tactile quality, and presentation that premium watches demand. The exterior is wrapped in flocked velvet or premium leatherette with gold or silver hot foil branding, while the interior features a removable satin pillow cushion that holds the watch securely. The magnetic closure lid provides a satisfying premium snap. Produced across our 18,000 sqm factory with 1.7M daily output capacity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What watch sizes does this box accommodate? A: Our standard watch box fits watches with case diameters 36-46mm and band lengths up to 210mm. We also manufacture boxes for oversized chronographs (48-55mm), slim dress watches (28-36mm), and smartwatches. Custom cushion sizes available — provide your watch specifications and we’ll design the perfect fit.

Q: How strong is the magnetic closure? A: We use N35-grade neodymium magnets (8-10mm × 1.5mm), providing approximately 1.2-1.8kg of holding force per pair. Two or four magnet pairs are used depending on box size. The closure is tested for 1,000+ open/close cycles without degradation. The lid should not open when the box is held upside-down and gently shaken.

Q: Can you include multiple watch cushions in one box? A: Yes. We manufacture single-watch, dual-watch (his & hers, or watch + bracelet), triple-watch, and up to 12-watch collector cases. Multi-watch boxes use layered trays or drawer configurations to present each timepiece individually.

Q: What are MOQ and lead times? A: Standard MOQ is 300-500 units. Lead time is 15-20 working days for standard configurations, 20-25 working days with custom cushion designs or accessory drawers. Rush orders available at 10-12 working days.

Q: Can you add a certificate/document slot? A: Yes. We can include a die-cut slot under the lid or a base drawer for warranty cards, certificates of authenticity, instruction booklets, or extra straps/links. The slot is lined with satin to match the interior.

Q: What exterior materials are available besides velvet? A: We offer: PU leatherette (most popular for watch boxes — smooth grain, easy to foil stamp), genuine leather (sourced to order, adds cost and lead time), linen-textured paper, carbon-fiber textured wrap, gloss/matte laminated art paper, and wood-veneer paper wrap. Each material has different foil stamping characteristics — we’ll advise on the best match for your logo.

Q: Is assembly required by the end customer? A: No. The box arrives fully assembled and gift-ready with the cushion in place. The watch is simply placed onto the cushion by your fulfillment team. For flat-ship configurations (where the box folds flat for freight savings), we offer collapsible rigid designs that snap into shape with pre-installed magnets.
