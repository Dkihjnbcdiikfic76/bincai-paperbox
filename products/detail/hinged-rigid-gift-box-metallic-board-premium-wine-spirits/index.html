Product Overview

Hinged rigid gift box with luminous metallic pearl board for premium wine and spirits packaging — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). Engineered for luxury wine, champagne, whiskey, cognac, and premium spirits brands requiring a prestigious unboxing experience that matches the quality of the liquid inside.

The book-style hinged lid opens to reveal a satin-lined interior with a precision die-cut bottle cradle that securely holds standard 750ml bottles, magnum 1.5L formats, and custom specialty bottle shapes. The exterior features luminous metallic pearl board (128–200gsm specialty substrate) with optional hot foil stamping for brand logos, vintage details, and crest designs. Constructed with 2.5mm greyboard walls for exceptional structural rigidity — the box feels substantial in hand, protects the bottle during gifting and transport, and presents beautifully on retail shelves and tasting room displays.

Produced on Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press with inter-deck UV curing for flawless metallic rendering, and Bobst SP 102 BMA foil stamping at ±0.15mm registration. Our 18,000 sqm Foshan dual-factory complex (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory) delivers 220,000+ rigid gift boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification. 23 years of Guangdong manufacturing, serving wineries, distilleries, and luxury beverage brands in 60+ countries.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What bottle sizes can your hinged rigid boxes accommodate? A: We engineer custom bottle cradles for any format — standard 750ml (Ø85×300mm), magnum 1.5L (Ø105×350mm), double magnum 3L, jeroboam, and specialty shapes like square-based or teardrop bottles. Our structural designers create a 3D CAD cradle model from your bottle dimensions within 2–3 days.

Q: Does the metallic pearl board scratch or show fingerprints easily? A: No. Our metallic pearl board is UV-cured inline on the Heidelberg 7+1 press, creating a durable surface that resists fingerprints, scuffing, and handling marks. In Sutherland Rub testing (4-lb, 100 cycles), we achieve ≤5% density loss — meaning the box looks pristine after retail handling, gift-giving, and shelf display.

Q: Can you do dual-bottle configurations (wine + glasses, or two bottles)? A: Yes. We offer dual-bottle cradles, bottle + 2 glasses configurations, and bottle + accessory compartment designs (for corkscrews, stoppers, tasting notes). Custom foam or die-cut greyboard inserts organize all components securely.

Q: What’s the minimum order for custom wine boxes with foil stamping? A: Standard MOQ is 500 units for full custom boxes with CMYK printing and foil stamping. For single-color foil-on-stock-material designs, we accept 300-unit trial orders. Volume pricing tiers at 1,000, 3,000, and 10,000+ units.

Q: How long does development take from concept to delivery? A: Structural design and 3D render: 2–3 days. Pre-production sample: 5–7 days. Production: 10–15 days for new artwork, 7–10 days for repeats. Rush service available at 5–7 days. We recommend starting 4–6 weeks before your launch date for first-time orders.

Q: Are your materials food-safe for direct bottle contact? A: Yes. All interior materials (satin, velvet, greyboard cradle wrap) use water-based adhesives compliant with FDA 21 CFR for indirect food contact. Inks meet EN 71-3 and CONEG standards. We can provide full material safety documentation and EU 1935/2004 compliance certificates on request.

Q: Can you create a numbered limited-edition series? A: Yes. We offer sequential numbering via hot foil stamping or digital print — each box receives a unique edition number (e.g., “057/500”). We can also integrate a certificate of authenticity slot, wax seal, or custom belly band for limited-release prestige.
