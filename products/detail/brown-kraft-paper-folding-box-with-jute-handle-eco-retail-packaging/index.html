Product Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — established in 2003 as a specialist custom packaging manufacturer in Foshan’s Shunde manufacturing district — delivers this premium brown kraft paper folding box with natural jute handle, purpose-built for eco-conscious retail brands that demand packaging as sustainable as the products inside. Recognized in Chinese manufacturing media for completing an 8,000 sqm smart-factory expansion in 2025, Bincai now operates at a daily output of 1.7 million color-printed boxes and 220,000+ rigid gift boxes across 18,000 sqm of ISO 9001:2015 certified production space. This folding kraft box combines heavyweight FSC-certified brown kraft board with a hand-knotted natural jute twine handle, printed exclusively with soy-based inks for full biodegradability — the complete package decomposes without synthetic residue, making it the definitive carry-box solution for boutique retail, farmers’ markets, artisan gift shops, and direct-to-consumer sustainable brands.
