Corrugated wine bottle shipper for 3-bottle die-cut with molded pulp dividers — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. Purpose-designed for direct-to-consumer (DTC) wine club shipments, this B-flute corrugated shipper features precision die-cut bottle cradles and molded pulp dividers that isolate each bottle from impact during transit. The integrated die-cut carry handle provides retail-ready convenience, while full-color flexo or litho label printing transforms a protective shipping box into a branded unboxing experience. ISTA 6-FEDEX-A tested and approved for single-parcel wine shipments. Produced on our BHS 2.5m corrugator across 18,000 sqm with 1.7M daily output capacity.

Quality Assurance

Every wine shipper undergoes Bincai’s 7-stage QC process. Flute bond strength tested per TAPPI T 821 (pin adhesion ≥ 50 lb/0.15m). Handle pull tested to 2× loaded weight (no failure). ISTA 6-FEDEX-A protocol: 10-drop sequence from 30 inches + random vibration simulation. Molded pulp dividers tested for compression strength ≥ 150 PSI.

FAQ

Q: Can this shipper accommodate Burgundy and Champagne bottle shapes? A: Yes. We offer universal die-cut cradles that fit standard Bordeaux (750ml), Burgundy, and Champagne bottles. Custom cradles available for magnum (1.5L) and split (375ml) formats.

Q: What temperature ranges can the shipper handle? A: Standard corrugated performs from -20°C to 50°C. For cold-chain wine shipping, we offer thermal liners with reflective foil + EPS insulation panels rated for 48-72 hour temperature maintenance.

Q: Do you offer branded unboxing features? A: Yes. Full-color flexo printing on exterior, custom-printed tissue paper, branded pulp dividers, foil-stamped belly bands, and personalized gift cards can all be integrated.

Q: What is your lead time for custom wine shippers? A: Standard production 7-10 working days after artwork approval. Rush orders in 5 working days. Die-making adds 2-3 days for new designs.
