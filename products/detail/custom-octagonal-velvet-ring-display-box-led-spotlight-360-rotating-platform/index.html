Product Overview

Our octagonal velvet ring display box with LED spotlight and 360° rotating platform represents the pinnacle of jewelry presentation engineering. The hinged octagonal lid opens to activate a warm-white LED spotlight that illuminates the ring from above, while a whisper-quiet motorized platform rotates the ring continuously at 0.5 RPM — ensuring every facet catches the light. The interior is fully flocked in premium black velvet with a precision die-cut ring slot accommodating sizes 4–13. Constructed from 2.0mm rigid greyboard wrapped in matching velvet, the octagonal shape adds architectural sophistication that sets it apart from conventional square boxes. A built-in 2000mAh rechargeable lithium battery with USB-C charging provides 8+ hours of continuous display — ideal for retail counters, exhibition showcases, and proposal moments. Manufactured at Bincai’s 18,000 sqm integrated factory with 23 years of jewelry packaging expertise. Every box undergoes 7-stage QC from LED module testing to rotation smoothness verification.

The 360° Rotation Advantage

No other ring box on the market combines motorized rotation with integrated lighting in an octagonal form factor. The rotation eliminates the need for customers to handle the ring to see all sides — critical for retail counter displays where hygiene and theft-prevention matter. For proposals, the rotation creates a cinematic moment that elevates the experience beyond “open box, see ring.” For exhibition showcases, 8+ hours of battery life means the ring presents itself all day without staff intervention.

FAQ

Q: How long does the battery last in real-world use? A: In continuous display mode (lid open, LED on, platform rotating): 8+ hours. In retail counter mode (intermittent open/close): 200+ cycles. The auto-on/off magnetic switch ensures the battery only drains when the box is open.

Q: Can the rotation speed be adjusted? A: The standard speed is 0.5 RPM (one full rotation every 2 minutes). Custom speeds (0.25–2.0 RPM) are available for orders of 2,000+ units.

Q: Is the LED replaceable? A: The LED module is rated for 50,000+ hours (MTBF) — effectively the lifetime of the box. We can provide replacement modules for warranty service if needed.

Q: What ring styles fit in the slot? A: The standard oval slot (18mm × 22mm × 8mm deep) accommodates solitaire, halo, three-stone, cluster, and signet rings up to US size 13. For unusually tall settings (>12mm), we can increase the slot depth.

Q: Can I customize the LED color temperature? A: Absolutely! Warm-white 3000K is standard. Cool-white 5000K (better for diamonds), neutral 4000K, and RGB color-changing (Bluetooth app-controlled) are all available.

Q: Does the box come with a charger? A: Each box includes a 1-meter USB-A to USB-C charging cable. USB wall adapters are available as an optional add-on for bulk orders.
