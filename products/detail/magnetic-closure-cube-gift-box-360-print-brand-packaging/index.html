Product Overview

The Bincai Magnetic Closure Cube Gift Box is a statement piece — a perfectly proportioned six-panel wrap-around canvas where every face (front, back, top, bottom, left, right) is printed in full-color CMYK as a continuous seamless wrap. This 360° design freedom is unmatched by conventional rigid boxes (which typically leave sides blank or printed separately), enabling brands to create immersive packaging that tells a story from every angle — from a lush botanical pattern wrapping all six faces of a luxury candle box to a continuous cityscape panorama encircling a premium tech accessory gift set. The magnetic lid (NdFeB N35 magnets in all four corners) snaps closed with a satisfying, premium “thunk” that becomes part of the unboxing ritual. At the core is a 1600gsm greyboard structure built on Heidelberg 7+1 UV and KBA Rapida 105 press lines, produced by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certified, with 23 years of premium packaging mastery delivering 220,000+ boxes daily.
