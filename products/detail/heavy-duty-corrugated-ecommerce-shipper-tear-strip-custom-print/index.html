Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — liner board grammage and moisture content, medium paper flute profile, FSC documentation verification
  2. Corrugated Board Production — flute height measurement, bond strength test (pin adhesion), caliper verification
  3. Printing — color consistency check against master proof every 500 sheets (offset) or rolls (flexo), ink adhesion tape test
  4. Die-Cutting — dimensional tolerance ±0.5mm, crease depth verification for clean folding, slot and tab alignment
  5. Tear Strip Application — strip positioning accuracy ±1mm, adhesion strength test, pull-function test on 2% sample
  6. Assembly & Gluing — joint strength test (lap joint or stitch), box squareness check, closure function test
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — AQL sampling per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4, ECT or BCT verification on retained samples, final visual inspection under D65 lighting

FAQ

Q: What’s the difference between B-flute and double-wall B/C-flute?

A: B-flute is 3mm thick — good for lightweight products under 5kg. Double-wall B/C-flute is 7mm thick with two layers of fluting — ideal for heavy or fragile products up to 30kg. B/C-flute provides roughly 3× the crush resistance and 2× the stacking strength of single-wall B-flute.

Q: Can the tear strip be a specific brand color?

A: Yes — we color-match tear tape to your brand’s Pantone specification. Available in standard red, white, black, or custom-matched colors with a minimum of 5,000 units.

Q: Is custom printing expensive for small quantities?

A: For runs under 2,000 units, we recommend digital print (no plate costs). Flexo print requires printing plates (one-time tooling) but becomes very cost-effective above 5,000 units. Offset litho delivers the highest print quality for runs of 10,000+.

Q: How does flat-packed shipping save money?

A: Corrugated boxes ship flat — 100 boxes occupy the space of approximately 20 assembled boxes, reducing freight costs by 40–50% and warehouse footprint by 80%. For international shipments from our Foshan facility, this savings is substantial.

Q: Can you include a return strip for customer returns?

A: Absolutely — we can add a second perforated tear strip with “Easy Return” printing for hassle-free customer returns. This is a popular feature for apparel and footwear DTC brands with high return rates.
