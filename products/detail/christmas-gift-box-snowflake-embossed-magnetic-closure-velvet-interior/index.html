Customization Options

  • Board Construction: 2.0mm, 2.5mm, or 3.0mm greyboard — choose rigidity level based on product weight. 157g or 200g C2S art paper wrap with gloss or matte lamination.
  • Printing: Full-color offset (KBA 1050) up to 4 colors + UV spot coating (Heidelberg 7+1) for metallic accents. CMYK + Pantone spot colors available. Gloss, matte, or soft-touch aqueous coating.
  • Surface Finishes: Hot foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic) with ±0.15mm registration. Deep embossing/debossing for tactile snowflake patterns. Spot UV gloss on selected design elements.
  • Closure Mechanism: N52-grade neodymium magnets (2 or 4 magnets per box) in black or white housing. Hidden magnet mounting with fabric cover. Optional ribbon tie closure for traditional look.
  • Interior: Velvet flocking (0.8mm pile, black/white/red/green/burgundy/custom Pantone). Satin or grosgrain ribbon ties. Die-cut foam insert for product-specific cavities. Optional LED light strip with concealed battery compartment.
  • Sizing: Custom dimensions from 80×80×30mm (small jewelry) to 350×250×100mm (large gift sets). Standard increments available for rapid sampling.
  • Additional Features: Die-cut window with PET film for product visibility. Interior printing on lid underside. Magnetic closure with audible click feedback. Flat-pack shipping option for overseas freight savings.

Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — greyboard grammage (±5% tolerance), velvet pile density, magnet grade (N52 verification via gauss meter), art paper whiteness
  2. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0 target, inline Prinect registration monitoring on Heidelberg
  3. Foil Stamping & Embossing — registration check (±0.15mm), foil adhesion tape test, embossing depth verification per batch
  4. Die-Cutting & Creasing — dimensional tolerance ±0.3mm, crease depth consistency, board delamination inspection
  5. Interior Lining & Assembly — velvet bond peel test, magnet polarity check, hinge alignment, excess glue inspection
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.5 (critical) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4, color match under D65 and A lighting
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — random sample pull (Level II), full functional test, packaging integrity check, barcode/label verification

FAQ

Q: Can the snowflake pattern be customized with our brand logo instead? A: Absolutely. The embossing die is custom-made for each design — you can replace the snowflake with any pattern, logo, or monogram. Die creation adds 2–3 days to sampling. Hot foil color can be gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for Christmas boxes? A: Standard MOQ is 500 units per design. For repeat orders or simpler constructions (no foil stamping), we can accommodate 300 units with a small surcharge. Rush orders of 200+ units are possible with expedited production at 7-day turnaround.

Q: Can you match our brand’s specific red or green color? A: Yes — we use Pantone spot color matching with X-Rite spectrophotometer verification. ΔE ≤ 2.0 tolerance ensures consistent brand color. We recommend providing a physical Pantone chip or previous printed sample as reference.

Q: How are the boxes shipped — assembled or flat? A: We recommend flat-packed shipping for international orders, which reduces freight volume by approximately 60% and container costs by 40–50%. The boxes are designed for easy assembly — the magnetic closure snaps into place in seconds. Pre-assembled shipping is available for domestic orders at no extra charge.

Q: What is the production timeline for Christmas orders? A: We recommend placing Christmas orders by September for October production to avoid peak-season surcharges. Standard production: 10–14 days. Sampling: 5–7 days. Peak season (October–November): 15–20 days. Sea freight adds 15–30 days depending on destination port.
