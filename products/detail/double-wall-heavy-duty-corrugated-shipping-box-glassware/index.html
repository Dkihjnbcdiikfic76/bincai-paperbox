Product Overview

Double-wall heavy-duty corrugated shipping box engineered specifically for glassware, ceramics, and fragile items — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). This BC-flute double-wall construction combines a 2.5mm B-flute and 3.6mm C-flute bonded with high-strength starch adhesive, delivering 12-18 kg/cm² Mullen burst strength and 32-44 lb/in Edge Crush Test (ECT) values that exceed ISTA 1A requirements for fragile product protection. Every box is engineered for certified ISTA protocol compliance with in-house drop-test, vibration, and compression testing equipment. Our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility combines a 10,000 sqm main factory with KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses and an 8,000 sqm smart factory (commissioned 2025) featuring dedicated corrugated production lines with Bobst flatbed automatic die-cutters and flexo folder-gluers. Daily output of 1.7 million boxes including 500,000+ medium-to-large corrugated boxes with ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certifications. MOQ 500-1000 with free pre-production samples in 3-7 working days.
