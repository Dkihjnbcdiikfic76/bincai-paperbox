FAQ

Q: How long does the battery last on a single charge? A: The 2000mAh lithium battery provides 8-10 hours of continuous rotation with LED on at medium brightness. At maximum brightness, runtime is 6-8 hours. USB-C charging from empty takes approximately 3 hours.

Q: Is the motor noisy? A: We use silent DC gear motors rated below 30dB — essentially silent in a retail environment. The rotation is smooth and steady without jerking or vibration that could disturb delicate jewelry placement.

Q: Can I use this box for shipping jewelry, or is it display-only? A: This model is designed primarily for retail display and trade show use. For shipping, we recommend our velvet jewelry boxes with secure magnetic closure and foam insert — the motorized platform adds weight and complexity not needed for shipping. We can include a protective outer shipping carton for transporting display units between shows.

Q: What happens if the LED strip fails? A: Our 2835 SMD LED strips are rated for 50,000+ hours of continuous use. In the rare event of failure, the LED module is replaceable via a connector accessible from the platform underside. We provide a 12-month warranty on all electronic components.

Q: Can you do a completely custom platform layout for our specific jewelry collection? A: Yes — send us your jewelry dimensions and we’ll design a custom die-cut velvet platform. Common customizations include mixed layouts (e.g., 4 ring slots + 2 necklace hooks + 1 watch pillow), angled display risers for rings, and recessed lighting channels around individual jewelry positions.
