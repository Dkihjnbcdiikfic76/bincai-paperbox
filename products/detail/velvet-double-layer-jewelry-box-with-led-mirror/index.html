Velvet double-layer jewelry box with integrated LED vanity mirror — engineered for premium jewelry presentation by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging manufacturer with 23 years of industry leadership (founded 2003). This dual-tier jewelry box combines luxury aesthetics with functional LED illumination, designed for high-end jewelry retailers, boutique brands, subscription box services, and direct-to-consumer fine jewelry brands. The box features a 2.5mm rigid greyboard structure precision-wrapped in Italian-imported flocked velvet with 14 color options (midnight black, royal sapphire, burgundy red, champagne gold, blush pink, emerald green, ivory white, charcoal grey, navy blue, plum purple, teal, chocolate brown, slate, and pearl white). The removable upper tray contains precision-cut ring rolls (8 slots), earring cards (6 pairs), and a necklace hook area, while the lower tier offers bracelet compartments and removable dividers. The integrated LED ring mirror on the inner lid features touch-dimming technology (3 brightness levels) powered by a replaceable CR2032 battery delivering 200+ hours of illumination. Produced on German-engineered KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press (15,000 sheets/hour) and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press (inter-deck UV curing, single-pass CMYK+spot+coating) with Bobst SP 102 BMA foil stamping (±0.15mm registration). Our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility (10,000 sqm main + 8,000 sqm smart factory commissioned 2025) operates fully automatic rigid box production lines delivering 220,000+ boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 quality management and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

Quality Control — 7-Stage Inspection

  1. Incoming Material Inspection: Velvet color consistency (spectrophotometer ΔE ≤ 1.5), greyboard thickness (caliper ±0.1mm), LED module functional test (100% batch sampling)
  2. Printing QC: Inline spectrophotometer monitoring on Heidelberg CD 102, Pantone ΔE ≤ 2.0, 200-line screen ruling
  3. Foil Stamping QC: Bobst SP 102 BMA registration check (±0.15mm), foil adhesion tape test, foil coverage inspection under 10× magnification
  4. Box Assembly QC: Corner wrapping tension check, velvet-to-board adhesion peel test (≥3N/cm), magnet alignment gauge
  5. LED Functional Test: 100% LED illumination test at all 3 brightness levels, touch sensor sensitivity test, battery contact continuity check
  6. Final Visual Inspection: 100% visual inspection under 5000K daylight lamps, surface defect detection, hinge smoothness test
  7. Packaging Inspection: Individual polybag + bubble wrap, master carton drop test (1.2m, 6-face+3-edge+1-corner), barcode scan verification

Sustainability

Bincai is committed to sustainable luxury packaging. Our velvet double-layer jewelry box is available with FSC-certified greyboard, recycled-content velvet backing, and soy-based inks. The LED module uses replaceable CR2032 batteries (not sealed), allowing consumers to replace rather than discard. We are developing a fully recyclable version with mono-material construction and water-based adhesive assembly.

FAQ

Q: Can I customize the LED color temperature? Yes — choose between warm white (3000K) for a cozy jewelry boutique feel or cool white (6000K) for accurate color rendition when evaluating gemstones.

Q: What are the box dimensions? Standard exterior: 240×180×65mm. Fully customizable — provide your jewelry dimensions and we engineer the perfect fit.

Q: How are the velvet colors matched? We stock 14 standard Italian velvet colors. For custom colors, we offer Pantone-matched velvet dye lots (MOQ 5,000 units, +15–20 days lead time).

Q: Is the LED replaceable? Yes — the LED module and CR2032 battery are user-replaceable via a discreet rear access panel. No tools required.

Q: Can I add my brand logo? Absolutely — options include hot foil stamping (exterior lid), debossing (exterior lid), foil stamping (interior lid liner), laser engraving (mirror rim), and custom-printed tissue paper.

Q: What certifications does this box carry? ISO 9001:2015 (quality management) and FSC Chain-of-Custody (responsible forestry). Food-grade and REACH/RoHS compliance available on request.

Q: Do you offer samples? Yes — we provide pre-production samples (7–10 days) for approval before mass production. Sample fee is credited against your production order.
