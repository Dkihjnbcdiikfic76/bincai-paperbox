Product Overview

Professional double-layer bracelet and bangle display box engineered for jewelry retailers, trade show exhibitors, and wholesale distributors. The upper tier features six precision-milled velvet grooves that cradle individual bracelets in separated channels — preventing tangling and scratching during transport and display. A removable lower drawer with eight partitioned compartments stores bangles, cuffs, and expandable bracelets securely. The magnetic flip lid closes with a satisfying snap via N48 neodymium magnets, while the integrated satin ribbon pull tab allows effortless opening. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) in our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility — 23 years of dedicated packaging expertise.

Applications

  • Jewelry wholesale showroom displays and traveling trunk shows
  • Retail bracelet and bangle point-of-sale presentation
  • Trade fair and exhibition booth product showcases
  • High-end bracelet gift sets for weddings, anniversaries, and corporate gifting
  • E-commerce bracelet fulfillment packaging (Amazon, Etsy, Shopify sellers)
  • Jewelry designer portfolio and sample presentation kits

Customization Options

  • Velvet Colors: Navy (standard), black, burgundy, emerald green, champagne, royal purple — 12 colors available with Pantone matching
  • Groove Configuration: Standard 6+8 layout; customizable to 4+6, 8+12, or any combination for different bracelet widths (3mm to 30mm)
  • Exterior Wrapping: Art paper (157gsm, matte/gloss lamination), specialty textured paper (linen, leatherette, carbon fiber), or PU synthetic leather
  • Finishing: Gold foil, silver foil, holographic foil, blind debossing, embossing, spot UV varnish
  • Logo & Branding: Hot foil stamping on lid (up to 200×150mm area), interior lid printing, drawer front branding
  • Inserts: Replaceable velvet tray inserts for seasonal collection changes without reordering full boxes
