FAQ

Q: What dome sizes are available? A: Standard dome formers range from 100mm to 350mm diameter. Custom dome dimensions available for orders above 2,000 units with a one-time tooling charge.

Q: Will the PVC window scratch in transit? A: Bincai applies a protective PE film to all PVC windows during production. This is peel-off upon delivery. For extra protection, we recommend adding a tissue or foam sheet between product and window.

Q: Can you do double-dome boxes? A: Yes — a dome lid and dome base create a full spherical or egg-shaped presentation. Popular for premium chocolate eggs, perfume gift sets, and jewelry.

Q: What’s the MOQ for custom dome shapes? A: 2,000 units for new dome formers (one-time tooling US$200-500 depending on complexity). Standard dome shapes start at 500 units.

Q: Is the gold foil durable? A: Yes. Hot foil stamping on Kolbus DA270 bonds the metallic layer into the paper surface at 120°C and 2 tons of pressure — it will not peel, flake, or fade under normal handling.

Q: Can the box be fully recyclable? A: Yes — choose the PET window option and specify water-based adhesives for a >95% recyclable box. The eco paper lattice window option achieves 100% paper recyclability.
