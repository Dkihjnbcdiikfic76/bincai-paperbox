Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? A: MOQ is 500 units per design. For new mold/tooling projects, we recommend 1,000 units to amortize setup costs. Rush orders as low as 300 units available with surcharge.

Q: What bottle sizes does the tube accommodate? A: Standard formats include 750ml (US), 700ml (EU), 375ml (half), 1L, and 1.75L. Custom diameters from 85mm to 130mm and heights from 180mm to 380mm cover virtually all spirit bottle shapes including square, oval, and flask bottles.

Q: Can the foil embossing wrap continuously around the tube — 360 degrees? A: Yes. Our Heidelberg 7+1 UV foil press with cylindrical substrate handling achieves seamless 360° foil registration with ±0.1mm tolerance. The curved surface foil application is one of our signature capabilities — unlike flat-sheet-only competitors.

Q: Is the silk lining safe for direct food/beverage contact? A: The silk lining contacts the bottle exterior, not the beverage. Our 210T polyester silk is OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified (Class I — safe for baby products). For distilleries requiring food-grade certification on all packaging materials, we provide full material traceability documentation.

Q: How does the tube protect the bottle during shipping? A: The 2.0mm greyboard cylinder with ultrasonically welded seam withstands 50kg lateral crush force. Combined with the silk-lined interior, the bottle is fully suspended with 5mm minimum clearance from all cylinder walls. ISTA 1A drop-test certification confirms survival from 1m drops on all faces, corners, and edges. For e-commerce direct-to-consumer shipping, we recommend an additional corrugated outer carton.

Q: What’s the lead time and sampling process? A: Free pre-production samples ship in 5–7 business days. New tooling production lead time is 12–15 working days. Repeat orders ship in 7–10 working days. Express 5-day production available for urgent brand launches (surcharge applies). We ship samples via DHL/UPS/FedEx express worldwide.

Q: Can you match our exact brand Pantone color? A: Yes. We use G7 Master Color certified workflows on both KBA 1050 (4-color) and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses. Pantone matching with ΔE ≤1.5 is standard. For metallic foil colors (gold, rose gold, copper, etc.), we maintain a library of 50+ foil shades and can custom-match to your brand’s PMS metallic specification.
