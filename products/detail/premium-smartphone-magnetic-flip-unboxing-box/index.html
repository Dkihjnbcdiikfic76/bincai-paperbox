FAQ

Q: Can the magnetic flip box be engineered for multiple device models with modular inserts? A: Yes. The outer rigid box shell can be standardized, and we produce modular accessory trays and device cradles that swap in for different models. This is ideal for smartphone brands launching multiple SKUs (standard, Pro, Pro Max) — the box exterior stays consistent while the interior insert changes per model. Tooling cost is reduced by approximately 60% versus producing fully custom boxes for each SKU.

Q: Does the magnetic lid maintain its closure strength after repeated use — for example, in retail display environments? A: Our N52-grade neodymium magnets are tested to maintain over 95% of their pull force after 10,000 open/close cycles. For retail environments where boxes are opened dozens of times daily, this translates to 3+ years of consistent magnetic performance. We also offer a dual-magnet upgrade (two magnets per closure point) for display-intensive applications, which doubles the closure life to 20,000+ cycles.

Q: Can the interior material be upgraded from microfiber to genuine suede or Alcantara? A: Yes, we offer premium interior upgrades including genuine suede (split leather), Alcantara (synthetic suede), and Japanese washi paper liners. These materials add $0.15–$0.80 per unit depending on grade and coverage. All premium liners are die-cut and precision-applied with the same ±0.3mm fit tolerance as standard microfiber. Lead time for premium interiors extends by 3–5 days due to material sourcing.

Q: How is the box designed to minimize freight cost — since smartphone boxes need to be sturdy but shipping volume drives cost? A: We use a flat-ship (knocked down) design where the rigid box ships as separate flat components — lid, base, accessory tray, and device cradle — with the magnetic assemblies pre-attached. This reduces shipping volume by 60–70% compared to pre-assembled boxes. Assembly at destination takes approximately 15–20 seconds per box and requires no special tools or equipment. We provide video assembly instructions and a Japanese-style one-page illustrated guide with every shipment.
