Kraft paper drawer box with jute rope handle for eco-friendly gift packaging — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). Designed for sustainable brands seeking plastic-free, fully recyclable gift packaging with a natural aesthetic. The slide-out drawer mechanism with thumb notch provides smooth, tool-free access to contents. A natural jute rope handle (6mm diameter) integrated through reinforced eyelets offers a rustic, tactile carrying experience. Printed with water-based inks on FSC-certified kraft board for complete biodegradability — zero plastic, zero lamination, zero compromise on aesthetics. Produced on KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press with water-based ink system at our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility. Daily capacity: 1.7 million boxes under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

7-Stage QC Gate

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — FSC chain-of-custody verification, kraft board grammage, jute rope diameter (6mm ±0.5mm)
  2. In-Process Printing — water-based ink density check every 500 sheets, SoyPrint certification validation
  3. Die-Cutting Verification — Bobst ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance, thumb notch precision
  4. Drawer Assembly QC — slide-fit tolerance check, eyelet reinforcement integrity
  5. Rope Handle Stress Test — 5kg static load on random sample every 500 units
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.5 (critical) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — random sample pull, eco-certification document package verification (FSC, compostability)
