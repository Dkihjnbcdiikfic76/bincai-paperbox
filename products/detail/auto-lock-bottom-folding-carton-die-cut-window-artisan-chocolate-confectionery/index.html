Auto-lock bottom folding carton with die-cut PET window, designed for artisan chocolate and confectionery packaging by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — a Diamond Member on Made-in-China.com with 23 years of manufacturing excellence (founded 2003). The auto-lock bottom mechanism enables rapid assembly — simply push the sides and the bottom locks into place without tape or glue. The die-cut PET window showcases the product while protecting it from dust and handling. Ideal for artisan chocolatiers, bakeries, and premium confectionery brands. Produced on KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses across our 18,000 sqm facility. Daily output: 1.7 million color boxes + 220,000+ gift boxes.

Applications

  • Artisan chocolate bars, truffles, and praline gift packaging
  • Premium confectionery and candy retail display packaging
  • Bakery pastry, cookie, and macaron gift box packaging
  • Specialty tea and coffee sachet retail packaging
  • Gourmet food gift packaging with product visibility window

Customization Options

  • Window shape: rectangle, oval, heart, custom die-cut silhouette
  • PET window thickness: 0.2–0.5mm with anti-fog or UV-blocking options
  • CMYK offset printing with matte, gloss, or soft-touch lamination
  • Gold/silver hot foil stamping on carton exterior for luxury branding
  • Custom auto-lock bottom geometry for irregular product shapes
  • Food-safe aqueous coating or grease-resistant barrier coating

Production Specifications

  • Material: 350gsm SBS (Solid Bleached Sulfate) or FBB (Folding Box Board), food-grade
  • Printing: KBA 1050 4-color offset + Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV for vibrant graphics
  • Finishing: Die-cut window, PET film insertion, auto-lock bottom creasing, gluing
  • QC: Food contact safety testing, window alignment ±0.3mm, color spectrophotometry
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015 quality management, FSC-C147399 chain of custody
