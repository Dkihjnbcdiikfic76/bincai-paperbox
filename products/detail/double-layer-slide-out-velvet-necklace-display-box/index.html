Product Overview

The double-layer slide-out velvet necklace display box features an innovative dual-tray design: a top compartment for pendant display with die-cut flocked insert, and a pull-out lower drawer for chain storage — both lined in premium satin. The 2mm rigid greyboard construction wrapped in 180gsm velvet flocking delivers luxury tactility, while the hidden magnetic side closure keeps both trays securely in place. Ideal for jewelry retailers, e-commerce brands, and bridal gifting where necklace presentation matters.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Box Construction: 2mm rigid greyboard core, 180gsm velvet flocking wrap on exterior, satin fabric interior lining
  • Slide Mechanism: Smooth-glide dual-tray system with positive-stop rails — no binding or tipping
  • Top Tray: Die-cut velvet display bed with necklace bust form, pendant slot, and satin pull ribbon
  • Bottom Drawer: Full-width chain storage channel with removable satin divider
  • Closure: Hidden magnetic side closure, ≥3.0N holding force, invisible seam when closed
  • Exterior Finish: Hot foil stamping on lid — gold, silver, rose gold, or copper
  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color offset + Heidelberg 7+1 UV
  • Factory: 18,000 sqm integrated facility, Foshan, Guangdong
  • Daily Output: 220,000+ rigid boxes
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody
