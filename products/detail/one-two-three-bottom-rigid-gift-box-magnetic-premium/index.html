Product Overview

Bincai’s 1-2-3 bottom rigid gift box combines the premium feel of a rigid box with the flat-shipping convenience of a folding carton. The patented 1-2-3 collapsible base pops up in seconds — no gluing, no assembly tools. Hidden N42 neodymium magnets in the lid provide a satisfying snap closure. Ideal for corporate gifts, premium apparel, candles, and luxury consumer goods. Ships flat to save 60%+ on freight costs versus pre-assembled rigid boxes.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color offset (max sheet 720×1050mm) + Heidelberg Speedmaster XL 7+1 UV coater (750×1050mm) — full CMYK coverage with inline UV curing for immediate scuff resistance
  • Board: 2.0mm greyboard core (85%+ recycled content), 157gsm C2S art paper wrap — alternative 2.5mm for larger sizes (>300mm)
  • Closure: N42 neodymium magnets (8mm diameter, 1.5mm thickness) embedded between board layers — invisible when closed, 3M 300LSE adhesive-backed
  • Base: Patented 1-2-3 collapsible mechanism — scored fold lines with auto-locking corner tabs, tool-free pop-up assembly under 5 seconds
  • Finish Options: Matte/gloss lamination (1.2 mil), soft-touch velvet lamination (1.5 mil), spot UV gloss varnish, gold/silver/rose gold/holographic foil stamping, single-level and multi-level embossing/debossing
  • Surface Treatments: Anti-scuff aqueous coating, anti-fingerprint matte laminate, textured linen-emboss laminate
  • Die-Cutting: Laser-guided flatbed die-cutting with ±0.3mm tolerance; in-house die-making workshop produces custom dies within 24 hours
  • Daily Output: 1.7 million boxes (post-2025 smart factory expansion to 18,000 sqm)
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody

Flat-Shipping Economics

The 1-2-3 collapsible mechanism is not just a convenience feature — it’s a logistics advantage. By shipping flat and assembling at destination (or by the end customer), you save:

  • 60-70% freight volume vs pre-assembled rigid boxes
  • 40-50% container costs on FCL shipments
  • Lower warehousing footprint — 1,000 flat boxes occupy the same space as ~300 pre-assembled units
  • Reduced damage in transit — flat boards resist crushing better than assembled box voids
