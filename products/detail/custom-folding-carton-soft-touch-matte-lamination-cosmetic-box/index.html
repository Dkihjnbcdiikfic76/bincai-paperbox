Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing’s custom folding carton with soft-touch matte lamination delivers premium tactile experience for cosmetics and skincare brands. The velvety soft-touch surface invites handling, while precise spot UV accents and gold foil branding create visual depth. Auto-lock bottom for quick assembly, food-safe soy inks optional. Manufactured in our ISO 9001:2015 certified 18,000 sqm factory (10,000+8,000 smart factory) — 23 years, Heidelberg 7+1 UV + KBA 1050 presses, 1.7M daily output.

Applications

  • Skincare: Serums, moisturizers, face masks, eye creams, treatment sets
  • Cosmetics: Lipstick, eyeshadow palettes, foundation, makeup gift sets
  • Fragrance: Perfume cartons, cologne boxes, rollerball packaging
  • Hair Care: Premium shampoo/conditioner sets, hair treatment packaging
  • Beauty Subscription Boxes: Monthly beauty box outer packaging
  • Gift with Purchase: Promotional cosmetic gift cartons, sample kit boxes
  • Men’s Grooming: Beard oil, shaving kits, premium men’s skincare

Customization Options

  • Style: Tuck-top (straight/reverse), auto-lock bottom, sleeve, drawer, pillow box
  • Sizes: Custom — 40×40×100mm (lipstick) to 300×250×120mm (gift sets)
  • Board: FSC certified SBS/CUK, 250–400gsm, 100% recycled option
  • Lamination: Soft-touch matte, standard matte, gloss, anti-scratch, anti-fingerprint
  • Spot UV: High-gloss accents on logo, pattern elements, or full design with matte contrast
  • Foil: Gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic — hot stamped or cold foil
  • Finishes: Embossing/debossing, blind emboss, registered emboss, die-cut windows
  • Printing: CMYK offset + Pantone spot, soy-based inks for green brands
  • Assembly: Auto-lock bottom, tucked, glued side-seam, or flat-packed
