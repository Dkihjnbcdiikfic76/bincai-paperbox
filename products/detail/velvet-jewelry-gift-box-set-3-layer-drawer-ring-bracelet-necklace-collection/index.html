FAQ

Q: Can the drawer configuration be customized? A: Yes — choose 1, 2, or 3 drawers and customize each layer: top for necklace hooks (2-4) + ring slots (4-8), middle for bracelet cradles (1-3), bottom for ring slots (8-12) or watches. We create a custom die-cut template for your approval before production.

Q: What velvet colors are available? A: 20+ colors including black, navy, burgundy, ivory, grey, chocolate, emerald, royal blue, and rose pink. Custom PMS-matched velvet is available for orders above 5,000 units. Exterior wrapping can be velvet, leatherette, or art paper with soft-touch lamination.

Q: How quickly can I get samples? A: Free pre-production samples ship within 5-7 business days of artwork approval. Expedited 3-day samples available for time-sensitive projects. Samples include your custom drawer configuration, selected velvet color, and foil branding.

Q: What is the MOQ for custom orders? A: Standard MOQ is 300-1,000 units depending on complexity. For trial orders with standard velvet colors and no custom tooling, we accept orders as low as 100 units. Full custom die-making (drawer inserts) requires 1,000-unit minimum.

Q: Can the LED mirror lid be customized? A: Yes — choose warm white (3000K), cool white (6000K), or RGB. Battery compartment is concealed in the lid lining. LED strip lifespan rated at 10,000+ hours. Mirror size and shape fully customizable.
