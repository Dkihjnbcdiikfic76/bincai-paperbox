Product Overview

Bincai’s velvet ring presentation box with LED spotlight and 360° rotating platform transforms the moment of reveal into a theatrical experience. The 2mm rigid greyboard construction wrapped in premium velvet flocking provides luxurious tactility, while the magnetic front-flap opens to activate three warm-white LEDs that illuminate the ring from optimal angles. The motorized rotating platform (1 RPM) slowly turns the ring for a complete view — powered by a whisper-quiet Mabuchi micro motor with 50+ hours of battery life. Ideal for engagement proposals, luxury jewelry retail, and high-end ring presentations.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Box Construction: 2mm rigid greyboard core, 180gsm velvet flocking wrap — seamless 4-corner wrapping
  • LED Module: 3× warm-white SMD LEDs (3000K color temperature, CRi >90 for accurate gemstone color rendering)
  • Power: 2× CR2032 coin cell batteries (replaceable), 50+ hours runtime, auto-off after 180 seconds
  • Motor: Mabuchi micro DC motor (1 RPM, <25dB noise level), auto-activates on lid opening
  • Platform: Die-cut ABS rotating disc with velvet flocking overlay, 38mm diameter
  • Ring Securing: Die-cut slot with elastic retention band — fits US ring sizes 3-13
  • Closure: Magnetic front-flap with invisible hinge, ≥3.5N holding force
  • Exterior Finish: Hot foil stamping on lid — gold, silver, or rose gold
  • Daily Output: 220,000+ rigid boxes
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody
