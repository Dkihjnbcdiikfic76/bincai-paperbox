Product Overview

The Velvet Travel Jewelry Roll Organizer combines portable elegance with complete jewelry protection — a detachable ring bar holds up to 8 rings, 6 elastic earring card slots secure studs and drops, 2 bracelet snap loops prevent tangling, and a removable zippered necklace pouch with anti-tarnish microfiber lining protects fine chains from oxidation. When unfolded, it presents as a luxurious full-length display; when rolled and tied with the elastic ribbon closure, it compresses to a compact 20×15×4cm — ideal for business travel, destination weddings, or retail gift-with-purchase promotions.

Applications

  • Luxury Jewelry Brands: Branded travel roll included with high-value purchases (necklace + earring sets, bridal collections)
  • Duty-Free & Travel Retail: Compact format ideal for airport and cruise ship retail displays
  • Hotel & Resort Amenities: In-room jewelry safekeeping for luxury hospitality brands
  • Bridal Party Gifts: Custom-embroidered rolls for bridesmaids with matching velvet colors
  • Direct-to-Consumer Subscriptions: Monthly jewelry subscription unboxing experience with reusable packaging
  • Jewelry Trade Shows: Portable display and transport for sales representatives

Customization Options

  • Velvet Color: 18 standard Italian-imported colors (blush pink, midnight navy, emerald green, burgundy, ivory, charcoal, etc.); custom Pantone-matched flocking available for orders 5,000+
  • Exterior Branding: Hot foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic), debossing, embossing, or full-color CMYK offset printing
  • Interior Configuration: Modular insert system — swap ring bar for additional earring cards, add watch pillow loops, or include a bracelet roll
  • Closure Style: Elastic ribbon tie (standard), magnetic snap flap, or leather buckle strap
  • Zipper Pouch Customization: Clear PVC window, monogram patch, or RFID-blocking lining upgrade
  • Eco Upgrade: Replace velvet with FSC-certified recycled felt; elastic with organic cotton ribbon

FAQ

Q: Can the velvet color match our brand’s Pantone? A: Yes — custom Pantone-matched flocking is available for orders 5,000+ units with a 3-week color development lead time. Standard orders use our 18 in-stock Italian velvet colors.

Q: Is the anti-tarnish lining effective for silver jewelry? A: Yes — our silver-ion treated microfiber has been independently tested to ASTM B117 standards, showing <5% tarnish after 30 days of accelerated corrosion testing. For high-sulfur environments, we offer an upgraded activated carbon interlayer option.

Q: What’s the minimum order for custom interior configurations? A: MOQ is 500 units for standard configurations. Custom modular layouts (different ring/earring/bracelet arrangements) are available at 1,000+ units with a one-time die-cutting fee of $80–150.

Q: Can you produce matching outer gift boxes? A: Absolutely — we manufacture complete packaging suites including magnetic closure outer boxes, matching shopping bags, tissue paper, and ribbon in coordinated velvet colors. This is our specialty as a full-service rigid box manufacturer.
