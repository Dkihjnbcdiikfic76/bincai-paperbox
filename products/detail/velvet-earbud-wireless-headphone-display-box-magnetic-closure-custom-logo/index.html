FAQ

Q: Does the magnetic closure stay securely closed during shipping? A: Yes. We use high-grade neodymium magnets calibrated to the box weight and lid dimensions, providing a firm closure that withstands transit vibration. Every magnetic closure design is tested through a 48-hour ISTA 3A simulated transit test — including drop, vibration, and compression — to ensure the lid stays shut and the product stays protected throughout the logistics chain.

Q: Can the foam insert be customized for different earbud models? A: Absolutely. We CNC-mill custom foam inserts with precision cavities matched to your specific earbud model, charging case dimensions, and accessory layout. You provide the 3D CAD file or physical sample, and our tooling team creates an insert with tolerance within ±0.5mm. Multi-layer foam inserts accommodate earbuds, charging case, cable, and manual in a single elegant presentation.

Q: What velvet colors and textures are available? A: We stock over 30 velvet paper colors including classic black, midnight navy, royal blue, burgundy wine, emerald green, charcoal grey, ivory white, blush pink, and chocolate brown. Texture options include smooth microfiber velvet, ribbed corduroy-style velvet, and crushed velvet. Custom Pantone-matched velvet colors are available for orders of 3,000+ units with a one-time color-matching fee of approximately $150.

Sustainability

Bincai is committed to environmentally responsible packaging. Our rigid boxes support your brand’s sustainability goals without compromising on quality or shelf impact:

  • FSC Certified Paper — All paperboard and wrapping paper used in our rigid boxes is sourced from FSC-certified forests and controlled sources. Forest Stewardship Council certification ensures that the fiber in your packaging comes from responsibly managed forests.

  • Recyclable Core Materials — The greyboard core of our rigid boxes is made from 100% recycled post-consumer and post-industrial fiber. The paper wrapping can be separated from the board core in standard recycling streams, maximizing end-of-life material recovery.

  • Soy-Based Inks — We offer soy-based and vegetable-based offset inks as an alternative to conventional petroleum-based inks. Soy inks produce vibrant color reproduction while emitting fewer VOCs and making the printed surface easier to de-ink during recycling.

  • Plastic-Free Construction — The entire box — from greyboard core to paper wrap to magnetic closure — is plastic-free. There is no PET laminate, no PVC window, and no plastic insert. This makes the box fully compatible with paper recycling streams and aligned with the EU Single-Use Plastics Directive.

  • Flat-Ship Efficiency — Our rigid display boxes can be designed with flat-ship (knock-down) construction, where the box ships as a flat sheet with pre-creased fold lines and self-assembly features. This reduces shipping volume by up to 80% versus pre-assembled boxes, cutting both freight cost and carbon footprint.
