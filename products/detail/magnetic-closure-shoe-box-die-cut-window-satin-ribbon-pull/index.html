Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What shoe sizes does this box accommodate? A: Our standard shoe boxes are manufactured to fit US Men’s 7-13 / EU 40-47 / UK 6-12. We produce boxes for all sizes including women’s, children’s, boot-height, and oversized basketball shoes. Custom sizing is available — provide your shoe dimensions and we’ll design the optimal box.

Q: Is the window film durable enough for retail handling? A: Yes. We use 0.3mm clear PET film (the same grade used in retail clamshell packaging) that resists scratching and yellowing. The film is rated UV-resistant for 3+ years of indoor display. For high-contact retail environments, we offer 0.5mm PET upgrade.

Q: Can you print inside the box as well as outside? A: Yes. We offer inside lid printing (brand story, care instructions, social media handles) in 1-2 colors. Inside printing adds 2-3 working days and a plate charge.

Q: What are MOQ and lead times? A: Standard MOQ is 500-1,000 units per size. Lead time is 15-20 working days for standard configurations, 20-25 working days for custom sizes or premium features. Rush orders available at 10-12 working days.

Q: Does the foam insert come in different materials? A: Yes. Standard is EVA foam (durable, moisture-resistant, available in black/white). We also offer PE foam (lighter, more economical), molded pulp (compostable, eco-friendly), and flocked foam (velvet-like finish for luxury shoes). Each material provides different levels of support and presentation quality.

Q: Can you add ventilation holes for leather shoes? A: Yes. We can die-cut ventilation holes (3-5mm diameter) in the side panels with mesh lining to allow air circulation while keeping dust out. This is recommended for leather footwear that requires breathability during storage.

Q: How flat does the box ship? A: Standard configuration ships fully assembled — ready to use. For freight savings, we offer flat-ship collapsible designs that fold flat (15-20mm height) and snap into shape via pre-installed magnetic corners. Flat-ship reduces shipping volume by 70% and is ideal for brands fulfilling from their own warehouses.
