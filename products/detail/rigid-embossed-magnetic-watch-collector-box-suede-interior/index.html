Rigid gift box with embossed pattern and magnetic closure — designed for luxury watch collector display by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. This premium two-piece rigid setup box features deeply embossed surface patterns, a secure magnetic snap closure, and a soft suede-lined interior tailored for single or multi-watch presentation. The 2.5mm greyboard core delivers exceptional crush resistance, while the embossed+foil surface creates a tactile luxury feel that elevates brand perception at retail and unboxing. Produced on our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press and Bobst SP 102 BMA die-cutter across our 18,000 sqm factory with 1.7M daily output capacity.

Customization Options

  • Board: 1.5mm, 2.0mm, 2.5mm, or 3.0mm greyboard core
  • Wrap: 128–200gsm art paper, specialty paper, leatherette, or fabric
  • Interior: suede, velvet, satin, flocked, or foam — single or multi-compartment
  • Embossing: blind deboss, registered emboss, or multi-level sculpted emboss
  • Foil: gold, silver, rose gold, holographic, or matte foil stamping
  • Closure: magnetic snap (standard), ribbon tie, or hidden magnet
  • Watch cushion: removable die-cut suede pillow in custom color
  • Additional features: LED spotlight lid, glass window, certificate drawer

Quality Assurance

Every watch collector box undergoes Bincai’s 7-stage QC process. Embossing depth is measured within ±0.05mm tolerance. Magnetic closure pull force is calibrated to 3–5N for secure closure with smooth opening. Suede adhesive is tested for 72-hour bond strength at 40°C/90% RH.

FAQ

Q: What watch sizes can this box accommodate? A: Custom die-cut cushions are made to your exact watch case diameter (28–48mm) and band length. Multi-watch inserts can hold 2–6 timepieces with individual compartments.

Q: Can you match our brand’s Pantone color? A: Yes. We use G7 color management and match across PMS coated, uncoated, and metallic ranges. Digital proofs provided before production.

Q: What is the lead time for custom embossing dies? A: Embossing and foil stamping die fabrication takes 3–5 working days. Digital proof approval within 24 hours.

Q: How are the boxes shipped to prevent damage? A: Each box is individually poly-bagged, packed in master cartons with corner protectors, and palletized. ISTA 3A drop-tested for transit safety.
