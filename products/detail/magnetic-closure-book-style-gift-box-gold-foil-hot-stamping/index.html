Product Overview

Elegant magnetic closure book-style gift box with gold foil hot stamping, manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003, Foshan, Guangdong). This premium packaging box opens like a book with a hinged spine and hidden neodymium magnet closure for a secure, satisfying snap. Built with 2mm rigid greyboard wrapped in 157gsm art paper, featuring matte lamination with spot UV accents and precision gold foil stamping on cover, spine, and interior panels. Output from Bincai’s 18,000 sqm integrated facility using KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses. Daily capacity: 1.7 million color boxes + 220,000+ gift boxes. Ideal for corporate gifts, luxury product launches, wedding favors, and premium cosmetic sets.

Features

  • Book-Style Hinge: Full-length hinged spine opening like a hardcover book; smooth 180-degree lay-flat design
  • Magnetic Closure: Hidden neodymium (NdFeB) magnets embedded in front cover and flap; precise snap alignment with 4-7 N pull force; tested to 500+ open/close cycles
  • Gold Foil Stamping: Hot foil stamping on cover, spine, and interior panels; available in gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic, and matte metallic
  • Rigid Construction: 2mm greyboard walls (density 950 g/m2) with 157gsm art paper wrap; warp-resistant, tested to 10kg stacking load
  • Premium Finish: Matte lamination with spot UV gloss on logo/title areas for striking contrast; soft-touch finish available
  • Interior Options: Velvet flocking, satin lining, custom-printed paper, or uncoated kraft; ribbon pull tab for easy opening
  • ISO 9001:2015 & FSC: Certified quality management system and sustainable material sourcing
  • MOQ 500-1000: Flexible order quantities for brand launches and corporate programs

QC Process (12 Checkpoints)

  1. Greyboard thickness and density verification (caliper + scale)
  2. Magnet grade and polarity check (Gauss meter)
  3. Magnet embedding depth and position (jig test)
  4. Art paper GSM and grain direction verification
  5. CMYK print registration (KBA 1050, +/- 0.15mm)
  6. Foil stamping temperature, pressure, dwell time (per foil type)
  7. Lamination peel test (ASTM D3359, every batch)
  8. Die-cutting precision and crease depth
  9. Gluing and assembly – wrap alignment, no bubbles
  10. Lid-to-base alignment and gap check (<= 1.5mm)
  11. AQL 2.5 sampling per ISO 2859-1
  12. Pre-shipment audit with full dimensional and visual inspection
