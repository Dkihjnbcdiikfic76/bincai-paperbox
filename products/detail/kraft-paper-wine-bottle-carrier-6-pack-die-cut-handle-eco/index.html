When to Choose Each Format

  • 6-Pack Kraft Carrier: Your default for volume — wine clubs, tasting room take-home, festival merchandising, and corporate gifts where cost efficiency and sustainability messaging drive the decision. The flat-pack design slashes warehousing and freight costs.
  • Individual Gift Box: When the bottle IS the gift — single-bottle retail display, holiday gifting, VIP client appreciation. Higher per-unit cost justified by the premium reveal experience.
  • Wooden Crate: Reserve for ultra-premium positioning — limited editions, auction lots, collector verticals. The cost and freight penalty is justified only when the packaging itself becomes a keepsake or display piece.

FAQ

Q: What’s the weight capacity of the die-cut handle? A: The standard die-cut handle with single-layer kraft supports 9–10 kg safely (full 6-bottle load). The reinforced double-layer handle option supports 15 kg for heavier configurations (Champagne bottles, 12-bottle carriers). All handles are static-load tested at 150% of rated capacity for 60 seconds.

Q: Is the carrier fully recyclable? A: Yes — when specified with uncoated FSC kraft board, soy-based inks, and kraft/cotton handle materials, the entire carrier is 100% recyclable in standard paper/cardboard recycling streams. No plastic separation required. For the PLA window option, PLA is industrially compostable (EN 13432) but should be noted as such in recycling instructions.

Q: Can the carrier be assembled without glue or tape? A: Yes! The entire carrier is designed with self-locking die-cut tabs — fold the divider tabs perpendicular, interlock the side panels, and the carrier is fully assembled in under 30 seconds. No glue, tape, staples, or tools required. Illustrated assembly instructions can be printed directly on the carrier for end-user assembly.

Q: Will the bottles clink together during transport? A: No — the interlocking divider tabs maintain a minimum 5mm separation gap between all bottles. The carrier has been vibration tested (ASTM D999, 30 minutes at 1.0G RMS) with zero bottle-to-bottle contact. For long-distance shipping, we recommend adding the optional bottle neck lock tabs for additional security.

Q: Can you print full-color photos on kraft board? A: Yes — our KBA 1050 offset press prints CMYK on kraft board with excellent results. While the natural brown kraft substrate shifts color gamut slightly (warmer tones, reduced brightness vs white paper), we achieve rich, appealing graphics. For maximum color accuracy, we recommend white kraft or coated kraft options. We always provide a printed proof on your chosen board before production.

Q: What’s the minimum order for custom printing? A: MOQ is 1000–3000 units for full custom CMYK printing with custom die tooling. For stock sizes with single-color or foil-stamp branding only, MOQ can be as low as 500 units. New die tooling cost: $200–400 (one-time, amortized on first order).

Q: How are the carriers shipped? A: Carriers are shipped flat (unassembled blanks) in export-grade corrugated master cartons on heat-treated ISPM 15 pallets. Flat-pack design achieves 80% volume reduction vs assembled carriers — 10,000+ carrier blanks fit on a standard pallet versus approximately 2,000 assembled carriers. This dramatically reduces freight costs and carbon footprint.

Q: What certifications apply to this product? A: ISO 9001:2015 (quality management), FSC Chain-of-Custody (responsible forestry), OK Biodegradable SOIL (TÜV Austria, for the kraft + soy ink + natural handle configuration), and we comply with REACH and FDA 21 CFR for indirect food contact (relevant for wine packaging).
