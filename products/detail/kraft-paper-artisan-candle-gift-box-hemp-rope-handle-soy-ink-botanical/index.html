Product Overview

The Kraft Paper Artisan Candle Gift Box brings together uncompromising sustainability credentials with artisanal aesthetics. Every material in this box has been selected for end-of-life circularity: the 1.5mm rigid greyboard is 100% post-consumer recycled, the exterior wrap is FSC-certified 157gsm kraft paper printed with soy-based botanical inks (zero petroleum solvents), the carry handle is natural undyed hemp rope, and the interior candle tray is molded from PLA (polylactic acid) certified to EN 13432 for industrial composting. The botanical print series — featuring six nature-inspired illustrations (lavender fields, eucalyptus sprigs, heritage roses, citrus groves, pine forests, chamomile blooms) — is printed on our KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press using soy-based inks that produce rich, matte colors without the chemical odor of conventional petroleum inks. When the candle is enjoyed and the box has served its gifting purpose, the entire package (minus the hemp rope, which is reusable) can be composted or recycled in standard paper streams.

Sustainability & End-of-Life

This product represents Bincai’s commitment to circular packaging design. The lifecycle pathway:

  1. Manufacturing: 100% PCR greyboard, FSC-certified kraft wrap, soy-based inks with <5% VOCs, hemp rope handle (rain-fed crop, no irrigation). Factory powered by 800kW rooftop solar (35% of energy)
  2. Use Phase: Reusable as storage box after candle consumption. Hemp rope handle can be repurposed. PLA tray can be hand-washed and reused
  3. End-of-Life Option A — Industrial Composting: PLA tray certified to EN 13432 (disintegrates in 12 weeks). Kraft box components compost in 8–12 weeks. Remove brass eyelets and magnetic snap before composting
  4. End-of-Life Option B — Paper Recycling: All paper components (greyboard, kraft wrap) are fully recyclable. PLA tray should be removed before paper recycling (sorting instructions printed on box base)
  5. Carbon Footprint: 0.42 kg CO₂e per box (cradle-to-gate, verified by Carbon Trust), offset through Gold Standard-certified reforestation

FAQ

Q: Is the box actually compostable — or just recyclable? A: The PLA tray is certified compostable to EN 13432 (industrial composting, 12 weeks). The kraft paper and greyboard components are compostable but are more efficiently processed through paper recycling streams. We recommend recycling the paper and composting the PLA insert. Recycling/composting instructions are printed on the box base.

Q: Can I use this box for products other than candles? A: Yes — the PLA tray can be custom-molded for soap bars, skincare jars, tea tins, chocolate bars, or any product within the cavity dimensions. Custom mold development is $200–400 depending on complexity, applicable to orders of 1,000+ units.

Q: Will the soy ink smudge or fade if the box is handled frequently? A: Soy-based inks on uncoated kraft paper have moderate rub resistance — suitable for gifting and shelf display. For applications requiring high rub resistance (e.g., frequent handling at markets), we recommend adding an aqueous matte coating which increases rub resistance by 80% while maintaining paper recyclability.

Q: How does the box perform in humid environments? A: Kraft paper has natural moisture absorption properties. In high-humidity environments (>80% RH), the box may experience slight dimensional changes (±1.5mm) and the greyboard stiffness may decrease by ~15%. For tropical markets, we recommend the aqueous coating option which provides a moisture barrier. The PLA tray is unaffected by humidity.
