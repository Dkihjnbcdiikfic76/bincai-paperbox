Customization Options

  • Board Grade: 100% post-consumer recycled Kraft (300gsm standard, upgrade to 350gsm for heavier products); FSC-certified virgin Kraft option for brands requiring chain-of-custody documentation
  • Finish: Natural brown Kraft (uncoated), white Kraft with recycled content, or clay-coated one-side (C1S) Kraft for sharper print quality
  • Printing: Soy-based ink offset (KBA 1050) — 1-4 colors; water-based flexo for large solid areas; custom rubber stamp option for ultra-low MOQ
  • Closure: Hemp twine (natural, dyed, or waxed), jute string, cotton tape, recycled paper ribbon, or tuck-lock with no tie

Quality Assurance

  1. Material Inspection — Recycled content certification verification, grammage check (ISO 536), Cobb moisture absorption (TAPPI T441)
  2. Board Integrity — Burst strength (Mullen test, TAPPI T810), ring crush, visual inspection for foreign particles
  3. Print QC — Soy ink coverage uniformity, color consistency ΔE ≤ 2.5 (higher tolerance for uncoated Kraft substrate), rub resistance test
  4. Die-Cutting — ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance, fold line depth calibration, locking tab engagement test
  5. Twine Attachment — Tension test (target: 15-25N break strength), knot security, dye transfer test for colored twine
  6. Assembly QC — Box folding accuracy, closure alignment, window alignment (if applicable)
  7. Pre-Shipment — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.0 (minor) per ISO 2859-1 Level II, compostability documentation packet
