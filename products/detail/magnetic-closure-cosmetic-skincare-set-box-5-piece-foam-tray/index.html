Product Overview

A premium magnetic-closure rigid box designed specifically for 5-piece cosmetic and skincare gift sets. The custom die-cut EVA foam tray cradles each product (serum bottles, cream jars, toner, etc.) with precision-fit cavities that prevent movement during shipping and create a striking unboxing reveal. Soft-touch matte lamination on the exterior delivers a velvety tactile experience, while gold foil hot-stamped branding on the lid communicates luxury. Manufactured at Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. with in-house die-cutting, lamination, and assembly.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Board: 2.0mm greyboard covered in 157gsm C1S art paper with soft-touch matte lamination

FAQ

Q: Can the foam tray be reconfigured for different bottle shapes and sizes? A: Yes. The EVA foam tray is fully customizable via our in-house CNC routing center. You provide product dimensions (diameter, height, cap style) and we produce precision-fit cavities with ±0.3mm tolerance. Lead time for new tray tooling: 5–7 days for sampling, 10–14 days for production.

Q: What is the difference between EVA foam and flocked foam inserts? A: EVA foam (45kg/m³ density, available in black or white) provides a precise, clean technical look ideal for modern skincare brands. Flocked foam (velvet-over-foam) adds a plush luxury feel and is preferred for high-end cosmetic brands. Both options are CNC die-cut and available in the same lead time.

Q: Can the soft-touch finish withstand high-humidity environments like bathrooms? A: Yes. The soft-touch BOPP 18-micron matte lamination is moisture-resistant and passes 100-cycle Sutherland Rub Testing with no visible wear. For extreme humidity environments, we offer an optional aqueous over-coat layer for enhanced moisture protection.

Q: What is the MOQ for custom color-matched foam? A: MOQ is 500 units for standard black or white EVA. For custom color-matched foam (matched to your brand’s Pantone), MOQ increases to 1,000 units due to material minimums from our foam suppliers.

Q: How are the magnets secured — can they come loose during shipping? A: Each N35 neodymium magnet (4 per box) is mechanically embedded into the greyboard cavity during assembly, not surface-glued. This provides 2.5kg total holding force and survives 1.2m ISTA 3A drop testing without magnet displacement. We perform a 50-piece batch pull test every shift to verify magnet retention.
