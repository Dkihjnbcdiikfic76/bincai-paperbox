FAQ

Is the bamboo fiber tray truly compostable? Yes. The molded bamboo fiber tray is certified home-compostable per EN 13432 and will break down in a home compost pile within 6-12 months.

What is the minimum order quantity? MOQ starts at 500 units. We offer eco-brand discovery runs of 250 units for new sustainable beauty brands.

Can you print full-color on recycled kraft? Yes. Our KBA 1050 press handles 4-color CMYK soy-based printing on recycled kraft board. Expect a slightly more muted, natural color palette compared to coated white board — this is intentional and aligns with eco-brand aesthetics.

Is the entire box plastic-free? Yes. We use no plastic components — the tray is bamboo fiber, the window (if specified) is compostable PLA, the closure is natural fiber twine, and all adhesives are water-based.

What certifications do you hold? Bincai holds ISO 9001:2015 (quality management), FSC Chain-of-Custody (sustainable forestry), and individual product certifications (EN 13432 for compostable components) are available upon request with batch documentation.
