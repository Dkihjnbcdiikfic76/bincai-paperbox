FAQ

Q: Will the chocolate truffles be visible through the window without the PET film fogging up? A: Yes. We use premium-grade PET window film (0.3mm standard) with optional anti-fog treatment that prevents condensation fogging — critical when chocolates are refrigerated and then displayed at room temperature. The film is optically clear with light transmission >92%, so your truffles look as vivid through the window as they do in open air. We also offer anti-scratch PET for high-handling retail environments where the box is repeatedly picked up and examined.

Q: Is the gold foil stamping durable, or will it scratch off during shipping and handling? A: Our hot foil stamping process uses a heated brass die (120-150°C) that bonds metallized polyester film directly into the paperboard surface under controlled pressure. The result is permanently fused — it won’t scratch, peel, or fade under normal handling conditions. We validate foil adhesion with a 3M 610 tape peel test on every production batch. For extra durability, we can apply matte lamination over the foil-stamped area for a tone-on-tone luxury effect.

Q: Can the window shape be custom — for example, shaped like our brand logo? A: Absolutely. We can create a die-cut window in virtually any shape — your brand logo silhouette, a heart for Valentine’s Day, a snowflake for Christmas, or an abstract geometric pattern. The custom window die adds approximately $80-150 in one-time tooling cost and 2-3 extra days in pre-production for die fabrication. The PET film is then cut and applied to match the exact window shape with precision alignment.

Q: How does Bincai ensure food safety for chocolate packaging? A: Our chocolate packaging production follows a strict food-safety protocol: (1) all inks are soy-based, low-migration formulations that meet FDA 21 CFR and EU 1935/2004 standards, (2) water-based adhesives and coatings eliminate solvent contamination risk, (3) the PET window film is food-grade and phthalate-free, (4) our production area for food-contact cartons is separated from non-food production to prevent cross-contamination, and (5) we provide a Certificate of Compliance with every food-packaging order documenting batch-level ink and material traceability.

Q: Can I order different sizes of the same design — say a 6-piece and 12-piece truffle box? A: Yes. Once your artwork and design are approved, we can produce the same design in multiple sizes. Because the printing plate and foil die are created once and then scaled, producing additional sizes typically adds only the window die tooling cost per new size ($80-150 one-time). This is a very common request for chocolate brands that want a cohesive gift collection — 4-piece, 6-piece, 9-piece, and 12-piece boxes all in matching design language.
