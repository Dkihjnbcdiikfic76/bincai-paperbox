This Rigid Two-Piece Cigar Presentation Box is a luxury packaging solution for premium cigar brands and tobacco retailers. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) at our 18,000 sqm dual-factory facility in Foshan, Pearl River Delta, this humidor-style box combines traditional craftsmanship with modern production precision. FAQ

Q: Is the Spanish cedar authentic and CITES-compliant?

A: Yes. We use genuine Cedrela odorata (Spanish cedar) sourced from CITES-compliant suppliers with full chain-of-custody documentation. Each shipment includes CITES export permits and phytosanitary certificates. We can provide documentation for your customs clearance.

Q: What cigar capacities can you manufacture?

A: We produce boxes for 1, 3, 5, 10, 15, 20, and 25 cigars. Tray routing is CNC-cut to your specific vitola dimensions — Robusto (50 ring × 124mm), Toro (52 × 152mm), Churchill (47 × 178mm), Corona (42 × 142mm), Lancero (38 × 190mm), or any custom gauge. MOQ is 500 units per size configuration.

Q: How does the humidity seal work without a traditional humidor clasp?

A: The two-piece telescopic lid design creates a precision friction-fit seal. The lid’s internal dimension is calibrated to be 0.3–0.5mm smaller than the base exterior, creating an interference fit that maintains a stable microclimate. For enhanced sealing, we can integrate a thin silicone gasket into the lid rim or add magnetic closures. We also offer an integrated Boveda or humidity pack holder as a built-in feature.

Q: Can I get gold foil stamping on textured or linen-wrapped surfaces?

A: Absolutely. We’ve developed specialized foil stamping techniques for textured substrates including linen, leatherette, and embossed papers. Our Bobst hot-foil press achieves ±0.15mm registration even on textured surfaces. We recommend combination embossing + foil (sculpted foil) for the most premium effect — the foil sinks into the embossed depression, creating a tactile, three-dimensional brand mark.

Q: What about international shipping — will the cedar lining cause customs issues?

A: We provide full CITES documentation, phytosanitary certificates, and FSC Chain-of-Custody certificates with every export shipment. Our logistics team handles customs clearance documentation for the Americas (USDA APHIS), EU (EU Timber Regulation), and Asia-Pacific markets. The cedar veneer is kiln-dried and treated to meet ISPM 15 standards where applicable. Typical air freight transit time is 5–7 days; sea freight 18–25 days.
