Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — board caliper, lamination film gauge, foil grade verification
  2. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤2.0 target
  3. Lamination QC — bond strength test, visual inspection for bubbles/wrinkles
  4. Foil Stamping QC — adhesion test on first-off and every 1,000th unit, ±0.15mm registration
  5. Die-Cutting & Gluing — dimensional verification, glue bead inspection, auto-bottom lock test
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.0 (minor) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — random sample pull, full functional test under D65 lighting

FAQ

Q: Will the soft-touch lamination scratch or wear during shipping?

A: Our 25μm BOPP soft-touch film is abrasion-resistant and passes ISTA 1A drop testing. For high-friction shipping environments, we offer an optional 30μm heavy-duty film. The matte surface also resists fingerprint marks better than gloss finishes.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom cosmetics cartons?

A: MOQ is 1,000 units for standard constructions. For cartons with foil stamping or soft-touch lamination, MOQ is 3,000 units. Sample turnaround is 5-7 days with free pre-production samples.

Q: Can you match a specific brand color for the carton?

A: Yes. We print CMYK + Pantone spot colors and match within ΔE ≤2.0 using X-Rite spectrophotometry. For foil stamping, gold, silver, rose gold, copper, and holographic foils are available — custom foil colors require 5,000+ unit orders.

Q: How long does production take?

A: Standard lead time is 10-14 days after artwork approval. Rush orders can be expedited to 7 days with a 20% surcharge. Sampling takes 5-7 days.

Q: Can the carton be made with a window to show the product?

A: Yes. We offer die-cut PET/PVC windows in any shape, full-panel acetate windows, and double-window configurations. Window film thickness ranges from 0.2mm to 0.5mm with anti-fog options for refrigerated products.
