FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom velvet bracelet boxes? A: MOQ starts at 500 units for standard configurations with existing cushion profiles. Custom tooling for unique bracelet pillow shapes requires MOQ of 1,000+ units. OEM/ODM trial orders are welcome.

Q: How many two-tone velvet color combinations are available? A: We stock 20+ nylon flock colors (black, navy, grey, burgundy, cream, chocolate, emerald, blush, ivory) — mix any two for interior contrast. Custom Pantone-matched flocking is available for orders of 3,000+ units.

Q: Can the box fit different bracelet styles and sizes? A: Yes. We offer 3 standard bracelet pillow profiles (round, oval, rectangular) with elastic retention bands. Custom pillow tooling supports cuff bracelets, charm bracelets, bangles, and anklets up to 200×150mm internal dimensions.

Q: How long do samples and production take? A: Pre-production samples ship within 5-7 days of artwork approval. Standard production lead time is 10-14 days. Complex dual-tone designs with custom foil dies and embossing may require 15-20 days.

Q: Are the materials jewelry-safe and anti-tarnish? A: Yes. All velvet flocking is applied to anti-tarnish treated greyboard. Adhesives are water-based PVA, VOC-free. We offer silver protection-grade lining with activated carbon interlayer for sterling silver and fine jewelry — prevents tarnish for up to 2 years under normal storage conditions.
