FAQ

Q: What’s the difference between satin ribbon and twisted paper rope handles? A: Satin ribbon handles (nylon or polyester, 15-40mm width) offer a luxurious feel with color-matching to your brand palette — ideal for fashion, jewelry, and premium retail. Twisted paper rope handles are kraft-colored, fully recyclable, and convey an eco-natural aesthetic — popular with organic food brands and sustainable fashion labels. Both achieve 15kg+ pull strength.

Q: Can you print inside the bag as well as outside? A: Yes — we can print logos, patterns, or messages on the interior surface. Interior printing is commonly used for brand storytelling, care instructions, or QR codes. Note that interior printing adds one additional pass and may extend lead time by 2-3 days.

Q: What’s the MOQ for custom sizes? A: Standard MOQ is 500-1000 units per design. For custom sizes requiring new die-cut tooling, MOQ may increase to 1,500 units to amortize tooling costs. We’re flexible on trial orders — contact us for small-batch options.

Q: Are your paper bags water-resistant? A: Our matte/gloss BOPP lamination (25-micron) provides water resistance for light rain and condensation — bags won’t disintegrate if caught in a drizzle. For full waterproofing, we can apply a heavier lamination or aqueous coating. Kraft paper without lamination is not water-resistant and is recommended for indoor retail use.

Q: Can I order different sizes in one production batch? A: Yes — we can produce multiple sizes within the same order as long as they share the same print design and paper stock. Each size requires its own die-cut tooling. This is common for retail chains needing small, medium, and large shopping bags with consistent branding.
