Product Overview

The custom printed multi-section drawer gift box combines the elegance of a rigid presentation box with the practicality of organized pull-out compartments — perfect for high-value gift sets where each item deserves its own showcase space. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003, Foshan, Guangdong), this rigid drawer box is built with dense 1200–1600gsm greyboard, wrapped in premium art paper, and lined with plush velvet or flocked interiors. Our 18,000 sqm facility (10,000+8,000 smart factory, expanded 2025) runs KBA 1050 4-color offset and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses, producing 220,000+ rigid boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certifications.

The pull-out drawer mechanism, precision-engineered with ±1mm gap tolerances, glides smoothly to reveal multi-section compartments. Each section can be customized with die-cut cavities, ribbon pull-tabs, and satin-lined nests to cradle individual items securely. Ideal for jewelry collections, luxury writing instruments, watch sets, premium chocolates, and corporate executive gifts.

Quality Control

Bincai’s 7-stage QC for multi-section drawer boxes:

  1. Material Inspection — Greyboard density ≥650 g/m², liner GSM verification, adhesive tack test
  2. Print Registration — Heidelberg Prinect Inpress inline spectrophotometry, ΔE ≤ 2.0
  3. Die-Cut Accuracy — Cavity dimensions ±0.15mm, drawer track alignment check
  4. Lining Inspection — Flocking density ≥ 150 g/m², no bare spots, adhesive bleed check
  5. Drawer Function — 500-cycle open/close durability test; slide resistance 3–8 N
  6. Assembly — AQL 2.5 sampling per ISO 2859-1, gap tolerance ±1mm, ribbon attachment pull-test ≥ 20 N
  7. Pre-Shipment — ISTA 1A drop test, humidity chamber 48h at 65% RH, 100% visual inspection
