FAQ

Q: How strong are the magnetic closures — will they hold if the box is turned upside down? A: Our standard magnetic closure boxes use two neodymium magnets rated for 0.8–1.2 kg of closure force each, tested to hold the lid closed when inverted with a full 1.5 kg payload. For heavier contents or extra-thick lids, we can upgrade to four magnets or larger N52-grade neodymium magnets. Every design undergoes a 100-cycle open/close test and inverted shake test as part of our ISO 9001 quality protocol.

Q: Can the magnetic closure box be customized for year-round use, not just Christmas? A: Absolutely. While this listing focuses on Christmas and holiday designs, the same magnetic rigid box construction is available in any color, finish, and theme — corporate blue, wedding white, birthday pastels, or everyday luxury black. The magnetic closure mechanism is season-agnostic. Simply specify your design theme and we will produce packaging that works for your full product calendar.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for a fully custom design vs using an existing template? A: For fully custom designs (new die, new size, new artwork), MOQ starts at 500 units. For existing templates with custom printing only (your artwork on our standard die), MOQ can be as low as 300 units. Free pre-production samples are provided in 5–7 days for approval before mass production begins.

Q: How do these boxes ship — are they assembled or flat? A: All magnetic closure rigid boxes ship flat (knocked down) with the magnetic flap separated but attached. This reduces shipping volume by 60–75% compared to pre-assembled boxes. Assembly takes approximately 10–15 seconds per box — simply fold up the side walls, tuck the flaps, and the magnetic closure is ready. We include assembly instructions with every shipment.
