The Hidden Compartment Experience

The magic is in the reveal. The recipient opens the box to find their jewelry beautifully presented. Then — a subtle tab, a smooth slide, and the hidden drawer emerges carrying words that make the moment unforgettable. For brands, this is the packaging equivalent of a QR code that leads to a personal video message — but more tactile, more intimate, and completely analog.

Applications

  • Marriage Proposals: Engrave “Will you marry me?” in the hidden drawer — a surprise after the ring reveal
  • Anniversary Gifts: A personal message marking years together, paired with a meaningful piece of jewelry
  • Luxury Brand Storytelling: Brands engrave their founding story or sustainability commitment in the hidden drawer
  • Mother’s Day / Valentine’s Day: Personalized messages that turn a jewelry purchase into a keepsake
  • Corporate Recognition: Service award messages engraved alongside the gift jewelry

  1. Board Density Verification: Caliper and grammage checked per pallet
  2. Velvet Adhesion: Bubble inspection under side-lighting, seam tolerance ±0.5mm
  3. Drawer Slide Test: 300 cycles of open/close — smooth operation, no binding
  4. Magnetic Pull Force: Measured per closure — 3.5–5.0N range
  5. Hinge Durability: 500 open/close cycles — zero loosening
  6. Engraving Alignment: Laser registration within ±0.2mm
  7. Final AQL 1.5: Full visual inspection including hidden drawer reveal experience
