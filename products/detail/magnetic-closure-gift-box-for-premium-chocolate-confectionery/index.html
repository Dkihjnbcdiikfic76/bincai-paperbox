Production Workflow

  1. Prepress & Design — artwork review, color separation, and CTP plate making with digital proof for color approval
  2. Board Laminating — greyboard laminated with selected art paper on high-speed sheet laminator
  3. Offset Printing — KBA 1050 CMYK printing at 15,000 sheets/hour with inline spectrophotometer color monitoring
  4. Finishing — lamination (gloss/matte/soft-touch), then hot foil stamping and embossing on Heidelberg cylinder press
  5. UV Specialty — Heidelberg 7+1 UV for spot UV accents, white underprint, and high-gloss effects
  6. Die-Cutting — flatbed die-cutter shapes the box blank with cavity holes for tray insertion
  7. Tray Forming — PET/PVC trays are thermoformed or CNC-cut, then inserted and bonded
  8. Magnet Insertion — neodymium magnets embedded into board layers with precise polarity alignment
  9. Assembly & Folding — box is folded, magnet closure tested, and flat-packed for efficient shipping
  10. QC Inspection — 100% visual check: foil registration, magnet strength, tray fit, surface finish uniformity

Food Safety & Quality Assurance

All food-contact materials — PET trays, interior paper liners, adhesives — comply with FDA 21 CFR and EU Regulation (EC) No 1935/2004 for food-contact articles. Paperboard is sourced from FSC-certified mills with full chain-of-custody traceability. Inline QC checks at every stage verify cavity precision (±0.5mm), magnetic closure strength (minimum 3N pull force), hot foil registration (±0.1mm), and surface finish consistency. Optional features include inner wax paper or greaseproof lining for direct food contact, tamper-evident sealing, and desiccant packet compartments for humidity-sensitive confections.
