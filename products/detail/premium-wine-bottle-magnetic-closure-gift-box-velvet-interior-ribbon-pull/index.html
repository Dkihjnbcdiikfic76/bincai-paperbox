Premium wine bottle magnetic closure gift box with plush velvet interior and elegant satin ribbon pull tab by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003). Designed for single or dual wine bottles, champagne, premium spirits, and craft beverages. The magnetic snap-shut closure creates a premium unboxing experience while the velvet-lined interior cradles and protects the bottle with die-cut neck and base form-fitting cavities. Gold foil logo stamping and UV spot varnish accents on Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press. Daily output: 1.7 million color boxes + 220,000+ gift boxes.

What sets this wine box apart is the precision bottle fit engineering: the velvet-wrapped die-cut insert is modeled from the exact bottle dimensions (provided by customer or measured from samples at Bincai’s incoming QC station). The insert securely holds the bottle neck and base in separate cavities, preventing lateral movement during transit while the soft velvet surface protects the label and glass from abrasion. The magnetic closure uses 4 N52 neodymium magnets calibrated to 8–12N opening force — firm enough for a satisfying “snap” feel, light enough for one-handed opening. This box has been specified by boutique wineries in Napa Valley, Bordeaux négociants, and Japanese whisky distilleries who demand packaging that communicates rarity and craftsmanship.

Why Bincai?

A wine bottle gift box is a packaging paradox: it must feel substantial and luxurious (communicating the value of the wine inside) yet remain lightweight enough for economical shipping. It must protect a heavy, fragile glass bottle during transit yet open elegantly at the unboxing moment. Bincai solves this paradox through precision insert engineering — the velvet-wrapped die-cut cavities are modeled from the exact bottle geometry, creating a form-fit that secures the bottle without excess material weight. The magnetic closure is calibrated to 8–12N: firm enough that the box won’t accidentally open in transit, light enough that the recipient can open it one-handed while holding the wine. This is packaging engineered for both logistics and emotion — protecting the bottle through global supply chains, then delivering a luxury reveal at the moment of gifting.

The Bincai Difference:

  • Precision Bottle Fit Engineering: Die-cut insert cavities are modeled from your exact bottle dimensions — neck grip, base cup, shoulder clearance all measured to ±1mm. Sample bottles are received at Bincai’s incoming QC and digitized for insert tooling
  • Calibrated Magnetic Experience: 4 N52 neodymium magnets per box, force-tested to 8–12N — the “goldilocks zone” where closure feels secure but opening is effortless
  • Label-Friendly Interior: Velvet and satin linings are tested for colorfastness and abrasion resistance — no dye transfer to wine labels, no surface scratching during insertion/removal
  • Foil Registration on Texture: Gold foil stamping on velvet texture is a technical challenge most factories avoid. Bincai’s Bobst SP 102 BMA with heated die achieves clean foil transfer even on textured surfaces
  • Full In-House Production: Offset printing → die-cutting → foil stamping → box wrapping → magnet insertion → velvet lining → insert fabrication → final assembly — one roof, one quality system, one accountable partner
