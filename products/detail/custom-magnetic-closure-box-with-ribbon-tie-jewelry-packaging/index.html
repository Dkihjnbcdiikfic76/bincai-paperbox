Product Overview

An elegant magnetic closure box with a satin ribbon tie — the signature packaging solution for high-end jewelry brands seeking a tactile, memorable unboxing experience. The concealed N52-grade neodymium magnets deliver a refined snap-shut action with zero visible hardware, while the hand-tied satin ribbon adds a luxurious ceremonial quality. Manufactured at Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003), our 18,000 sqm total facility in Foshan operates KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses, producing 1.7 million boxes daily. ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certified with full chain-of-custody traceability.

Customization Options

  • Box Style: Book-style magnetic flap, two-piece lid-and-base, drawer-slide with ribbon pull, clamshell with ribbon hinge
  • Sizing: 100×80×40mm (single ring) to 400×300×100mm (multi-piece jewelry suite)
  • Exterior Wrapping: 128–200gsm art paper (matte, gloss, soft-touch velvet, linen-textured, pearlescent, metallic); PU leatherette; handcrafted wood veneer
  • Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, copper, rose gold, holographic — flat stamp, multi-level emboss/deboss, registered micro-stamp on ribbon knot area
  • Finishes: Matte/gloss lamination, soft-touch coating, spot UV, aqueous varnish, textured embossing, edge gilding (gold/silver foil on board edges)
  • Magnets: N52 neodymium (6–10mm diameter) — single coin, double strip, or full-perimeter magnetic frame for 300×250mm+ formats
  • Ribbon: 15–25mm double-face satin in 20+ Pantone-matched colors; grosgrain, velvet, or organza ribbon options; pre-tied bow, wrap-around corset-style, or knot-and-pull closure
  • Ribbon Embellishments: Wax seal charm, metal logo plaque, pearl bead, or crystal pendant on ribbon ends
  • Interior: Electrostatic velvet flocking (12+ colors), satin jacquard lining, custom-printed paper with brand pattern, die-cut EVA foam tray with velvet wrap
  • Interior Compartments: Ring slot with slitted foam insert, necklace groove with elastic retainer, earring card slots, bracelet roll, removable tiered tray system
  • Inserts: Custom die-cut flocked platform, vacuum-formed flocked PET tray, satin-covered foam riser, elastic security bands for product hold
  • Additional Features: Certificate/document pocket under lid, hidden drawer in base, magnetic accessory compartment, LED lighting with lid-activated switch

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV offset — crisp whites, deep blacks, vibrant Pantone with inline UV coating; KBA Rapida 105 4-color for high-volume runs
  • Board: Rigid greyboard 1200–1600gsm, precision double-wrapped construction with ±0.3mm edge alignment; MDF core 9–12mm option for extra heavyweight premium feel
  • Magnets: N52-grade neodymium, 6–10mm diameter, inserted between board layers during wrapping — fully concealed, zero visible hardware
  • Ribbon Integration: Heat-sealed between board and wrapping paper — zero adhesive bleed, zero ribbon fraying at attachment point
  • Foil Stamping: Bobst SP 102 BMA with multi-zone temperature control, ±0.15mm registration, up to 6,000 sheets/hour
  • Velvet Flocking: Electrostatic flocking at 1.0–1.5mm nylon fiber length, ≥95% coverage density, 12+ color options
  • Assembly: Automatic rigid box line — corner taping, wrapping, magnet insertion, ribbon placement; manual interior finishing station
  • Daily Output: 220,000+ rigid boxes, including magnetic closure and ribbon tie variants
  • Lead Time: 10–15 working days new tooling; 7–10 days repeat orders

Quality Assurance

  • Magnet Alignment: 100% automated inspection — lid closes flush, ≤0.3mm gap on all four corners and center seam
  • Magnetic Pull Force: Each magnet ≥1.2kgF holding force (N52 grade); closure requires deliberate lift — no accidental opening in transit
  • Ribbon Pull Test: 5kgF sustained load for 60 seconds — zero detachment, zero fraying, ribbon remains securely bonded
  • Ribbon Colorfastness: Xenon arc test 40 hours per ISO 105-B02 — ≤4.0 ΔE color shift; wet crock test — ≥Grade 4 (AATCC 8)
  • Hinge Durability: 5,000 open/close cycles at 30 cycles/minute — zero hinge looseness, zero paper delamination, zero magnet displacement
  • Velvet Adhesion: Cross-hatch tape test per ASTM D3359 — 5B rating, zero fiber shedding; abrasion test — zero visible wear after 100 rub cycles
  • Foil Adhesion: Sutherland rub test — 50 double rubs with 4-lb weight, zero visible wear, ≤1% foil transfer
  • Print Quality: Spectro-densitometer verification — ΔE ≤2.0 vs Pantone reference on all production sheets
  • Drop Test: ISTA 1A — 10 drops from 760mm on all faces, edges, and corners — zero structural failure, contents protected
  • Dimensional Tolerance: ±0.5mm on exterior dimensions; ±0.2mm on interior compartment fit
  • ISO 9001:2015 quality management system with documented inspection gates at every production stage
