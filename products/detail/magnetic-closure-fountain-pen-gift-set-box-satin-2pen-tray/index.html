FAQ

Q: What pen sizes can the tray accommodate? A: Standard die-cut slots fit fountain pens up to 150mm length and 16mm diameter. Custom dimensions available for oversized, pocket, or specialty pen formats. We request sample pens or CAD drawings for precise tray engineering.

Q: Can you add our brand’s logo to the satin interior? A: Yes. We offer gold foil hot stamping or silk-screen printing on satin lining for subtle interior branding. Embossed logos on the tray surface are also available.

Q: What is the minimum order for custom foil stamping dies? A: MOQ is 300 units. Foil stamping die fabrication takes 3-5 working days. No die charge for repeat orders.

Q: Can the box include space for additional accessories? A: Yes. We can add cavities for ink cartridges, converters, cleaning cloths, certificates of authenticity, or USB drives. Multi-tier designs with a lower drawer for accessories are also available.
