Product Overview

Bincai’s straight tuck end folding carton with braille embossing serves the regulated pharmaceutical and OTC healthcare market. The 400gsm SBS C1S board provides rigid structural support with a bright white printing surface for full-color product graphics, while the aqueous coating adds moisture resistance for shelf stability. Compliant braille embossing on the face panel meets EU FMD (Falsified Medicines Directive) and FDA accessibility requirements. Optional tamper-evident tear strip and child-resistant tuck lock mechanisms complete the safety package. Manufactured on KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses with Bobst die-cutting for ±0.2mm dimensional accuracy.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color offset — batch-to-batch color consistency for pharmaceutical branding
  • UV Enhancement: Heidelberg 7+1 UV press — high-gloss spot UV on brand logo areas
  • Board: 400gsm SBS C1S (Solid Bleached Sulfate, clay-coated one side)
  • Coating: Aqueous coating (water-based) — moisture barrier, scuff-resistant, food-safe
  • Closure: Straight tuck end (STE) with optional CR (child-resistant) reverse tuck lock
  • Braille: Mechanical embossing — compliant raised dot patterns per EU FMD / Marburg Medium standard
  • Tamper Evidence: Perforated tear strip or adhesive seal label application area
  • Die-Cutting: Bobst flatbed die-cutter, ±0.2mm dimensional tolerance
  • Daily Output: 1.7M boxes
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody
