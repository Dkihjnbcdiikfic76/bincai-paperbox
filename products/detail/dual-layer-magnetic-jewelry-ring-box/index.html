Product Overview

The Bincai Dual-Layer Magnetic Jewelry Ring Box offers versatile presentation with two compartments — a top velvet ring slot for the hero piece and a lower drawer for accessories. The magnetic closure secures both layers simultaneously. Built on 2.0mm greyboard with flocked velvet lining, this box combines functionality with luxury aesthetics.

Features

  • Dual-Layer Design: Top ring cushion (velvet-wrapped foam insert) + pull-out bottom drawer for earrings, chain, or certificate
  • Magnetic Closure: Dual magnet pairs secure both layers with satisfying snap-lock
  • Ring Cushion: 40×15mm velvet-wrapped foam with slit; accommodates ring sizes 4-12
  • Bottom Drawer: 20mm deep with satin lining, perfect for accompanying items
  • Custom Sizes: Standard 80×80×55mm, customizable 60×60×40mm to 150×120×70mm
  • Foil & Finish: Gold/silver/rose gold hot stamping, matte or gloss lamination, spot UV

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: Heidelberg 7+1 UV and KBA 1050 4-color offset — Pantone spot color accuracy, ±0.15mm print registration
  • Board: 2.0mm rigid greyboard core wrapped in 157gsm art paper
  • Interior: 1.5mm black velvet flocking throughout; 40×15mm velvet-wrapped EVA ring cushion
  • Magnetic Closure: Dual N35 neodymium magnet pairs, concealed beneath wrap; tested 5,000+ open/close cycles
  • Drawer Mechanism: Precision die-cut slide rails with 20mm-deep satin-lined cavity
  • Daily Output: 220,000+ gift boxes + 1.7M color boxes
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management, FSC Chain-of-Custody
