Product Overview

The premium earring display box with built-in LED base and crystal-clear acrylic lid delivers a retail-counter showcase experience inside a gift box. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing (founded 2003, Foshan, 18,000 sqm, ISO 9001:2015 & FSC-C147399 certified), this box integrates a USB-C rechargeable LED module that illuminates earrings from below, with a transparent acrylic lid that lets customers see the product without opening. Daily output reaches 1.7 million boxes on KBA Rapida 105 and Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV presses.
