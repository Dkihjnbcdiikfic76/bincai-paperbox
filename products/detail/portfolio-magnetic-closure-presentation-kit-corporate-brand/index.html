Customization Options

  • Format: landscape (A4/Letter), portrait, or square folio
  • Cover material: art paper wrap, linen-textured paper, PU leatherette (20+ colors), genuine leather, or bookbinding cloth
  • Cover finishing: gold/silver foil stamping, blind debossing, UV spot varnish, or full-coverage digital print
  • Magnet: N35 (standard), N42 (medium), or N52 (ultra-strong) neodymium magnets; quantity from 2 to 8
  • Interior lining: velvet flocking, satin, suede-effect paper, or uncoated kraft
  • Insert tray: precision die-cut EVA foam, PU foam, molded pulp, or velvet-wrapped platform
  • Compartments: any layout — brochure slot, pen loop, USB cavity, card slots, product recess
  • Edge finishing: gold/silver gilt edging on board, painted edges, or natural kraft
  • Additional: integrated elastic band closure, ribbon pull-tab, or magnetic coin closure

Production Specifications

  • Press: Heidelberg XL 75 7+1 UV (max sheet 605×750mm) for premium surface finishing
  • Mounting: automatic rigid box production line — greyboard wrap with ±0.2mm alignment
  • Die-cutting: Bobst SP 102/106 automatic platen for foam insert cavities
  • Magnet insertion: automatic magnet placement with polarity verification
  • Quality: 100% magnet polarity and strength check, alignment inspection
  • Lead time: 10–15 days for tooling (foam mold) + 15–25 days production
