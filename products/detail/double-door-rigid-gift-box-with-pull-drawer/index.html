Product Overview

The double-door rigid gift box with pull-out drawer delivers the most theatrical unboxing experience in Bincai’s rigid box portfolio — two side-panel doors swing open on concealed piano hinges to reveal a smooth-gliding interior drawer. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003, 23 years), an ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified Diamond Member on Made-in-China.com. Each box requires precision hinge alignment (±0.3° door symmetry), CNC-routed drawer channels (±0.1mm clearance), and hand-applied electrostatic velvet flocking. Produced on KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset (15,000 sheets/hour) and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses across our 18,000 sqm dual-factory complex (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory, commissioned 2025) in Foshan, Guangdong. Daily output of 1.7 million boxes serves 200+ global brands across 60+ countries.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes the double-door design different from a standard rigid box? The double-door architecture creates a theatrical reveal — two side doors swing open to expose the drawer, rather than having a lift-off lid. This makes the unboxing experience more ceremonial and Instagram-worthy. It’s ideal for products where the packaging experience is part of the brand story.

Q: How durable are the concealed hinges? Our nickel-plated piano hinges are embedded between greyboard layers and rated for 20,000+ open/close cycles — equivalent to opening the box once daily for 54+ years. The alignment jig ensures perfect door symmetry (±0.3°).

Q: Can the drawer hold heavy items like wine bottles? Yes. The drawer rides on CNC-routed channels with 1600gsm greyboard construction. We reinforce the drawer base for spirits bottles (700-750ml, ~1.5kg loaded weight). For extra-heavy items, we add a second layer of greyboard to the drawer base.

Q: What interior options work best for jewelry versus watches? Jewelry sets use velvet-lined cavity trays with partitioned compartments (ring slot, necklace channel, earring posts). Watches use die-cut EVA foam with a precision watch pillow — the cushion holds the watch securely even when the drawer is tilted.

Q: Is the flocking colorfast and anti-tarnish? Our electrostatic velvet flocking uses colorfast nylon fibers tested to 500+ drawer cycles without shedding. For jewelry applications, we offer anti-tarnish treated flocking that absorbs atmospheric sulfur compounds to protect silver and gold-plated items.

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity for full custom tooling? MOQ is 500 units for standard configurations. Multi-process orders (foil stamping + embossing + custom flocking + insert) start at 1,000 units. We offer free 3D structural mockups within 24 hours and pre-production samples in 5-7 working days.
