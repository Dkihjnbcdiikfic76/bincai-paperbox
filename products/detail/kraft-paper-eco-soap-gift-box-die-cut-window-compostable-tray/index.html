Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — kraft board grammage and FSC documentation verification, PLA film optical clarity check, molded pulp tray integrity
  2. In-Process Printing — ink adhesion test on kraft substrate (tape test), color consistency against master proof every 1,000 sheets
  3. Die-Cutting Verification — window aperture dimensions ±0.3mm, board blank dimensional check, edge finish quality
  4. Window Film Application — PLA film bond inspection, haze measurement, bubble and wrinkle check
  5. Tray Insertion — product fit verification, cavity depth check, tray-to-box alignment
  6. Assembly & Closure — tuck tab integrity, auto-lock bottom deployment test, overall box squareness
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — AQL sampling per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4, full functional test, final visual inspection under D65 lighting

FAQ

Q: Is the entire box compostable?

A: Yes — the kraft board, PLA window film, molded pulp tray, soy-based ink, and water-based adhesive are all industrially compostable to EN 13432. The entire package can go into municipal compost streams.

Q: Does the kraft board look premium enough for gift packaging?

A: Absolutely. Our 300gsm recycled kraft board has a refined, natural texture that high-end artisan and natural brands actively choose for its authentic, eco-premium aesthetic. We can enhance it with gold or white foil stamping, embossing, or spot UV for an elevated look.

Q: Can I get FSC certification documentation for my brand’s marketing?

A: Yes — every shipment includes FSC Chain-of-Custody documentation. We can also provide FSC on-product labeling and a certificate of conformance for your brand’s sustainability claims.

Q: What if my soap bar is an unusual shape?

A: Our molded pulp tray tooling is fully custom. Send us a CAD file or physical sample of your soap bar and we’ll create a precision-molded cavity that holds it securely. Tooling lead time is 7–10 days.

Q: How does the PLA window hold up in humid bathroom environments?

A: PLA film is stable in normal retail and home environments. For products displayed in high-humidity conditions (spa steam rooms, tropical retail), we offer a PET window alternative — still recyclable but with enhanced humidity resistance.
