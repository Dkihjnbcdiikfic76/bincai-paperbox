Product Overview

A premium multi-strand necklace display box engineered for jewelry retailers and luxury brands. Features three independent necklace hooks suspended behind a soft velvet backboard, each capable of holding a full 18–24 inch strand without tangling. The magnetic flip-lid closure opens to a 120° angle for retail countertop presentation, while the gold foil hot-stamped logo and border elevate brand perception. Manufactured at Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. with precision die-cutting and manual velvet flocking for a flawless interior finish.

  • Board: 2.0mm greyboard core wrapped in 160gsm flocked velvet paper — no visible seams

Production Workflow

  1. Design & Prepress — CAD structural layout with 3-tier hook positioning, CTP plate output on Kodak Magnus VLF, gold foil die preparation
  2. Board Preparation — 2.0mm greyboard cut to blank, mitered corners ±0.3mm, hinge crease pre-scored
  3. Velvet Flocking — 160gsm flocked velvet paper PVA cold-glued to greyboard at 40°C, roller-pressed for zero-bubble adhesion, seam alignment checked under UV light
  4. Gold Foil Stamping — KURZ gold foil hot-stamped on Heidelberg 7+1 UV press, 110°C die temperature, 0.5s dwell, 2.5 MPa pressure, registered to artwork ±0.15mm
  5. Hook Installation — 3× stainless steel S-hooks with velvet sleeves, staggered at 45mm vertical intervals, threaded through backboard and secured with velvet washers
  6. Magnet Embedding — 2× N35 neodymium magnets (20×10×2mm) embedded into lid edge and base front, polarity-matched, pull-force tested at assembly station
  7. Ribbon Assembly — 15mm satin ribbon pull tab + optional ribbon tie closure, color-matched to velvet interior
  8. QC Gate — Hook load test, magnet pull-force gauge, flocking cross-hatch adhesion test, gold foil Sutherland rub, hinge cycle test (sampling), dimensional check, visual inspection
  9. Packing & Shipping — Individually tissue-wrapped, 25 units per corrugated master carton, fumigation-free export pallets, DHL/FedEx/UPS/Sea freight

FAQ

Q1: What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for custom jewelry boxes? A1: Our standard MOQ is 500 units for custom jewelry boxes. For stock designs without customization, MOQ can be as low as 200 units. Volume discounts apply at 2,000, 5,000, and 10,000+ unit tiers.

Q2: What materials are used for the velvet jewelry box with magnetic closure? A2: The box uses a 2.0mm greyboard core wrapped in 160gsm electrostatic velvet flocking paper, with 100% polyester velvet interior (1.5mm pile height). The closure features N52 neodymium magnets embedded flush into the lid and base, providing a secure snap-shut action with 2.5–5.0N pull force. All materials are FSC-certified and VOC-free.

Q3: What customization options are available (logo, foil, color)? Q4: What is the lead time for samples and mass production? A4: Pre-production samples: 5–7 business days (free of charge, shipping at buyer’s cost). Q5: How are jewelry boxes shipped, and can they ship flat? A5: Jewelry boxes ship via DHL, FedEx, UPS (air), or sea freight in fumigation-free export pallets.
