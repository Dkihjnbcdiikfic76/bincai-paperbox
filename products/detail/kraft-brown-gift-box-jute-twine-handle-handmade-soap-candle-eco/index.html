Quality Assurance

  1. Incoming Material Verification: Kraft paperboard grammage and caliper per TAPPI T410; FSC chain-of-custody certificate verification; jute twine tensile strength and diameter uniformity.
  2. Print Quality: Soy-ink density verification on kraft substrate (lower contrast than white paper — density adjusted for kraft’s natural tone); visual inspection for ink adhesion on uncoated surface.
  3. Die-Cutting Precision: ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance; score-line depth verification to prevent cracking on thicker kraft board (350gsm+).
  4. Handle Attachment: Jute twine pull-tested to 5kg minimum (3× the expected load); knot security and attachment point reinforcement verified.
  5. Adhesive Bond: Side-seam bond strength tested to minimum 6N/25mm on kraft substrate; 24-hour cure time before structural testing.
  6. Recyclability Verification: All components (board, ink, adhesive, twine) verified compatible with standard paper recycling streams per EN 13430.
  7. Finished Goods Audit: AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.0 (minor) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4; stacking compression test to verify structural integrity in transit.
