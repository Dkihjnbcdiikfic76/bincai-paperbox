With 23 years of manufacturing heritage (founded 2003), ISO 9001:2015 & FSC Chain-of-Custody certifications, and an 18,000 sqm factory (10,000+8,000 smart factory) featuring KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses, we produce watch packaging trusted by brands in over 40 countries. Applications

Watch display boxes with velvet pillows serve as the critical first touchpoint between a timepiece and its buyer — they must communicate quality, protect the product, and support retail display:

  • Luxury Watch Brands: Presentation boxes for mechanical, automatic, and quartz watches across entry-level luxury to haute horlogerie
  • Smart Watch Packaging: Premium unboxing for high-end smart watches and wearable tech — balances tech-forward design with traditional luxury cues
  • Watch Retailers & Boutiques: In-store display boxes that elevate shelf presence while protecting inventory from dust and handling
  • E-Commerce Watch Fulfillment: Rigid mailer-ready boxes with secure pillow retention for direct-to-consumer shipping
  • Corporate & Promotional Gifts: Branded watch boxes for employee recognition awards, client gifts, and milestone celebrations
  • Limited Edition & Collector’s Releases: Numbered editions, commemorative models, and collaboration drops requiring premium presentation
  • Watch Repair & Service: Return packaging for serviced timepieces with secure cushioning for transit protection

Customization Options

  • Box Structure: Classic book-style with magnetic flap, two-piece rigid (lid + base), drawer-slide box with pull ribbon, or hinged clamshell with stay-open arm; internal tray with die-cut pillow recess for secure positioning
  • Outer Wrapping: 128–200gsm art paper (matte, gloss, soft-touch velvet, linen-textured, pearlescent, carbon fiber pattern, leatherette-effect); genuine wood veneer or fabric wrap options for ultra-premium lines
  • Printing & Decoration: Full CMYK offset (KBA 1050) + up to 7 PMS spot colors (Heidelberg 7+1 UV); hot foil stamping in gold, silver, rose gold, or holographic; registered embossing/debossing for raised logo and patterns; spot UV (gloss-on-matte) for logo highlight; full-wrap or panel-specific design placement
  • Velvet Pillow: Custom die-cut pillow in polyester velvet (0.8–1.5mm pile), suede microfiber, or satin; single-pillow for standard watches or dual-pillow for bracelet + watch sets; pillow color matched to brand palette (black, navy, grey, burgundy, cream, champagne, chocolate, emerald, royal blue, blush pink, white, charcoal)
  • Interior Lining: Coordinated velvet, suede, or satin lining on lid interior and base cavity; optional foil-stamped interior logo for premium unboxing moment
  • Closure: Hidden neodymium magnet for clean exterior and satisfying snap-close; optional satin ribbon pull-tab for easy opening; clasp or buckle hardware for vintage-style boxes
  • Add-Ons: Outer sleeve/obi band with product specs, gold edge gilding on board edges, printed warranty card slot, USB/document compartment, LED interior illumination triggered by lid opening
  • Size: Fully custom — standard sizes 100×100×80mm to 200×150×100mm; larger sizes for multi-watch collectors’ boxes

Production Specifications

  • Rigid Box Line: Automated greyboard cutting, corner taping, wrapping, and assembly; precision 90° sharp corners (R3–R20 rounded available)
  • Greyboard Core: 1200–1800gsm density; single-ply for standard boxes, double-ply laminated for heavy watches (200g+)
  • Velvet Pillow Fabrication: CNC die-cut foam core (EVA or PU, 38–60 shore) wrapped in precision-cut velvet; seamless corners with no visible adhesive
  • KBA 1050 4-Color: CMYK offset at 15,000 sheets/hour, max sheet 1050×740mm for efficient layout nesting
  • Heidelberg 7+1 UV: 7 colors + inline UV coating in one pass for elaborate multi-color designs
  • Embossing: Copper or magnesium die with multi-level sculptured emboss; ±0.1mm print-to-emboss registration
  • Foil Stamping: Bobst SP 102 BMA Autoplaten with multi-zone temperature control (±2°C) and programmable foil advance for optimal material usage
  • Lead Time: 10–15 working days for new tooling; 7–10 days for repeat orders
  • Daily Capacity: 50,000–80,000 rigid watch boxes per day

Quality Assurance

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified — documented QC checkpoints at greyboard cutting, corner wrapping, velvet application, pillow insertion, and final inspection
  • FSC Chain-of-Custody — certified sustainable paper and board materials throughout the supply chain
  • Pillow Fit Test: 100% fit-check with actual watch sample; ±0.5mm cavity tolerance for secure retention without scratching
  • Velvet Adhesion: Accelerated aging — 72 hours at 57°C / 95% RH; zero peeling, bubbling, or delamination
  • Magnet Function: Pull-force test (≥3N hold); 5,000 open-close cycle durability test
  • Drop Test: ISTA 1A compliance — 76cm free-fall drop (6 faces, 3 edges, 1 corner) with watch-weight simulator
  • Print Registration: ±0.1mm foil-to-print alignment inspected under D50 5000K lighting
  • Odor Testing: Residual adhesive/solvent test per GB/T standards — safe for direct contact with watch straps and cases
