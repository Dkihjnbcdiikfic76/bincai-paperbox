Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — greyboard grammage and caliper verification, magnet grade certification (N52, gauss meter measurement), art paper GSM check ±2%, ribbon tensile strength test
  2. Prepress Calibration — spectrophotometer color profiling, foil die alignment verification, Pantone spot color match within ΔE ≤ 2.0
  3. In-Process Printing — inline Heidelberg Prinect color monitoring every 500 sheets, foil registration ±0.15mm, inline aqueous coating uniformity check
  4. Die-Cutting & Board Preparation — ±0.3mm dimensional verification, magnet pocket depth check, ribbon slot alignment, corner miter precision
  5. Rigid Box Assembly — automatic line quality gates: corner tape integrity, wrapping adhesion (peel test), magnet insertion polarity, ribbon anchor pull test (5 kgf)
  6. Insert Fabrication & Assembly — EVA foam dimensional check ±0.5mm, flocking density test, item fit test with production samples, insert-to-box alignment
  7. Finished Goods Audit & Pre-Shipment — AQL 2.5 Level II sampling, full functional open/close cycle test (10 cycles), foil adhesion rub test, D65 lighting visual inspection, packaging integrity check

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom magnetic closure stationery boxes? A: Standard MOQ is 500 units with full customization (printing, foil stamping, ribbon color, interior configuration). For new client partnerships, we offer trial runs of 200 units with a modest per-unit premium. Our stationery box production line is optimized for batch sizes from 500 to 20,000+ units.

Q: Can I get custom interior compartments for specific stationery items? A: Absolutely. We CNC die-cut EVA foam inserts with ±0.5mm precision to fit your exact products — whether it’s a fountain pen, ink bottle, business card holder, letter opener, and wax seal stamp all in one box. You send us the items (or precise dimensions), we design the insert layout, produce a sample for your approval, then manufacture. Tooling for custom EVA foam dies is US$80–150 per cavity, one-time cost.

Q: How strong are the N52 magnets? Will the box stay closed during shipping? A: N52 is the highest commercially available grade of neodymium magnet. Our dual-perimeter magnet configuration (strips along top and bottom edges) delivers ≥3.5N closure force — tested to remain sealed through ISTA 1A drop testing (10 drops from 760mm). For extra-heavy stationery sets (1.5kg+), we add center-positioned disc magnets that increase closure force to ≥6N.

Q: Can the ribbon pull tab be customized with a different material or color? A: Yes. Our standard offering is 15–25mm double-face satin ribbon in 20+ stock colors. For custom orders, we offer grosgrain (textured, more durable), velvet (luxurious, thicker), and organza (sheer, delicate) ribbons in any Pantone-matched color at 1,000+ units. We can also add foil-stamped text or logo on the ribbon itself — popular for brand-name ribbon pulls.

Q: What surface finishes are available for the exterior? A: We offer matte lamination (most popular — fingerprint-resistant, modern matte look), gloss lamination (high-shine, color-enhancing), soft-touch velvet coating (tactile, premium feel), linen-textured paper wrap (classic stationery aesthetic), pearlescent/metallic (shimmering, luxury), and spot UV (gloss pattern on matte background). All finishes are applied in-line on our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press for consistent quality.

Q: How long does production take from order to delivery? A: Standard lead time is 10–15 working days for first-time orders (including tooling setup and sample approval). Repeat orders ship in 7–10 working days. Rush service (5–7 working days) is available with a 15% surcharge during non-peak seasons. Sea freight to the US West Coast adds 18–22 days; air freight via DHL/FedEx delivers in 3–5 days.

Q: Can Bincai handle drop-shipping to multiple corporate gift recipients? A: Yes — we offer split-shipment logistics for corporate gifting programs. Provide a CSV of recipient addresses and quantities, and we palletize by destination with custom labeling and gift messaging inserts. We ship to 60+ countries with full export documentation (commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, FSC COC certificate). Gift messaging cards can be printed and inserted per-recipient at no additional charge.

Why Choose Bincai

A magnetic closure box is only as good as its weakest component — a misaligned magnet ruins the closure experience, a frayed ribbon looks cheap, and a delaminated corner betrays poor manufacturing. The Bincai Difference:
