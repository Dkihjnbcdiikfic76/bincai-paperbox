Straight tuck end folding carton for premium candle and home fragrance packaging — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. Designed for luxury candle brands, artisan chandlers, and home fragrance companies, this folding carton combines the cost-efficiency of a straight tuck end structure with premium finishing techniques — spot UV varnish and gold foil stamping — to create shelf presence that competes with luxury rigid boxes at a fraction of the cost. The carton ships flat and assembles in seconds, making it ideal for brands that self-fulfill. Produced across our 18,000 sqm factory with 1.7M daily output capacity.

Applications

  • Premium scented candle retail packaging (single and multi-wick)
  • Artisan and handcrafted candle brand presentation
  • Reed diffuser and home fragrance set packaging
  • Wax melt and tealight multi-pack cartons
  • Room spray and linen spray packaging
  • Candle gift set and holiday collection outer cartons
  • Natural and organic candle brand packaging (FSC certified, soy inks)

Customization Options

  • Board: SBS (solid bleached sulphate, premium white, 300-450gsm), CCNB (clay-coated newsback, economical, 350-450gsm), or FBB (folding boxboard, balanced, 350-400gsm)
  • Print: CMYK 4-color offset + spot colors (Pantone), line screen up to 200 LPI
  • Finishes: Spot UV (selective high-gloss), full gloss/matte lamination, soft-touch coating, aqueous coating (water-based), or grit/texture varnish
  • Foil: Gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic — applied via heated brass die, up to 3 foil colors in registration
  • Structure: Straight tuck end (standard), reverse tuck end, auto-bottom, or crash-lock bottom
  • Window: Die-cut window with PET or PLA film to showcase candle color/scent
  • Interior: Printed inside for unboxing messaging, candle care instructions, or scent notes
  • Special Features: Embossing/debossing, QR code print, perforated tear strip for tamper evidence, hang tab for retail peg hooks

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What board weight should I choose for my candle carton? A: For standard 200-300g candle jars (8-10 oz), SBS 350gsm provides excellent structural support. For larger 400-600g multi-wick candles, we recommend SBS 400gsm or CCNB 400gsm for added rigidity. Heavier candles (800g+) benefit from SBS 450gsm with crash-lock bottom for load-bearing. We’ll recommend based on your candle weight and retail display requirements.

Q: Can you combine spot UV and gold foil on the same carton? A: Yes, and this combination creates exceptional shelf impact. We typically apply gold foil first (Bobst hot stamp), then spot UV (screen-printed varnish). The two finishes can overlap or be registered adjacent within ±0.3mm. We recommend spot UV on brand name/logo with foil on decorative elements for maximum contrast, or vice versa.

Q: What are MOQ and lead times? A: Standard MOQ is 1,000-3,000 units depending on board and finish complexity. Foil stamping requires a brass die with MOQ 3,000+ to amortize die cost. Lead time is 12-18 working days for standard configurations, 18-22 working days with foil stamping. Rush orders available at 7-10 working days.

Q: Can you print the inside of the carton? A: Yes. Inside printing (1-2 colors) is available for candle care instructions, scent notes, brand story, or social media handles. Inside printing adds 2-3 working days and a plate charge. For eco-conscious brands, uncoated inside with soy ink printing reinforces the natural brand message.

Q: Is the carton recyclable? A: Yes. Standard configurations (SBS/CCNB board, aqueous coating, water-based glue) are 100% recyclable in standard paper streams. Lamination (matte/gloss BOPP film) and foil stamping affect recyclability — the carton is still recyclable but the plastic film and metallic foil are separated during repulping. For maximum recyclability, we recommend aqueous coating instead of lamination and blind deboss instead of foil.

Q: Can you accommodate odd-shaped candles (hexagonal, sculptural)? A: Yes. We design custom die-lines for non-rectangular candle shapes — hexagonal, octagonal, triangular, and custom sculptural profiles. Custom die-line development adds 3-5 working days to lead time and a one-time die charge.

Q: Does the carton ship flat or assembled? A: Standard configuration ships flat (glued, knocked down) for maximum shipping efficiency. Assembly takes seconds — simply square the carton and fold the bottom tuck. For brands using automated packaging lines, we offer crash-lock bottoms that pop open with a single push — faster assembly but slightly higher unit cost due to additional gluing points.
