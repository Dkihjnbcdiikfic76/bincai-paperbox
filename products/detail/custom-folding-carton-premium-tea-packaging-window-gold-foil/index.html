Customization Options

  • Board: 300gsm, 350gsm, or 400gsm C1S (coated one side) art board; C2S (coated two sides) for double-sided graphics; uncoated kraft or textured board for artisanal brands; FSC certified options
  • Printing: CMYK offset on KBA Rapida 105 — up to 4-color process at 175–200 LPI; PMS spot color matching with ΔE ≤ 2.0
  • Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic foil — Bobst press with ±0.15mm registration; combination foil + embossing for 3D metallic effects
  • Surface Finishes: Matte or gloss aqueous coating, soft-touch velvet lamination, spot UV on logo/pattern, or overall UV high-gloss
  • Window: Die-cut window with PLA film (compostable), PET film (crystal clear), or PP film (matte translucent); window shape fully customizable — rectangular, oval, teardrop, or brand-logo silhouette
  • Construction: Auto-lock bottom (fast assembly), tuck-end with dust flaps, or sleeve-and-tray for gift presentation
  • Size Range: 60×40×100mm (single tin) to 200×150×80mm (full gift set); custom dimensions available

Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — board grammage verification, FSC documentation review, foil roll quality check (no scratches or oxidation), window film clarity test
  2. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0 target, ink density and dot gain monitoring
  3. Foil Stamping — registration check on first sheet, adhesion tape test, visual inspection for skipped areas or edge blur
  4. Surface Finishing — aqueous coating coverage check, lamination bond test, spot UV alignment verification
  5. Die-Cutting — window aperture dimensions ±0.3mm, crease depth check for clean folding, board blank dimensional verification
  6. Window Film Application — bond strength test (≥ 2.5 N/25mm), bubble and wrinkle inspection, clarity re-check
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — AQL sampling per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4, auto-lock bottom deployment test, full dimensional check, final visual inspection under D65 lighting

FAQ

Q: Can the window film be compostable?

A: Yes — we offer PLA (polylactic acid) window film that is industrially compostable to EN 13432. For brands requiring crystal-clear clarity with recyclability, PET is also available. Both options meet food-contact safety standards.

Q: What’s the minimum order for gold foil stamping?

A: Gold foil stamping is included in the standard MOQ of 1,000 units. There’s no additional MOQ for foil — the one-time die cost covers your design, and foil color changes between production runs incur no extra charge.

Q: How well does the auto-lock bottom hold tea tins?

A: The auto-lock bottom is engineered to hold 3× the product weight — a 100g tea tin (~150g filled) is well within specification. For heavier products, we reinforce the bottom with an additional board layer or switch to crash-lock construction.

Q: Can I print on the inside of the carton?

A: Yes — using C2S (coated two sides) board, we can print brand messaging, brewing instructions, or ingredient information on the inside panels. This adds a premium unboxing detail at minimal additional cost.

Q: What’s the turnaround time for a new tea carton design?

A: Digital proofs within 24 hours, physical samples in 5–7 working days, and production delivery in 10–14 working days after sample approval. For rush orders, we can compress the timeline to 7–10 working days with an expedite fee.
