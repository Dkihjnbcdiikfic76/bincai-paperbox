FAQ

Q: How long do the LED batteries last? A: Two CR2032 coin cells power approximately 5,000 open/close cycles at 60-second auto-off per cycle. For retail display where the box is opened frequently, batteries last 3-6 months and are user-replaceable via a concealed compartment under the watch cushion.

Q: Can the LED color temperature be changed? A: Standard is warm white 3000K which flatters gold/steel watch cases. Cool white 6000K is available for contemporary/modern watch aesthetics. RGB programmable (with remote control) is available at >3,000 MOQ with 10-day lead time adder.

Q: Does the magnetic closure affect mechanical watches? A: No — the neodymium magnets are positioned in the lid-wall interface, producing a field that drops below 0.5 gauss at the watch cushion position (50mm+ from magnets). We can provide a gauss meter test report on request for brands concerned about magnetization.

Q: What’s your minimum for custom watch cushion cavities? A: 300 units for a single watch cavity with our standard cushion. Custom multi-watch cavities (2-6 watches) or branded cushion embroidery require 500+ MOQ. Cavity tooling typically costs 100-300 USD and samples arrive within 7-10 days.
