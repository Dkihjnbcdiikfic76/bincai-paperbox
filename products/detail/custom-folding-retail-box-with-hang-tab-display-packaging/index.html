Custom folding retail box with hang tab for pegboard and hook display — the workhorse of retail packaging, engineered for shelf efficiency and brand visibility. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. on KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses in our 18,000 sqm (10,000+8,000 smart factory) ISO 9001:2015 & FSC certified factory. With 23 years of experience (founded 2003) and a daily output of 1.7 million boxes, we supply hang-tab retail cartons to brands and retailers across 40+ countries.

Applications

Hang-tab folding cartons maximize retail space utilization while keeping your brand at eye level. Ideal for:

  • Consumer Electronics Accessories: Earphones, charging cables, phone cases, screen protectors, power banks, USB drives — pegboard display is the standard for small electronics retail
  • Cosmetics & Beauty: Lipsticks, mascaras, compacts, nail polish, makeup brushes, travel-size skincare — hang tabs suit checkout-aisle and end-cap impulse displays
  • Small Toys & Novelties: Action figures, collectibles, party favors, blind-box surprises, fidget toys, card games
  • Stationery & Office Supplies: Pens, markers, sticky notes, correction tape, desk accessories for supermarket and office supply store pegboards
  • Hardware & DIY: Screws, hooks, brackets, small tools, cable ties — blister-carded or window-box with hang tab
  • Health & Wellness: Supplement blister packs, essential oil rollers, first-aid kits, travel-sized toiletries
  • Fashion Accessories: Socks, belts, ties, hair accessories, sunglasses — hang-tab boxes for department store spinning racks
  • Seasonal & Holiday Items: Christmas ornaments, Halloween accessories, Easter basket fillers, Valentine’s Day gift sets

Customization Options

  • Hang Tab Style: Standard Euro slot (centered or off-center), reinforced hang tab with double-layer board, J-hook compatible, sombrero-style (wide top), or custom die-cut shape with integrated hanging hole
  • Box Structure: Reverse tuck end (RTE), straight tuck end (STE), auto-lock bottom for heavier products, seal-end with tear strip, or sleeve-and-tray for premium presentation
  • Board Material: 250–450gsm art paper (C1S/C2S), 300–500gsm greyboard, 250–400gsm kraft board (recycled or virgin), or white-lined chipboard (WLC) for economy options
  • Printing: Full CMYK offset on KBA 1050 (15,000 sheets/hour); up to 7 PMS spot colors + UV coating on Heidelberg 7+1 in one pass; photo-realistic stochastic (FM) screening available
  • Surface Finishes: Matte lamination (most popular for retail — reduces glare under store lighting), gloss lamination, soft-touch velvet lamination for premium tactile feel, spot UV (gloss-on-matte) for logo highlight, aqueous varnish, glitter UV
  • Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, rose gold, holographic on logo or decorative elements — Bobst SP 102 BMA with ±0.1mm registration
  • Embossing/Debossing: Blind emboss, registered emboss, multi-level sculptured emboss for dimensional logo impact
  • Window Options: Die-cut window with clear PVC/PET film (0.2–0.5mm), frosted PP window, or fully open window (no film) for touch-before-buy products
  • Add-Ons: Perforated tear strip for easy opening, euro-slot reinforcement patch, QR code printing, sequential numbering, barcode/UPC printing, security seal tape
  • Size: Fully custom — from small 50×50×20mm to 300×200×100mm; multi-cavity trays for combo packs

Production Specifications

  • Press #1: KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset — 15,000 sheets/hour, max sheet 1050×740mm, automated plate changing for fast job turnaround
  • Press #2: Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 with UV — 7 colors + inline coating in one pass, instant UV curing for specialty substrates and metallic papers
  • Die-Cutting: Bobst SP 102-SE Autoplaten with automatic stripping station — precision die-cutting for hang tabs, windows, and complex structural shapes; ±0.1mm registration
  • Folder-Gluer: Bobst Ambition 106 A2 — automatic crash-lock and 4&6-corner capability, barcode verification system for mixed-SKU runs
  • Hang Tab Reinforcement: Automatic patch-applicator for reinforced hang tabs on heavy products (patented reinforcement film or double-layer board lamination)
  • Prepress: CTP (computer-to-plate) output; GMG color management calibrated to ISO 12647-2 for consistent brand colors across reorders
  • Lead Time: 7–10 working days for standard hang-tab cartons; 5–7 days for repeat orders
  • Daily Capacity: 800,000 folding cartons per day across production lines

Quality Assurance

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified — documented quality checkpoints at prepress, printing, die-cutting, gluing, and final inspection
  • FSC Chain-of-Custody certified — FSC Mix and FSC Recycled board options available for sustainability-conscious brands
  • Hang Tab Strength: Pull-force test — reinforced hang tabs withstand ≥5kg sustained load without tearing; standard tabs rated for product weights up to 500g
  • Color Consistency: X-Rite spectrophotometer measurement per ISO 12647-2; ΔE ≤ 2.0 throughout the production run

  • Window Clarity: Optical clarity measurement on PVC/PET windows; scratch-resistance test for window films
