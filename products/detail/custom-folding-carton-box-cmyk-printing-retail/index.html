Product Overview

Our custom folding cartons deliver premium shelf presence with vibrant CMYK offset printing on high-quality paperboard. Designed for retail, e-commerce, and brand packaging, these flat-shipped cartons fold into sturdy, precision-fit boxes. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) in our 18,000 sqm integrated factory (10,000+8,000 smart factory, commissioned 2025) in Foshan, Guangdong — operating KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses with closed-loop Prinect color management. ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified, daily output 1.7 million boxes serving 200+ global clients.

Design Features

  • Auto-Bottom or Tuck-End: Choose self-erecting auto-bottom for fast assembly (ideal for e-commerce fulfillment centers) or classic tuck-end for secure retail closure
  • Full CMYK + Spot: 4-color process plus up to 2 Pantone spot colors on Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press at 15,000 sheets/hour
  • Soft-Touch Finish: Velvet matte lamination option for luxury tactile experience — fingerprint-resistant and scuff-proof
  • Die-Cut Precision: Steel-rule die-cutting on Heidelberg cylinder die-cutter with automatic stripping, ±0.3mm tolerance for clean edges and consistent folds
  • Flat Shipping: Cartons ship flat to minimize freight costs (70% volume reduction vs assembled boxes), assemble in seconds
  • Inline Quality: Heidelberg Prinect Inpress spectrophotometry — real-time color measurement, ΔE ≤ 2.0 across entire production run
