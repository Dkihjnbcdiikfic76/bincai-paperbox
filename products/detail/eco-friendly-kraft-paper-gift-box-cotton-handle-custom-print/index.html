Product Overview

Eco-friendly kraft paper gift box with natural cotton rope handle and custom print — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). Designed for brands committed to plastic-free, sustainable packaging without compromising on structural integrity or print quality. Built on KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses with soy-based inks across an 18,000 sqm integrated campus (10,000+8,000 smart factory, commissioned 2025) in Foshan, Guangdong. Daily output 1.7 million boxes + 220,000+ gift boxes. ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified. Ideal for organic cosmetics, artisan food, sustainable apparel, and eco-conscious lifestyle brands seeking zero-plastic, fully recyclable gift packaging.

Features

  • 100% recyclable and biodegradable unbleached kraft paperboard construction (250–450gsm)
  • Natural cotton rope handle for easy carrying and rustic artisan aesthetic — available in natural white, kraft brown, and custom-dyed colors
  • FSC Chain-of-Custody certified materials with soy-based ink printing (ASTM D6866 bio-content ≥60%)
  • Custom CMYK or single-color printing on natural kraft surface — Heidelberg Prinect color management, ΔE ≤ 2.0
  • Sturdy double-wall bottom for heavier gift items up to 3kg
  • Water-based PVA adhesives — ASTM D6400 biodegradable, zero VOC emissions

  • ISO 9001:2015 & FSC certified quality management throughout production
