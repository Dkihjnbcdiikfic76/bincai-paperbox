FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for hexagonal rigid gift boxes?

A: Standard MOQ is 500 pcs for existing hexagonal die shapes. For custom window shapes (non-standard cutout, logo-shaped window), MOQ is 1000 pcs due to custom die tooling. Free pre-production sample available — 7–10 working day turnaround.

Q: Can the window shape be customized?

A: Absolutely. Any vector shape can be die-cut into the lid panel — geometric patterns, brand logo silhouettes, product-specific cutouts. Custom window dies require 3–5 working days for tooling and are amortized over production quantities. Send us your AI/EPS/PDF artwork.

Q: What materials can the window be made from?

A: Standard is 0.3–0.5mm clear PET with ≥90% light transmission. We also offer anti-fog PET for refrigerated products, UV-blocking PET for light-sensitive products, and frosted PET for diffused visibility. All PET materials are food-safe and recyclable.

Q: How does the hexagonal shape affect shipping and storage?

A: Hexagonal boxes ship flat (lid + base nested separately) to maximize container utilization. Assembly is simple — the magnetic closure snaps into position. For pre-assembled shipping, we use custom hexagonal dividers. Our logistics team optimizes carton layouts to minimize volumetric waste.
