The die-cut corrugated display mailer with window is a dual-purpose packaging solution that transitions seamlessly from protective shipping mailer to point-of-sale display unit. Designed for brands shipping directly to retail shelves — subscription boxes, beauty products, snack assortments, and consumer electronics — this box eliminates the need for separate shipping and display packaging, reducing material waste, handling labor, and total packaging cost. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003, 23 years) at our 18,000 sqm factory in Foshan, China, with ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certifications.

The integrated die-cut window allows consumers to see the product without opening the box, while precision score lines create a clean tear-away top section that reveals the product for retail display. B-flute construction provides robust crush protection during transit, while the smooth kraft or white-top liner accepts high-quality flexographic or offset lithographic printing for bold brand graphics.

Customization Options

  • Board Construction: B-flute (3mm, 125# burst) for heavy items or E-flute (1.5mm, 80# burst) for lighter contents. Double-wall BC-flute available for fragile items over 5kg.
  • Liner Material: Kraft (natural brown), white-top kraft for superior print quality, or coated white liner for offset lithographic printing.
  • Printing: Flexographic (up to 4 colors, cost-effective for runs of 5,000+) or offset lithographic (KBA 1050, full-color CMYK + spot colors, up to 150-line screen).
  • Die-Cut Features: Product display window (with optional 0.3mm PET or rPET film); tear-away top panel with perforated score line; retail hang-tab for pegboard display; carry handle cutouts for consumer convenience.
  • Surface Finishes: Aqueous coating for scuff resistance; matte or gloss varnish; spot coating to highlight brand elements; anti-slip coating for stackability.
  • Closure: Tuck-top with slit-lock, crash-lock auto-bottom for rapid assembly, or partial glue flap for tamper evidence.
  • Interior: Corrugated dividers or partitions for multi-SKU sets; die-cut inserts to hold products in position behind the window.
  • Sizing: From compact 100×100×50mm single-product mailers to 400×300×150mm multi-item display shippers.

Quality Assurance

  1. Raw Material Verification: Flute height and liner grammage per FEFCO standards; edge crush test (ECT) minimum 5.0 kN/m for B-flute; burst strength (Mullen) minimum 1,100 kPa for single-wall.
  2. Print Registration: Flexographic registration verified on first-off and every 1,000 sheets thereafter; offset litho on KBA 1050 with Heidelberg Prinect inline monitoring.
  3. Die-Cutting Precision: ±0.5mm tolerance verified with go/no-go gauge; score-line depth check to prevent cracking during fold-to-display transformation.
  4. Window Bond QC: PET-to-liner adhesive bond pull-tested to 2kg minimum; UV-cured adhesive uniformity under black light inspection.
  5. Perforation & Tear Strip: Tear force tested to 8–15N — clean separation without premature failure during transit.
  6. Assembly Verification: 100% first-article check of auto-bottom erection and flat-to-display transformation sequence.
  7. Finished Goods Audit: AQL 2.5 (major defects), AQL 1.5 (critical defects) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4; ISTA 3A drop test simulation for e-commerce shipping.
