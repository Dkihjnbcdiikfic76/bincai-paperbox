Product Overview

The Bincai Double-Wall Corrugated Champagne Shipper solves the most demanding challenge in wine logistics: delivering six glass bottles intact across continents. Built from BC-flute double-wall corrugated with a precision die-cut EPE foam cradle that isolates each bottle with 25mm foam walls between compartments. ISTA 3A certified for international courier transit (DHL, FedEx, UPS), this shipper has been drop-tested at 1.2m on all faces and edges with full bottle weight. The self-locking bottom assembles in seconds without tape, and the tear-strip opening provides a premium unboxing experience for direct-to-consumer champagne sales.
