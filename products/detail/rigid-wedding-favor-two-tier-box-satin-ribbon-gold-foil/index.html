Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — greyboard grammage verification, art paper GSM check ±2%, ribbon tensile strength test
  2. Prepress Calibration — spectrophotometer color profiling, foil die alignment verification, Pantone spot color match
  3. In-Process Printing — inline Heidelberg Prinect color monitoring every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0 target per ISO 12647-2
  4. Die-Cutting & Tray Fabrication — ±0.3mm dimensional verification, tray slot precision check, ribbon slot alignment
  5. Rigid Box Assembly — corner miter inspection (±0.3mm), ribbon anchor pull test (8 kgf minimum), lid-base fit verification
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 Level II sampling, foil adhesion rub test, full functional open/close cycle test
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — random sample pull at 10% of shipment, full D65 lighting visual inspection, tray function test, packaging integrity check

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom wedding favor boxes? A: Standard MOQ is 500 units for custom printing and foil stamping. For new client partnerships, we offer trial runs of 200 units with a slight per-unit premium. Our wedding product line is optimized for batch sizes from 500 to 10,000+ units.

Q: Can I order samples with our wedding monogram before production? A: Absolutely. We produce 2–3 pre-production samples with your exact logo/foil color/ribbon choice within 5–7 business days. Samples are free — you only cover express shipping (DHL/FedEx, typically US$30–50 depending on destination). This lets you approve every detail before committing to full production.

Q: How long does production take for a wedding order? A: Standard production lead time is 7–12 working days after sample approval for first-time orders. Repeat orders typically ship in 5–7 working days. Rush service (3–5 working days) is available during non-peak seasons with a 15% surcharge. We recommend ordering at least 4 weeks before your event date.

Q: What ribbon colors and foil options are available? A: We stock double-face satin ribbon in 40+ colors with custom Pantone-matched dyeing available at 1,000+ units. Foil options include gold, rose gold, silver, copper, champagne, and holographic — all applied on our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press with ±0.2mm registration precision.

Q: Are the boxes food-safe for chocolates and confectionery? A: Yes. Our interior tray linings are food-safe with soy-based inks and water-based adhesives certified for indirect food contact (FDA 21 CFR, EU 1935/2004). For direct food contact applications, we offer food-grade PET tray inserts as an alternative to flocked paper lining.

Q: Can you ship directly to multiple wedding venues or guest addresses? A: Yes — we offer drop-shipping and split shipments. Provide a CSV of addresses and quantities, and we’ll palletize by destination with custom labeling. We ship to 60+ countries with full export documentation (commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, FSC COC certificate).

Q: What makes Bincai different from other wedding box manufacturers? A: Three things: (1) We own the entire production line — printing (KBA 1050 + Heidelberg 7+1 UV), foil stamping (Bobst), rigid box assembly, and QC — under one 18,000 sqm roof, meaning no subcontractor delays. (2) 23 years of expertise means we understand wedding-tight deadlines. (3) ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certification gives wedding planners documented sustainability credentials for eco-conscious couples.
