Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) crafts this premium rigid cigar collector box for luxury tobacco brands, duty-free retailers, and limited-edition releases. Built in our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certification, each box features genuine Spanish cedar (Cedrela odorata) wood veneer interior lining — the traditional material of choice for cigar storage due to its natural moisture-regulating properties and aromatic oils that complement cigar aging.

The humidor-style construction pairs a 3.0mm greyboard rigid body with a bookcloth or leatherette exterior wrap, solid brass hinges rated for 20,000+ open/close cycles, and recessed N52 neodymium magnets for a secure yet refined closure. The Spanish cedar interior is precision-cut to hold cigars in individual grooves, with an optional Boveda humidity pack pocket for long-term storage. Hot foil stamping on the lid — with ±0.15mm registration — delivers a premium branded presentation worthy of the finest cigars.

Quality Assurance

  1. Material Inspection — Cedar veneer moisture content verification (10-12% target), grain consistency check, FSC chain-of-custody documentation
  2. Board Construction — 3.0mm greyboard lamination bond test, dimensional tolerance ±0.3mm
  3. Cedar Interior — Groove cut precision (±0.5mm), sanding finish inspection (320 grit, no splinters), aromatic oil retention verification
  4. Hardware Installation — Hinge alignment (±0.5mm), cycle test on sample batch (20,000 open/close), magnet pull force verification
  5. Foil/Print QC — ±0.15mm registration, tape adhesion test, D65 light booth color check
  6. Assembly QC — Lid alignment, closure force, cedar-to-board glue bond, hygrometer calibration (if equipped)
  7. Pre-Shipment — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.0 (minor) per ISO 2859-1 Level II

Why Bincai?

The Spanish cedar interior is what separates a true cigar collector box from an ordinary rigid box. Bincai sources FSC-certified Cedrela odorata veneer directly from managed forests, kiln-dries it to the optimal 10-12% moisture content in-house, and precision-cuts cigar grooves with CNC routing. This integrated process — from cedar conditioning to final assembly — happens entirely within our 18,000 sqm facility, ensuring no moisture variance or contamination from multi-vendor handling. The result is a box that not only presents cigars beautifully but actively contributes to their preservation.
