Production Workflow

  1. LED Module Procurement — CE-certified warm white LED ribbon strips (2700K, 3528 SMD LEDs, 60 LEDs/m); incoming QC: function test, CCT verification, solder joint inspection per IPC-A-610 Class 2
  2. Greyboard Preparation — 1200–1600gsm greyboard cut to blank; PVA cold-glue lamination of 157gsm C1S art paper; mitered corner wrap ±0.3mm; 24-hour curing under weighted stack
  3. Offset Printing — CMYK on KBA 1050 4-color press; aqueous coating base; optional soft-touch coating or matte lamination on automatic laminator
  4. Hot Foil & Embellishment — Gold/silver/rose gold foil stamping on Heidelberg 7+1 UV; embossing/debossing at 0.6–0.8mm depth; spot UV registration within ±0.15mm
  5. Velvet Flocking — Electrostatic flocking machine; 6-denier × 0.5mm nylon fiber; adhesive: water-based polyurethane; curing at 60°C × 30 min; cavity die-cut to match product dimensions
  6. LED Module Installation — Pre-tested LED ribbon mounted in lid interior channel; CR2032 battery holder or AAA pack installed in base; magnetic reed switch embedded in lid edge; wiring concealed behind velvet lining
  7. Magnet Embedding — 2× N35 neodymium magnets (Ø12mm × 2mm) embedded in front flap and mating base, polarity-matched; satin ribbon pull tab attached
  8. QC Gate — 100% LED function test, magnet pull-force gauge, velvet adhesion cross-hatch, foil registration optical comparator, dimensional tolerance ±1.0mm, ISTA 1A drop test sampling
  9. Packing & Shipping — Individually poly-bagged with silica gel; corrugated master cartons with partition dividers (24–48 units per outer); fumigation-free palletization; DHL/FedEx/sea freight

FAQ

Q: How long do the LED batteries last? A: A single CR2032 coin battery provides approximately 200 hours of continuous illumination, or roughly 3,600 activation cycles (assuming 20-second average open duration). For longer life or higher brightness, we offer AAA battery packs (500+ hours) and USB-C rechargeable modules (unlimited cycles, 4-hour runtime per charge).

Q: Is the LED module safe for jewelry — does it generate heat? A: Our 3528 SMD LEDs operate at <0.1W each (total module <1W) and generate negligible heat — the surface temperature never exceeds 3°C above ambient. CE certified for safety. The LED ribbon is mounted behind the velvet lining with no direct contact to jewelry pieces, and all wiring is concealed and insulated.

Q: Can I customize the LED color or activation method? A: Absolutely. We offer warm white (2700K), cool white (4000K), and RGB multi-color LED modules. Activation methods include magnetic reed switch (lid-open → light-on), manual slide switch, touch sensor, or motion sensor (activates when box is picked up). All options are pre-tested and CE certified.

Q: Do you offer rechargeable options? A: Yes. Our USB-C rechargeable module includes a 500mAh Li-Po battery with charging indicator LED, providing 4+ hours of continuous illumination per charge. Ships with USB-C cable. This is our most popular option for premium brands and eliminates the customer need to replace batteries.
