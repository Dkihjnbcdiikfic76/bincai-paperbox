FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for velvet bangle display boxes?

A: Standard MOQ is 500 pcs. For custom velvet color matching, custom satin pillow shapes, or integrated LED modules, MOQ is 1000 pcs. Free pre-production sample with full customization available — 7–10 working day turnaround, freight collect.

Q: Can the satin pillow be customized for different bangle shapes?

A: Yes. We can create satin pillows with custom-stitched channels for any bangle shape — round hinged, oval, square cuff, open-cuff with elastic retention. Send us your bangle dimensions and we will engineer a pillow that holds the piece securely during shipping and displays it beautifully at retail. Minimum 1000 pcs for custom pillow tooling.

Q: Is the velvet exterior durable for retail display and shipping?

A: Yes. Our velvet-flocked paper is tested to 50,000+ Martindale rubs (ISO 12947-2) and maintains colorfastness rating 4–5 (ISO 105-B02). The flocking process uses electrostatic application for uniform fiber density. For additional durability in high-traffic retail environments, we offer a clear protective overlaminate.

Q: What is your lead time for production orders?

A: Standard lead time is 12–18 working days from artwork approval. Repeat orders with no design changes typically ship in 7–10 working days. Rush service (7–10 days) available with 20% surcharge. We ship via sea freight (FOB Shenzhen/Yantian) or air express (DHL/UPS/FedEx) based on your preference.
