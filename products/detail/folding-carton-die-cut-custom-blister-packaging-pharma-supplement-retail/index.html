Regulatory Compliance Landscape

Whether you’re targeting the US market (FDA 21 CFR Part 211 — cGMP for Finished Pharmaceuticals), the European Union (EU Falsified Medicines Directive 2011/62/EU with unique identifier serialization), or emerging markets with their own pharmacopoeia requirements, Bincai’s pharma folding cartons are designed for compliance:

  • FDA 21 CFR Part 211: GMP-compliant production environment with documented batch records, material traceability, and validated processes
  • EU FMD 2011/62/EU: Carton design accommodates 2D Data Matrix barcode with unique identifier (UI), serial number, batch, expiry date, and national reimbursement number
  • USP <671>: Moisture vapor transmission rate (MVTR) testing for blister-packaged products requiring moisture protection
  • USP <661>: Plastic packaging components testing — applicable to PVC/PET blister trays
  • ISO 15378:2017: Primary packaging materials for medicinal products (Bincai is certified, on request)

Production Workflow — Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Artwork & Regulatory Review: Pharmaceutical labeling compliance check (drug facts, NDC, lot/expiry placement, braille content verification); serialization data matrix verification
  2. Raw Material Qualification: SBS board caliper, brightness, and stiffness certification; PVC/PET blister sheet thickness and MVTR certification; FSC chain-of-custody verification; incoming adhesive lot certification
  3. Press Make-Ready: KBA 1050 registration calibration; color density pull on first 100 sheets; variable data printing verification (barcode readability to ISO/IEC 15415 Grade C minimum)
  4. Die-Cutting QC: First-article dimensional check (carton L×W×H ±0.3mm, blister cavity positions ±0.2mm); blister tray snap-fit test (≥3.0N pull-out resistance); 100% visual on first 50 units
  5. Braille & Embossing: Braille dot height measurement (0.5–0.6mm); braille content verification against approved artwork; registered emboss/deboss alignment
  6. Assembly & Gluing: Inline camera inspection for glue bead placement; random pull every 200 units for peel-strength testing; tamper-evident tear-strip functionality test; child-resistant closure certification sampling
  7. Pre-Shipment Audit: AQL 1.0 Level II sampling (pharmaceutical grade); moisture content verification; final barcode readability scan; batch record review and sign-off; Certificate of Analysis (CoA) issued
