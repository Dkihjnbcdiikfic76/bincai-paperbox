Product Overview

The telescopic rigid gift box with gold foil monogram window is designed for luxury apparel brands — from premium shirts and silk scarves to cashmere sweaters and designer accessories. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing (founded 2003, Foshan, 18,000 sqm, ISO 9001:2015 & FSC-C147399 certified), this two-piece telescopic box features a soft-touch laminated exterior with a precision die-cut window that frames the brand monogram printed on the inner lid. The telescopic lid slides smoothly over the base, creating a reveal experience that communicates luxury. Daily output reaches 1.7 million boxes on KBA Rapida 105 and Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV presses with Bobst SP 102 BMA/E die-cutters, Bobst Visioncut 106 LER, and Bobst Expertfold 110 A2.

Production Specifications

  • Press: Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV for exterior wrap with soft-touch coating; KBA Rapida 105 for volume
  • Die-Cutting: Bobst SP 102 BMA/E for window aperture; Bobst Visioncut 106 LER for intricate shapes (±0.1mm)
  • Board: 2.5mm greyboard for body, 3.0mm for larger sizes
  • Wrap: 157gsm art paper with soft-touch aqueous or UV coating
  • Gold Foil: Hot foil stamping, temperature-controlled for consistent transfer
  • Window Bonding: Ultrasonic or solvent-free adhesive PET film lamination
  • Folding/Gluing: Bobst Expertfold 110 A2 with telescopic precision alignment
  • Daily Output: 1.7M color boxes + 220,000+ rigid boxes
