Product Overview

Our custom luxury paper shopping bag elevates the retail unboxing experience with premium materials and refined finishes. Constructed from 210gsm art paper with a velvety matte lamination, the bag features reinforced twisted rope handles for comfortable carrying and a square-bottom base insert that supports up to 8kg. The spot UV treatment highlights your brand logo with a striking gloss-on-matte contrast that catches light at every angle. Manufactured on our Heidelberg 7+1 UV offset press at Bincai’s 18,000 sqm integrated factory, each bag passes through 7 quality control gates — from paper grammage verification (ISO 536) to final dimensional inspection. With 23 years of packaging expertise and daily output of 1.7 million units, we deliver retail-quality paper bags that reflect your brand’s premium positioning. Ideal for fashion boutiques, luxury cosmetics stores, jewelry retailers, and corporate gifting programs.

Design Features

  • Matte Lamination Finish: Full-surface matte lamination provides a silky-smooth tactile experience while protecting the printed surface from scuffs, moisture, and fingerprints — tested to 500+ Sutherland rub cycles
  • Spot UV Branding: Selective high-gloss UV coating over your logo or design elements creates a dramatic gloss-on-matte contrast with ±0.2mm registration accuracy
  • Reinforced Twisted Rope Handles: 8mm twisted paper/cotton rope handles (6 color options) anchored through reinforced eyelets, tensile tested to ≥20kg combined pull strength
  • Square-Bottom Construction: Flat reinforced base with 2mm greyboard stiffener insert ensures the bag stands upright on retail counters without sagging, rated to 8kg load capacity
  • Full CMYK + Spot Colors: Offset printing on KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses with Pantone spot color matching (ΔE ≤ 2.0)
  • Custom Size Range: 6 standard sizes from small (200×100×250mm) to extra-large (450×180×400mm), with fully custom dimensions available
  • Optional Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, rose gold, or holographic foil stamping on logos and decorative elements for ultimate luxury appeal

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity? A: MOQ is 500 pieces per size/design. For trial orders we can accommodate 300 pieces with a small surcharge.

Q: Can you match our exact brand color? A: Yes, we use Pantone spot color matching with closed-loop spectrophotometer control maintaining ΔE ≤ 2.0 for consistent brand reproduction across batches.

Q: Are the paper bags recyclable? A: Yes! Our paper bags are 100% recyclable in standard paper waste streams. The rope handles should be removed before recycling (they are also paper-based and biodegradable).

Q: What sizes are available? A: We offer 6 standard sizes from 200×100×250mm to 450×180×400mm. Custom dimensions are available at no additional tooling cost — simply provide your desired measurements.

Q: How long does production take? A: Standard lead time is 7–15 days after sample approval. Expedited service (5–7 days) is available for urgent orders.

Q: What file formats do you accept for artwork? A: We accept AI (Adobe Illustrator), PDF, CDR (CorelDRAW), and EPS files. Please provide 3mm bleed and convert all fonts to outlines.
