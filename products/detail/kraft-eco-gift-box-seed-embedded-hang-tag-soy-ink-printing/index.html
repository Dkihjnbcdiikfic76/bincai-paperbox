Product Overview

Packaging that outlives its purpose — quite literally. The Kraft Paper Eco Gift Box with Seed-Embedded Hang Tag is designed for brands that understand sustainability is not a checkbox but a brand statement. The box is made from 100% recycled kraft board, printed with soy-based inks, assembled with water-based adhesives, and closed with natural cotton twine. But the defining feature is the hang tag: embedded with a mix of wildflower seeds, it transforms from packaging into a garden when planted.

Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — 23 years, ISO 9001:2015 & FSC® certified, 18,000 sqm facility, 1.7M boxes/day — this box embodies the circular economy principle: nothing is waste, everything is a resource.

The Plantable Hang Tag

The seed-embedded tag is made from post-consumer recycled paper pulp mixed with a curated blend of non-invasive wildflower seeds. To plant: soak the tag in water for 24 hours, place in soil, water regularly, and within 7–14 days the first sprouts appear. The paper decomposes naturally, leaving behind a small wildflower patch. Each tag carries the brand’s logo and a simple instruction: “Plant me.”

Quality Control

  1. Board Grade: Recycled content certification verified per batch (FSC Recycled)
  2. Soy Ink Verification: Batch certification from ink supplier (EN 13432 compliance)
  3. Seed Germination Test: 100-tag sample per batch — 80% minimum germination within 14 days
  4. Adhesive Bond: Peel test on all glued joints
  5. Print Registration: Within ±1mm for flexo, ±0.3mm for offset
  6. Twine Integrity: Tensile test — 5kg minimum break strength
  7. Final AQL 1.5: Visual inspection — kraft consistency, print clarity, tag attachment
