Product Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing’s personalized design gift box combines premium rigid construction with fully customized inner tray configurations. Built in our 18,000 sqm integrated factory in Foshan, each box features a precision-fit insert tray that holds products securely while presenting them with elegance. From cosmetics and jewelry to electronics and gourmet confectionery, the custom tray insert transforms a beautiful box into a complete packaging solution — all produced on German-engineered KBA 1050 and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses with 23 years of expertise.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color + Heidelberg 7+1 UV offset — ΔE ≤ 2.0 color consistency across runs up to 100,000 units
  • Board: 1200–1600gsm greyboard core with 157–250gsm art paper wrap

  • Surface Finishes: Matte/gloss lamination, soft-touch velvet matte, spot UV, hot foil stamping (gold/silver/rose gold/copper/holographic)

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for personalized rigid gift boxes with custom tray inserts? A: Our standard MOQ is 500 units for boxes using existing tray die-cut tooling. For fully custom die-cut inserts with new cavity layouts, the MOQ starts at 1,000 units due to tooling investment. Free pre-production samples are provided within 5–7 working days before bulk production begins.

Q: Which inner tray materials are available and how do I choose? A: We offer greyboard with velvet flocking (most popular for cosmetics and jewelry), satin lining, EVA foam in 30–60 Shore A hardness (ideal for electronics and fragile items), PU leather wraps, and food-safe cardboard partitions with grease-resistant coating. Your account manager will recommend the best option based on product weight, fragility, and target market positioning.

Q: Can I combine multiple surface finishes on the same gift box? A: Absolutely. Many of our clients combine matte soft-touch lamination on the exterior with spot UV highlights on the logo, hot foil stamping on the brand name, and blind embossing on a pattern element — all on the same box. Our Bobst hot foil press achieves ±0.1 mm registration, allowing precise multi-finish layering without misalignment.

Q: How do you ensure color consistency across large production runs? A: We use inline spectrophotometry on both our KBA Rapida 105 and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 presses, maintaining ΔE ≤ 2.0 across the entire run. Density checks occur every 500 sheets, and each job starts with an Epson contract proof approved against your Pantone targets at ΔE ≤ 1.5. ISO 12647-2 is our governing color standard.

Q: What certifications does Bincai hold for export-quality rigid gift boxes? A: Bincai holds ISO 9001:2015 for quality management, FSC Chain-of-Custody for responsibly sourced paper and board, and Sedex SMETA for ethical manufacturing. All materials can be tested to SGS or Bureau Veritas standards for market-specific compliance (FDA, EU Toy Safety, REACH, California Prop 65) upon request.
