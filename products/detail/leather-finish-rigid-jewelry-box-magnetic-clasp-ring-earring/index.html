Leather-finish rigid jewelry box with magnetic clasp for rings and earrings — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). Designed for fine jewelry brands, artisan jewelers, and luxury accessories requiring an elegant presentation that matches the quality of the piece inside. The exterior features premium PU leatherette in a range of textures — smooth, pebbled, or crocodile-embossed — with a discreet magnetic clasp closure for secure, satisfying opening. The interior is fully lined with plush velvet flocking and precision die-cut slots for rings, earrings, or bracelet presentation. Bobst SP 102 BMA foil stamping provides crisp metallic brand logos. Constructed with 2.5mm greyboard walls and wrapped corners for long-lasting durability. Our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility delivers 220,000+ rigid boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity for custom jewelry boxes? A: MOQ is 500 units for standard configurations (magnetic clasp, one die-cut slot layout). For complex multi-piece designs with embossing + foil stamping, MOQ starts at 1,000 units. Trial orders accepted for market testing.

Q: Can Bincai match a specific leather texture or Pantone color? A: Yes. We source PU leatherette in smooth, pebbled, crocodile, saffiano, and custom-embossed textures. Color matching to any Pantone code — we maintain a library of 200+ standard colors and can custom-dye for orders above 3,000 units.

Q: How are the die-cut slots sized for my specific jewelry pieces? A: You provide ring/earring dimensions (or ship us samples), and our CAD team creates a CNC die-cut template. Slot tolerances are ±0.3mm ensuring a snug, secure fit without scratching. We provide a digital proof for approval before cutting.

Q: What certifications do your jewelry boxes carry for EU/US retail? A: Full compliance package: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody (FSC-C147358), REACH (SVHC-tested), California Prop 65, and Sedex SMETA 4-Pillar audited. We provide on-request lab test reports for phthalates, heavy metals, and formaldehyde.

Q: How long from order to delivery? A: Standard lead time is 15–20 days from sample approval to production completion. Complex orders (foil stamping + embossing + multi-slot inserts) require 20–30 days. Sea freight to US/EU ports adds 25–35 days; air freight available for urgent orders at 5–7 days.
