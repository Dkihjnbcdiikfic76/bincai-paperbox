Customization Options

  • Exterior Finish: Flocked paper (20+ colors — black, navy, burgundy, cream, blush pink), leatherette paper, linen-textured paper, or soft-touch matte lamination
  • Magnetic Flap: N48 neodymium magnet pair, recessed in 2.0mm greyboard for flush closure; magnet strength calibrated for easy open with deliberate closure feel
  • Decoration: Hot foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic), debossing/embossing, full-color offset printing, silk-screen logo

Quality Assurance

  1. Material Inspection — Flocked paper pile density check, adhesion test (cross-hatch ASTM D3359), color consistency vs. swatch
  2. Board Cutting — ±0.2mm dimensional tolerance on CNC cutting table, flap alignment verification
  3. Magnet Installation — Pull force verification (6-10N target), polarity check, recess depth gauge
  4. Foil Stamping QC — ±0.15mm registration, adhesion tape test, D65 light booth visual inspection
  5. Insert Fit Check — Velvet-to-tray adhesion peel test, clasp channel clearance verification with sample necklace
  6. Assembly QC — Flap alignment, magnetic closure force, lid-to-base gap ≤ 0.5mm, ribbon pull functionality
  7. Pre-Shipment — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.0 (minor) per ISO 2859-1 Level II

Why Bincai?

Bincai’s flocking process is performed in-house with electrostatic application equipment that embeds 1.2mm nylon fibers vertically into the paper surface — producing a uniform, durable velvet-like texture that won’t shed or mat with handling. Unlike outsourced flocking that can vary batch-to-batch, our controlled process ensures every box in a 50,000-unit run looks and feels identical. The magnetic flap closure uses N48 neodymium magnets calibrated for jewelry-weight boxes: strong enough to stay closed during shipping, light enough for a customer to open with one hand. Combined with ±0.15mm foil registration on our Bobst hot foil presses, your logo will appear in exactly the same position on every box.
