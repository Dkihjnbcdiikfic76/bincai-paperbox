FAQ

Q1: What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for luxury watch and jewelry rigid boxes? A: Our standard MOQ is 500 pieces per design. For first-time customers evaluating quality, we can accommodate trial orders as low as 300 pieces with a minor small-order surcharge. Repeat orders benefit from reduced pricing at volume tiers of 1,000, 5,000, and 10,000+ units.

Q2: Can I fully customize the exterior finish, interior lining, and insert layout? A: Absolutely. Every element is customizable — exterior wrapping (art paper, leatherette, wood veneer), foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold, holographic), interior flocking color (12+ velvet options plus suede and satin), and die-cut insert cavities sized precisely to your watch or jewelry dimensions. Our in-house design team provides free 3D mockups before production.

Q3: What type of magnets do you use, and how strong are they? A: We use N52-grade neodymium magnets — the strongest commercially available permanent magnets. Each magnet is machine-pressed between board layers with polarity-sensor verification on-line. Pull force ranges from 0.8 to 2.5 kgF depending on box size and configuration. Every unit undergoes 100% magnet alignment inspection to ensure the flap closes flush with ≤0.3 mm gap tolerance.

Q4: How do you handle international shipping for fragile rigid boxes? A: Rigid boxes are nested inside custom corrugated master cartons with foam corner protectors and divider inserts. We palletize to export standards (fumigation-treated wood or cardboard pallets) and ship via sea freight (FOB Shenzhen/Guangzhou), air freight, or express courier (DHL/FedEx/UPS). Full container loads (FCL) and less-than-container loads (LCL) are both available. Transit insurance is recommended and can be arranged on request.

Q5: How long does it take to receive a pre-production sample? A: Free pre-production samples with full decoration (foil stamping, velvet flocking, magnet closure, and your custom watch/jewelry insert) are delivered within 5–7 working days from design approval. Samples ship via international express courier at our cost for qualified orders. Bulk production lead time is 10–15 working days for new tooling or 7–10 working days for repeat orders.
