Product Overview

Premium velvet-lined pendant necklace gift box with magnetic flip lid, manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003, Foshan, Guangdong). This elegant jewelry box features a soft velvet interior, hidden magnetic closure, and custom die-cut insert to cradle pendants and necklaces securely during retail display and shipping. Built with 2mm rigid greyboard, wrapped in 157gsm art paper with matte or soft-touch lamination. QC Process (15 Checkpoints)

  1. Greyboard thickness caliper check (2.0 +/- 0.1mm)
  2. Art paper GSM verification
  3. CMYK print registration (KBA 1050, +/- 0.15mm)
  4. Color density check (densitometer, every 500 sheets)
  5. Lamination peel test (ASTM D3359)
  6. Foil stamping temperature/pressure verification
  7. Die-cutting precision (blade sharpness, +/- 0.2mm)
  8. Gluing/folding angle and alignment
  9. Magnet insertion position and polarity
  10. Velvet flocking thickness and coverage
  11. Insert fit test (pendant retention, shake test)
  12. Lid alignment and gap check (<= 1mm)
  13. Surface inspection (scratches, dust, foil voids)
  14. AQL 2.5 sampling per ISO 2859-1
  15. Pre-shipment audit with photographic record
