The velvet ring presentation box with LED spotlight and certificate drawer is designed for the most significant jewelry moment — the reveal. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — a 23-year Foshan paper box specialist with 18,000 sqm across two facilities — this box wraps 1.5mm rigid greyboard in premium velvet with a magnetic closure that opens with satisfying precision. A warm white LED spotlight automatically illuminates the ring as the lid opens, while the concealed pull-out drawer elegantly presents the certificate of authenticity, diamond grading report, or brand story.

Bincai operates KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses for precision brand printing, with Bobst SP 102-E die-cutters for flawless dimensional accuracy. Every box passes seven-stage QC inspection and is ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified.

Customization Options

  • Velvet Exterior: Premium polyester velvet in 30+ colors — classic black, midnight blue, burgundy, emerald green, blush pink, ivory, royal purple. Short-pile (1.5mm) for elegant matte finish
  • LED Spotlight: Warm white 3000K LED with 45° beam angle focused on the ring. Magnetic reed switch activation — lights on when lid opens, off when closed. Battery (CR1632, 150+ hours) or USB-C rechargeable
  • Ring Pillow: Removable satin pillow in color-matched or contrasting white/cream. Firm density foam core with horizontal slit for ring band insertion. Optional dual pillow for engagement + wedding band sets
  • Certificate Drawer: Pull-out drawer beneath ring platform with ribbon pull tab. Fits standard GIA/IGI diamond grading report (folded) or custom brand certificate. Optional RFID-blocking lining for diamond security tags
  • Interior Lining: Tone-on-tone velvet interior or contrasting satin lining. Optional gold or silver foil-printed brand logo on lid interior
  • Closure: Hidden neodymium magnets for seamless exterior. Closure force 8-12N for one-handed opening
  • Branding: Hot foil stamping on lid exterior (gold, silver, rose gold, copper). Debossing for understated luxury. Full-color UV print for intricate brand patterns
  • Size Options: Standard (fits rings up to US size 13), Petite (up to size 8), Grand (up to size 16 with wider presentation platform)
