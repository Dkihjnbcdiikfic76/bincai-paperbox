Kraft eco-friendly wine bottle gift box with natural jute twine handle and die-cut display window — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. Designed for boutique vineyards, craft distilleries, and specialty wine retailers seeking sustainable luxury packaging, this box combines the natural appeal of kraft board with the rustic elegance of jute twine. The die-cut window allows the bottle label to be showcased without opening, while the integrated jute handle provides easy carry. Produced across our 18,000 sqm factory with 1.7M daily output capacity and FSC certified materials.

Customization Options

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the kraft board truly eco-friendly? A: Yes. We use FSC certified kraft board with 30-80% post-consumer recycled content. Our standard formulation is 80% recycled fiber with virgin kraft top liner for print quality. Soy-based inks and water-based adhesives ensure the entire box is recyclable in standard paper streams. For fully compostable requirements, we offer PLA window film certified to EN 13432.

Q: Can the jute handle support a full wine bottle? A: Yes. Each jute twine handle is pull-tested to 15kg — well above the 1.5kg weight of a standard 750ml wine bottle. The handle passes through reinforced die-cut slots with folded kraft reinforcement tabs that distribute weight across the box structure, not just the die-cut hole edges.

Q: What are the MOQ and lead times? A: Standard MOQ is 500-1,000 units depending on complexity. Lead time is 12-18 working days for standard configurations, 15-22 working days for custom sizes or premium window films. Rush orders available at 7-10 working days with surcharge.

Q: Can you print inside the box as well? A: Yes. We offer 1-color or 2-color inside printing for brand messaging, vineyard story, tasting notes, or care instructions. Inside printing adds 2-3 working days and a plate charge.

Q: Does the window film come in compostable options? A: Yes. We offer PLA (polylactic acid) window film certified compostable to EN 13432 standards — degrades in industrial composting facilities within 90 days. Also available: rPET (recycled PET) for brand recycling programs, or open die-cut window with no film for maximum sustainability.

Q: What bottle sizes does this box accommodate? A: Standard 750ml wine bottle (Ø75-80mm × 290-330mm tall). We also manufacture for 375ml half-bottles, 1.5L magnums, champagne/sparkling wine bottles (wider diameter), and custom spirit bottle shapes. Each size requires its own custom die, included in the setup cost.

Q: Can you match my vineyard’s specific kraft shade? A: Yes. We can dye-match kraft board to your brand’s shade — natural light kraft, medium brown kraft, dark kraft, or even black kraft. Dye-matching adds 3-5 working days for sampling and a small color-matching fee.
