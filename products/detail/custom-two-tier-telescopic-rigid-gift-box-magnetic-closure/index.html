Product Overview

Our custom two-tier telescopic rigid gift box delivers an elevated unboxing experience through its dual-level presentation. The telescopic lid lifts off to reveal two stacked trays — each foam-lined and wrapped in luxury art paper — creating a dramatic reveal for premium products. Constructed from 2.5mm rigid greyboard and manufactured on our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press with precision Bobst die-cutting, this box combines structural integrity with visual sophistication. The soft-touch matte lamination adds a velvet-like tactility while the hidden neodymium magnetic closure ensures the lid seats perfectly every time. Produced at Bincai’s 18,000 sqm integrated factory with 23 years of premium packaging expertise, each box undergoes a rigorous 7-stage quality control process — from incoming board inspection (ISO 186) to final packaging audit. Perfect for gourmet chocolate collections, corporate gift sets, premium cosmetics duo packs, and luxury confectionery assortments.

Design Features

  • Telescopic Two-Tier Design: Upper and lower trays slide apart to present products in a curated, gallery-style layout — ideal for multi-product gift sets
  • Hidden Neodymium Magnetic Closure: N42-grade neodymium magnets embedded within 2.5mm greyboard provide a satisfying snap-close action without visible hardware, tested to 10,000+ open/close cycles
  • Soft-Touch Matte Lamination: Luxurious velvety surface finish that resists fingerprints and scratches, rated 500+ Sutherland rub cycles
  • Color-Matched Satin Ribbon Pull: 15mm satin ribbon (12 color options) for effortless lid removal, tensile tested ≥15N pull strength
  • Custom Die-Cut EVA Foam Inserts: Precision die-cut high-density EVA foam cradles each product securely, custom-shaped to your product dimensions with ±0.5mm tolerance
  • Full CMYK + Spot UV: Offset printing on Heidelberg 7+1 UV press with closed-loop spectrophotometer color management (ΔE ≤ 2.0) for brand-accurate reproduction
  • Embossing/Debossing: Custom logo, monogram, or pattern embossing available on lid surface and tray interiors — multi-level embossing dies with brass tooling

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom two-tier telescopic boxes? A: MOQ starts at 500 units for standard specifications with existing tooling. Custom tooling (new die, embossing plate, foil stamping die) typically requires 1,000+ units to amortize setup costs economically.

Q: Can the interior foam inserts be customized to fit my specific products? A: Yes. Bincai’s in-house die-making team creates precision EVA foam molds based on your product CAD files or physical samples. Cavity tolerance is ±0.5mm for a secure friction fit. We can accommodate bottles, jars, electronics, cosmetics, and irregularly shaped items.

Q: What printing quality standards do you maintain for color accuracy? A: We use closed-loop spectrophotometer color management on both KBA 1050 and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses, maintaining ΔE ≤ 2.0 against the ISO 12647-2 standard. Pre-production color proofs are provided for approval before mass production.

Q: How does the telescopic two-tier mechanism work in practice? A: The lid is a separate piece that slides down over both trays. Once removed, the two foam-lined trays can be lifted apart or slide horizontally — depending on the design — to reveal products in a gallery-style presentation. The hidden neodymium magnets ensure the lid seats squarely every time.

Q: Can I get free samples before placing a bulk order? A: Yes. Bincai provides free pre-production samples within 3–5 working days, including material swatches, print proofs, and structural samples. Custom tooling costs apply if new dies or plates are required — these are typically credited against the first production order.
