FAQ

Q: How is the pen secured inside the satin tray — will it shift during shipping? A: Each pen slot features a precision die-cut satin-covered insert with elastic retention bands at two points (barrel and cap) that hold the pen firmly in place. For single-slot configurations, we recommend a secondary ribbon loop under the lid that wraps over the pen when closed for maximum shipping security. Our packaging is ISTA 1A drop-tested with a filled pen weight equivalent (30–60g) to ensure zero movement during standard parcel carrier handling.

Q: Can the satin lining be customized to match my brand’s Pantone color? A: Yes. Our satin fabric supplier provides a library of 120+ standard colors, and we offer custom Pantone-matched dye-to-order satin for orders of 5,000+ units. All satin fabrics meet OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification for textile safety, and we provide lab dips for color approval before production. The custom color lead time adds approximately 7–10 days to the standard production schedule.

Q: Does the magnetic closure interfere with fountain pen nibs or electronics? A: The N35 neodymium magnets are positioned in the front flap and base board, typically 40–60mm away from the pen slots when the box is closed. At this distance, the magnetic field strength at the pen slot is below 0.5 gauss — well under the threshold that could affect fountain pen nibs (steel or gold), which are non-ferromagnetic. For boxes containing USB flash drives or electronic stylus pens, we offer a magnet-free ribbon-tie closure variant.
