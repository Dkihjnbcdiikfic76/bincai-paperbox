FAQ

Q: Can the PVC window be custom shaped to match my earring design? A: Absolutely. Our automatic die-cutting lines can produce windows in any shape — rectangular, oval, circular, heart-shaped, or a custom contour that follows your earring profile. We create a custom cutting die (included at no extra charge for orders above 5,000 units), and the PVC/PET film is precision-patched with ±0.5mm tolerance.

Q: What’s the difference between PVC and PET window material? A: PVC offers excellent clarity at a lower cost and is the most common choice for earring card boxes. PET (polyester) provides superior clarity, better scratch resistance, and is a more environmentally friendly option — fully recyclable and accepted in most municipal recycling streams. PET costs approximately 15–20% more than PVC. Both are available in 0.2–0.5mm thicknesses.

Q: Can I order a small batch to test the market before committing to a large order? A: Yes. While our standard MOQ is 500 units, we welcome trial orders of 100–300 units for market testing. Unit pricing is higher at these volumes due to setup costs, but it’s an excellent way to validate packaging design before scaling. We also provide free pre-production samples with full finishing so you can evaluate quality before any commitment.

Q: How do hanging earring card boxes hold up during shipping and retail handling? A: Our card boxes are engineered for durability. 300–400 gsm paperboard with matte or gloss lamination resists bending and moisture. The euro-slot hang tab is reinforced and tested to support 500g+ without tearing. For e-commerce shipments, we recommend adding a corrugated mailer or padded envelope — the flat profile of card boxes stacks efficiently and resists crushing better than rigid boxes.
