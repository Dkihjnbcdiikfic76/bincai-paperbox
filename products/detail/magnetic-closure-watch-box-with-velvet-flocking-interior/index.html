7-Stage QC Gate

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — greyboard grammage, velvet pile height, magnet grade N35 verification
  2. In-Process Printing — Heidelberg Prinect inline, color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤2.0 target
  3. Die-Cutting Verification — ±0.15mm dimensional tolerance, 100% panel alignment
  4. Velvet Flocking Inspection — fiber bond strength, colorfastness under D65 lighting, pile uniformity
  5. Assembly QC — magnet recess depth, hinge alignment, pillow retention, closure mechanism test
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.5 (critical) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — random sample pull, full functional test, 100% magnet and closure function check

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom watch boxes? A: MOQ starts at 500 units for standard configurations. For fully custom designs with new tooling or custom cushion molds, MOQ is 1,000+ units. Trial orders and OEM/ODM are welcome.

Q: How long does sampling and production take? A: Free pre-production samples ship within 5-7 days of artwork approval. Production lead time is 10-14 days for standard orders, 15-20 days for complex builds with multi-compartment inserts or specialty finishes.

Q: What velvet colors and types are available for the interior? A: We offer 20+ nylon flocking colors (black, navy, grey, burgundy, cream, chocolate, emerald) with 1.0–1.5mm pile height. Suede microfiber and satin linings are also available. Colorfast and rub-tested for 500+ cycles.

Q: Can I use my own watch pillow design or shape? A: Yes. We produce custom satin-covered foam pillows to match your watch case profile — standard round, rectangular, or cuff-style. Custom tooling for unique pillow shapes is available with a one-time setup fee.

Q: Are the boxes FSC certified and export-ready? A: Yes. All watch boxes ship with FSC Chain-of-Custody documentation, ISO 9001:2015 compliance records, and are ISTA 1A drop-test certified for international freight. Flat-pack shipping option reduces freight cost by up to 60%.
