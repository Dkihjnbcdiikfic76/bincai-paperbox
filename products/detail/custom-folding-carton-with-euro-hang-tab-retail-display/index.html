Product Overview

A versatile folding carton with an integrated Euro hang tab — engineered for retail pegboard display while maintaining the structural integrity and print quality of premium folding carton packaging. The Euro hang tab is die-cut in-line during production on the Heidelberg cylinder die-cutter, ensuring perfect registration with the carton body and zero additional assembly cost. This eliminates the need for secondary retail-ready packaging — the shipping carton IS the display carton. Produced on the KBA 1050 4-color offset press at Guangdong Bincai’s 18,000 sqm factory (10,000+8,000 smart factory), this carton delivers vibrant CMYK printing with optional Pantone spot colors on board stocks from 300gsm to 500gsm. Daily folding carton output reaches 1.7 million units across four high-speed production lines.

Production Workflow

  1. Design & Prepress — artwork review, structural CAD design, color separation with digital proof for client approval
  2. CTP Plate Making — computer-to-plate imaging at 200 LPI for KBA 1050 offset cylinders
  3. Offset Printing — KBA 1050 CMYK at 15,000 sheets/hour with inline spectrophotometer for real-time color monitoring
  4. Specialty Finishing — Heidelberg 7+1 UV for spot UV accents, gloss effects, and white underprint on kraft stock
  5. Lamination — gloss, matte, or soft-touch film applied on high-speed laminator
  6. Die-Cutting — Heidelberg cylinder die-cutter stamps carton outline, Euro hang tab slot, fold lines, and optional window in single pass
  7. Window Patching — PET or PLA film applied (if specified) with hot-melt adhesive, ±0.5mm position tolerance
  8. Folding & Gluing — high-speed folder-gluer forms carton, applies adhesive to manufacturer’s joint, counts, and stacks
  9. QC Inspection — spectrophotometer color verification, dimensional check, glue bond peel test, hang tab pull strength, visual inspection
  10. Packing — flat-packed in corrugated master cartons with moisture barrier, palletized for export

  • Size range: From compact 60mm × 30mm × 120mm to generous 300mm × 200mm × 100mm — custom dimensions always available
  • Hang tab styles: Standard Euro slot (20mm × 5mm), reinforced double-ply tab for heavier products, or sombrero-style wide hook for high-traffic retail

  • Spot UV: High-gloss accents on matte backgrounds for dramatic logo and pattern highlighting
  • Board options: White kraft (natural elegance), CCNB (economy), SBS (premium white), kraft-lined (eco image)
  • Soft-touch lamination: Velvet-like tactile finish that consumers instinctively reach for on retail shelves

Quality Assurance

In-line quality control at every production stage ensures consistent excellence:

  • Color Verification — X-Rite spectrophotometer measures every 500th sheet against customer-approved digital proof
  • Dimensional Check — ±0.3mm tolerance on all die-cut edges, verified by digital caliper sampling
  • Glue Bond Testing — peel test on manufacturer’s joint verifies minimum 3N/cm adhesive strength
  • Hang Tab Integrity — pull force test verifies minimum 5kg load capacity before tear — exceeding typical retail product weight (0.2–2kg) by 2.5× safety margin
  • Surface Finish — visual inspection under D65 lighting for lamination uniformity, foil registration, and spot UV alignment
  • Final Audit — AQL 2.5 sampling per ISO 2859-1 before carton packing and shipment
