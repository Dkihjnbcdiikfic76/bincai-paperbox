FAQ

Q: How long does the LED battery last, and can it be replaced? A: Standard CR2032 battery configuration provides approximately 120 hours of continuous illumination (equivalent to 7,200 open/close cycles at 1 minute each). The battery compartment is concealed under the interior velvet tray with a screw-secured panel for easy replacement without damaging the box. For high-volume retail environments, we offer a USB-C rechargeable variant with a 300mAh battery that provides 50+ hours per charge.

Q: Is the anti-tarnish lining effective for both silver and gold jewelry? A: Yes. Our anti-tarnish lining uses activated carbon-impregnated velvet fabric that absorbs hydrogen sulfide and sulfur dioxide — the primary gases that cause silver tarnishing. Independent lab testing (SGS, Intertek) shows our anti-tarnish lining extends tarnish-free storage from 3–6 months (untreated velvet) to 18–24 months in normal indoor conditions. The lining is effective for sterling silver, gold-plated, and fine gold jewelry.

Q: Can the LED light color temperature be customized for my brand? A: Absolutely. Standard configuration is warm white (3000K) which provides the most flattering illumination for gold and rose gold jewelry. We also offer cool white (5000K) for diamond and platinum pieces, neutral white (4000K) for mixed collections, and RGB LED options with a remote control for dynamic color-changing displays. Color temperature samples are included in your pre-production sample.
