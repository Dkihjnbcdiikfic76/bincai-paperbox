FAQ

Q: Is the kraft box truly plastic-free — even the adhesive? A: Yes, every component is plastic-free. The greyboard core uses water-based starch adhesive (repulpable, solvent-free). The kraft wrap is bonded with PVA water-based glue — no hot melt, no solvent adhesives. The jute ribbon is 100% natural plant fiber. There is no plastic lamination (no BOPP, no PET), no synthetic coating, and no plastic window. This means the entire box can be recycled in standard paper waste streams without separation — and in the fully compostable configuration, it will biodegrade in home composting conditions within 3–6 months.

Q: Can you print white or metallic ink on kraft paperboard? A: Yes. While kraft’s natural brown base limits the brightness of CMYK alone (similar to printing on brown cardboard), we achieve excellent results using opaque white under-base printing followed by CMYK overprint — the white layer creates a bright canvas on the kraft surface. Metallic gold, silver, and copper Pantone inks also print beautifully on kraft, creating a striking contrast against the natural brown background. For the most vibrant results on kraft, we recommend single-color or two-color designs using opaque inks rather than full photographic CMYK.

Q: What is the difference between recycled kraft and virgin FSC kraft? A: Recycled kraft paperboard is made from 100% post-consumer and post-industrial recovered fiber — it has a slightly darker, more textured appearance with visible fiber flecks that contribute to the authentic rustic look. Virgin FSC kraft is made from first-use wood fiber from FSC-certified forests — it has a cleaner, more uniform surface with slightly brighter color and better print fidelity for detailed designs. Both are fully recyclable and FSC certified. Recycled kraft is preferred for maximum sustainability credentials; virgin FSC kraft is preferred when print detail and color accuracy are critical. We stock both options.

Q: Can the jute ribbon be replaced with other natural fiber closures? A: Yes. In addition to natural jute (most popular — rustic, affordable), we offer: organic cotton twill tape (softer, dyeable to any Pantone), hemp twine (extra-strong, slightly coarser texture), linen ribbon (premium European aesthetic), and bamboo fiber cord (innovative, ultra-smooth). All are natural fiber, biodegradable, and plastic-free. Minimum order for non-jute closures is 1,000 units due to minimum spool quantities from our fiber suppliers.

Q: What are the lead times for custom-printed kraft boxes? A: Standard lead time is 15–18 working days from artwork approval to shipment for offset-printed production runs (500–10,000 units). Digital short-run printing (50–500 units) delivers in 10–12 working days. Rush service (10 working days) is available with a 15% surcharge. Pre-production digital proofs are provided within 2–3 working days; physical press proofs (recommended for color-critical projects) add 5–7 working days.
