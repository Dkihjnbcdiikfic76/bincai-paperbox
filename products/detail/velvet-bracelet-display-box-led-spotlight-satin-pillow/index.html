FAQ

Q: How long does the LED battery last? A: The standard CR2032 battery configuration provides 200+ hours of continuous LED illumination. The USB-C rechargeable option supports 500+ charge cycles with 8–10 hours of illumination per charge. A low-battery indicator (LED dims to 50% brightness) alerts retailers when replacement is needed.

Q: Can the LED color be customized for my brand? A: Yes. Standard options include warm white (3000K), cool white (6000K), and RGB (programmable). For brand-specific color temperatures, we can source custom SMD LED chips with minimum 5,000 unit orders. The RGB option can be pre-programmed to cycle through brand colors or pulse in a specific pattern.

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity for custom LED configurations? A: Standard CR2032 battery-powered configuration: MOQ 300 units. USB-C rechargeable: MOQ 500 units. Custom LED color or RGB programming: MOQ 1,000 units. Free pre-production samples with 7–10 day turnaround for approval before mass production.

Q: Does the velvet flocking shed over time? A: No. Our electrostatic velvet flocking achieves ≥95% adhesion per ASTM D3359 cross-hatch testing (Grade 4B+). The Nylon 6.6 fibers are applied with 50kV electrostatic charge and cured at 150°C for permanent bonding. We warranty against flocking shed for the rated product lifespan under normal retail display and storage conditions.

Q: Is the magnetic closure strong enough for shipping? A: Yes. The hidden N35 neodymium magnets provide >800g pull force per magnet pair, tested through ISTA 1A 10-drop sequence at 760mm height — zero accidental opening. For international air freight, we recommend adding a removable paper belly band for additional shipping security (included at no extra charge).

Q: Can I order different sizes for different bracelet styles? A: Absolutely. The satin pillow groove width ranges from 8mm (delicate chain bracelets) to 25mm (chunky bangles and cuffs). Box sizes from 180×80×40mm to 250×120×60mm cover all standard bracelet formats. Custom dimensions for oversized statement pieces are available with MOQ 500.

Q: What happens if an LED module fails? A: Our SMD 2835 LED modules are rated for 50,000+ hours to L70 (70% of initial brightness). We test 100% of LED modules with integrating sphere measurement before assembly. In the rare event of failure (<0.1% defect rate), we provide replacement modules and installation instructions, or full box replacement for orders under warranty.
