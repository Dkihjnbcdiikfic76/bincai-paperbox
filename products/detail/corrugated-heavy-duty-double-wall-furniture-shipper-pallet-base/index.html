Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) produces heavy-duty double-wall corrugated shipping boxes engineered specifically for international furniture export. Our 18,000 sqm factory in Foshan runs a BHS 2.5m corrugator producing BC-flute and EB-flute board with edge crush ratings from ECT 32 to ECT 44+, supported by KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV flexo/offset presses for high-quality exterior branding. Every box is manufactured under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification, with total daily output of 1.7 million boxes across all categories.

Designed for flat-pack furniture, sofas, cabinetry, and oversized home goods, this double-wall shipper features a reinforced pallet-base construction with die-cut fork-lift channels, integrated stacking corners, and moisture-resistant Kraft liners rated for 85% RH maritime container conditions. The double-wall structure absorbs impact across the BC-flute or EB-flute profile, while the heavy-duty outer liner resists punctures during cross-docking and LCL consolidation.

Quality Assurance

Every furniture shipper passes through Bincai’s seven-stage QC gate:

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — Grammage verification (ISO 536), Cobb test for moisture absorption (TAPPI T441), ring crush of each liner
  2. Corrugator Line QC — Flute height measurement, bond strength (pin adhesion TAPPI T821), edge crush every 50 linear meters
  3. Print Registration — Spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0 target, barcode scannability verification
  4. Die-Cutting Verification — ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance, slot/score alignment, fold-line integrity
  5. Assembly & Gluing QC — Hot-melt adhesive temperature and bead placement, self-locking bottom engagement force test
  6. Box Compression Test — BCT sampling per batch, ISTA 3E simulated distribution testing for export pallet loads
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.0 (minor) per ISO 2859-1 Level II, 100% pallet fit check
