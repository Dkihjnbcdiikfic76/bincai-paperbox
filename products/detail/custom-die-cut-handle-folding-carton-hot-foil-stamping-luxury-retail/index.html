FAQ

Q: How strong is the die-cut handle — can it carry heavy products like glass bottles? A: The die-cut handle is engineered with reinforced crease lines and a double-layer fold-over construction that distributes load across a wider paper area. Our testing shows:

  • 350gsm FBB with soft-touch: safe working load 2.5 kg
  • 450gsm SBS with reinforced handle: safe working load 4.0 kg
  • 500gsm with fiber-tear reinforcement tape: safe working load 5.0+ kg We validate every design at 3× the rated payload. For glass bottles or heavy items, we recommend the reinforced handle specification with an internal fiber-tear strip that prevents the handle from tearing along the grain direction.

Q: Does the soft-touch coating show fingerprints or wear over time? A: Our premium-grade soft-touch aqueous coating is formulated with anti-fingerprint additives and passes the Sutherland Rub Test at 1,000+ cycles with 4 lb weight without visible wear. The coating maintains its velvety feel through retail handling, shipping, and customer unboxing. For extended display environments with heavy handling, we offer an upgraded soft-touch laminate film (12μm PET) that provides fingerprint resistance and durability for 2+ years of retail display.

Q: Can the box ship flat, and how easy is assembly at retail? A: All folding cartons ship flat (knocked down) for maximum freight efficiency — pallets hold 2,000–5,000 flat cartons versus 200–400 assembled. Assembly at retail takes approximately 5–8 seconds: fold side panels, tuck bottom flap, fold top flap, and the integrated handle pops into position. For high-volume retail environments, we include a simple 3-step visual instruction card. Assembly training takes under 2 minutes for new staff.
