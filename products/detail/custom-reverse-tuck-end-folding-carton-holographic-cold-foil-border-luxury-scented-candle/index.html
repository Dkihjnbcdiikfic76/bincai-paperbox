Product Overview

This reverse tuck end (RTE) folding carton elevates scented candle packaging with holographic cold foil border detailing and soft-touch matte lamination — a tactile and visual combination that communicates luxury before the customer even lights the wick. The RTE structure (top tucks in reverse direction from bottom) provides secure closure while enabling efficient automated filling on candle production lines. Each carton is printed on Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses with in-line cold foil transfer capability, then die-cut to ±0.15mm precision on Bobst SP 102 BMA flatbed systems. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing with over 23 years of folding carton expertise, ISO 9001:2015 quality management, FSC Chain-of-Custody certification, and daily output of 1.7 million color boxes from an 18,000 sqm integrated facility in Foshan, Guangdong.

Quality Control — 15 Checkpoints

Every folding carton passes through this 15-stage QC gate system:

  1. Material Inspection — Board caliper and GSM verification, C1S brightness check, moisture 6–8%
  2. Pre-Press Proof — Color bars, cold foil registration marks, die-line match verification
  3. Print Color — ΔE ≤ 2.0 spectrophotometer reading against GRACoL 2013 reference
  4. Cold Foil Adhesion — Tape test per ASTM D3359, zero peel or flaking
  5. Foil Registration — ±0.3mm alignment to printed border artwork
  6. Lamination Bond — ≥2.5 N/cm² peel strength, zero bubbling, zero delamination
  7. Soft-Touch Durability — 50-cycle Crockmeter rub test, no visible wear
  8. Die-Cutting Precision — Dimensions ±0.15mm, crease depth ±0.05mm
  9. Crease Quality — Clean fold lines, zero fiber cracking, consistent folding tension
  10. Glue Seam — Bond strength ≥8 N/25mm, zero pop-open at side seam
  11. Tuck Flap Fit — Friction engagement within spec, zero spontaneous opening
  12. Insert Fit — Candle jar cradled firmly, ≤1mm free movement
  13. Flat Fold — Cartons fold flat without spring-back for efficient shipping
  14. AQL Sampling — ISO 2859-1, Level II, AQL 2.5 for major defects
  15. Pack-Out Audit — Final 100% visual check, cartons oriented correctly for automated filling
