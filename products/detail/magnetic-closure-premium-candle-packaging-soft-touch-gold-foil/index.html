Product Overview

Bincai’s magnetic closure candle packaging box is designed for premium candle brands that understand packaging is half the product experience — before the customer smells the fragrance, they feel the box. The 2.0mm greyboard core delivers substantial weight and rigidity. A soft-touch matte wrap with gold foil branding creates shelf presence that competes with Diptyque, Jo Malone, and Byredo. Inside, a flocked PET tray cradles the candle vessel securely with ±0.5mm precision. Dual N35 neodymium magnets provide a satisfying, silent close. Manufactured in our Foshan smart factory (18,000 sqm) on Heidelberg 7+1 UV offset, KBA 1050 4-color presses, and Bobst BMA foil stampers. 80+ skilled workers, 23 years of packaging expertise.

Applications

  • Scented Candle Packaging: 3-wick, 2-wick, and single-wick candle boxes with glass vessel protection
  • Luxury Candle Gift Sets: Multi-compartment boxes for candle + matches + snuffer + wick trimmer sets
  • Artisanal & Small-Batch Candles: Bespoke branding for indie candle makers with foil and embossing
  • Corporate Candle Gifts: Branded candle boxes for hotel amenities, corporate gifting, and wedding favors
  • Seasonal & Holiday Candles: Christmas, Valentine’s Day, and Mother’s Day limited-edition packaging
  • Reed Diffuser Sets: Candle + reed diffuser combo boxes with dual compartments
