Discreetly luxurious folding carton with hidden magnetic closure — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of manufacturing expertise (founded 2003). The carton appears as a minimalist monolithic block with no visible closure — two N35 neodymium magnets are embedded between the paperboard layers during lamination, creating an invisible magnetic clasp that snaps the flap shut with satisfying precision. The 350gsm FSC-certified SBS board is wrapped in soft-touch lamination that feels like suede to the fingertips — fingerprints wipe clean and the tactile experience signals luxury before the product is even seen. Spot UV accents and hot foil stamping on Kolbus DA270 add branding brilliance. The auto-lock bottom erects in one motion with no adhesive required. Produced on KBA 1050 4-color offset and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses across Bincai’s 18,000 sqm dual factory (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory) in Foshan, Guangdong. ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certified. FAQ

Q: Are the magnets truly invisible? A: Yes — N35 disc magnets (8mm×2mm) are embedded between two layers of 350gsm board during lamination. The paper surface shows no bulge, depression, or discoloration. QC Gate 7 inspects every 50th carton under grazing light.

Q: Will the soft-touch coating wear off? A: BOPP soft-touch lamination passes a 500-cycle abrasion test (Sutherland Rub Tester, 4lb weight) with no visible wear. For aqueous soft-touch coating, we recommend avoiding prolonged friction against rough surfaces.

Q: Can the auto-lock bottom really hold without glue? A: Yes — the die-cut auto-lock mechanism locks the bottom panel tabs together through tension alone. Tested to hold 1.5kg (350gsm board) without failure. This is a 100-year-old folding carton technology refined by Bobst precision die-cutting.

Q: What’s the minimum order for custom die lines? A: 3,000 units for new die lines (one-time die cost US$100-200). Standard stock sizes (with Bincai’s 50+ library die lines) start at 1,000 units.

Q: Can the carton be fully recyclable? A: Choose aqueous soft-touch coating (instead of BOPP lamination) for a fully recyclable paperboard carton. Magnets should be removed before recycling (they’re visible once the carton is torn open despite being hidden when intact).

Q: What’s the lead time? A: Standard: 15-20 working days. Rush: 10 working days (1.2× pricing). Magnet-embedded cartons add 2-3 working days for magnet lamination step.
