Product Overview

Double-wall corrugated export pallet box engineered for heavy industrial machinery parts — engine components, gearboxes, hydraulic cylinders, motor assemblies, automotive spares, and construction equipment accessories. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) in our 18,000 sqm Foshan dual-factory complex (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory). Our heavy-duty export boxes combine BC/BE flute double-wall or triple-wall 7-ply construction with reinforced bottom pallet integration, providing up to 1,500kg dynamic stacking strength for ocean freight and intermodal container transport. Daily output of 1.7 million boxes under ISO 9001:2015 quality management and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

Applications

  • Engine blocks, cylinder heads, and crankshaft components for export
  • Automotive transmission and gearbox assemblies
  • Hydraulic pumps, motors, and cylinders for construction equipment
  • Industrial valves, pipe fittings, and flange assemblies
  • Electric motor and generator components
  • Agricultural machinery spare parts and attachments
  • Mining equipment component packaging
  • Rail and marine equipment parts export
