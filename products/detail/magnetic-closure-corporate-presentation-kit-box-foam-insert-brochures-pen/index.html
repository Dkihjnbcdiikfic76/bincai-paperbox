Customization Options

Every presentation kit box is engineered precisely for your content:

  • Magnetic Closure: Hidden N52 neodymium magnets (2, 4, or 6 depending on box size) engineered for a satisfying snap-close. N52 grade ensures consistent magnetic force after 20,000+ open/close cycles. Optional magnetic + ribbon pull for larger formats (A4+). Counter-sunk magnet pockets precision-milled by CNC for flush, invisible integration.

  • Foam Insert: Custom die-cut EVA foam (40-70 shore hardness) in black, charcoal, or white with laser-cut precision ±0.2mm. PU memory foam for luxury tactile feel. Flocked PET for scratch-free document presentation. Configurable cavities for brochures (A4, A5, US Letter), business cards (standard 90×54mm), pens (standard diameter + custom), USB drives, product samples, certificates, and seals.

  • Surface Finishes: Soft-touch matte lamination (suede-like feel, fingerprint-resistant, 12+ color options), glossy lamination (high-shine wet look), leatherette wrap (PU or genuine leather texture in 8+ colors), linen-textured paper (natural, premium), or pearlescent coating. Full CMYK + Pantone spot color offset printing with aqueous or UV overcoating.

  • Foil Stamping & Embossing: Hot foil stamping in gold, silver, copper, rose gold, or custom Pantone metallics on Bobst SP 102 BMA with ±0.15mm registration. Blind embossing or foil embossing for dimensional logos. Debossing for subtle, elegant branding. Multi-level embossing for complex logo topography.

  • Construction: 2.5mm greyboard (1200-1600gsm), CNC corner wrapping with ±0.3mm 90° precision. Bobst Visioncut 106 LER for precision panel cutting. Bobst Expertfold 110 A2 for consistent assembly. Optional wrap-around spine hinge for book-style opening or top-lid lift-off design. Interior lining in colored art paper, suede, velvet, or satin.

  • Additional Features: Ribbon closure, elastic band, magnetic flap, drawer-style pull-out tray, multi-tier stacking levels, certificate/document pocket sleeves, and custom-shaped cavities for irregular product samples.
