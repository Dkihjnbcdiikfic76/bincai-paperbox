Product Overview

Bincai’s kraft paper sustainable fashion apparel mailer is engineered for clothing brands that have made sustainability a core brand value — and need packaging that proves it. Constructed from 100% post-consumer recycled kraft board on our BHS 2.5m corrugator, this mailer uses soy-based inks for printing and water-based adhesive for closure. The integrated tear strip provides a premium unboxing experience — no scissors, no frustration — while the self-seal strip eliminates plastic tape entirely. Flat-packed for efficient warehousing, these mailers assemble in under 3 seconds. Every component — board, ink, adhesive — is designed for curbside recycling in a single stream. Manufactured in our Foshan smart factory (18,000 sqm) with Heidelberg 7+1 UV offset for brand-quality printing even on kraft surfaces. FSC chain-of-custody certified.

Applications

  • DTC (Direct-to-Consumer) Clothing: E-commerce apparel shipping boxes with tear-strip unboxing
  • Subscription Box Services: Monthly fashion and lifestyle subscription mailers

  • Accessories & Small Goods: Scarves, belts, hats, and fashion accessories in minimalist kraft
