Product Overview

Bincai’s corrugated literature mailer combines protective B-flute cushioning with a fast self-seal closure — no tape, no staples, no glue guns. The perforated tear strip opens cleanly in one pull, giving customers a frustration-free unboxing. Sized for standard book formats, A4 catalogs, and flat merchandise. Custom flexo printing on the kraft exterior with your brand logo and messaging. For premium full-color graphics, we offer offset litho printing on our KBA 1050 4-color press and UV-coated finishes on our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press — delivering magazine-quality imagery on corrugated board. ISTA 3A drop-test validated for e-commerce shipping. 23 years of corrugated box manufacturing from our 10,000 sqm Foshan factory.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Fluting: Single-wall B-flute — 2.5mm thickness, crush-resistant
  • Material: 125gsm kraft liner / 100gsm medium — FSC certified available
  • Closure: Hot-melt self-seal adhesive strip with silicone release liner
  • Opening: Full-width perforated tear strip — one-pull clean open
  • Printing: Flexographic 1-3 color on exterior, CMYK label option
  • Testing: ISTA 3A drop test, edge crush test (ECT) ≥23 lb/in

Customization Options

Every literature mailer is engineered to your exact product dimensions and brand requirements:

  • Size & Dimensions: Custom length × width × depth from 200×150×20mm to 400×300×50mm, with precision ±1mm tolerance on die-cutting for snug product fit
  • Flute Profile Selection: B-flute (2.5mm, standard crush resistance), E-flute (1.2mm, thin and elegant for premium catalogs), C-flute (3.5mm, heavy duty for valuable books), or EB-double-wall for maximum protection
  • Printing & Branding: Flexographic 1–3 color printing on kraft exterior, CMYK offset litho label application, or full-color litho-laminate for photographic-quality graphics — all with your brand logo, messaging, and QR codes
  • Closure Mechanism: Self-seal hot-melt adhesive strip with silicone release liner (fastest assembly), double-sided tape strip, or standard tuck-top with optional adhesive dot
  • Opening Experience: Full-width perforated tear strip for one-pull clean open, or zipper-style perforation for controlled reveal
  • Structural Add-Ons: Return label perforation for easy returns, Euro slot hang-hole for retail pegboard display, window cutout with PET film for product preview, or integrated business card slot
  • Material Options: 100% recycled kraft (FSC Recycled certified), virgin kraft (FSC Mix), or white-top kraft for brighter printing surface
  • Sustainability: PLA-based compostable adhesive strips available, water-based inks for eco-conscious brands
