Product Overview

Bincai’s hexagonal velvet earring box reimagines jewelry packaging with its distinctive six-sided geometry and integrated transparent PET window — allowing customers to see the earrings without opening the box. The 2mm rigid greyboard construction wrapped in 180gsm velvet flocking provides a luxurious hand feel, while the die-cut foam insert holds earrings securely in dedicated slots. Satin-lined interior and magnetic front-flap closure complete the premium unboxing experience. Perfect for retail display, e-commerce shipping, and bridal party gifting.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Shape: Regular hexagon (flat-to-flat distance 85mm standard, custom sizing available)
  • Box Construction: 2mm rigid greyboard core, 180gsm velvet flocking exterior wrap
  • Window: Die-cut aperture with 0.5mm crystal-clear PET film, ultrasonically welded
  • Interior: Satin fabric lining (white/black/champagne) with die-cut foam earring insert
  • Foam Insert: 10mm EVA foam, flocked surface, 2-4 earring slots with finger-cut removal channels
  • Closure: Magnetic front-flap, ≥3.0N holding force, invisible seam
  • Exterior Finish: Hot foil stamping + optional embossing/debossing on lid and spine
  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color offset + Heidelberg 7+1 UV
  • Factory: 18,000 sqm integrated facility, Foshan, Guangdong
  • Daily Output: 220,000+ rigid boxes
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody

When Hexagonal Wins — Scenario Selector for Jewelry Brands

The hexagonal format isn’t for every product. Use this decision guide:

Artisan/handcrafted jewelry, limited editionsHexagonal BoxDistinctive shape signals “handcrafted” before box opens — stands out at craft fairs and boutique displays

Sweet spot: Artisan jewelers, bridal collections, and brands where packaging IS a differentiator (not a cost center). For these use cases, the hexagon’s uniqueness advantage outweighs the 20-30% cost premium over square boxes.
