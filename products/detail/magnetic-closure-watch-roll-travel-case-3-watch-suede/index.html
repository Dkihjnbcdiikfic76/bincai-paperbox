Product Overview

Elegant watch roll travel case combining the protective structure of a rigid box with the portability of a roll-up design. The exterior is wrapped in pebbled PU leather with soft-touch lamination, while the interior features three removable suede watch cushions on snap-button mounts — allowing watches of different sizes to be securely held during travel. A wide magnetic flap with hidden N52 neodymium magnets provides secure closure without zippers or buckles that could scratch timepieces. The roll-up-and-tie design compresses to a compact cylinder for luggage packing. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) — 23 years crafting premium packaging for luxury goods.

  • Watch collector travel storage — fits dress watches, divers, and chronographs up to 48mm
  • Luxury watch brand gift-with-purchase and VIP client packaging
  • Authorized dealer and boutique watch presentation
  • Corporate executive gifts for watch enthusiasts
  • E-commerce watch fulfillment packaging (premium tier)

Customization Options

  • Exterior Material: Pebbled PU (standard), smooth PU, saffiano-texture PU, genuine leather (upgrade), or linen-wrapped
  • PU Colors: Black, brown, navy, burgundy, grey, tan — custom Pantone-matched PU available at MOQ 1,000+
  • Interior: Microsuede (standard) in beige, grey, or black; velvet flocking upgrade; genuine suede upgrade
  • Watch Capacity: Standard 3-watch; customizable to 1, 2, or 4-watch configurations
  • Closure: Magnetic flap (standard); optional snap-button strap, elastic band, or leather buckle strap

  • Cushion System: Snap-button removable (standard); fixed sewn-in cushions; metal spring-bar cushions for bracelet watches
