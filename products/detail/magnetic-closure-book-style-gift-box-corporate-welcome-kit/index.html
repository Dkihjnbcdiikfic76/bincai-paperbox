Product Overview

The Bincai Magnetic Closure Book-Style Gift Box elevates corporate gifting from ordinary to exceptional. Opening like a hardcover book, this 2.5mm rigid greyboard box features a satisfying magnetic closure that snaps shut with precision — the tactile experience that luxury brands demand. Inside, a recessed tray with ribbon pull-tab organizes welcome kit contents: branded notebook, pen, company swag, welcome letter, and onboarding materials. Exterior wrapped in premium art paper with gold foil logo stamping on our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press. With 23 years of rigid box manufacturing expertise and 220,000+ gift boxes daily from our Foshan facility, Bincai is the partner behind some of the world’s most recognized corporate welcome experiences.

FAQ

Q: How does the book-style hinge hold up after repeated use — will it wear out?

A: Bincai’s proprietary groove-and-fold hinge construction is tested to 5,000+ open/close cycles without spine separation, magnet loss, or visible wear. This significantly exceeds the typical 500–1,000 cycle lifespan of generic book-style boxes. The secret is our 2.5mm greyboard with precision-grooved hinge channel (cut to exactly 50% of board thickness at a 90° V-angle), reinforced with a flexible kraft paper backing strip that acts as a living hinge. For corporate welcome kits that employees may keep on their desks and open repeatedly, this durability ensures the box remains a brand asset — not a single-use throwaway.

Q: Can we customize every element — the exterior, interior tray, ribbon, and any inserts?

A: Absolutely. Every component is configurable: exterior art paper in 30+ Pantone-matched colors (matte, linen-textured, pearlized, or PU leather-look); foil stamping in gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic with multi-foil capability on Heidelberg 7+1 UV; interior tray configured precisely to your kit contents — die-cut foam for tech gadgets, velvet-lined cavities for premium items, ribbon-suspended trays for elegant presentation, or molded pulp for eco-conscious brands; ribbon pull-tab in custom colors with your brand message hot-stamped on the ribbon itself. We can also add a custom-printed dust cover, belly band, or outer shipping sleeve. Free structural design consultation included with every order.

Q: What’s the minimum order and how fast can we get samples?

A: MOQ is 500 units for standard A5/A4 sizes with existing tooling. Custom dimensions, unique tray layouts, or multi-foil designs may require MOQ 1,000+. Free pre-production samples are provided within 7 business days — we’ll produce 2–3 samples with your artwork so you can evaluate the physical box before committing to production. For urgent corporate programs (new hire class starting in 3 weeks, investor event deadline), we offer expedited sampling at 3–5 days and rush production at 10–12 working days for standard configurations.

Q: Is the box suitable for international corporate shipping?

A: Yes. The 2.5mm rigid greyboard construction with magnetic closure passes ISTA 1A drop testing (10 drops from 760mm) and provides excellent crush protection for standard courier shipping (FedEx, DHL, UPS). For fragile contents (glass awards, ceramic mugs, electronics), we can add EVA foam cushioning to the outer carton. We recommend polybag-sealing each box individually before packing into master cartons (typically 12–24 boxes per carton) for moisture protection during international transit. Our master cartons are double-wall corrugated with custom partitions to prevent box-to-box contact — we ship corporate kits to all major markets including US, EU, Middle East, and Southeast Asia.
