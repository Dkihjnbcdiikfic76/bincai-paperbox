Product Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing manufactures customizable fuchsia corrugated mailer boxes purpose-built for e-commerce shipping, subscription fulfillment, and direct-to-consumer packaging. These mailers combine vibrant full-color offset printing on the outer liner with crush-resistant corrugated fluting that protects contents during transit without adding excessive dimensional weight. Produced in our 18,000 sqm smart factory in Foshan, each mailer is precision die-cut for consistent folding and easy assembly on fast-paced packing lines.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Flute profiles: B-flute (2.5 mm — best for printing quality), C-flute (3.6 mm — balanced cushioning), E-flute (1.2 mm — retail-grade fine surface), F-flute (0.8 mm — rigid-board substitute), BC double-wall (6.1 mm — heavy-duty), EB double-wall (3.7 mm — premium e-commerce)
  • Board grades: Kraft liner (brown or white top), test liner, fully bleached white liner for vibrant print reproduction
  • Burst strength: 200-275 PSI (Mullen) for single-wall; 350-400 PSI for double-wall; ECT ratings from 32 to 48 available
  • Printing: KBA 1050 4-color offset on coated white-top liner for photographic-quality reproduction; Heidelberg 7+1 UV for metallic fuchsia, PMS spot, and UV varnish effects
  • Closure systems: Self-sealing adhesive strip with silicone release liner, tear-strip opening, tuck-top with locking tab, or tape-strip closure
  • Coating and finish: Aqueous coating, gloss UV, matte UV, soft-touch, or high-gloss lamination over printed surface
  • Sustainable options: 30-100% recycled content, FSC-certified liners and mediums, water-based adhesives, soy-based inks
  • Production capacity: 1.7 million color boxes daily, with dedicated corrugated converting lines for mailer production

FAQ

Q: What is the MOQ for custom printed mailer boxes? A: Minimum order quantity is 500 pieces per design for single-color printing, and 1,000 pieces for multi-color CMYK or PMS-matched printing. Lower trial quantities are available for sampling and market testing — contact us for pilot-run options.

Q: Are your mailer boxes FBA sizing compliant? A: Yes. We manufacture to common Amazon FBA tier dimensions (small standard, large standard, oversized) and can custom-size mailers to fit your product within FBA dimensional-weight brackets. Our design team optimizes flute grade and board weight to minimize shipping costs while preserving ISTA-verified protection.

Q: What is the shelf life of the self-seal adhesive strip? A: The self-sealing hot-melt adhesive strip has a minimum 12-month shelf life when stored at 10–30°C and 40–60% relative humidity, away from direct sunlight. Accelerated aging tests confirm adhesion retention beyond 18 months under recommended storage conditions.

Q: Can you use recycled content in the corrugated board? A: Absolutely. We offer 30%, 50%, 70%, and 100% recycled content across liners and fluting mediums. FSC-certified virgin and recycled grades are available with full chain-of-custody documentation. Soy-based inks and water-based adhesives further reduce environmental impact.

Q: How do flat-ship mailer boxes save on freight costs? A: Our flat-ship mailers are die-cut and creased to ship flat in bulk, occupying up to 60% less volumetric space than pre-erected boxes. This translates directly to lower ocean-freight or LTL charges. Your fulfillment center folds and assembles them on demand using the integrated self-seal strip — no tape or additional labor required.

Q: Can I get samples before placing a production order? A: Yes. We provide free structural samples (plain brown kraft) for fit testing within 5–7 business days. Custom-printed samples are available within 10–12 business days with a nominal setup charge, fully credited against your production order.

Q: What is the typical lead time for 5,000 units? A: Standard lead time for 5,000 custom-printed mailer boxes is 15–18 business days after artwork approval. Rush production (10–12 business days) is available for a surcharge. Ocean freight adds 18–30 days depending on destination port; air freight options available for urgent orders.
