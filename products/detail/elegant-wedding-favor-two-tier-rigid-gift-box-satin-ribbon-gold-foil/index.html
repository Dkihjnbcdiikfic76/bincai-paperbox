FAQ

Q: Can you print the couple’s names and wedding date on each box?

A: Absolutely. We can personalize every box with the couple’s names, wedding date, or a custom monogram using gold foil, blind debossing, or multi-level embossing. Personalization is included in the standard per-unit price — no setup fees for foil stamping dies on orders of 1,000+ units. The same die is used across the entire production run for perfect consistency.

Q: What’s the maximum weight each tier can hold?

A: Each tier supports up to 500g when the box is carried by its base. For heavier favors (mini champagne bottles, 200g+ candle jars), we recommend the reinforced 2.5mm greyboard upgrade — adds approximately 12% to the unit cost but doubles the load capacity to 1kg per tier.

Q: Can the two tiers have different colors or finishes?

A: Yes. Many wedding planners choose ivory satin for the upper tier and champagne velvet for the lower tier to create a visual reveal effect. Each tier is independently customizable for exterior color, interior lining, and foil color. The only constraint is that both tiers share the same base dimensions for proper stacking.

Q: How are the boxes shipped — assembled or flat?

A: Flat-packed for maximum freight efficiency. Each box folds together in under 30 seconds without tools or adhesive — the rigid greyboard construction holds its shape via engineered crease lines. This saves approximately 65% on shipping volume vs. pre-assembled boxes, translating to significant container-cost savings for international orders.

Q: What if I need more than two tiers?

A: We offer 3-tier and 4-tier configurations. Each additional tier adds approximately 35-40mm to the overall height and 15-20% to the unit cost. Lead time remains 10-14 days regardless of tier count. For wedding favor programs exceeding 3 tiers, we recommend a sample run first to validate structural stability.
