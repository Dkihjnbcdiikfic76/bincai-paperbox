FAQ

Q: Is the LED module replaceable once the battery runs out?

A: Yes. The CR2032 battery is housed in a concealed compartment accessible from the box base or lid rim. Simply slid open the battery door and replace with a new CR2032 cell (widely available). The LED module itself is rated for 100+ hours of continuous operation — well beyond typical seasonal display use.

Q: Can you customize the snowflake window to a different shape?

A: Absolutely. While the classic snowflake is our most popular holiday motif, we can die-cut virtually any shape — Christmas trees, stars, reindeer, candy canes, company logos, or custom patterns. We maintain a library of over 500 die templates, and custom dies are fabricated in-house within 3–5 days at no charge for production orders.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for holiday packaging?

A: Standard MOQ is 500 units. For seasonal test runs or limited edition releases, we can accommodate 300-unit minimums with a small surcharge. We understand holiday packaging often starts as a trial before scaling — our team works with you to find the right quantity balance.

Q: How long does production take for Christmas packaging orders?

A: Standard production lead time is 10–14 days from artwork approval. For Q4 holiday orders (September–November), we recommend placing orders 4–6 weeks ahead to secure production slots, as our seasonal capacity fills quickly. Rush orders (7-day turnaround) are available with priority scheduling.

Q: Can the LED light be warm white instead of cool white?

A: Yes. We stock warm white (2700K), pure white (4000K), cool white (6000K), and RGB color-changing LED modules. Simply specify your preferred color temperature or multicolor requirement during order placement. Warm white (2700K) is our default for holiday packaging as it creates the coziest, most inviting glow."
