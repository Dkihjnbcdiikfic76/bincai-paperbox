Design Features

  • Gold Foil Stamp: Hot foil application on Bobst SP 102 BMA — gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic foil with ±0.15mm multi-level registration on textured, linen, or smooth surfaces
  • Magnetic or Ribbon Closure: Hidden N52 neodymium magnets (6–8mm) for clean, satisfying snap-shut closure; or satin ribbon pull for classic elegance — aligned within ±0.3mm for flush lid seating
  • Rigid Construction: 1200–1600gsm greyboard with double-wrapped precision construction — lid and base wrapped in premium art paper (157–250gsm) with mitered corners
  • Embossed/Debossed Logo: Raised or recessed branding adds dimensional sophistication — blind emboss, foil-registered emboss, or multi-level sculpted emboss
  • Matte Lamination: Soft-touch velvet finish that resists fingerprints, scratches, and scuffing — maintains premium appearance through handling
  • Spot UV: Selective high-gloss UV varnish over foil or specific design elements for dimensional contrast
