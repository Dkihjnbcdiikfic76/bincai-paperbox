Product Overview

Our magnetic closure cigar humidor box combines 23 years of precision rigid-box manufacturing with authentic Spanish cedar interior lining — the gold standard for cigar storage. The 3mm rigid greyboard construction with full-perimeter neodymium magnetic seal strip maintains a stable 65–72 % RH internal environment, while the 5mm Spanish cedar (Cedrela odorata) veneer lining naturally regulates humidity, repels tobacco beetles, and imparts the classic cedar aroma that enhances cigar flavor over time. A precision hygrometer slot and humidification element compartment complete the functional design. Custom deep-embossed branding on the lid surface — printed on a KBA Rapida 105 and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV line, then foil-stamped on a Bobst hot-foil press at ±0.15 mm registration — adds unmistakable brand prestige to every box.

Design Features

  • Spanish Cedar Interior: 5mm genuine Cedrela odorata veneer lining — naturally hygroscopic for 65–72 % RH regulation; kiln-dried to ≤ 12 % MC before fitting
  • Magnetic Seal: Full-perimeter neodymium magnetic strip ensures airtight closure with ≥ 1.2 N pull force per linear centimetre for humidity retention
  • Rigid Construction: 3mm greyboard wrapped in 157 gsm C1S art paper for structural integrity and printability
  • Hygrometer Slot: Precision-cut front panel opening for analog or digital hygrometer (not included)
  • Humidification Compartment: Internal slot for humidification element (crystal gel beads, Boveda 69 % pack, or propylene glycol foam)
  • Embossed Lid: Custom deep-embossed logo or pattern (up to 2 mm depth) with gold, silver, or rose-gold metallic foil
  • Cigar Capacity: Standard configuration holds 10–25 coronas; custom tray layouts accommodate up to 50 cigars
  • Divider Options: Removable cedar dividers with finger-pull notches for flexible compartment sizing
