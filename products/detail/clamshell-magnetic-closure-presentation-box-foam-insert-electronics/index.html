Customization Options

  • Board Construction: 2.5mm or 3.0mm greyboard for standard electronics; 1.5mm for lightweight accessories. Double-layer board available for products over 1kg.
  • Wrap Material: 157gsm coated art paper (120–200gsm range); specialty papers (linen-textured, metallic, pearlescent); PU leather wrap for ultra-premium look.
  • Printing: Full-color CMYK offset on KBA 1050; up to 4 Pantone spot colors; Heidelberg 7+1 UV with inline aqueous or UV coating.
  • Surface Finishes: Soft-touch matte lamination (fingerprint-resistant); gloss lamination (color vibrancy); spot UV for contrast; full-coverage spot UV for high-gloss wet look.
  • Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic on Bobst presses — ±0.1mm registration; combination foil+emboss for dimensional foil effects.
  • Magnet System: N52 grade neodymium magnets (strongest commercial grade); 8–12mm diameter discs embedded in board; closure force 1.5–3.0kg; polarity-matched for consistent alignment.
  • Foam Insert: EVA foam (ethylene-vinyl acetate, semi-rigid, excellent shape retention); PE foam (polyethylene, softer, lighter weight); flocked PET tray for jewelry-grade presentation; anti-static foam for ESD-sensitive electronics.
  • Sizing: Fully custom — from compact 120×90×35mm earbud boxes to 350×250×80mm headphone or multi-device presentations.

Quality Assurance

  1. Incoming Material Inspection: Greyboard caliper and grammage verification; magnet grade verification (N52 pull-force test); foam density and Shore hardness check per ASTM D2240.
  2. In-Process Printing: Spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0; Heidelberg Prinect inline registration monitoring.
  3. Magnet Embedding: Magnet polarity orientation verification (100% check); pull-force test on 5% sample; magnet-to-magnet alignment gap ≤ 0.3mm for consistent closure feel.
  4. Die-Cutting & Assembly: ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance on board components; lid-to-base fit clearance 0.5–1.0mm; hinge integrity test — 5,000 open/close cycles without delamination.
  5. Foam Insert QC: Cavity dimensions verified against device CAD with go/no-go gauge; foam density and firmness verified; anti-static foam surface resistivity ≤ 10¹¹ Ω/sq per ANSI/ESD S20.20.
  6. Drop Test: ISTA 1A 10-drop sequence from 760mm — box must protect device from structural damage; foam must retain device after impact.
  7. Finished Goods Audit: AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.0 (minor) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4; full functional test under D65 lighting.
