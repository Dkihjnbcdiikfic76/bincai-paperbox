Velvet watch collector display box with 8 individual watch slots, tempered glass viewing lid, integrated LED lighting, and remote control operation, manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — a Diamond Member on Made-in-China.com with 23 years of manufacturing excellence (founded 2003). The 8-slot layout accommodates a full watch collection with individual suede-lined compartments and removable watch cushions. The tempered glass lid provides dust-free display while the LED strip lighting with remote control offers adjustable brightness and color temperature for optimal presentation. N52 neodymium magnetic closure with lock and key ensures secure storage. Produced across our 18,000 sqm facility (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory, 2025 expansion).
