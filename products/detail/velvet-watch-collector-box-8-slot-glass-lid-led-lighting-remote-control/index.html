Product Overview

This 8-slot velvet watch collector box combines premium display presentation with secure, dust-free storage. Each slot features an individual suede-lined compartment with a removable watch cushion, accommodating watches up to 50mm case diameter. The tempered glass lid offers full collection visibility while protecting against dust and handling. Integrated LED strip lighting with remote control provides adjustable brightness and color temperature — warm white (3000K), cool white (6000K), or RGB modes — for optimal presentation in showroom, retail, or personal collection settings. N52 neodymium magnetic closure with lock-and-key mechanism ensures secure storage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the minimum order quantity? A: Standard MOQ is 100 units. For fully customized configurations with biometric locks, integrated watch winders, or custom Pantone velvet, MOQ is 300–500 units. Rush orders available with surcharge.

Q: Can I get a different number of watch slots? A: Yes — the tray layout is fully customizable. Common configurations include 4, 6, 8, 10, and 12 slots. Mixed slot sizes (e.g., 6 standard + 2 oversized) are also available.

Q: How does the LED lighting work? What power source is needed? A: The LED strip runs on 5V DC via USB-C connection (power adapter included, 100–240V worldwide compatible). Battery-powered version available (3× AA, ~200 hours runtime). Remote control handles on/off, brightness, color temperature, and timer settings.

Q: Can the glass lid be customized with etching or printing? A: Yes — acid-etched brand logos, sandblasted patterns, or UV-printed designs can be applied to the tempered glass lid. Minimum order 200 units for etched glass customization.

Q: How is the box packaged for shipping? A: Each box is individually polybagged with foam corner protectors, then packed in a 5-ply corrugated master carton. The glass lid receives additional 10mm EPE foam sheet protection. ISTA 3A drop-test certified packaging ensures safe arrival.
