Product Overview

Custom folding carton for electronics accessories with precision die-cut insert and full-color CMYK offset printing — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). Engineered for earphones, USB chargers, data cables, power banks, phone cases, screen protectors, smartwatch bands, and small consumer electronics where product protection and retail shelf appeal are equally critical. The flat-packed folding design minimizes shipping volume by up to 80% versus rigid boxes, while the precision die-cut cardboard insert — cut to ±0.3mm tolerance on Bobst SP 102 BMA flatbed die-cutters — secures products against movement during transit and positions them perfectly for retail display. Produced on KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset (15,000 sheets/hour) and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses with inline aqueous or UV coating across our 18,000 sqm dual-factory complex (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory, commissioned 2025) in Foshan, Guangdong. Daily output of 1.7 million boxes. ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody, and Sedex SMETA certified. MOQ 1000 units with free pre-production samples in 5-7 working days.
