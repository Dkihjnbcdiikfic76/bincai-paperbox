Why This Bracelet Display Box? — Differentiated From Standard Jewelry Packaging

Unlike generic jewelry boxes that treat all pieces the same, this design solves the specific challenges of bracelet and bangle retail display:

  • Removable Satin Pillow with Elastic Retention: The satin-covered EVA foam pillow features a built-in elastic strap that holds bangles secure — no rolling, no shifting during transport. Remove the pillow and the customer can try the bangle on without removing it from the box. This is a tactile upgrade over foam inserts where customers must dig out the piece.
  • Magnetic Flip-Lid at 180°: The full-length cloth hinge opens flat to 180°, creating a natural display surface. On a retail counter, the box transforms into a mini presentation podium — the bangle sits elevated on the satin pillow, fully visible, with the lid serving as a branded backdrop.
  • 40+ Velvet Colors for Brand Matching: Whether your brand palette is deep burgundy, millennial pink, or classic black, we match your box to your bracelet collection. Jewelry brands selling in multiple colorways can order matching boxes per collection — the velvet dye lot is documented for reorder consistency.
  • Elastic Strap = One Box Fits Multiple Bracelet Sizes: The adjustable elastic retention strap accommodates bangles from 55mm to 75mm diameter without tooling changes. One SKU covers your entire bracelet and bangle product line — no per-size box inventory complexity.
  • Reusable Display-Grade Construction: Unlike throwaway e-commerce packaging, this box is built for reuse. Customers keep it as a jewelry organizer, extending your brand presence for years. The magnetic snap stays crisp through 5,000+ open/close cycles — tested with N52 neodymium magnets calibrated to 3.5–5.0N pull force.
