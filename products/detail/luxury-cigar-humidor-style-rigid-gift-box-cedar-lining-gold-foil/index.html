Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Raw Material Inspection — greyboard density verification (2.5mm ±0.1), Spanish cedar species authentication (Cedrela odorata), art paper grammage (157gsm ±5%), magnet grade certification (N35)
  2. Pre-Press Proofing — woodgrain color match to physical swatch, gold foil die alignment on Heidelberg registration system, cedar veneer thickness gaugeing (0.6mm ±0.05)
  3. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0 vs master proof, woodgrain pattern consistency across full sheet
  4. Foil Stamping QC — Heidelberg 7+1 UV foil registration ±0.1mm, adhesion tape test (3M 610), 100% visual for foil voids and hot-stamp temperature consistency
  5. Die-Cutting & Assembly — ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance on box panels, cedar veneer glue bond inspection (peel test), magnet polarity verification and recessed flush-mount check
  6. Humidity System Verification — Boveda pack holder fit test, 72-hour RH stability chamber test (65-72% RH maintained), cedar aroma retention under sealed conditions
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — AQL 2.5 Level II per ISO 2859-1, full magnetic closure function test (50 cycles), ISTA 1A drop test on all 6 faces, foil wear test after rubbing 20×, cedar panel integrity under 40°C/80% RH stress

FAQ

Q: Is the Spanish cedar real wood or printed texture?

A: Genuine Spanish cedar (Cedrela odorata) veneer — 0.6mm thick, sourced from FSC-certified Spanish forests. The characteristic aroma is natural, not added fragrance. Each batch is species-verified before production to ensure authenticity. This is the same cedar grade used in premium walk-in humidors.

Q: How long does the cedar aroma last inside the closed box?

A: Under normal storage conditions (20-25°C, sealed box), the cedar aroma remains detectable for 3-5 years. The Boveda humidity pack holder actually helps — maintaining stable 69% RH prevents excessive drying that would accelerate aroma loss. We test cedar aroma retention in a 72-hour sealed chamber as part of our QC Gate 6.

Q: Can you do a genuine wood exterior instead of woodgrain art paper?

A: Yes, we offer real wood veneer exteriors in walnut, oak, mahogany, and sapele — applied to the rigid greyboard base. This adds approximately $2.00-5.00/unit depending on wood species and box size. Woodgrain art paper (our standard offering) delivers 90% of the visual impact at 30% of the cost and is our recommended specification for production runs.

Q: How are magnets installed — are they visible?

A: Magnets are recess-mounted flush into the greyboard thickness before the exterior art paper is applied. They are completely invisible from both exterior and interior. We use N35-grade neodymium magnets (8-12mm diameter, depending on box size) rated for 10,000+ open/close cycles without weakening.

Q: What humidity level should the Boveda pack be?

A: 69% RH is the industry standard for cigar storage and what we recommend. We can accommodate 65% RH or 72% RH Boveda packs on request. The holder cavity is sized for standard Boveda size 8 (60g) packs — enough to maintain humidity for 2-4 months in a sealed box. Replacement packs are widely available from Boveda directly.

Q: Do you provide the Boveda packs with the boxes?

A: We can include Boveda packs as an optional add-on ($1.50-3.00/pack depending on quantity), or you can source them separately and insert during fulfillment. The holder cavity is designed to fit standard Boveda size 8 packs regardless of sourcing.
