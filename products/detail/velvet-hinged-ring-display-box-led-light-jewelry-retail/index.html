FAQ

Q: How long does the LED battery last, and is it replaceable? A: The standard CR2032 coin cell battery provides approximately 200–250 lid-open activations. The battery compartment is accessible from the base of the box — simply slide open the battery door to replace. We include one battery pre-installed and can include spare batteries per your requirements. For high-volume retail applications, we recommend advising end customers to replace batteries every 6 months of regular display use.

Q: Can the velvet color match our brand’s specific shade? A: Yes — we offer Pantone-matched velvet dyeing for orders of 3,000 units or more. For smaller orders, we carry 12 standard velvet colors: black, white, ivory, navy, royal blue, burgundy, dark green, grey, charcoal, blush pink, chocolate brown, and lavender. Fabric swatches are provided with pre-production samples for final color approval.

Q: Does the LED light affect the ring’s gemstone appearance? A: Our LED modules use warm white (2700K) as standard, which enhances diamond brilliance and gold warmth without the harsh blue cast of cool white LEDs. For colored gemstones (sapphire, ruby, emerald), we can adjust to 3000K or 4000K to optimize each stone’s color saturation. A CRI (Color Rendering Index) of 90+ ensures accurate color representation under the LED light.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for this LED ring box? A: MOQ is 500 units for fully custom designs (custom size, custom velvet color, custom LED module). For standard sizes with custom branding only, MOQ can be as low as 300 units. Free pre-production samples are provided in 5–7 days. The LED module adds approximately 2 days to the sampling timeline for electrical testing and certification.
