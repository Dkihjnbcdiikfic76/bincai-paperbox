Black magnetic rigid box for electronics premium packaging — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). Engineered for high-end electronics including headphones, smartwatches, mobile accessories, and premium gadgets. The hidden N52 neodymium magnetic flap closure provides a satisfying tactile unboxing experience while protecting contents during shipping. Each box features 2.5mm precision-wrapped greyboard construction, soft-touch matte lamination for luxury tactile feel, and velvet-flocked or custom die-cut foam interior. Produced on German-engineered KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press (15,000 sheets/hour) and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press (inter-deck UV curing, single-pass CMYK+spot+coating) with Bobst SP 102 BMA foil stamping (±0.15mm registration). Our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility (10,000 sqm main + 8,000 sqm smart factory commissioned 2025) operates fully automatic rigid box production lines with CNC corner wrapping, delivering 220,000+ rigid boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 quality management and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

Customization Options

Each magnetic rigid box is tailored precisely to your product and brand identity:

  • Magnetic Closure: Hidden N52 neodymium magnets — 2, 4, or 6 magnets depending on flap size — engineered for a satisfying snap-close with zero misalignment. N52 grade ensures consistent magnetic force after 10,000+ open/close cycles
  • Surface Finishes: Soft-touch matte lamination (suede-like feel), glossy lamination (high-shine premium), leatherette wrapping (PU or genuine leather texture), or linen-textured paper wrapping (organic luxury) — all with full CMYK + Pantone spot color offset printing
  • Foil Stamping & Embossing: Hot foil stamping in gold, silver, copper, rose gold, or custom Pantone metallics on Bobst SP 102 BMA with ±0.15mm registration. Blind embossing, foil embossing, or deep-deboss for dimensional brand logos
  • Spot UV & Specialty Coatings: Spot UV gloss on matte background for striking visual contrast, soft-touch aqueous coating, or textured soft-feel coating for unique tactile experience
  • Interior Design: Velvet-flocked insert (black, grey, cream, or custom color), precision die-cut foam (EVA, PE, or PU in any density), satin-lined rigid insert, or molded pulp tray with custom cavities for device + accessories + cables + documentation
  • Closure Styles: Hidden magnetic flap (clean, invisible closure), magnetic lid with ribbon pull tab, or magnetic side-opening book-style cover
  • Size Range: Custom dimensions from 80×80×30mm (earbuds) to 350×250×80mm (headphones with accessories)
  • Material Construction: 2.5mm greyboard (1200–1600gsm) as standard, upgradeable to 3.0mm for extra-large boxes. Surface paper 128–200gsm art paper, specialty textured stock, or soft-touch laminated wrap
  • Add-Ons: Die-cut window with PET film for product preview, magnetic product display easel, integrated USB charging cable compartment, NFC tag integration, or serialized holographic security labels

Production Specifications

  • Printing: KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press at 15,000 sheets/hour — industry-leading speed with consistent color reproduction. Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press with inter-deck UV curing for single-pass CMYK + spot + coating production
  • Foil Stamping: Bobst SP 102 BMA hot foil stamper — ±0.15mm registration accuracy for precision metallic branding
  • Rigid Box Assembly: Fully automatic rigid box production line with CNC corner wrapping — ±0.3mm corner precision, consistent panel alignment, and tight wrapping tension
  • Greyboard Construction: 800gsm–2500gsm greyboard for structural rigidity; 2.5mm thickness standard, 3.0mm for heavy-duty applications
  • Surface Paper: 128gsm–200gsm art paper, soft-touch laminated stock, specialty textured papers, or fabric-wrapped covers
  • Magnet Specification: N42 or N52 grade neodymium magnets — 8mm, 10mm, or 12mm diameter with 2–5mm thickness — tested for consistent snap force across production
  • Quality Standards: ISO 9001:2015 quality management system, FSC Chain-of-Custody certified paper sourcing
