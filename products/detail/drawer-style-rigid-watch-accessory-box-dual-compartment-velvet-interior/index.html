Product Overview

Premium drawer-style rigid watch and accessory gift box with dual compartments — designed and manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). This innovative two-tier design combines a top-level watch display platform with a smooth-gliding pull-out drawer for accessories, merging premium presentation with functional storage that watch collectors and luxury brands demand. The top compartment features a custom die-cut EVA or PU foam watch pillow (±0.2mm tolerance) in your choice of velvet, suede, or leatherette lining, while the drawer below accommodates extra straps, bracelet tools, warranty cards, and owner’s manuals. The magnetic slide-lock mechanism uses N42 neodymium magnets for secure closure with a satisfying glide action — tested for 20,000+ open/close cycles with zero degradation. Produced on KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset (15,000 sheets/hour) and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses (inter-deck UV curing, single-pass CMYK+spot+coating) with Bobst SP 102 BMA hot foil stamping at ±0.15mm registration at our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility. CNC corner wrapping ensures ±0.3mm precision at all four corners. Daily output of 220,000+ rigid boxes under ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody, and Sedex SMETA certification.
