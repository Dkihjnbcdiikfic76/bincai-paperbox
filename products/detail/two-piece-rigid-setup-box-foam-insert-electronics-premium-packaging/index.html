FAQ

Q: Can you design the foam insert based on my product dimensions? A: Absolutely. Send us your product’s 3D CAD file (STEP/IGES), a 2D dimension drawing, or a physical sample, and our engineers will design a precision-fit foam cavity with appropriate clearances (typically 0.5–1.0mm tolerance).

Q: What foam densities are available for different protection levels? A: EVA foam ranges from 40 Shore A (soft, for lightweight consumer items) to 80 Shore A (firm, for heavy industrial components). Cross-linked PE foam offers superior rebound for repeated use. We’ll recommend based on your product weight and drop-test requirements.

Q: Can the foam insert hold multiple components with different shapes? A: Yes. CNC routing creates multi-cavity layouts with different depths for each component. We can include finger-pull recesses, accessory compartments, and cable-management channels.

Q: Is the telescopic lid easy to open but secure during shipping? A: Our telescopic fit is precision-engineered with 0.5–1.0mm clearance between lid interior and base exterior. Combined with shrink-wrap or a branded belly band, the box stays securely closed during transit while remaining easy to open for the end user.
