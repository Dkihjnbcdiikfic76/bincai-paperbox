Product Overview

Premium single-watch winder display box combining luxury presentation with precision watch care. The magnetic flip lid opens to reveal an acrylic viewing window and a suede-effect watch cushion mounted on a whisper-quiet Japanese Mabuchi motor. Four programmable rotation modes (650/900/1200/1800 TPD) accommodate all automatic watch movements. The carbon-fiber-textured exterior with soft-touch matte lamination and hidden neodymium magnetic closure creates a sophisticated unboxing experience worthy of luxury timepieces. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) in our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility with 1.7M daily box output capacity.
