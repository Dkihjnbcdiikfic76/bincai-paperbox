Production Specifications

  • Press: KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset at 15,000 sheets/hour for wrapping paper + Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV for premium registered graphics, spot UV, and specialty coatings
  • Board: Rigid greyboard 1200-1600gsm — double-wrapped construction with precision 90° corner formation, board thickness selected by box size for proportional aesthetics and structural integrity
  • Velvet Flocking: Electrostatic flocking process — 1.0-1.5mm nylon fiber length, ≥95% surface coverage density, adhesive curing at 80°C for permanent bond
  • Foil Stamping: Bobst SP 102 BMA automatic foil stamping press — brass die creation from customer artwork, ±0.15mm multi-color foil registration, 3,000 sheets/hour throughput
  • Magnetic Closure: N52-grade neodymium disc magnets (6-10mm diameter, 1-2mm thickness) — recessed-mounted between board layers, invisible from exterior, holding force 0.8-2.5 kg per magnet pair
  • LED Assembly: Pre-wired LED module with magnetic reed switch — inserted during interior assembly between velvet layer and greyboard, battery compartment with screw-secured or magnetic access panel
  • Assembly Line: Automatic rigid box line for board wrapping and corner formation + manual LED integration and interior flocking station + final QC inspection station
  • Daily Capacity: 220,000+ rigid gift boxes, of which 30,000+ are LED-equipped jewelry boxes
  • Lead Time: 12-18 working days for new tooling (die creation + sample approval) / 8-12 working days for repeat orders with existing tooling

Quality Assurance

  • LED Function: 100% illumination test — brightness ≥85% rated lumens, sustained for ≥200 hours continuous operation
  • Switch Reliability: 10,000 open/close activation cycles — zero switch failure, zero intermittent activation, zero delay >0.3 seconds
  • Battery Life Verification: 200-hour minimum tested runtime on CR2032 at 20°C ambient — accelerated aging test equivalent to 2 years shelf life
