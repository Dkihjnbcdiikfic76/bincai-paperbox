Aromatic-preserving folding carton for premium tea packaging — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a 23-year packaging enterprise (founded 2003) serving tea brands across China and internationally. Available with optional aluminum foil inner lining for moisture and aroma barrier, this tea box protects delicate tea leaves from oxidation, UV degradation, and flavor loss. The straight-tuck or auto-lock bottom folding carton ships flat for economical freight and assembles in seconds. Full-color CMYK offset printing on KBA 1050 4-color press with Heidelberg 7+1 UV for metallic inks and spot coatings. Hot foil stamping in gold, silver, or copper creates the elevated aesthetic tea packaging demands. Produced across Bincai’s 18,000 sqm dual factory (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory) in Foshan, Guangdong. ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certified. Daily output 1.7 million boxes. MOQ 500 units.

  • Pyramid tea bag cartons (15/20/30 count) with inner divider
  • Gift tea sets — combination box with multiple tea varieties and tasting notes
  • Matcha powder tins and ceremonial-grade tea presentation
  • Herbal and floral tea blends (chamomile, rooibos, jasmine, chrysanthemum)
  • Tea ceremony accessories and tea ware gift sets
  • Corporate tea gifts and hospitality amenity packaging

Customization Options

  • Box style: straight-tuck end (STE), reverse-tuck end (RTE), auto-lock bottom, crash-lock bottom, or two-piece lid-and-base
  • Material: CCNB 350–450gsm, SBS C1S 15–24pt, or FSC kraft board 300–400gsm
  • Inner lining: aluminum foil laminate (moisture + aroma barrier), glassine paper, or uncoated kraft
  • Printing: CMYK offset on KBA 1050 4-color + Pantone spot colors + metallic gold/silver ink
  • Surface finish: soft-touch matte lamination, gloss lamination, aqueous coating, or spot UV
  • Foil stamping: gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic — on lid, spine, or full wrap
  • Embellishments: embossing, debossing, blind emboss, raised UV spot doming
  • Window: die-cut window with PET, PLA compostable film, or anti-fog film
  • Closure: tuck-flap, magnetic, ribbon-tie, or wax seal string-and-button
  • Interior: cardstock divider, EVA foam insert, molded pulp tray, or satin pouch
  • Size range: 60×40×20mm (sample sachet) to 300×250×100mm (gift tea collection); fully custom

Production Specifications

  • Board: CCNB (clay-coated newsback) 350–450gsm or SBS (solid bleached sulfate) C1S 15–24pt
  • Inner foil lining: 7μm aluminum foil + 12μm PET laminate, heat-sealed seams for aroma lock
  • Printing: KBA 1050 4-color offset for wrap sheets; Heidelberg 7+1 UV for metallic inks and spot coatings
  • Foil stamping: Bobst SP 102 BMA — gold foil 22K imitation, silver matte, holographic; multi-pass for 2-3 colors
  • Die-cutting: Bobst flatbed; carton blank tolerance ±0.15mm; window cut tolerance ±0.3mm
  • Gluing: side-seam cold PVA adhesive; auto-lock bottom hot-melt adhesive for instant assembly
  • Assembly: flat-packed shipping; auto-lock bottom pops open in under 1 second
  • Daily output: up to 200,000 folding cartons per shift
  • Lead time: 10–15 days new die; 7–10 days repeat; 5–7 days rush
