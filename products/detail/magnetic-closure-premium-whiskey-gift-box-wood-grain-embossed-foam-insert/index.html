FAQ

Q: Can the foam insert accommodate different bottle shapes?

A: Yes. Our EVA foam inserts are custom die-cut per design — we work from your bottle’s 3D specifications or a physical sample. Standard cavities fit 750ml standard, Bordeaux, Burgundy, and square-shoulder whiskey bottles. One-time die charge: approximately $60–100 per cavity layout.

Q: Is the wood-grain finish durable enough for retail handling?

A: Absolutely. The wood-grain pattern is formed by an embossing roller under heat and pressure — it is not a printed or applied layer that can peel. The aqueous coating provides additional scratch resistance. Our internal abrasion testing confirms 3,000+ retail-handling touches without visible grain degradation.

Q: Can I get a genuine wood veneer instead of embossed paper?

A: Yes. We offer genuine wood veneer (oak, walnut, cherry, bamboo) as a premium upgrade for orders of 250+ units. Veneer adds approximately $1.80–2.50 per unit and extends production lead time by 3–5 days for veneer sourcing and conditioning.

Q: What glassware fits in the accessory cavity?

A: The standard layout accommodates two Glencairn-style whiskey tasting glasses (65mm diameter × 115mm height) or two standard rocks glasses (80mm × 90mm). Custom cavities for brand-specific glassware, coasters, or accessory items are available with no additional die charge beyond the one-time cavity layout fee.

Q: Does the magnetic closure stay secure during shipping?

A: Yes. The dual N52 magnets provide 2.0kg combined pull force — tested to remain closed through ISTA 1A drop testing (10 drops from 760mm with bottle weight simulator). For extra security during international freight, we offer an optional clear PVC sleeve or paper band that reinforces closure without affecting presentation.
