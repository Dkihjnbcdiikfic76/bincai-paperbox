FAQ

Q: What is the MOQ for this auto-bottom folding carton?

A: Standard MOQ is 1,000 units. For first-time sample orders we can accommodate 500 units to help you evaluate quality before committing to production volumes.

Q: Can the auto-bottom mechanism be changed to a different closure style?

A: Yes. We also offer tuck-top with crash-lock bottom, straight tuck, reverse tuck, and seal-end styles. The auto-bottom is recommended for cosmetic filling lines because it assembles in under 3 seconds per box without adhesive.

Q: What foil colors are available for hot stamping?

A: Gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic, and matte gold. We can also source custom foil colors to match your brand’s Pantone. Our Bobst hot foil press achieves ±0.15 mm registration accuracy.

Q: Is the PET window available in eco-friendly alternatives?

A: Yes. We offer biodegradable PLA (polylactic acid) film as an alternative to traditional PET. PLA is compostable under industrial conditions and provides the same optical clarity. We also offer FSC-certified paperboard windows for fully recyclable packaging.

Q: What is the production lead time?

A: Standard lead time is 12-20 days depending on order volume. Pre-production samples ship in 5-7 business days. Rush orders can be accommodated at 10 days for repeat orders with existing tooling.
