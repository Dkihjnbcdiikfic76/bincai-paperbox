FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ), and is it flexible for startups? A: Standard MOQ is 1,000 units per SKU. For startups and Kickstarter brands testing eco packaging, we offer a trial run of 500 units at a 15-20% surcharge to cover tooling setup. Orders above 5,000 units qualify for volume pricing tiers that reduce unit cost by 10-25%.

Q: How does soy-based ink compare to petroleum-based ink for kraft packaging? A: Soy-based ink contains 20-25% soy oil vs. 0% renewable content in petroleum ink. It produces sharper dot gain on uncoated kraft (30% finer detail at 150 LPI), generates 80% fewer VOC emissions during printing, and is 3× easier to de-ink during paper recycling — making the entire box truly circular. Color gamut is comparable to petroleum ink on kraft substrates.

Q: Is the PLA window film really compostable? Under what conditions? A: Yes — our PLA film is certified to EN 13432 and ASTM D6400. It achieves 90% disintegration within 12 weeks and 90% biodegradation within 6 months under industrial composting conditions (58°C ± 2°C, controlled moisture). It will not biodegrade in a home compost bin or landfill. We provide certificates of compostability with every shipment for your brand’s sustainability claims.

Q: What is the lead time for custom die-cut shapes or structural designs? A: Tooling design and CAD approval takes 3-5 business days. Die fabrication requires 5-7 days. First article samples ship within 5-7 days after die completion. Full production runs complete in 15-25 days depending on quantity. Total from concept to delivery: 28-44 days for a brand-new custom shape. Re-orders of existing dies ship in 15-20 days.

Q: What is the minimum order for custom tooling (new die-cut or emboss plate)? A: There is no separate minimum — tooling cost is amortized into the per-unit price at 1,000+ units. A standard die costs $150-300 (one-time); a deboss/emboss plate runs $80-200 depending on complexity. Dies are retained at our factory for 2 years for free re-orders. If your annual volume exceeds 50,000 units, tooling is complimentary.
