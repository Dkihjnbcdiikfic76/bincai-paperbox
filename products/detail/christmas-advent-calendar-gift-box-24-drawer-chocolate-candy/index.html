Product Overview

Our Christmas advent calendar gift box is a premium 24-drawer countdown packaging solution for chocolates, candies, mini toys, cosmetics, and spirits samplers. Each numbered drawer pulls out smoothly to reveal a compartment sized for truffles, pralines, or miniature products. The rigid outer shell — constructed from 2.5mm greyboard wrapped in 157gsm C1S art paper — maintains structural integrity through the full 24-day countdown, while festive CMYK printing with gold foil accents creates an irresistible holiday retail display. Outer graphics are printed on our KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press for rich, vibrant Pantone-matched colours, and drawer wraps run on the Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV line for instant-cure gloss and scratch resistance. All drawer blanks are precision-cut on the Bobst SP 102 BMA flatbed die-cutter with ±0.15mm registration for consistent slide-fit across all 24 positions.

Design Features

  • 24 Numbered Drawers: Individual pull-out compartments numbered 1–24 with custom drawer knob, ribbon-pull, or finger-notch options
  • Rigid Outer Frame: 2.5mm greyboard construction withstands daily use without warping or bowing
  • Festive Printing: Full CMYK offset on KBA Rapida 105 with Christmas motifs — snowflakes, reindeer, winter village, nutcracker, or fully custom designs
  • Gold Foil Accents: Hot foil stamping on numbers, borders, and brand logo for a premium holiday shelf presence
  • UV Spot Varnish: Optional selective UV gloss on Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV for tactile contrast effects
  • Custom Drawer Size: Standard 40×40×30mm compartments, customizable up to 60×60×50mm for larger items
  • Flat-Pack Option: Ships knocked-down for assembly at destination, reducing international freight costs by up to 60%
  • Food-Safe Interior: FDA-compliant food-grade paper liners available for direct chocolate contact
