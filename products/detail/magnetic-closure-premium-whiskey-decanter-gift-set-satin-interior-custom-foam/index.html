Seven-Stage QC Gates

  2. Pre-press color verification — Pantone matching, registration proof, foil stamping die check

  4. Post-forming structural inspection — hinge alignment, lid fit, corner squareness
  5. Magnetic closure verification — pull force measurement, alignment check, cycle testing
  6. Interior assembly inspection — foam insert fit, satin adhesion, edge finish
  7. Final audit per AQL 2.5 Level II — full product assembly and unboxing simulation

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity? MOQ starts at 300 units. We offer trial runs of 150 units for new brand launches at a small premium.

Can you accommodate custom foam for unique decanter shapes? Yes. We CNC-cut EVA foam inserts to your exact specifications using 3D CAD data or a physical sample of your decanter.

How are the magnets installed? Neodymium magnets are embedded within the greyboard wall during assembly — invisible from the exterior and interior for a seamless premium finish.

Is this box suitable for e-commerce shipping? Yes. We recommend an additional corrugated outer shipper for direct-to-consumer e-commerce. The magnetic box itself is designed as the primary presentation packaging.

What is the typical production lead time? Standard production lead time is 18-25 working days after sample approval. Rush orders are available at 12-15 days.
