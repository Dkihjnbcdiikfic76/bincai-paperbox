FAQ

Q: Is the bamboo fiber tray truly home compostable, or does it require industrial facilities?

A: The bamboo fiber tray is certified to both EN 13432 (industrial composting) and OK Compost HOME (TÜV Austria) — meaning it will biodegrade in a home compost pile within 6–12 months under typical backyard conditions (ambient temperature, natural humidity, microbial activity). Unlike PLA which requires 58°C industrial composting, bamboo fiber breaks down at ambient temperatures. The tray is made from bamboo fiber (a rapidly renewable grass, not a tree) bonded with a plant-based binder — no synthetic polymers. After use, the entire box (kraft body + bamboo tray) can be placed in home compost or paper recycling.

Q: How does the bamboo tray compare to a traditional plastic or foam insert?

A: Bamboo fiber trays offer comparable structural performance with dramatically better environmental credentials. Weight: similar to EVA foam, lighter than rigid plastic. Strength: passes ≥15N bend test — adequate for typical gift items (tech gadgets, skincare bottles, gourmet food jars). Feel: warm, natural texture similar to molded pulp egg cartons but smoother and more refined. Appearance: natural light tan color that complements kraft packaging beautifully. The key trade-off: bamboo fiber is slightly less precise than injection-molded plastic (±0.5mm vs ±0.1mm tolerance), but for gift packaging applications this is imperceptible to the end user.

Q: Can the bamboo tray be custom-colored to match our brand?

A: Yes, using plant-based dyes only (to maintain compostability certification). Available colors include: natural (light tan, most popular), charcoal (bamboo charcoal), terracotta (mineral pigment), sage green (chlorophyll-based), and indigo (plant-based). Custom Pantone matching is not available for the tray as synthetic pigments would invalidate the compostability certification. However, the kraft exterior can be printed with your full brand colors using soy-based inks, with the natural bamboo tray providing an earthy, authentic contrast.

Q: What’s the minimum order and can we brand the box with our corporate logo?

A: MOQ is 500 units for standard sizes with existing tray tooling. Custom tray cavities require new mold fabrication (MOQ 1,000 units, mold cost approximately $300–500 depending on complexity). The box exterior can be printed with your full corporate branding — logo, pattern, sustainability message — using soy-based CMYK printing. We can also add a laser-engraved bamboo brand plate attached to the box lid or embedded in the tray. Free pre-production samples are provided within 7–10 working days.

Q: Is the box suitable for international shipping of corporate gifts?

A: Yes. The box passes ISTA 1A drop testing and the magnetic closure with reinforced kraft exterior provides adequate protection for international courier shipping. For fragile items (glass bottles, ceramics), the bamboo fiber tray provides shock absorption similar to molded pulp. For high-value gifts shipping internationally, we recommend adding a kraft paper outer shipping carton (also FSC-certified and recyclable) for additional crush protection during transit.
