FAQ

Q: Can the foam tray hold irregularly shaped items like USB drives, pens, and brochures together?

A: Yes. Our in-house die-making team designs custom EVA foam cavities for any combination of items. We work from your product dimensions or physical samples. Cavity tolerances are ±1mm and foam density is selected based on item fragility.

Q: How strong are the magnets — will the box stay closed during shipping?

A: Each N52 neodymium magnet pair provides ≥8N pull force. With 2 pairs (standard), the lid requires deliberate effort to open — it will not accidentally open during shipping or handling. The closure is tested through 10,000+ open/close cycles with no loss of holding force.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity?

A: MOQ is 500 units per design. Sample turnaround is 5-7 days with free pre-production samples. For simpler designs without foam inserts, we can accommodate 300-unit trial orders.

Q: Can the exterior be wrapped in leatherette or fabric instead of paper?

A: Yes. We offer leatherette (PU), linen-textured paper, and specialty fabric wraps. Leatherette options include 50+ colors and textures. Fabric wrapping is hand-applied and adds 2-3 days to production lead time.

Q: How long does production take?

A: Standard lead time is 10-14 days after artwork and foam tooling approval. Rush orders can be expedited to 7 days. Foam tooling for first-time designs adds 3-5 days.
