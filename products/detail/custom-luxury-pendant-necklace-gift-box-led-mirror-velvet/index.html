Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does the LED light last, and can customers replace the battery? A: The CE-certified LED module runs on a standard CR2032 coin battery with 50+ hours of continuous runtime (equivalent to thousands of openings). The magnetic reed switch activates the LED only when the lid opens past 25–35°, preventing battery drain during storage. The battery compartment is accessible (with small Phillips screwdriver) for customer replacement, and we include a spare battery in bulk orders. For brands preferring sealed units, we offer a non-replaceable 100+ hour extended-life module.

Q: Does the velvet flocking shed or transfer color onto jewelry? A: No. Our electrostatic velvet flocking uses 0.8–1.2mm nylon fibers bonded with hot-melt adhesive at 160–180°C. We test every batch with a Martindale abrasion test (500 cycles, 9kPa pressure) and colorfastness to rubbing (ISO 105-X12) — no shedding and no color transfer. This is critical for white gold, silver, and pearl jewelry where any fiber contamination would be unacceptable. Many luxury jewelry brands have used our boxes for 5+ years without shedding complaints.

Q: What pendant/necklace sizes can this box accommodate? A: Our standard interior accommodates pendants up to 40mm diameter and chains up to 50cm length (princess). We offer custom sizing for: choker (35–40cm), matinee (50–60cm), opera (70–85cm), and rope/lariat (90–120cm). For multi-chain configurations or necklace+earring sets, we add additional satin-grooved slots and earring card inserts. Each design is prototyped with your actual jewelry to ensure perfect fit.

Q: Can the LED mirror box be shipped internationally? Are there battery restrictions? A: Yes. We’ve shipped LED jewelry boxes to 50+ countries. For air freight and express courier (DHL/UPS/FedEx), CR2032 lithium coin cells are permitted under IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations (Section II, PI 968–970) when properly packaged. We insert an insulating pull-tab that customers remove on first use (prevents activation during transit). Full shipping documentation and MSDS provided. For sea freight, no restrictions apply.

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity and can I get prototypes first? A: MOQ is 500 units per design for standard configurations. For first-time buyers, we offer a prototype program: 5–10 hand-assembled samples in your exact specifications (materials, colors, foil, LED, mirror) delivered in 7–10 business days. This lets you validate the unboxing experience, photograph for marketing, and get stakeholder approval before committing to full production. Sample cost is credited against your production order.

Q: How does the LED + mirror box compare to plain velvet boxes in terms of perceived value? A: Our jewelry brand clients consistently report 25–40% higher perceived value ratings when using LED+mirror boxes vs. plain velvet boxes in consumer testing. The interactive lighting moment — opening the box and seeing the pendant sparkle under the LED spotlight with the vanity mirror reflection — creates an emotional response that plain packaging cannot match. For e-commerce brands, this unboxing moment drives social media sharing (Instagram/TikTok unboxing videos), generating organic marketing reach. The per-unit cost premium ($0.80–1.50 over plain velvet) typically yields 5–8x ROI through reduced return rates and increased brand loyalty.

Q: Do you offer repair or replacement for LED failures? A: Yes. All LED modules are individually tested before assembly (Gate 5) and again during final QC (Gate 6). Our defect rate is below 0.3%. However, we provide a 1% overage on LED components in every shipment (e.g., 10 spare LED modules per 1,000 boxes). For any reported LED issues, we ship replacement modules via express courier at no cost. CE certification and RoHS compliance ensure the electronics meet international safety standards.
