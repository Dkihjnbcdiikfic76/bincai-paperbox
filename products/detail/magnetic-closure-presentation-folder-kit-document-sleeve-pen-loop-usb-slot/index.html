Product Overview

The Bincai Magnetic Closure Presentation Folder Kit transforms corporate materials into a branded experience. Built on a 2.0mm greyboard rigid base wrapped in premium art paper with gold foil-embossed logo, this A4-sized folder opens via a satisfying magnetic flap closure to reveal: a clear PET document sleeve for brochures, an elastic pen loop holding a custom-branded pen, a die-cut USB drive slot, and a business card pocket. Every component is precisely positioned within the rigid folder structure — no shifting during transport. Manufactured at our Foshan facility with 23 years of premium rigid box expertise. FAQ

Q: What’s the MOQ for custom corporate presentation folders?

A: 500 units minimum. For multi-component kits with custom die-cut insert trays, the MOQ is typically 1,000 units to amortize tooling costs. We offer flexibility for pilot projects — contact us with your requirements.

Q: Can you include our branded pen, USB drive, or other accessories?

A: Yes. We accept client-supplied accessories and integrate them into the die-cut tray during assembly. Alternatively, we can source branded accessories through our partner network. All inserted items are verified at QC checkpoint for fit and presentation.

Q: What’s the lead time for a custom presentation folder kit?

A: Design and sampling: 7-10 working days (includes CAD structural design + foil stamping die). Production: 15-20 working days for standard orders. Rush 10-day service available. Repeat orders: 7-10 working days.

Q: Can you match our exact corporate brand color?

A: Yes. Our G7 Master Color certified workflow reproduces Pantone colors to ΔE ≤ 2.0. We digitally proof all colors before production. Heidelberg foil registration at ±0.1mm ensures your logo appears exactly where designed.

Q: What sizes are available beyond A4?

A: A4 (210×297mm) is standard. We also manufacture A5, US Letter (216×279mm), US Legal (216×356mm), and fully custom dimensions. Multi-panel gate-fold and accordion layouts are available for complex presentation needs.
