Product Overview

Our custom magnetic closure fountain pen display box wraps premium greyboard in elegant faux leather, creating a sophisticated presentation befitting luxury writing instruments. The book-style lid opens on a hidden magnetic closure to reveal a plush suede-lined interior with a precision die-cut pen tray — cradling each fountain pen, rollerball, or ballpoint in its own sculpted recess. The faux leather exterior is available in classic black, rich cognac brown, deep navy, or custom colors with optional debossing and hot foil stamping for brand logos. Manufactured in our 10,000 sqm ISO 9001-certified facility with Heidelberg 7+1 UV printing for any interior branding, each box reflects Bincai’s 23 years of premium packaging expertise.

FAQ

Q: Can the pen tray be customized for different pen sizes? A: Yes — we create a custom die-cut template matching your exact fountain pen, rollerball, or ballpoint dimensions. Common configurations: single pen, dual pen, triple pen, or 6-pen collector set. We keep your custom die on file for reorders at no additional tooling charge.

Q: What faux leather colors and textures are available? A: 20+ colors including classic black, cognac brown, navy, burgundy, charcoal grey, and emerald green. Textures range from smooth calfskin to pebbled grain and carbon fiber pattern. Custom PMS-matched faux leather is available for orders above 3,000 units.

Q: Can I add custom branding beyond debossing? A: Yes — combine debossing with hot foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic) on the exterior lid, spine, or interior. Screen printing, UV digital printing, and custom foil-stamped satin ribbon pulls are also available. Multi-technique branding creates a truly bespoke presentation.

Q: What is the sample turnaround time? A: Free pre-production samples ship within 5-7 business days of artwork approval. Samples include your custom pen tray, selected faux leather color, debossing/foil branding, and suede lining color. Expedited 3-day samples available for time-sensitive projects.

Q: How durable is the magnetic closure over time? A: The N35+ neodymium magnets are tested to 5,000 open/close cycles without measurable force degradation. Unlike mechanical clasps, magnetic closures have no wearing parts — the hold force remains consistent for the lifetime of the box.
