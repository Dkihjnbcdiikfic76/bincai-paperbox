Product Overview

The Bincai Velvet Jewelry Travel Case solves the universal jewelry traveler’s dilemma: how to carry multiple pieces securely without tangling, scratching, or losing small items. Designed with a full-wrap YKK zipper closure, elastic retention straps, and a padded center divider, this compact case organizes necklaces, rings, earrings, and bracelets in one portable package.

Manufacturing Excellence

Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — an ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified factory with 23 years of expertise since 2003 — each travel case undergoes 12-point QC inspection including zipper cycle testing (2,000+ open/close), velvet abrasion resistance (Martindale 50,000+ rubs), and compartment fitment verification. Custom logos are hot-stamped using the Bobst SP 102 BMA foil stamping press with ±0.15mm registration accuracy, while printed art paper wraps are produced on the KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press at 15,000 sheets/hour or the Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV with inter-deck UV curing for instant-dry special finishes.

Our 18,000 sqm dual Foshan facility (10,000 sqm main plant + 8,000 sqm smart factory commissioned in 2025) produces 1.7 million color boxes + 220,000 rigid gift boxes daily, with automated guided vehicles and inline camera QC systems ensuring consistent quality at scale. MOQ starts at just 500 units with free pre-production samples including full zipper and elastic function testing, shipped within 7–10 working days.
