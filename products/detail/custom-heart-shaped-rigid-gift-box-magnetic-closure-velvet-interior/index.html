Production Workflow

  1. Design & CAD Engineering — 3D structural design in ArtiosCAD; heart contour geometry with lid/base clearance 0.5mm; magnet pocket positions calculated for balanced holding force; CTP plate output on Kodak Magnus VLF
  2. Board Preparation — 2.5mm greyboard (2000gsm equivalent) die-cut to heart silhouette blank on Bobst Autoplaten flatbed die-cutter; kerf tolerance ±0.15mm; 157gsm C1S art paper PVA cold-glued to greyboard; mitered corner wrap ±0.3mm; 24-hour curing
  3. Offset Printing — CMYK on KBA 1050 4-color press; aqueous coating base; optional soft-touch coating or matte/gloss lamination on automatic sheet laminator; D65 light booth color verification
  4. Hot Foil & Embellishment — Gold/silver/rose gold/copper foil stamping on Heidelberg Speedmaster XL 7+1 UV; embossing/debossing at 0.6–0.8mm depth with custom magnesium die; spot UV registration within ±0.15mm; single-pass foil + emboss capability
  5. Velvet Flocking — Electrostatic flocking machine; 6-denier × 0.5mm nylon fiber; water-based polyurethane adhesive; curing at 60°C × 30 min; heart-shaped cavity die-cut to ±0.3mm; color options: red, black, cream, navy, burgundy, custom PMS
  6. Magnet Embedding — 2× NdFeB N35 neodymium magnets (Ø10mm × 2.5mm) embedded in front flap and mating base, polarity-matched and nickel-plated; magnet pockets CNC-routed into greyboard; epoxy-fixed
  7. Ribbon & Hardware — 20mm satin ribbon color-matched to interior velvet; grosgrain or organza options; ribbon feed-through channel die-cut in base; optional metal corner protectors or leather handle
  8. QC Gate — Shape contour overlay check, magnet pull-force gauge (100% inline), velvet cross-hatch adhesion, foil registration optical comparator, board rigidity Taber test per batch, ISTA 1A drop test per batch, D65 color verification
  9. Packing & Shipping — Individually poly-bagged; flat-packed or nested in corrugated master cartons (24–48 units); fumigation-free palletization; DHL/FedEx (3–5 day) or sea freight (15–25 day)

FAQ

Q: What’s the smallest and largest heart shape you can produce? A: Our heart-shaped rigid boxes range from 100mm × 100mm (compact jewelry/pendant size) up to 400mm × 400mm (large gift/chocolate presentation). For sizes outside this range, our CAD team can evaluate feasibility — contact us with your specifications.

Q: How consistent is the heart shape across thousands of units? A: We maintain ±0.5mm contour deviation across an entire production batch via optical comparator overlay inspection at 1:50 sampling. Our Bobst flatbed die-cutter uses hardened steel-rule dies that maintain edge sharpness for 50,000+ impressions before re-tooling, ensuring consistent cut quality from first unit to last.

Q: Can I have a window cutout in the heart-shaped lid? A: Yes. We can incorporate a die-cut window (with PET film insert) in the lid to show the product while maintaining the heart silhouette. The window shape can follow the heart contour for aesthetic consistency. Window boxes require additional structural engineering to maintain rigidity — our CAD team will optimize the design for your product weight and size.
