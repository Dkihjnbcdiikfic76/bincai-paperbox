Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Raw Material Inspection — Greyboard density and caliper, art paper grammage, velvet flocking pile density, magnet grade (N52 verification), ribbon tensile strength, foil color match
  2. Pre-Press & Die Engineering — Heart-shape die profile verification by optical comparator, foil stamping die alignment to curved surface, color calibration to Pantone references
  3. In-Process Printing — Spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0, Heidelberg Prinect inline defect detection
  4. Die-Cutting Inspection — ±0.3mm tolerance on compound curves, lid-to-base match test (every 50th unit measured), edge quality (no crushing or burring on curved profile)
  5. Interior Assembly QC — Velvet flocking uniformity (pile density, color consistency), EVA foam insert fit, LED module function (if equipped), glue bond integrity
  6. Final Assembly & Finishing — Magnet polarity verification (100% check), foil alignment to curved surface, ribbon pull test (50 cycles), lid-to-base closure gap ≤ 0.5mm
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — AQL random sample per ISO 2859-1 Level II, full function test including magnet cycle, drop test per ISTA 1A, cosmetic inspection under D65 and TL84 lighting

Why Bincai?

Special-shape boxes — especially compound-curve heart shapes — demand die-cutting expertise that many packaging factories lack. The heart profile requires a custom-engineered die with precise curve transitions, and the lid and base must be cut as matched pairs to within ±0.3mm for seamless closure. Bincai’s 23 years of die-cutting experience on Bobst SP 102-E Autoplatens and our in-house die fabrication shop mean we can produce custom heart shapes (as well as stars, hexagons, triangles, and fully custom silhouettes) with the same precision as our standard rectangular boxes.

The Bincai Difference:

  • Compound Curve Expertise — Heart shapes involve continuous curve radius changes — not simple arcs. Our die engineers optimize steel rule placement and rubber ejection density specifically for each curve transition, eliminating the edge crushing common on lower-quality heart boxes
  • In-House Die Shop — Custom heart profiles designed and fabricated in 3–5 days, eliminating the 2–3 week lead time when outsourcing die production to third-party toolmakers
  • Magnetic Closure Engineering — N52 magnets precisely positioned using CAD-optimized placement templates ensure consistent snap force across the entire heart curve — no weak spots at the apex or lobe transitions
  • Romantic Packaging Program — Beyond heart-shaped boxes, we offer coordinating ribbon bows, proposal-message hang tags, LED-lit ring platforms, and matching tissue paper for a complete romantic unboxing experience

FAQ

Q: Can you create heart shapes in different proportions — taller, wider, or more angular?

A: Yes. Our standard heart die is a classic symmetrical shape (equal width and height), but we can adjust the proportions to your preference — taller and narrower for an elegant elongated heart, wider for a more traditional Valentine’s silhouette, or more angular with defined lobe points for a modern look. Custom proportion adjustments require a new die (3–5 days fabrication, no charge for production orders). We provide 3D renderings of your custom heart profile before die fabrication so you can approve the exact silhouette.

Q: How does the magnetic closure work on a curved heart shape?

A: We position N52 neodymium magnets at 2–4 strategic points along the heart curve (depending on box size) using CAD-optimized placement. For a 180mm heart box, two magnets are typically placed at the top lobes and one at the bottom apex. The magnets are recessed into the greyboard and covered by the interior lining, so they’re invisible to the user. Closure force is consistent across the entire heart profile — the lid snaps shut with a satisfying feel regardless of where pressure is applied.

Q: Can you add LED lighting inside the heart box for ring proposals?

A: Absolutely — this is one of our most requested features. We integrate a warm white LED spotlight (2700K) powered by a user-replaceable CR2032 battery module. The LED illuminates automatically when the box opens (magnetic reed switch trigger) and turns off when closed. The LED platform is positioned to spotlight the ring or jewelry at the center of the heart. For an even more dramatic effect, we offer a 360° rotating platform option with the LED spotlight.

Q: What’s the smallest heart box you can produce for a single ring?

A: Our minimum practical size is approximately 100×100×40mm — large enough to hold a standard ring box insert but small enough to fit in a jacket pocket (the ultimate proposal-ready size). At this scale, we use a single magnetic closure point and a delicate 10mm satin ribbon pull. The die-cutting precision is maintained at ±0.2mm for these miniature boxes, requiring our most experienced die-cutting operators.

Q: Is velvet flocking available in custom colors to match our brand?

A: Yes. We stock velvet flocking in 20+ colors including classic red, black, white, navy, burgundy, champagne, and rose gold. For custom brand colors, we can color-match flocking to your Pantone specification with a 500-unit minimum and 5–7 additional days lead time. The flocking pile is consistent at 0.5mm height with uniform color density — verified by spectrophotometer and visual inspection under D65 lighting for every batch."
