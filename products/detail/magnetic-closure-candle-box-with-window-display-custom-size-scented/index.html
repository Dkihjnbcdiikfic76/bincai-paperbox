Magnetic Closure Candle Box with Window Display

Premium custom magnetic closure candle box with window display by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — a 23-year ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified packaging enterprise (founded 2003). Our magnetic closure candle boxes combine rigid 2.0mm greyboard construction with a precision die-cut clear PET or anti-fog PVC window, giving customers a direct, unobstructed view of your scented candles while the N52 neodymium magnetic closure delivers a satisfying premium unboxing experience — rated for 5,000+ open/close cycles without strength degradation. The window-to-board bonding uses ultrasonic or solvent-free adhesive with 72-hour peel testing to ensure zero delamination through temperature cycling and humidity exposure. Available with soft-touch matte lamination, hot foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold, copper, holographic), blind embossing, and custom die-cut EVA foam inserts for multi-candle gift sets. Produced on German-engineered KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses with Bobst flatbed die-cutting across 18,000 sqm of factory space (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory, expanded 2025). Daily output of 1.7 million color boxes plus 220,000+ rigid gift boxes. MOQ 500 with free pre-production samples in 5–7 working days.
