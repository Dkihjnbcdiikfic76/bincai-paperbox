Product Overview

Rigid telescopic two-piece flat-pack gift box engineered for wholesale bulk shipping efficiency. The patented flat-pack score design allows the lid and base to ship completely flat — reducing shipping volume by up to 70% compared to pre-assembled rigid boxes — while assembling in seconds at the destination with no glue, tools, or skilled labor required. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) in our 18,000 sqm Foshan dual-factory complex (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory). The 2.0mm greyboard construction with 157gsm art paper wrapping delivers the same premium rigid-box feel and durability as fully assembled boxes at a fraction of the freight cost. Daily output of 1.7 million boxes under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.
