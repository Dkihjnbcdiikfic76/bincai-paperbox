FAQ

Q: How long do the LED batteries last and are they replaceable? A: The CR2032 battery provides 50+ hours of continuous illumination. It’s user-replaceable via a slide-out battery compartment on the box base — no tools required. We include a spare battery in every order. For bulk orders, we can pre-install batteries or ship them separately per your preference.

Q: Can the LED be set to a different color temperature? A: Yes. Standard is warm white 2700K, ideal for gold and warm-toned jewelry. Cool white 6000K is available for silver, platinum, and diamond presentations. RGB LED (upgrade option) supports 16 million colors via remote control for multi-brand display environments.

Q: How are the LED electronics protected during shipping? A: Each box undergoes ISTA 1A drop testing (10 drops from 760mm) with the LED system installed. The reed switch is shock-resistant and the battery compartment has a locking retainer. We apply a conformal coating to all solder joints for humidity protection during ocean freight.

Q: What is the minimum quantity for custom window shapes? A: Standard shapes (rectangle, circle, oval) are available from MOQ 500. Custom die-cut window shapes (logo silhouette, heart, star) require MOQ 2,000 to cover custom die tooling cost.

Q: Can you include both a pendant cavity and earring slots? A: Yes. The die-cut velvet insert can accommodate any combination — pendant + earrings, pendant + chain channel, brooch + cufflinks, etc. Provide your product dimensions and we’ll design a precision-fit layout. Sample turn time: 5–7 days.
