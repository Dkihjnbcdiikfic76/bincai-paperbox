Product Overview

This custom printed rigid box combines the tactile prestige of embossed logo detailing with the brilliance of gold foil stamping — a premium packaging format trusted by luxury brands worldwide. Rigid set-up boxes (also called “hard boxes” or “turn-edge boxes”) are constructed by wrapping printed paper over dense greyboard, creating a substantial, non-collapsible structure that conveys quality from the first touch. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing, every box passes through 15 precision QC checkpoints across our 18,000 sqm integrated facility. With Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses for exceptional color fidelity, KBA 1050 offset for volume production, and over 23 years of rigid box expertise (founded 2003), Bincai delivers over 220,000+ gift boxes daily — all under ISO 9001:2015 quality management and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

Quality Control — 15 Checkpoints

Every rigid box passes through this 15-stage QC gate system:

  1. Material Inspection — Greyboard caliper ±0.1mm, paper GSM verification
  2. Pre-Press Proof — Color bars, registration marks, foil alignment check
  3. Print Color — ΔE ≤ 2.0 spectrophotometer reading against approved proof
  4. Foil Adhesion — Tape test per ASTM D3359, zero peel
  5. Emboss Registration — ±0.2mm alignment to printed artwork
  6. Lamination Bond — ≥2.5 N/cm² peel strength, zero bubbling
  7. Board Cutting — Dimensional tolerance ±0.3mm
  8. Grooving/Scoring — Channel depth within 0.1mm of target
  9. Wrapping Tension — Wrinkle-free, edge fold ±0.5mm tolerance
  10. Magnet Bond — Pull force ≥8N, zero displacement
  11. Interior Fit — Insert flush with walls, gap ≤ 1mm
  12. Lid Alignment — Overhang uniform within ±0.5mm all sides
  13. Hinge Test — 500 open/close cycles without delamination
  14. AQL Sampling — ISO 2859-1, Level II, AQL 2.5 for major defects
  15. Pack-Out Audit — Final 100% visual check before carton sealing
