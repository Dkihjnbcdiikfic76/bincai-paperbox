Product Overview

The Two-Piece Rigid Gift Box with Embossed Linen-Texture Wrap combines tactile sophistication with practical elegance. The telescopic lid and base system — precision-cut to a 3mm overlap tolerance — ensures a smooth, satisfying fit that speaks quality before the recipient even sees the contents. Wrapped in imported linen-embossed art paper from Fedrigoni (Italy) or Gmund (Germany), the texture invites touch — natural linen, silk weave, leather grain, sand finish, and 8 additional patterns create a sensory unboxing experience that flat-printed boxes cannot match. Dual satin ribbon pulls on the base allow effortless lid removal without tilting or shaking the contents, making it ideal for delicate items like silk scarves, hand-rolled chocolates, or fine stationery.

FAQ

Q: What’s the difference between embossing and debossing on linen texture? A: Embossing raises the design above the surface; debossing presses it into the paper. On our linen-textured wraps, both techniques are available. Blind debossing (no foil) is particularly striking on textured papers — the pressed-in design creates subtle shadow play that changes with lighting angle. Multi-level debossing (up to 3mm depth) allows for intricate sculptural effects.

Q: Can I mix textures — e.g., linen-wrap lid with leather-grain base? A: Yes — our two-piece construction allows independent material selection for lid and base. Popular combinations include linen-texture lid with smooth matte base, or leather-grain lid with silk-weave base. No additional tooling cost for different wrap papers on lid vs. base.

Q: What are the standard turnaround times? A: Standard production: 12–15 business days from artwork approval. Custom embossing plate fabrication adds 5–7 business days. Rush production (7–10 business days) available with a 25% surcharge. Sampling: 5–7 business days for stock textures, 10–12 days for custom.

Q: Is the ribbon pull removable for a cleaner look? A: Yes — the ribbon pull can be omitted entirely (friction-fit lid), replaced with a finger notch cutout, or upgraded to a concealed magnetic assist that pushes the base up when the lid is removed. Each option has different MOQ implications; please discuss with our sales team.
