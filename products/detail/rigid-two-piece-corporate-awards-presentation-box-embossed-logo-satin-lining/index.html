Product Overview

This rigid two-piece corporate awards presentation box delivers the gravitas that prestigious recognition deserves. The telescopic lid-and-base construction creates a dramatic unboxing moment — lift the lid to reveal the award nestled in a precision-cut foam cradle against a backdrop of premium satin. The lid exterior features your organization’s logo in raised blind embossing or gold foil, communicating prestige before the box is even opened. Designed for corporate awards, employee recognition plaques, achievement medals, crystal trophies, and ceremonial gifts, this box transforms any award into a memorable presentation experience. Produced by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing — manufacturing premium packaging since 2003 — each box uses 2.5mm FSC-certified greyboard with 200gsm art paper wrap and precision die-cut EVA foam inserts. Our 18,000 sqm facility with daily output of 220,000+ rigid boxes ensures consistent quality at any volume. The two-piece rigid construction provides superior protection during shipping and creates a permanent storage case that recipients will keep long after the ceremony.

Features

  • Telescopic Two-Piece: Separate lid and base with precise fit for smooth telescoping action
  • Embossed Logo: Blind emboss (raised without color) or foil-stamped emboss on lid surface
  • Satin Interior: Premium satin lining in lid and base for luxurious presentation backdrop
  • Die-Cut Foam Insert: CNC-routed EVA foam insert custom-shaped to the award item
  • Rigid Construction: 2.5mm greyboard core resists crushing and corner damage
  • Art Paper Wrap: 200gsm coated art paper with matte, gloss, or soft-touch finish
  • Hot Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, rose gold, or copper accents on lid and interior card
  • Ribbon Pull: Optional satin ribbon pull tab for easy lid removal
  • Print: Heidelberg 7+1 UV for crisp foil registration and vibrant CMYK
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody
