Double-wall BC-flute corrugated heavy-duty e-commerce shipping mailer — engineered for direct-to-consumer brands shipping fragile, heavy, or high-value products, from Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003). This BC-flute double-wall construction (7mm combined thickness) features a 5-ply structure: 125gsm Kraft liner → 110gsm Medium (B-flute) → 125gsm Kraft liner → 110gsm Medium (C-flute) → 125gsm Kraft liner. The result: 2.5× the burst strength of single-wall B-flute and an Edge Crush Test (ECT) rating of 44 lb/in — suitable for products up to 25kg. Integrated features include a self-locking crash-lock bottom (no tape required, assembles in 2 seconds), dual tear-strip opening for recipient convenience, and dust flaps for secure closure. Produced on Bincai’s BHS 2.5m corrugator line — the world’s most advanced corrugated production system — with inline 4-color flexo printing, rotary die-cutting, and automatic gluing. Our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility delivers 1.7M boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

Quality Control — 7-Stage Inspection

  1. Paper Roll Inspection: Kraft liner basis weight verification (125gsm ±3%), moisture content (6–8%), ring crush test per roll
  2. Corrugator QC: BHS inline process monitoring — flute height, bond strength, caliper at 100% production speed
  3. Board Testing: ECT (Edge Crush Test) — target 44 lb/in minimum, BCT (Box Compression Test) — size-dependent, flat crush test, pin adhesion test
  4. Printing QC: Flexo registration check, color consistency (ΔE ≤ 3.0 on kraft), rub resistance test
  5. Die-Cutting QC: Dimensional verification (±0.5mm), crease line depth, slot width tolerance, crash-lock mechanism function test
  6. Final Inspection: 100% visual for defects, stack compression simulation, assembly speed test
  7. Packaging QC: Flat-packed bundles (25 units/bundle), strapped with PET banding, palletized, stretch-wrapped. Drop test ISTA 1A

Sustainability

Bincai’s corrugated mailers are among the most recycled packaging products globally — corrugated has an 89% recycling rate in the US and over 90% in Europe. Our boxes use FSC-certified kraft liners from responsibly managed forests. Water-based flexo inks contain no heavy metals or VOCs. Production trim from the BHS corrugator is baled and returned to paper mills, achieving near-zero production waste.

FAQ

Q: How much weight can this mailer hold? The BC-flute double-wall construction supports products up to 25kg in standard shipping conditions. For heavier items, we offer triple-wall corrugated options.

Q: Do I need tape to close it? No — the self-locking crash-lock bottom assembles without tape. Simply push the sides inward and the bottom locks automatically in under 2 seconds.

Q: What’s the difference between B-flute and BC-flute? B-flute is single-wall (3mm, ECT ~32). BC-flute is double-wall (7mm, ECT ~44) — providing 2.5× the stacking strength and significantly better crush protection for heavier or fragile items.

Q: Can I print my brand colors on kraft? Yes — up to 4-color flexo printing on kraft or white-top liner. For premium photo-quality printing, our litho-laminate option applies offset-printed art paper to the corrugated surface.

Q: Is the tear strip easy for customers to use? Yes — the dual tear strip requires minimal force and opens cleanly without tearing the box body. We can add “OPEN HERE” text and directional arrows.

Q: What sizes are available? Fully custom — we manufacture to your exact internal dimensions. Common e-commerce sizes range from 200×150×80mm to 500×400×300mm.
