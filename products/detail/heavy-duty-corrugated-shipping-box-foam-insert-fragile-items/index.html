Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing’s heavy-duty corrugated shipping box with custom die-cut foam insert provides military-grade protection for fragile, high-value items during transit, courier handling, and international freight. Double-wall BC flute corrugated board (6–7mm thickness, ECT 44–55 lb/in, BCT 400–800 lbs) combined with precision die-cut EVA, PE, or PU foam cradles each item individually — eliminating internal movement and absorbing shock, vibration, and impact forces. ISTA 1A and 3A drop-tested and vibration-validated for real-world courier conditions. Self-locking bottom eliminates assembly tape; optional tear strip enables frustration-free recipient opening. Available with anti-static ESD-safe foam for electronics, medical devices, and precision instruments. Manufactured in our ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified 18,000 sqm factory (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory, expanded 2025) in Foshan, Guangdong — 23 years of corrugated packaging expertise (founded 2003), KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset + Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses, Bobst flatbed die-cutting, 1.7M boxes daily output, 220+ skilled workers. MOQ 500 units with free pre-production samples in 7–10 working days.

Applications

  • Electronics: Smartphones, tablets, laptops, hard drives, circuit boards, sensors — ESD-safe foam available
  • Medical Devices: Diagnostic equipment, surgical instruments, implant packaging — cleanroom-compatible foam
  • Optics & Lenses: Camera lenses, microscopes, telescopes, precision optics — anti-static + lint-free foam
  • Glass & Ceramics: Laboratory glassware, artisanal ceramics, wine glasses, bottles — multi-cavity die-cut cradles
  • Luxury Goods: High-end watches, jewelry, designer accessories in transit — velvet-lined foam insert option
  • Industrial Parts: Precision machined components, aerospace parts, automotive sensors — high-density PU foam
  • E-Commerce: Direct-to-consumer shipping of fragile high-value products — branded exterior + protective interior
  • Exhibition & Trade Show: Safe transport of display samples and prototypes — reusable box option

Customization Options

  • Corrugated Board: B-flute (3mm), C-flute (4mm), BC double-wall (6–7mm), triple-wall (10mm+)
  • Box Style: Regular slotted container (RSC), self-locking bottom, die-cut mailer, telescoping lid-and-base
  • Sizes: Custom — 150×100×50mm to 600×400×300mm; larger on request
  • Foam Insert: EVA (closed-cell, shock absorbent), PE (cross-linked, resilient), PU (open-cell, soft-touch), anti-static (ESD safe, surface resistivity 10⁶–10⁹ Ω)
  • Foam Density: 25–80 kg/m³ depending on item weight and fragility class
  • Foam Color: Black, white, grey, red, blue, custom Pantone
  • Printing: Flexo 1–4 color direct print, offset litho-laminated (KBA 1050 4-color), or digital short-run — CMYK + Pantone spot
  • Finishes: Aqueous coating, matte/gloss varnish, spot UV, soft-touch coating
  • Features: Tear strip, perforated opening, self-locking bottom, die-cut handle cutouts, tamper-evident seal, QR code printing
  • Interior: Corrugated dividers, molded pulp trays, custom die-cut partitions, recycled content option
  • Sustainability: 100% recycled liner available, FSC certified board, water-based inks, fully recyclable
