FAQ

Q: Is the PET tray food-safe for direct chocolate contact? A: Yes. Our PET trays are manufactured from virgin food-grade PET resin compliant with FDA 21 CFR and EU 1935/2004 regulations. They’re odorless, tasteless, and won’t transfer plasticizers to chocolate. We provide migration test certificates with production lots.

Q: What’s the advantage of hexagonal over square boxes for confectionery? A: Hexagonal boxes offer 3 key advantages: (1) superior shelf presence — the 6-sided shape stands out among rectangular competitors; (2) better chocolate arrangement — radial layouts look more artisanal and intentional; (3) perceived value — consumers associate non-rectangular packaging with higher quality and are willing to pay 15-25% more for hexagonally packaged products (Packaging Digest, 2024).

Q: Can the ribbon be tied by machine or must it be hand-tied? A: We offer both. Machine-tied bows provide consistency for high-volume orders (5000+). Hand-tied bows with double loops deliver a more artisanal, premium feel preferred by luxury chocolatiers. Hand-tying adds approximately $0.15-0.30 per unit.

Q: How do you ensure the magnets don’t affect chocolate flavor? A: Our N52 neodymium magnets are fully encapsulated within the greyboard walls — they never contact the interior cavity or food tray. We use 2-3mm greyboard buffer between magnet pockets and the inner cavity. SGS sensory testing confirms zero flavor transfer.

Q: Can you do a window version to show the chocolates inside? A: Yes. We can add a die-cut window on the lid with clear PET or PLA film, framed by the hexagonal shape. For maximum visual impact, we recommend an asymmetrical window that reveals 2-3 chocolates while preserving the gold foil logo area. Window adds approximately $0.20-0.40 per unit.
