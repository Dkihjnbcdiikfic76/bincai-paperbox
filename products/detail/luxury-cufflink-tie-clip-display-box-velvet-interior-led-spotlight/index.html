Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — velvet density verification, magnet grade check (N35 minimum), board grammage testing
  2. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0 target
  3. Die-Cutting & Assembly Prep — ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance on all panels
  4. Flocking Application — electrostatic flocking density check, 100% visual inspection under 5000K D65 lighting
  5. LED Module Integration — 100% functional test with 3-cycle on/off verification, reed switch sensitivity test

FAQ

Q: How long does the LED battery last?

A: The CR2032 battery powers the LED for approximately 50,000 hours of illumination. Under normal retail display use (opening ~20 times per day for 10 seconds each), the battery lasts 12-18 months. Batteries are user-replaceable via a rear access panel — no tools required.

Q: Can you customize the compartment layout for different accessory sizes?

A: Yes. Our in-house die-making team can create custom foam or flocked insert trays to accommodate any cufflink size (standard 15-20mm), oversized tie clips, collar stays, lapel pins, or combination sets. Send us your accessory dimensions and we’ll design the perfect tray — included in the sampling process at no extra charge.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity?

A: MOQ is 500 units for standard configurations. For fully custom sizes with new die-cutting tools, MOQ is 1,000 units. Rush orders of 300-500 units are possible with a small surcharge during non-peak periods.

Q: Can the LED color temperature be changed?

A: Yes. Choose warm white (3000K) for a classic jewelry-store ambiance, cool white (6000K) for modern retail settings, or RGB programmable LEDs for brand-color matching. All options use the same housing — specify your preference during sampling.

Q: What certifications does this product carry?

A: ISO 9001:2015 quality management, FSC Chain-of-Custody for sustainable paper sourcing, OEKO-TEX Standard 100 for velvet materials, RoHS for the LED module, and CE for the electronic components. Full compliance documentation provided with every shipment.
