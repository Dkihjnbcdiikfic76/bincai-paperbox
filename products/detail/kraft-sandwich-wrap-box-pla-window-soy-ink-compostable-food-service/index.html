Product Overview

Bincai’s kraft paper sandwich wrap box with PLA compostable window combines sustainability with food service functionality. The 350gsm unbleached kraft board provides a natural, earthy brand aesthetic while the PLA window — derived from renewable corn starch — offers product visibility and full compostability. Printed with soy-based inks and featuring a water-based grease-resistant interior coating, this box meets EN 13432 and ASTM D6400 standards for industrial and home composting. The auto-lock bottom design enables rapid assembly, making it ideal for high-volume food service operations.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Board: 350gsm unbleached kraft board — natural brown finish, no bleaching agents
  • Window Material: 40-micron PLA (polylactic acid) film — certified compostable per EN 13432, clarity >92%
  • Printing: Soy-based ink, up to 4-color offset on KBA 1050 press
  • Interior Coating: Water-based grease barrier — repels oil/moisture without plastic lamination
  • Closure: Auto-lock bottom with tuck-top lid
  • Assembly: Flat-packed, pops open in 2 seconds
  • Compostability: Home compostable (6 months) and industrial compostable (90 days)
  • Daily Output: 1.7M boxes
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody, EN 13432 compostable
