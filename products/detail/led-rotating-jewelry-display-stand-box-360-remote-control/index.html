FAQ

Q: How long do the batteries last? A: 3× AA alkaline batteries provide approximately 200 hours of continuous rotation with LED on. USB-C rechargeable option provides 40 hours per charge (2000mAh). AC adapter offers unlimited runtime.

Q: Can the rotation be customized? A: Yes — Bincai can program custom rotation profiles: slow reveal (1 RPM), standard display (3 RPM), fast scan (6 RPM), or intermittent (rotate 30 seconds, pause 10 seconds). Firmware customization requires 1,000 unit minimum.

Q: Is the motor audible? A: At <25dB, the motor is effectively silent — quieter than a whisper. Suitable for quiet retail environments and intimate proposal settings.

Q: What jewelry types fit? A: The standard model accommodates rings, earrings, pendants, bracelets, and watches. Custom platform inserts available for specific jewelry types — provide your product dimensions for a tailored fit.

Q: Can I get the box without electronics? A: Yes — the same box is available in passive (non-motorized) version. The turntable well and LED channel become decorative recessed areas. MOQ 300 with 15% cost reduction.

Q: Is it safe for international shipping with batteries? A: AA alkaline batteries (standard) are unrestricted. USB-C Li-ion (2,000mAh, 7.4Wh) is below the 100Wh IATA threshold and ships under Section II of PI 967. Bincai includes shipping safety documentation.
