Product Overview

Bincai’s rigid perfume gift box is engineered for luxury fragrance brands that demand shelf presence and unboxing drama. The 2.0mm greyboard core wrapped in soft-touch 157gsm art paper creates a substantial, premium feel. A color-matched satin ribbon closure provides elegant access while Bobst BMA hot foil stamping delivers metallic branding with ±0.2mm registration accuracy. The interior features a flocked PET tray that cradles bottles of any shape — rectangular, cylindrical, or avant-garde. Manufactured in our Foshan smart factory (18,000 sqm) on Heidelberg 7+1 UV offset and KBA 1050 4-color presses, every box meets the exacting standards of prestige fragrance houses.

Applications

  • Eau de Parfum & Eau de Toilette: 30ml to 100ml bottle presentation with secure bottle cavity
  • Niche & Artisanal Fragrance: Small-batch luxury packaging with bespoke foil colors and textured wraps
  • Perfume Gift Sets: Multi-compartment boxes for bottle + travel atomizer + body lotion sets
  • Limited Edition Fragrances: Numbered editions with holographic foil and serial plaques
  • Corporate Fragrance Gifts: Branded perfume boxes for VIP client gifting and corporate events
  • Discovery Sets: Multi-vial sampler boxes with individual vial wells and scent description cards
