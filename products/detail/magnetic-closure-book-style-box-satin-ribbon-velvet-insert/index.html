Product Overview

The Bincai Magnetic Closure Book-Style Box combines the elegance of a hardcover book with the security of embedded N42 neodymium magnets and the soft luxury of a satin ribbon tie. The spine-hinged construction opens like a treasured volume, revealing a custom velvet-flocked insert tray that cradles products in plush refinement. Designed for premium corporate gifts, executive stationery sets, luxury accessory presentation, and commemorative packaging, each box is built on 2.0-3.0mm rigid greyboard wrapped in 157gsm art paper with gold or silver foil stamping on both the cover and spine. Produced at our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility running KBA 1050 and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses, Bincai delivers over 220,000+ gift boxes daily with ISO 9001 and FSC certifications.

Features

  • Book-Style Hinge: Spine-hinged construction with reinforced hinge that opens flat 180° for dramatic product reveal
  • Magnetic Closure: 2× N42 neodymium disc magnets (10mm ∅) embedded in front and back panels — 3.0-4.5 N/cm² closure force, 10,000+ cycles
  • Satin Ribbon Tie: 25mm satin ribbon (color-matched to box design), sewn into spine for secure secondary closure
  • Velvet Insert Tray: CNC die-cut velvet-flocked tray with product-specific cavities for perfect product positioning
  • Cover & Spine Foil: Gold, silver, or rose gold foil stamping on both front cover and spine for bookstore-shelf aesthetics
  • Wrap Options: 157gsm art paper, leatherette, linen-textured paper, or pearlescent finish

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Construction: 2.0-3.0mm greyboard book-style shell with spine hinge, wrapped in 157gsm art paper
  • Magnet System: 2× N42 neodymium disc magnets (10mm ∅) embedded in front and back panels
  • Closure Force: 3.0-4.5 N/cm² — secure snap without over-closure
  • Ribbon: 25mm satin ribbon tie (color-matched to box), sewn into spine with reinforced stitching
  • Interior Tray: CNC die-cut velvet-flocked tray (30-45 Shore A foam base) with custom product cavities
  • Foil Stamping: Bobst flatbed hot stamping on cover and spine, ±0.15mm registration
  • Finish: Matte lamination (standard), gloss lamination, or soft-touch aqueous coating
  • Die-Cutting: Bobst flatbed die-cutter, ±0.5mm tolerance
  • QC: 15-point checklist covering magnet alignment (±1mm), ribbon tension, lid gap (±1mm), velvet adhesion, and foil registration
