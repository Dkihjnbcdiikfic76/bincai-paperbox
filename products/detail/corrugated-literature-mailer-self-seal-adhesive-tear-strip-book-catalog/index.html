Product Overview

The Corrugated Literature Mailer with Self-Seal Adhesive solves the two biggest pain points in book and catalog fulfillment: taping labor and frustrated recipients struggling to open packages. The integrated self-seal adhesive strip eliminates manual tape application — simply peel the release liner, fold, and press for a permanent bond that exceeds ISTA 3A shipping standards. On the receiving end, dual tear strip perforations allow the recipient to open the mailer cleanly in under 3 seconds without scissors or box cutters, preserving the contents in pristine condition. Manufactured on Bincai’s BHS 2.5m corrugator line with B-flute (3mm caliper, ECT 32), this mailer provides the ideal balance of crush protection and lightweight shipping economics for books, catalogs, magazines, and document sets.

Applications

  • Book Fulfillment: Hardcover and paperback direct-to-consumer shipping (Amazon Seller, Shopify, independent bookstores)
  • Catalog Distribution: Annual product catalogs, lookbooks, and corporate capability brochures
  • Direct Mail Marketing: Targeted promotional material mailings with branded exterior printing
  • Academic Publishing: Journal issue distribution, thesis submission packaging
  • Legal & Financial: Contract sets, annual reports, prospectus documents
  • Government & Institutional: Form sets, information packets, public consultation documents

Customization Options

  • Flute Profile: B-flute (3mm, standard), E-flute (1.5mm, slim), or EB-double-wall (4.5mm, heavy)
  • Exterior Surface: Natural kraft, white-top kraft, or full-color flexographic printing (up to 4 colors)
  • Adhesive Type: Standard hot-melt (permanent), removable adhesive (clean peel), or tamper-evident security adhesive
  • Tear Strip: Single or dual perforation, custom tear strip color for branding
  • Size Range: Custom die-cut to any dimensions within 600×400mm flat sheet
  • Interior Printing: Branded messaging, return instructions, or social media handles printed inside

FAQ

Q: Will the self-seal adhesive hold during summer shipping to hot climates? A: Yes — our hot-melt adhesive is formulated for a service temperature range of -20°C to +65°C. The adhesive is applied at 160°C and cures to a thermoset bond that does not re-melt in transit. We’ve validated performance through ISTA 7E summer profile testing (72 hours at 50°C/90% RH).

Q: Can I get smaller quantities for testing? A: Yes — we offer sample packs of 10–20 units in your chosen size and print configuration for $30–60 including DHL/FedEx shipping. Lead time for samples is 5–7 business days.

Q: Does the tear strip work with heavy contents? A: Yes — the dual tear strip is designed to function reliably with contents up to 5kg. For heavier items (5–15kg), we recommend upgrading to double-wall EB-flute construction with reinforced tear strip. The tear force scales proportionally with board grade.

Q: Can the mailer be used for Amazon FBA? A: Absolutely — this mailer meets Amazon Frustration-Free Packaging (FFP) Tier 1 (Certified Frustration-Free) requirements: no scissors or box cutters needed, recyclable, and right-sized to the product. We can provide ISTA 6-Amazon.com-SIOC test reports for your specific SKU.
