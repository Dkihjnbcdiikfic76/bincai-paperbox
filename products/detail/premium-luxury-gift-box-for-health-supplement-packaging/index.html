Production Specifications

  • Greyboard core: 2.0–3.0mm FSC-certified recycled greyboard; 1200–1600gsm density
  • Exterior wrap: 157–250gsm art paper or 0.6mm PU leatherette, PVA cold-glued with mitered corners
  • Printing: KBA 1050 4-color offset press for wrap sheets; Heidelberg 7+1 UV for foil registration and spot coating
  • Foil stamping: Bobst SP 102 BMA — temperature 110–130°C, dwell 0.5–1.0s, multi-pass for up to 3 foil colors
  • Die-cutting: Bobst flatbed die-cutter; box blank tolerance ±0.15mm
  • Foam/cavity insert: CNC-milled EVA foam at 80–100 kg/m³ density, Shore A 45 hardness, cavity tolerance ±0.5mm
  • Assembly: semi-automatic wrapping + manual foam insert placement + magnet alignment QC
