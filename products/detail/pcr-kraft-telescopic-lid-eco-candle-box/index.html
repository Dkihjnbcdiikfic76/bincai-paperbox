Quality Control — 7-Stage Inspection

  1. PCR Material Verification: Mill certificate review for every batch, PCR content verification via fiber analysis, non-toxicity compliance (EN 71-3)
  2. Printing QC: Soy-based ink adhesion test, color consistency (ΔE ≤ 2.5 for kraft substrates), rub resistance test
  3. Foil Stamping QC: Registration ±0.15mm, foil adhesion test on textured kraft surface
  4. Box Assembly QC: Telescopic fit tolerance (±0.5mm gap), glue bond strength test (>3N/cm), crease line integrity
  5. Window QC: PLA film clarity check, film-to-board adhesion test, die-cut edge quality inspection
  6. Final Inspection: 100% visual under 5000K daylight, surface defect detection, dimensional verification
  7. Packaging QC: Individual polybag (recycled LDPE option), master carton drop test (1.2m ISTA 1A), barcode scan verification

FAQ

Q: Is this box truly 100% recycled? Yes. We use 100% post-consumer recycled (PCR) kraft paperboard with mill traceability certificates. The FSC Recycled label verifies this independently.

Q: Does the recycled content affect print quality? Slightly. Due to recycled fiber flecks and a more textured surface, we recommend bold, simple designs for best results. For photographic-quality printing, our 70% PCR / 30% virgin blend offers a good compromise between eco-credentials and print fidelity.

Q: Is the PLA window really compostable? Yes — our PLA window film is OK Compost INDUSTRIAL certified and will biodegrade in commercial composting facilities within 90–180 days. It is not home-compostable; the entire box without the window is recyclable in standard paper streams.

Q: Can I mix sizes in one MOQ? Yes — combine up to 3 sizes within a single 3,000-unit MOQ minimum (1,000 per size).

Q: Do you offer FSC on-product labeling? Yes — we can print the FSC Recycled logo on your box with your FSC license number (or ours: FSC-C147399) after chain-of-custody verification.

Q: What’s the carbon footprint of this box? Approximately 0.12 kg CO₂e per box (cradle-to-gate), ~60% lower than a virgin-fiber equivalent. Full life-cycle assessment available on request.
