Double-wall corrugated heavy-duty fine china dinnerware shipper with precision honeycomb cell divider — purpose-engineered for protecting fine china, porcelain, and ceramic dinnerware during domestic and international shipping. The BHS 2.5m corrugator produces consistent BC/BE/AB flute double-wall board with superior edge crush test (ECT) ratings for stacking strength. The die-cut honeycomb cell divider isolates each plate, bowl, or platter from adjacent items, preventing edge chipping and surface scratching. Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming paper roll inspection — basis weight, moisture content, ring crush test
  2. Corrugator in-line monitoring — flute height, bond strength, board caliper
  3. Board testing — ECT, BCT (box compression), burst strength (Mullen) per batch
  4. Die-cut accuracy verification — cell divider dimensions, crease scores, slot alignment
  5. Print quality check — color registration, legend clarity, barcode scannability
  6. Assembly and fit test — plate insertion, divider integrity, lid closure force
  7. Final audit per AQL 2.5 Level II with ISTA drop test on random sample

FAQ

What double-wall flute combination should I choose? BC flute is our standard recommendation for dinnerware weighing up to 30kg total. BE flute offers higher stacking strength for export container loads. AB flute provides maximum cushioning for extremely fragile antique porcelain.

Can you perform shipping tests? Yes. We offer ISTA 3A drop, vibration, and compression testing with full documentation — ideal for Amazon Frustration-Free Packaging certification.

What is the minimum order quantity? MOQ starts at 500 units. Smaller quantities (200+) available at a premium with longer lead times.

How many place settings fit per box? Standard configurations accommodate 4, 6, 8, or 12 place settings. Custom configurations for specific dinnerware sets are fully supported.

Is the honeycomb divider recyclable? Yes. The divider is made from the same corrugated material as the box and is 100% recyclable in paper streams.
