FAQ

Q: Are these favor boxes truly compostable — can guests throw them in their home compost? A: Yes. When configured with 100% recycled kraft paperboard, soy-based inks, water-based adhesives, and natural jute twine (no synthetic coating), these boxes meet ASTM D6400 standards for industrial composting and will break down in a home compost pile within 3–6 months. The kraft paperboard provides carbon, while the jute twine and soy ink provide negligible nitrogen — the box decomposes just like autumn leaves. For the fastest home composting, we recommend avoiding metallic foil stamping and opting for water-based inks only.

Q: Can you print custom baby names, dates, or individual messages on each box? A: Yes, we offer variable data printing through digital inkjet or digital foil systems. For baby shower favors, popular customizations include: the baby’s name, shower date, a short thank-you message (“Love, Sarah & Mike”), or table numbers for assigned seating. Variable data printing adds approximately $0.08–$0.25 per unit depending on print coverage. Setup cost is approximately $50 for the variable data program, and orders of 100+ units are cost-effective for personalization.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom-printed kraft favor boxes? A: Our standard MOQ is 500 units per design. However, for baby showers and small events, we offer a low-MOQ digital print option starting at 200 units with a small-lot surcharge of approximately 20%. Digital printing eliminates plate costs, making it economical for short runs. Lead time is 10–12 working days for digital + assembly versus 15–18 working days for offset production runs. We also stock blank kraft favor boxes in standard sizes (80×80×80mm, 100×100×80mm) for immediate dispatch with minimum 100 units.

Sustainability

Bincai is committed to environmentally responsible packaging. Our kraft eco boxes embody sustainability at every level:

  • 100% Recycled Content — The kraft paperboard used in our favor boxes is made from 100% post-consumer and post-industrial recycled fiber. No virgin tree fiber is used — your baby shower favors are made from paper that has already served a previous purpose.

  • FSC Certified Chain of Custody — Our FSC certification extends through the entire supply chain, from recycled fiber sourcing to finished packaging. This provides third-party verification of our environmental claims and supports your brand’s sustainability storytelling.

  • Soy-Based Inks — All printing uses soy-based and vegetable-based offset inks instead of petroleum-based alternatives. Soy inks are renewable, produce fewer VOCs during printing, and are non-toxic — safe for guests and safe for the planet.

  • Home Compostable — In the fully biodegradable configuration (recycled kraft + soy ink + water-based adhesive + jute twine, no synthetic coatings), the entire box is home compostable. It will break down in 3–6 months in a healthy compost pile, returning nutrients to the soil.

  • Zero Plastic Construction — There is no plastic anywhere in the box — no PET window, no plastic laminate, no synthetic twine, no hot melt glue. The entire construction is paper, plant fiber (jute), and water-based chemistry, making it fully compatible with both recycling and composting end-of-life pathways.

  • Carbon-Conscious Shipping — Favor boxes ship flat (knocked down), maximizing pallet density. A standard pallet carries 5,000–12,000 flat favor boxes versus only 400–800 if pre-assembled — reducing transport carbon emissions by 85% or more.
