Product Overview

The tuck-top auto-bottom (TTAB) folding carton is the workhorse of e-commerce packaging — designed for speed, efficiency, and brand impact. The auto-bottom locks into place with a single push, no tape required, while the tuck-top closure provides secure transit protection. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certified, with 23 years of folding carton expertise — each carton ships flat for 60% freight savings and assembles in under 2 seconds at the fulfillment station. Our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press prints vibrant CMYK with inline aqueous coating for scuff resistance during shipping.

Design Features

  • Auto-Bottom Mechanism: Pre-glued bottom with interlocking flaps — push the sides and the bottom snaps into a load-bearing platform rated for 2-5 kg (depending on board weight). No tape, no assembly tools.
  • Tuck-Top Closure: Full-width tuck flap with friction-lock ears — secure closure without tape or adhesives. Easy open, easy reclose for returns.
  • Flat-Ship Design: Ships as folded flats — 6× more cartons per pallet vs. pre-assembled boxes. Reduces freight cost by 60% and warehouse space by 80%.
  • Scuff-Resistant Surface: Inline aqueous coating on Heidelberg 7+1 press — protects print quality through fulfillment handling, transit, and shelf display.
  • Tear-Strip Option: Perforated tear strip on tuck flap for tamper-evident opening — customers know their product hasn’t been opened in transit.

Applications

  • Cosmetics & Skincare: Serums, creams, palettes, and gift sets — premium print quality with scuff resistance for fulfillment handling.
  • Dietary Supplements: Vitamin bottles, supplement pouches, and wellness kits — auto-bottom handles bottle weight without bottom blowout.
  • Electronics Accessories: Earbuds, chargers, cables, and small gadgets — foam insert option for drop protection.
  • Subscription Boxes: Monthly curated boxes for beauty, snacks, books, and lifestyle — quick assembly at scale, brand-consistent printing.
  • DTC (Direct-to-Consumer): Unboxing experience matters — vibrant CMYK printing on exterior, brand messaging on interior flaps.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How fast can Bincai turn around a custom TTAB carton order? A: Standard lead time is 5-7 days for pre-production samples and 10-15 days for mass production. Rush orders can ship in 7 days for standard configurations with no foil stamping or embossing.

Q: Can I print different designs on the interior of the carton? A: Yes. We can print brand messaging, care instructions, social media handles, or decorative patterns on interior flaps and panels. This adds no tooling cost — just an additional plate and pass on press.

Q: What’s the cost difference between aqueous coating and BOPP lamination? A: Aqueous coating (inline on Heidelberg 7+1 UV) is included at no extra cost and provides good scuff resistance for standard e-commerce. BOPP soft-touch lamination adds approximately $0.02-0.04/unit and delivers a premium tactile finish with higher durability for luxury brands.

Q: Can the auto-bottom handle heavy products like glass bottles? A: With 400gsm C1S board and engineered flap geometry, our TTAB cartons handle up to 3 kg. For products 3-5 kg, we recommend C2S 400gsm with reinforced auto-bottom geometry. We test every design with actual product weight before production.

Q: Are Bincai’s folding cartons compatible with Amazon FBA SIOC certification? A: Yes. We design to Amazon Ships In Own Container (SIOC) Tier 1 (Frustration-Free Packaging) and Tier 2 standards. Our structural engineers optimize the auto-bottom geometry for ISTA 6-Amazon.com-SIOC testing. We provide test reports for your Amazon certification submission.
