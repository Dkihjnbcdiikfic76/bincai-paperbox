Customization Options

  • Paperboard Grade: 300–400gsm SBS for standard cosmetics; 18pt CCNB (clay-coated newsback) for value-positioned products; 16pt C1S (coated one side) for economical options.
  • Printing: Full-color CMYK offset lithography on KBA 1050 4-color; up to 4 Pantone spot colors; Heidelberg 7+1 UV with inline aqueous coating for instant cure and scuff resistance.
  • Lamination: Matte film (fingerprint-resistant, velvety feel); gloss film (color pop, wet-bottle moisture barrier); soft-touch velvet (premium tactile); anti-scratch laminate for high-handling retail.
  • Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic foil; registered to ±0.1mm; combined foil+emboss for dimensional metallic branding.
  • Embossing & Debossing: Single-level or multi-level sculptured emboss; blind emboss for subtle texture; registered emboss aligned with print elements.
  • Structural Options: Reverse tuck (standard); straight tuck (both flaps same direction); tuck-top auto-bottom (auto-erecting for high-speed lines); seal-end (glue-sealed for tamper evidence).
  • Window Options: Die-cut window with 0.2mm PET film for product visibility; shaped windows (heart, circle, brand silhouette).
  • Sizing: Fully custom — from compact 60×60×25mm single-product cartons to 200×140×60mm multi-product gift sets.

Quality Assurance

  1. Incoming Board QC: SBS caliper and grammage verification; brightness (ISO 2470) and whiteness (CIE) measurement; moisture content 6–8% for dimensional stability.
  2. Print Quality: Spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0; Heidelberg Prinect inline registration monitoring; 100% visual for print defects (hickeys, ghosting, misregister).
  3. Lamination QC: Peel adhesion per ASTM D903; visual inspection under D65 lighting for bubbles, wrinkles, delamination; 24-hour cure verification before die-cutting.
  4. Foil Stamp Quality: Registration verified against print registration marks; foil adhesion pull-tested; edge definition under 10× magnification.
  5. Die-Cutting Precision: ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance; score-line depth uniformity preventing cracking at fold lines.
  6. Gluing & Assembly: Side-seam bond strength tested to minimum 8N/25mm; carton erection test on automated packaging line simulator.
  7. Finished Goods Audit: AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.0 (minor) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4; barcode readability verification.

Why Reverse-Tuck Carton for Cosmetics

The reverse-tuck folding carton outperforms alternative structures across the metrics that matter most for cosmetics retail packaging:

  • Faster line speeds — Opposite-direction flaps allow single-station automated erection at 15,000+ cartons/hour on modern packaging lines, vs. 10,000–12,000 for tuck-top designs requiring sequential flap folding.
  • ±0.1 mm foil registration — Bobst hot foil presses register gold/silver/rose gold accents with sub-millimeter precision, keeping logos razor-sharp across 10,000+ impressions per hour.
  • Fingerprint-proof matte finish — 1.2 mil matte PET lamination creates a velvety, non-reflective surface that resists oils and scuffing through months of retail shelf handling.
  • ΔE ≤ 2.0 color consistency — X-Rite eXact spectrophotometer verification every 500 sheets ensures batch-to-batch color fidelity critical for brand recognition across SKUs.
  • MOQ 1,000 with 10–14 day lead — Low minimums enable limited-edition launches and influencer collab packaging without warehouse commitment.
