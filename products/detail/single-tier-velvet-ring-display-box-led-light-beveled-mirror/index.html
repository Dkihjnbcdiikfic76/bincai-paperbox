FAQ

Q: How long does the LED battery last, and can the customer replace it?

A: The standard CR2032 battery provides approximately 500–800 open/close cycles at 30 seconds of illumination per cycle. For typical retail use (ring displayed, box opened 10–20 times per day), this translates to 25–80 days of operation. The battery is user-replaceable: the battery compartment is accessible from the underside of the ring cushion (lift cushion to reveal battery). We include a spare CR2032 battery with every box. For proposal packaging where battery reliability is critical, we install a fresh battery within 7 days of shipment and test it immediately before packing.

Q: Will the LED light make the ring look different from natural lighting — how accurate is the color?

A: We use 3000K warm white LEDs with CRI (Color Rendering Index) >90 — this is close to halogen gallery lighting and renders diamond sparkle and gemstone color very accurately. The light temperature is deliberately warm (not the cold blue-white of office LEDs) to create an intimate, romantic illumination. For diamonds, the warm light enhances fire (spectral color dispersion). For colored gemstones (sapphire, ruby, emerald), the high CRI ensures accurate color rendering without spectral distortion. We provide a sample box for your evaluation — your jeweler’s eye will confirm the light quality.

Q: Can you custom-shape the ring slot for unusual ring designs — split shank, bypass, halo, cluster?

A: Yes. For standard solitaire and three-stone rings, our universal slot design fits most styles. For unusual ring profiles (split shank, bypass, double halo, cluster settings), we recommend sending us the ring or a 3D model so we can CNC-route a custom-fit cavity. The custom cavity adds approximately $50–80 one-time tooling cost with no MOQ increase — even a single box can have a custom cavity if needed for a proposal.

Q: Can the box be personalized with the couple’s names or proposal date inside the lid?

A: Yes. We offer interior lid printing (full-color CMYK on velvet paper insert), hot foil stamping (metallic or pigment foil on velvet paper), or laser engraving (on a metal plaque mounted to the lid interior). Popular personalization: proposal date, couple’s names, GPS coordinates of the proposal location, or a short message. Personalization adds approximately $0.50–1.50 per box depending on technique and adds 2–3 days to the production timeline.

Q: What’s the minimum order and can I order a single box for a proposal?

A: MOQ for production orders is 300–500 units depending on complexity. For a single proposal box, we offer a sampling service: 1–3 fully customized boxes (your exterior color, interior color, logo, ring cavity, LED) at a sample fee of approximately $80–150 including express shipping. The sample fee is credited toward your first production order. We ship worldwide via DHL/FedEx (3–5 days to most destinations). Many of our jewelry brand customers started with a single sample for their own proposal and returned for production quantities afterward.
