FAQ

Q: Is the bag truly 100% recycled? A: Yes — Bincai sources FSC Recycled certified kraft paper from verified post-consumer waste streams. Chain-of-custody documentation available on request.

Q: How much weight can the bag hold? A: 120gsm bags handle up to 3kg with even distribution. 150gsm up to 5kg. 200gsm up to 8kg. All bags pass a 2× safety factor load test in QC Gate 7.

Q: Can you print full-color photos on kraft paper? A: Yes — KBA 1050 4-color offset handles photographic reproduction on kraft. Note that the natural brown base will affect color rendering compared to white-coated paper. We recommend water-based white underprint for vibrant photos.

Q: What’s the MOQ for custom printing? A: 1,000 bags for 1–2 color print. 3,000 bags for 4-color process. Custom handle colors require 5,000 bags minimum.

Q: Are the bags food-safe? A: Yes — water-based inks are FDA-compliant for indirect food contact. For direct food contact (bakery, produce), we recommend an inner food-grade liner. Bincai can supply bags with PE or PLA compostable inner liners.

Q: Can you do rush orders? A: Standard lead time is 15-20 working days. Rush orders (7-10 days) available at 1.3× standard pricing for quantities under 10,000 bags.
