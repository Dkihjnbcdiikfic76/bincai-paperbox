Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) presents the ultimate jewelry presentation solution — a two-tier velvet jewelry display box with solid brass lock and key closure. This premium rigid box features a lift-out upper tray revealing a second deep storage tier beneath, providing organized display for rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and watches in a single elegant case.

Manufactured in our 18,000 sqm dual-factory in Foshan, Guangdong, the box combines the precision of KBA Rapida 105 offset printing, Heidelberg 7+1 UV foil accents, and CNC-milled EVA foam inserts (±0.5mm) for perfectly fitted jewelry cavities. The electrostatic velvet flocking (1.0mm pile density) in black, navy, cream, or burgundy provides a luxurious backdrop that makes jewelry pieces appear to float. An optional CR2032-powered LED module (50,000-hour rated) with reed-switch activation illuminates the jewelry automatically when the lid is opened — creating an unforgettable unboxing experience at retail counters or for e-commerce unboxing videos.

FAQ

Q: Can the LED module be customized for different lighting effects?

A: Yes. The standard configuration uses a warm white CR2032-powered LED (3000K color temperature) with reed-switch activation (LED turns on automatically when the lid is opened). Custom options include: cool white LED (6000K), RGB LED with remote control, multiple LED positions (1–4 LEDs per box), and tilt-switch activation instead of reed-switch. LED modules are rated for 50,000 hours and undergo a 48-hour burn-in test at G5. Minimum order for custom LED configurations is 1,000 units.

Q: What types of jewelry can the foam inserts accommodate?

A: Our CNC-milled EVA foam inserts can be custom-configured for any jewelry type: rings (standard ring bar slots holding 1–24 rings), necklaces (display bust form or flat-lay with chain channel), bracelets (curved C-channel with snap closure), earrings (stud holes with card slot or display card), watches (pillow insert with strap channel), and brooches/pins (flat cavity with pin backing). Mixed configurations are common — e.g., a two-tier box with rings in the upper tray and a necklace display bust in the lower tier. Send us your jewelry dimensions and we’ll design the optimal layout.

Q: Is the lock and key truly functional or just decorative?

A: Fully functional. Each box includes a solid brass lock and 2–3 keys. The lock mechanism is tested for 500 open/close cycles at G6 and rated for daily retail use. It provides genuine security for valuable jewelry during transport and display. Zinc alloy and custom-branded (engraved lock face) options are available. We also offer combination locks and magnetic child-safety locks as alternatives.

Q: Can I order the box without LED lighting to reduce cost?

A: Absolutely. The LED module is optional — removing it reduces the per-unit price by approximately 15–20% and shortens lead time from 12–16 working days to the standard 10–14 days. Many clients start with the non-LED version for their initial order and add LED to subsequent orders once they’ve validated the design. The foam insert and flocking quality are identical in both versions. We recommend starting with samples of both configurations at no charge.

Q: What’s the minimum order and lead time for a fully custom jewelry box?

A: MOQ is 500 units for standard sizes with our existing tooling. For fully custom sizes requiring new die fabrication, MOQ is 1,000 units. Standard lead time is 10–14 working days (without LED) or 12–16 working days (with LED). Free pre-production samples are provided within 5–7 working days so you can validate the design, lock function, LED experience, and foam fitment before committing to full production.
