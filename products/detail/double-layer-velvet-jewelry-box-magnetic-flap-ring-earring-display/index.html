Product Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — founded 2003 in Foshan’s Shunde district with 23 years of packaging manufacturing heritage — presents this premium double-layer velvet jewelry box with magnetic flap closure, purpose-engineered for jewelry brands and retailers seeking elegant, organized display for ring and earring collections. The double-layer architecture features a removable upper tray with precision Bobst die-cut ring slots (configurable 8–24 positions) and vertical earring card holders, plus a spacious lower compartment for necklaces, bracelets, certificates, and care cards. Black premium electrostatic flocked velvet interior (1.2mm nylon pile height with anti-shed treatment) cradles each piece in non-abrasive luxury. The N42 neodymium magnetic flap provides a satisfying, silent closure with invisible magnet integration — no visible hardware disrupts the clean exterior aesthetic. Produced on German-engineered KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press with fingerprint-resistant soft-touch matte lamination at our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility (10,000+8,000 smart factory expansion completed 2025). Daily output of 220,000+ luxury gift boxes under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

FAQ

Q: What ring sizes do the standard slots accommodate? A: Our standard 16mm ring slots accommodate US sizes 4–9 (EU 46–60) comfortably. We also offer 20mm wide slots for larger rings (US 10–13, EU 62–72). Custom slot widths and shapes are available — send us your ring size distribution and we’ll engineer the perfect fit.

Q: Will the velvet shed fibers onto the jewelry? A: No — our electrostatic nylon flocking process uses a high-strength water-based acrylic binder (formaldehyde-free) that achieves a pile pull-out resistance exceeding 1.5 N per fiber tuft. The velvet is tested for 10,000+ insertion cycles in our Martindale abrasion tester without bare-board exposure.

Q: Can you do custom colors beyond the standard options? A: Yes! We Pantone-match velvet flocking to any color in the formula guide. Custom color MOQ is 1000 units, with a one-time $150 color-matching fee (waived on orders over 5000 units). The lead time for a custom color sample is 10–12 working days.

Q: Does the magnetic closure keep the box securely shut during shipping? A: Absolutely. The N42 neodymium magnets (4.0+ N pull force per pair) hold the flap firmly closed. We ISTA 1A drop-test every design from 760mm on all corners, edges, and faces. For e-commerce shipping where boxes may experience rough handling, we offer N52 upgraded magnets with 5.8+ N pull force.

Q: Can the lower compartment be customized for specific jewelry pieces? A: Yes! We CNC-machine custom EVA foam inserts for the lower compartment to hold specific necklace chains, bracelet forms, watch cushions, or brooch pins. We can also add certificate pockets, care-card slots, or a separate hinged compartment for cleaning cloths.

Q: What’s the difference between your velvet and cheaper alternatives? A: Many factories use spray-on flocking (flocking fibers + adhesive sprayed onto board), which results in uneven density, rapid shedding, and a harsh feel. We use electrostatic flocking — nylon fibers are charged and oriented vertically into the adhesive by an electric field, producing dense, uniform coverage with a soft, plush hand-feel that lasts for years, not months.

Q: How are these boxes packaged for international shipping? A: Each box is individually poly-bagged, then nested in export-grade double-wall corrugated master cartons. Trays are packed separately to prevent compression of ring slots during transit. All pallets are heat-treated ISPM 15 compliant. We can also provide flat-packed boxes (customer assembles trays) for maximum container efficiency on large orders.

Q: What certifications do you hold? A: ISO 9001:2015 (quality management since 2018), FSC Chain-of-Custody (responsible forestry), High-Tech Enterprise (Guangdong provincial), and we comply with REACH, RoHS, and EN 71-3 (toy safety — relevant for children’s jewelry packaging).
