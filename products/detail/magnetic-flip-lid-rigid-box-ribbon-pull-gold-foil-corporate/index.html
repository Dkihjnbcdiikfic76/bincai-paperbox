FAQ

Q: What magnet strength do you recommend for a box this size? A: For boxes up to 200×150mm, 2× N52 12mm magnets provide reliable closure. For larger boxes or heavier contents, 4× magnets or larger 15mm magnets ensure the lid stays securely closed during handling and shipping.

Q: Can you match our brand’s specific gold color? A: Yes. We maintain an extensive foil library with 50+ gold variants — bright gold, matte gold, antique gold, champagne gold, and rose gold. We can also custom-order Pantone-matched foil for runs of 5,000+ units.

Q: Is the ribbon pull tab durable for repeated opening? A: The ribbon is anchored between the greyboard and wrap paper with industrial adhesive, then reinforced with a hidden stitching point. Our ribbon pulls are tested to 1,000+ open/close cycles without detachment.

Q: Can the interior be customized for specific products? A: Absolutely. We offer die-cut EVA foam trays, molded velvet inserts, satin-covered platforms, and multi-compartment cardstock dividers — all custom-designed to your product dimensions.

Q: What’s the difference between your rigid box and a folding magnetic box? A: Rigid boxes use 1600-2000gsm greyboard wrapped in custom paper — they don’t fold flat, giving them superior structural integrity and luxury weight. Folding magnetic boxes use thinner board and ship flat for lower freight costs. Rigid is recommended for premium corporate gifting; folding for e-commerce retail.
