Product Overview

A revolutionary dual-purpose kraft paper gift box that transforms into a planter after use. The removable seed-infused paper liner — embedded with a mix of wildflower seeds (poppy, daisy, cornflower, baby’s breath) — can be placed in soil, watered, and within 7–14 days sprouts into a living wildflower garden. The kraft outer box, printed with soy-based ink and assembled with water-based adhesive, is 100% biodegradable and home-compostable. Manufactured at Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., this box delivers a zero-waste unboxing experience that literally gives back to the earth.

Sowing Instructions (Printed Inside Lid)

  1. Remove seed paper liner from box
  2. Soak seed paper in water for 1 hour
  3. Place on moist potting soil in a pot or garden bed
  4. Cover with a thin layer of soil (5mm)
  5. Water daily, keep moist
  6. Sprouts appear in 7–14 days at 18–22°C

FAQ

Q: How long does the seed paper liner remain viable? A: When stored in a cool, dry environment (≤25°C, ≤60% RH), the seed paper liner maintains ≥85% germination rate for 18 months from manufacture. Each batch is date-stamped and germination-tested per ISTA protocols before release.

Q: Can you customize the seed mix for our brand? A: Yes. Standard is a 6-species wildflower blend (poppy, daisy, cornflower, baby’s breath, black-eyed Susan, calendula). Custom seed mixes are available — herbs (basil, cilantro, mint), vegetables (tomato, lettuce), or regional native wildflower mixes specific to your market. MOQ for custom seed blends is 2,000 units.

Q: Is the box truly home-compostable? A: Yes. The kraft outer box (FSC-certified, 350gsm, unbleached), soy-based ink, and water-based PVA adhesive are all ASTM D6400 certified for home composting — full biodegradation within 180 days in a home compost pile. No industrial composting facility required. The seed paper liner itself biodegrades in soil within 90 days (ASTM D5988 verified).

Q: Can you add a biodegradable window? A: Yes. A PLA (polylactic acid) biodegradable window can be added to showcase the product inside while maintaining 100% compostability. PLA is derived from renewable plant starch (corn or sugarcane) and meets ASTM D6400 standards for industrial composting.

Q: What’s the minimum order and lead time? A: Standard MOQ is 500 units, with 10–14 days production lead time plus shipping. Pre-production samples are provided free of charge within 5–7 days. For custom seed mixes, add 3–5 days for seed sourcing and blend verification. Trial orders and OEM/ODM arrangements are welcome.
