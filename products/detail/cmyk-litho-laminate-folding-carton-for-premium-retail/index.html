CMYK litho-laminate folding carton for premium retail display packaging — delivering photo-quality print on a rigid-feel substrate, from Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (23 years, founded 2003). This carton combines two packaging technologies into one premium solution: offset lithographic printing on 157gsm C2S (coated-two-sides) art paper, laminated to 1.5mm E-flute micro-corrugated board. The result is a folding carton with the visual impact of a luxury rigid box — 200-line screen CMYK printing with Pantone spot colors and UV coating on Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press — with the structural protection and flat-shipping efficiency of corrugated. The auto-lock bottom pops open instantly with tuck-top closure for quick assembly. Surface enhancement options include hot foil stamping (gold/silver/copper/rose gold), spot UV gloss coating, sculpted embossing, and soft-touch matte lamination. Our 18,000 sqm facility delivers 1.7M boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification.

Quality Control — 7-Stage Inspection

  1. Paper Inspection: 157gsm C2S brightness (92+ ISO), opacity (>94%), caliper, moisture content — per-roll QC
  2. Printing QC: Heidelberg inline spectrophotometer, Pantone ΔE ≤ 2.0, 200-line screen, dot gain control (15% ±3 at 50% tone)
  3. Lamination QC: Bond strength peel test (>4N/cm), no blistering or delamination, flatness check (no curl)
  4. Foil Stamping QC: Bobst registration ±0.15mm, foil adhesion tape test, coverage inspection under 10× magnification
  5. Die-Cutting QC: Dimensional accuracy (±0.3mm), crease line depth, auto-lock bottom function test (100%)
  6. Final Inspection: 100% visual under 5000K daylight, surface defect detection, print-to-cut registration check
  7. Packaging QC: Individual polybag, 5-ply corrugated master carton, drop test ISTA 1A, barcode verification

Sustainability

Bincai’s litho-laminate cartons are available with FSC-certified paper throughout the supply chain — from the C2S art paper to the corrugated substrate. Soy-based offset inks replace conventional petroleum-based inks. Water-based lamination adhesives eliminate solvent VOCs. The E-flute corrugated component is fully recyclable in standard paper streams; the laminate structure, while not easily separable by consumers, is recoverable in industrial paper recycling facilities. For brands prioritizing mono-material recyclability, we offer unlaminated FSC folding carton alternatives with direct print.

FAQ

Q: What’s the difference between litho-laminate and direct print? Litho-laminate prints on premium art paper first, then laminates it to corrugated board — achieving photo-quality 200-line resolution. Direct flexo printing on corrugated is limited to ~85-line screen and cannot match the color fidelity. Litho-laminate is ideal for retail display where visual impact drives purchase decisions.

Q: How flat do the cartons ship? Completely flat — the auto-lock bottom mechanism allows the carton to be stored and shipped flat, then pop open instantly at the fulfillment center. This saves 60–80% on shipping volume vs. pre-assembled rigid boxes.

Q: Can you match my brand’s specific Pantone color? Yes — the Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV prints CMYK + 1 Pantone spot color + UV coating in a single pass. We achieve Pantone ΔE ≤ 2.0 using inline spectrophotometer control.

Q: Is E-flute strong enough for shipping? E-flute micro-corrugated (1.5mm) is suitable for lightweight products up to 2kg and provides excellent crush resistance for retail display. For heavier items, we recommend B-flute (3mm) litho-laminate.

Q: Do you offer soft-touch finish? Yes — soft-touch matte lamination creates a suede-like tactile experience that elevates perceived product value. It also resists fingerprints and scratches better than gloss lamination.

Q: Can I order samples before production? Yes — pre-production samples with your artwork in 7–10 days. Sample fee credited against production order.
