The corrugated cosmetic subscription box is a high-performance ecommerce packaging solution engineered for beauty brands running monthly curation programs, skincare sampling kits, and cosmetic discovery boxes. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — a 23-year Foshan paper box specialist with 18,000 sqm of dual-factory production space — this mailer combines full-color CMYK flexo printing, a clean perforated tear strip for customer unboxing, and a self-locking crash-lock bottom that assembles in under three seconds without tape or glue.

Bincai runs KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses for premium printing, while the BHS 2.5m corrugator produces in-house double-wall board — reducing lead time and ensuring consistent flute quality. Every box ships flat to minimize freight cost (60-70% volume savings vs. pre-assembled) and is ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified.

Customization Options

  • Board Construction: B-flute (3mm) for standard weight or E-flute (1.5mm) for a premium, compact feel. Double-wall B+E combination available for heavy multi-product boxes exceeding 1.5kg
  • Printing: Full-color CMYK water-based flexo printing on white-top kraft liner for vivid brand graphics. Optional single-color kraft-print for an eco-minimal aesthetic
  • Surface Finishes: Matte or gloss aqueous overprint varnish for scuff resistance during transit. Spot UV accents on logo areas
  • Tear Strip: Perforated tear strip with reinforced start tab — engineered for a clean, frustration-free unboxing experience. Pull force calibrated to 8-12N for accessibility compliance
  • Bottom Closure: Self-locking crash-lock bottom (auto-bottom) — assembles instantly without tape, adhesive, or staples. Supports up to 2.5kg payload
  • Interior: Custom die-cut corrugated or molded pulp dividers to separate and protect individual products. Optional tissue paper, crinkle fill, or branded inner printing
  • Sizing: Fully custom dimensions from 150×100×50mm (sample kit) to 350×250×120mm (full curation box). ±1.5mm dimensional tolerance
