Product Overview

The Bincai Velvet Multi-Row Watch Display Box with 10 slots delivers museum-grade presentation for fine watch retailers and discerning collectors. Each box features a 3mm tempered glass viewing lid, deep plush velvet flocking throughout, removable watch pillows, and precision antique brass push-button latch hardware. Manufactured at our 18,000 sqm integrated facility in Foshan, Guangdong, Bincai leverages 23 years of paper box expertise and ISO 9001:2015 quality systems to produce over 1.7 million color boxes and 220,000+ gift boxes daily across KBA 1050 and Heidelberg 7+1 UV press lines.
