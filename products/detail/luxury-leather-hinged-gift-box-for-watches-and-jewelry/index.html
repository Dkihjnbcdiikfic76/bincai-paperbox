Product Overview

The Luxury Leather Hinged Gift Box combines the timeless elegance of fine leather craftsmanship with Guangdong Bincai’s precision manufacturing capabilities. Each box is built on a rigid greyboard core (1000–1400gsm, 1.5–2.5mm thickness) wrapped in premium PU leatherette or genuine Nappa leather in your choice of matte, pebbled, or croc-embossed textures. The hinged construction — available as a piano-style zinc alloy hinge or a continuous leatherette hinge — is rated for 10,000+ open/close cycles, ensuring years of daily use for watch collectors, jewelry retailers, and luxury brands.

Inside, the box features hand-applied velvet flocking (0.8–1.5mm pile), satin, or suede-finish microfiber lining in custom Pantone colors. CNC die-cut EVA or PU foam inserts cradle watches, rings, bracelets, necklaces, and earrings with surgical precision — every cavity is routed to your product’s exact dimensions. Optional LED lighting (CR2032-powered, 80+ hours) activates automatically when the lid opens via a magnetic reed switch, creating a boutique unboxing experience that transforms product presentation into a brand moment.

Produced in our 18,000 sqm ISO 9001:2015 and FSC-certified facility on Heidelberg 7+1 UV and KBA 1050 4-color offset presses, with Bobst flatbed hot stamping for multi-level foil + embossing in a single pass. From the first sample to the final master carton, every step — printing, die-cutting, foil stamping, interior lining, insert routing, magnet installation, and final QC — happens under one roof in Foshan, Guangdong, ensuring consistent quality and 12–18 day lead times.

FAQ

Q: What’s the difference between PU leatherette and genuine leather for these boxes? A: PU leatherette offers 90%+ of the look and feel of genuine leather at roughly 40% of the cost — with superior color consistency, scratch resistance, and availability in a wider range of textures (matte, pebbled, croc-embossed, carbon fiber pattern). Genuine Nappa leather provides the authentic grain, natural patina development over time, and the unmistakable leather scent that luxury watch brands often prefer. We offer both and will recommend based on your brand positioning and budget.

Q: Can the LED lighting be customized — different colors, brightness, or trigger mechanism? A: Yes. Standard configuration is 3× warm white (3000K) LED strip powered by CR2032 battery with magnetic reed switch triggering on lid open. Custom options include: cool white (6000K), RGB single-color (red, blue, green, amber), dimmable brightness, motion sensor activation (no reed switch), or USB-rechargeable lithium battery module. Contact us with your lighting requirements — our engineering team will propose the optimal configuration.

Q: What’s the maximum number of watch/jewelry compartments in a single box? A: We can CNC-route up to 12 individual compartments in a single insert — for example: 6 watch cushions + 2 ring slots + 2 bracelet grooves + 2 necklace hooks with anti-tangle channels. Box dimensions scale accordingly (maximum 350×250×80mm). For larger collections, we recommend our multi-tier or drawer-style boxes rather than cramming compartments into a single level.

Q: How long does it take from order to delivery? A: Digital color proof within 48 hours. Physical sample (white-glue mockup) in 5–7 working days — this includes the exact leather/leatherette, lining, foil stamping, and insert configuration you’ll receive in production. Mass production lead time is 12–18 working days after sample approval. Sea freight to major ports (Los Angeles, Rotterdam, Dubai, Sydney) adds 18–30 days; air freight available for urgent orders.

Q: Can I order different exterior colors and textures within the same production batch? A: Yes. You can mix up to 4 different leatherette colors/textures within a single MOQ of 500 units total (e.g., 125 black croc + 125 navy matte + 125 burgundy pebbled + 125 cream matte). The minimum per color variant is 125 units. For more than 4 variants, we recommend splitting into separate production batches to maintain efficiency.
