Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) brings 23 years of premium rigid box manufacturing excellence to this hexagonal tea chest gift box — a distinctive six-sided design with a solid wood carrying handle that elevates tea gifting to a luxury experience. The hexagonal geometry creates a striking shelf presence that standard rectangular boxes cannot match, while the magnetic closure and velvet-flocked interior ensure tea bags, loose-leaf pouches, or tea accessories are presented with gallery-quality refinement.

Manufactured in our 18,000 sqm dual-factory in Foshan, Guangdong, each box leverages our KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press for precise decorative printing and the Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press for gold-foil accents and spot UV highlights on the lid panels. The solid wood handle — available in beech, bamboo, or walnut finish — is precision-machined and securely affixed with reinforced mounting. Inside, a CNC-milled EVA foam insert (45 Shore A, ±0.5mm tolerance) provides perfectly sized compartments for tea bags, sachets, honey sticks, or tea tools, while electrostatic velvet flocking in black, cream, or burgundy provides a luxurious tactile experience.

FAQ

Q: Can the hexagonal tea chest accommodate different tea bag sizes and formats?

A: Yes. The CNC-milled EVA foam insert is custom-configured to your exact tea bag dimensions — from standard rectangular bags (60×50mm) to pyramid sachets and round tea cakes. We can mix different cavity sizes within the same insert for sampler collections (e.g., 4 large cavities for loose-leaf pouches + 8 small cavities for tea bags). Simply send us your tea bag dimensions and desired layout, and we’ll design the insert accordingly.

Q: What wood options are available for the handle?

A: Three standard options: solid beech (natural light tone, most economical), bamboo (eco-friendly, FSC-certified), and walnut (dark premium finish, highest perceived value). All handles are sanded smooth, sealed with a food-safe clear coat, and load-tested to 12kg. Custom wood species and finishes are available at 1,000+ MOQ.

Q: Is the hexagonal shape more expensive than a standard rectangular box?

A: The hexagonal shape requires a custom die-cutting die (one-time tooling cost, typically $150–300 depending on size) and slightly more assembly labor due to the six-sided construction. The per-unit cost premium is approximately 15–25% over an equivalent rectangular rigid box. However, the distinctive shelf presence typically delivers 3–5× higher perceived value, making it an excellent investment for premium tea brands. Our team can provide a cost comparison for your specific size requirements.

Q: Can the tea chest be used for tea accessories instead of tea bags?

A: Absolutely. Many of our clients use the hexagonal tea chest for tea accessories — glass infuser bottles, tea spoons, honey dippers, tea strainers, and porcelain cups. The CNC foam insert can be configured with precision cavities for any accessory shape. We’ve also produced model with a removable foam layer, allowing the chest to double as a keepsake box after the tea is consumed.

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity and lead time?

A: MOQ is 500 units for standard sizes using our existing hexagonal tooling. For custom sizes requiring new die fabrication, MOQ is 1,000 units. Standard lead time is 10–14 working days. Free pre-production samples are provided within 5–7 working days. Rush orders (7–10 days) are available subject to line capacity.
