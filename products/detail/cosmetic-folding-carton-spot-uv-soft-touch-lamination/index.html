Product Overview

This premium cosmetic folding carton combines spot UV varnish with soft-touch matte lamination to create a striking tactile contrast — glossy brand marks floating on a velvety matte surface. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (established 2003, Foshan, China), the carton is produced on German-engineered KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses for uncompromising print quality. The dual-finish approach — spot UV highlighting key design elements while soft-touch lamination wraps the entire surface — is the industry standard for luxury skincare, fragrance, and cosmetics brands seeking premium shelf presence. With daily capacity of 1.7 million boxes from our 18,000 sqm facility, Bincai delivers consistent quality from startup brand trials to multinational volume runs.
