Product Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing’s hexagonal lid-and-base gift box is a standout for Christmas collections and seasonal gift packaging. The six-sided geometry departs from standard rectangular boxes, creating instant shelf differentiation. A wraparound satin ribbon closure adds gift-ready elegance, while the rigid greyboard construction protects contents during retail handling and shipping. Manufactured in our 18,000 sqm ISO 9001 & FSC certified factory on KBA 1050 and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses, each box benefits from 23 years of premium packaging expertise.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color offset + Heidelberg 7+1 UV for premium finishes — ΔE ≤ 2.0 consistency
  • Board: 1200–1600gsm greyboard core, 2.0mm caliper for rigid hexagonal panels
  • Wrap: 157–250gsm art paper with full-bleed CMYK + up to 2 Pantone spot colors
  • Ribbon: 15mm/25mm satin ribbon (gold/silver/red/green/custom), heat-sealed ends to prevent fraying
  • Foil: Hot stamping on lid center and edges — gold, silver, rose gold, holographic, matte gold
  • Interior: Velvet flocking (0.5–1.5mm pile) or satin lining in custom colors, die-cut insert available
  • Surface Finishes: Matte/gloss lamination, soft-touch velvet matte, spot UV varnish, aqueous coating
  • Daily Rigid Box Output: 220,000+ units across all box styles

Design Features

  • Hexagonal Geometry: 6 equal panels with 120° corners — precision die-cutting ensures consistent facet alignment
  • Wraparound Ribbon: Full-perimeter satin ribbon with bow tie closure, pre-attached at factory for retail-ready presentation
  • Magnetic Option: Hidden N35 neodymium magnets (≥4.5N pull) under ribbon for secure closure without compromising aesthetics
  • Holiday Finishes: Snowflake pattern embossing, holly motif foil stamping, winter scene full-bleed printing
  • Window Option: Die-cut window with PET/PVC film for product visibility — ideal for candles, ornaments, chocolates
  • Personalization: Individual name/logo debossing on lid, foil-stamped year dates for annual collections

Applications

  • Christmas Confectionery: Premium chocolates, panettone, gingerbread, stollen — food-grade interior with grease-resistant coating
  • Holiday Candles: Scented jar candles, pillar candles, tea light gift sets — snug die-cut insert prevents glass-on-glass contact
  • Jewelry Gifting: Earring sets, charm bracelets, pendant necklaces — velvet-lined tray with earring card slots
  • Corporate Holiday Gifts: Branded appreciation boxes for employees and clients — custom logo + seasonal messaging
  • Advent Calendar: Multi-cavity tray with numbered compartments, magnetic lid for daily opening
  • Ornament Storage: Die-cut foam insert for glass baubles, personalized family name on lid
