Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — board grammage, ribbon tensile strength, foil adhesive grade verification
  2. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤2.0 target
  3. Die-Cutting Verification — ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance on greyboard panels
  4. Foil Stamping QC — adhesion test on first-off and every 1,000th unit, ±0.15mm registration check
  5. Assembly QC — tray alignment, ribbon integrity, corner wrapping precision
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.0 (minor) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — random sample pull, full functional test under D65 lighting, ribbon tie/un-tie cycle

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom wedding favor boxes?

A: MOQ is 500 units per design. For simpler constructions without foil stamping, we can accommodate 300-unit trial orders. Sample turnaround is 5-7 days with free pre-production samples.

Q: Can the ribbon color match my wedding theme?

A: Yes. We stock satin ribbon in 250+ Pantone-matched colors and can custom-dye for orders above 5,000 units. Provide your Pantone code and we’ll match within ΔE ≤2.0.

Q: What customization options are available for the interior tray?

A: We offer custom die-cut EVA foam (any cavity shape), flocked PET vacuum-formed trays, satin-lined rigid dividers, and removable lift-out trays. Foam densities from 20-80 kg/m³ are available depending on product weight.

Q: How long does production take?

A: Standard production lead time is 10-14 days after artwork approval. Rush orders can be expedited to 7 days for an additional 20% surcharge. Sampling takes 5-7 days.

Q: Can you produce in different sizes for different favor assortments?

A: Absolutely. We manufacture custom sizes from 100×100×40mm to 350×250×100mm per tray. The two-tier configuration allows mixing tray depths — e.g., a shallow 25mm top tray for chocolates and a deeper 50mm bottom tray for larger items.
