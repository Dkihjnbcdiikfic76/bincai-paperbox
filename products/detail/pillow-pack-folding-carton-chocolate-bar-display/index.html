FAQ

Q: Can the pillow-pack carton be produced with compostable or biodegradable materials? A: Yes. We offer FSC-certified uncoated kraft with water-based inks for fully compostable pillow boxes, and PLA (polylactic acid) window film derived from corn starch as a compostable alternative to PET windows. The auto-lock bottom uses mechanical interlocking — no glue or adhesive — making the entire structure plastic-free and industrially compostable (EN 13432 certified). MOQ for compostable variants starts at 3,000 units.

Q: How does the pillow shape affect filling and packing line efficiency? A: The pillow-pack carton is designed for manual or semi-automatic filling — the auto-lock bottom pops open in under 3 seconds, and the contoured top tucks closed without tape or adhesive. For high-speed automated packing lines, we can engineer the top closure as a tuck-flap with optional tear-strip for tamper evidence. The flattened cartons stack flat on standard pallets and feed through semi-automatic carton erectors with minor adjustments. Our engineering team provides packing line compatibility assessments during the sample phase.

Q: What food safety certifications do the inks and coatings carry? A: Our standard food-grade inks (Siegwerk and Flint Group) comply with FDA 21 CFR for indirect food contact and EU Regulation 1935/2004. Low-migration UV inks are available for products where the chocolate or food item directly contacts the printed surface. Water-based aqueous coatings are used as a functional barrier between printed ink and food product. Full material safety data sheets (MSDS) and migration test certificates are provided with every food-grade order.

Q: Can the pillow shape be adapted for non-chocolate products like cosmetics or electronics? A: Absolutely. The pillow-shaped contoured design works exceptionally well for soap bars, bath bombs, compact electronics (earbuds, charging cases), jewelry, candles, and cosmetic compacts. The same die-cutting precision and printing quality apply — we simply adjust the interior dimensions and add appropriate inserts (foam, pulp tray, cardboard divider) based on your product. The pillow silhouette is highly versatile and creates a premium unboxing experience across categories.
