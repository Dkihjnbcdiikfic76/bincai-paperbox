Magnetic Closure Rigid Candle Gift Box with Satin Ribbon — Premium Home Fragrance Packaging

Present your artisan candles in packaging as refined as the product inside. Bincai’s magnetic closure rigid candle gift box combines structural integrity with luxury aesthetics, creating a memorable unboxing experience that elevates your brand and justifies premium pricing.

Key Features

  • Two-Piece Rigid Construction: 1.5–2.0mm greyboard wrapped in premium art paper with matte or gloss lamination for a sturdy, high-end feel.
  • Magnetic Flip Lid: Neodymium magnets embedded in the lid and base create a satisfying closure with tactile snap — no cheap glue tabs or friction fits.
  • Satin Ribbon Pull: Color-matched satin ribbon makes opening effortless and adds a boutique elegance touch.
  • Custom Die-Cut Foam Insert: Securely cradles candle jars (standard 8oz, 12oz, 16oz sizes) preventing movement during transit and keeping the label facing forward.
  • Custom Foil Stamping: Brand logo, scent name, or decorative pattern in gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic foil on lid and/or sides.
