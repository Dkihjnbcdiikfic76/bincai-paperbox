Textured linen-wrapped rigid gift box with satin ribbon tie closure, purpose-designed for premium apparel and luxury fashion brands by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — a Diamond Member on Made-in-China.com with 23 years of manufacturing excellence (founded 2003). The tactile linen surface and hand-tied ribbon closure create an elevated unboxing experience that reflects the quality of the garments within. Produced on KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses across our 18,000 sqm facility (10,000+8,000 smart factory, 2025 expansion). Daily output: 1.7 million color boxes + 220,000+ gift boxes.

Applications

  • Luxury fashion and designer apparel gift packaging for retail and ecommerce
  • Premium shirt, tie, scarf, and accessory presentation boxes
  • Bridal and wedding attire packaging with elegant ribbon closure detailing
  • Seasonal collection launch boxes for fashion brands and boutiques
  • Corporate gifting for executive apparel, branded merchandise, and VIP presents

Customization Options

  • Wrap materials: natural Belgian linen, cotton canvas, silk-touch woven textile, or custom-supplied fabric
  • Wrap colors: 30+ standard options including ivory, charcoal, navy, blush, sage, and custom Pantone
  • Ribbon closure: double-face satin (15mm/25mm widths), grosgrain, or velvet ribbon
  • Ribbon colors matched to wrap, contrast accent, or brand palette
  • Debossing on lid: blind deboss or hot foil stamping in gold, silver, rose gold, copper
  • Interior: tissue paper lining, suede-feel flocking, or printed pattern paper
  • Box dimensions: length 200–500mm, width 150–400mm, depth 40–150mm
  • Optional magnetic closure beneath ribbon for dual secure closure

Production Specifications

  • Material: Rigid greyboard 1200–1600gsm core, hand-wrapped in textured linen
  • Ribbon: Double-face satin polyester or cotton grosgrain, 15–25mm width
  • Wrapping: Precision hand-wrap with ±1mm corner mitre tolerance
  • Debossing: Hot foil stamping on KBA 1050 4-color press or Heidelberg 7+1 UV
  • Closure: Satin ribbon tie (bow) with optional internal magnetic snap
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015 quality management, FSC-C147399 chain of custody

Quality & Capacity

Every linen-wrapped rigid box is hand-inspected for fabric tension uniformity, corner fold precision, ribbon alignment, and debossing registration. Our 18,000 sqm ISO 9001:2015 certified facility combines high-speed automated greyboard production with skilled hand-wrapping stations to deliver luxury quality at competitive pricing. With 23 years of experience and a daily output capacity of over 220,000 gift boxes, Bincai is the trusted manufacturing partner for fashion brands worldwide requiring consistent premium packaging.
