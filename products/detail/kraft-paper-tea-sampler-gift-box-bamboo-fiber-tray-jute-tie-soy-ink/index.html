Product Overview

The kraft paper tea sampler gift box with bamboo fiber compartment tray and jute tie closure is a fully compostable packaging solution engineered for premium loose-leaf tea brands, herbal tea gift sets, and specialty tea subscription boxes. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing (founded 2003, Foshan, 18,000 sqm, ISO 9001:2015 & FSC-C147399 certified), this box combines unbleached 350gsm kraft board with a molded bamboo fiber compartment tray — eliminating plastic at every touchpoint. Soy-based inks print directly on kraft with vibrant, low-VOC color, and a natural jute twine tie replaces synthetic ribbons. Daily output reaches 1.7 million boxes on KBA Rapida 105 and Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV presses.

Production Specifications

  • Press: KBA Rapida 105 4-color for soy-ink kraft printing; Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV for coated accents
  • Die-Cutting: Bobst SP 102 BMA/E for kraft board; custom tooling for bamboo fiber trays
