Product Overview

Our kraft paper soap sleeve box combines minimalist eco-design with product visibility — the die-cut window lets customers see and smell your artisanal soap, bath bomb, or natural skincare product before purchase while the sleeve format keeps packaging material to an absolute minimum. Printed with soy-based offset inks for vibrant yet fully biodegradable graphics, each sleeve is manufactured from FSC-certified kraft board using water-based adhesives. The result: a 100% recyclable, home-compostable package that aligns with the values of eco-conscious brands and consumers. Produced in our ISO 9001-certified facility with daily capacity of 1.7 million boxes, Bincai delivers sustainable packaging at competitive scale.

Design Features

  • Minimal Sleeve Format: Open-ended sleeve slides over the product — minimal material, maximum impact
  • Die-Cut Window: Custom-shaped window cutout reveals product color, texture, and ingredients

  • FSC-Certified Kraft Board: Responsibly sourced 350gsm kraft paper with natural brown aesthetic

Why This Kraft Soap Sleeve

  • Die-Cut Window Visibility: Unlike generic tuck-end boxes that hide the product entirely, the custom die-cut window lets customers see color, marbling, and texture before purchase — proven to increase “pick-up” rate by 35% in A/B retail testing.
