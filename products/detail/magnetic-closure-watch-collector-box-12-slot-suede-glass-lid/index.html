Product Overview

The Magnetic Closure Watch Collector Box with 12-Slot Suede Tray combines gallery-quality display with secure, dust-protected storage for serious watch collections. The full-width tempered glass lid (5mm, polished edges) provides a crystal-clear view of all 12 timepieces while protecting against dust, humidity, and accidental contact. Each of the 12 removable suedette pillows (70×40×35mm) is individually wrapped and sized to accommodate watches from slim dress pieces (32mm case) to oversized dive watches (52mm case). Four N52 neodymium magnets create a satisfying, secure closure with zero lid sag, while the stainless steel hinges include a 90° stop mechanism that prevents over-opening. Whether displayed on a dresser, used as retail point-of-sale presentation, or gifted as a corporate milestone award, this collector box communicates the value of the watches inside.

FAQ

Q: Will my oversized dive watches (e.g., 47mm Panerai, 44mm Rolex Deepsea) fit? A: Yes — the 12-slot configuration accommodates case diameters up to 52mm. The removable pillows can be repositioned to create additional clearance for watches with crown guards or helium escape valves. For collections heavily weighted toward oversized watches, we recommend the 8-slot configuration with larger 55×40mm pillows.

Q: Can you add a lock? A: Yes — we offer a lock-and-key hardware upgrade (brass or zinc alloy, 2 keys included) installed in the front panel for an additional $1.20–2.50/unit depending on lock quality. Combination locks are also available.
