Velvet Necklace Pendant Display Box with LED Light — Luxury Jewelry Packaging

Elevate your jewelry presentation with Bincai’s velvet necklace pendant display box featuring an integrated LED spotlight system. Each box combines premium materials, precision manufacturing, and thoughtful design to create an unforgettable unboxing experience.

Key Features

  • Integrated LED Spotlight: Battery-powered warm white LED illuminates the pendant when the lid opens, drawing attention to the jewelry piece. Auto-off magnetic sensor conserves battery.
  • Premium Velvet Flocking: Soft, non-abrasive velvet lining in black, navy, burgundy, or custom colors protects delicate chain and pendant surfaces from scratches.
  • Satin Interior Pillow: Removable satin-covered insert secures the necklace pendant at the display angle while the chain tucks neatly into a rear compartment.
  • Custom Gold Foil Stamping: Your brand logo, monogram, or pattern embossed in gold, silver, rose gold, or holographic foil on the exterior lid.
  • Magnetic Flip Lid: Strong neodymium magnets ensure a satisfying snap-close feel and prevent accidental opening during transport.
