FAQ

Q: How long does the LED battery last? A: The standard CR2032 lithium coin-cell provides approximately 60–80 hours of continuous illumination or 6–12 months of intermittent retail use (touch-activated, ~20 activations/day). AAA battery modules extend runtime to 200+ hours. Batteries are user-replaceable. We can also supply spare batteries with bulk orders.

Q: What velvet color options are available for the interior? A: Our standard velvet flocking colors include black, navy blue, burgundy, dark grey, charcoal, cream, and white. Custom Pantone-matched velvet colors are available for orders above 3,000 units. The velvet pile height is 1.0–1.5 mm with a uniform, non-shedding surface. We also offer satin polyester (190T–210T) in 20+ colors and suede-like microfiber alternatives.

Q: Can you engineer a custom foam insert for a specific jewelry shape? A: Yes. Our in-house tooling team creates custom EVA foam or flocked-foam inserts precisely die-cut to your jewelry dimensions. The process: (1) you provide the jewelry sample or 3D CAD file; (2) we produce a foam insert prototype within 5–7 days; (3) after your approval, the BOBST Autoplaten die-cutting tool is fabricated and production begins. One-time tooling cost applies and is typically amortized across the order quantity.

Q: What is the MOQ for custom jewelry shapes and sizes? A: Standard sizes (ring, necklace, bracelet, earring boxes) carry an MOQ of 500 units per design. Fully custom dimensions and custom foam inserts carry an MOQ of 1,000 units per SKU. Mixed-SKU orders are accepted; contact us for consolidated shipping options.

Q: How quickly can I receive pre-production samples? A: Free pre-production samples ship within 5–7 business days for standard configurations. Custom sizes or complex LED layouts may require 7–10 business days. Express shipping (DHL/UPS/FedEx) typically delivers in 3–5 days worldwide. Sample cost is fully credited against your production order.

Q: What is the lead time for a 10,000-unit order? A: Standard production lead time is 12–20 days after sample approval for orders up to 10,000 units. Larger quantities scale proportionally: 10,000–50,000 units: 20–30 days; 50,000+ units: 30–45 days. Our daily rigid box output of 220,000+ units ensures we can accommodate rush orders — contact us for expedited scheduling.
