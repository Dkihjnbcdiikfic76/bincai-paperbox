Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What reverse tuck end (RTE) board grade should I choose for cosmetic packaging?

We recommend 350gsm SBS for premium cosmetics and skincare — it provides the brightest white printing surface with excellent stiffness for shelf display and unboxing. 350gsm CCNB is a cost-effective alternative with a grey-brown reverse for budget-conscious lines. For eco-positioned brands, 18-24pt FSC-certified coated unbleached kraft provides a natural, organic look with full offset printability. We stock all three and can advise based on your product weight, retail environment, and sustainability goals.

Q: Can you handle complex multi-finish combinations on a single carton?

Yes — this is where our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press excels. In a single pass we can combine CMYK printing, Pantone spot colors, aqueous coating, and UV spot varnish. Post-press we can add soft-touch matte lamination, hot foil stamping (gold/silver/rose gold/holographic), embossing, debossing, and textured patterns. All finishes are applied with precise registration — foil-to-print alignment within ±0.1mm.

Q: How do soft-touch and matte finishes affect the unboxing experience?

Soft-touch lamination creates a velvety, premium tactile feel that consumers associate with luxury skincare brands. It resists fingerprints and scuffs better than matte lamination, making it ideal for retail display where boxes are handled repeatedly. Both finishes accept foil stamping and spot UV. We recommend soft-touch for serum, cream, and premium mask cartons; matte for clean, minimalist brands. Both are tested for 200+ rub cycles without gloss-up.

Q: What window options do you offer for product visibility?

We offer die-cut window patching with PET (crystal clear, recyclable), PVC (economical), or PLA (compostable bioplastic) film — suitable for any custom shape including rectangles, ovals, brand logo outlines, and contour windows. Our Bobst die-cutter achieves ±0.3mm window-to-print registration. For skincare products, PET is our standard recommendation for the clearest view. For eco-conscious brands, PLA windows paired with FSC board provide a fully sustainable window box.

Q: What is the typical lead time for custom folding carton orders?

Pre-production samples ship in 5-7 working days (digital proof with actual board stock). After sample approval, mass production takes 10-15 working days — complex finishing (foil + embossing + window) adds 2-3 days. Die-cut tooling requires 3-5 working days for new sizes (one-time). Sea freight: 12-15 days to US West Coast, 22-28 days to Europe. Air freight available for urgent orders (3-5 days). We ship FOB Shenzhen/Guangzhou and can arrange door-to-door DDP service. MOQ starts at 500 units for standard configurations.

Q: Are your inks and materials safe for direct cosmetic contact?

Yes. We use low-migration UV and conventional offset inks that meet EU Directive 94/62/EC and FDA 21 CFR for indirect food and cosmetic contact. For products with direct cosmetic contact (loose powders, unwrapped items), we can specify food-grade inks and barrier coatings. All materials are phthalate-free and REACH-compliant. We provide material safety documentation including MSDS and migration test reports on request.
