FAQ

Q: Are soy-based inks really as vibrant as conventional inks? A: Yes. Modern soy-based CMYK inks achieve comparable color density and gamut to petroleum inks — our KBA 1050 and Heidelberg 7+1 presses deliver 175 LPI precision with both. Soy inks actually produce brighter colors on uncoated kraft because they penetrate less and sit more on the surface.

Q: Is the entire box compostable? A: With our full eco configuration — FSC kraft board, soy ink, water-based coating, and starch adhesive — the box is industrially compostable to EN 13432. If you choose PLA window film, it’s also compostable in industrial facilities. For home compostability, we recommend the glassine paper window or no window.

Q: Can you do small runs for a startup brand? A: Our MOQ is 500 units — accessible for indie and startup beauty brands. We also offer digital printing for runs of 100-300 units (higher per-unit cost but no plate charges) for market testing and limited editions.

Q: Will kraft board warp or degrade in humid bathroom environments? A: Our 300-450gsm kraft board with water-based coating provides good humidity resistance for normal bathroom use. For extremely humid conditions (steam rooms, wet zones), we recommend upgrading to 450gsm with double-sided aqueous coating.

Q: How do I communicate our brand’s eco-story on the packaging? A: We include FSC and OK Compost certification logos on your carton at no extra charge. We also recommend a short sustainability statement printed on the inside or bottom panel — our design team can help craft messaging that resonates with eco-conscious consumers.
