Product Overview

A premium custom rigid gift box featuring a satin ribbon closure and elegant gold foil embossed logo — the signature luxury packaging solution for high-end brands. Handcrafted in our dedicated rigid box production line at Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., each box combines 2.5mm greyboard rigidity with premium art paper wrapping and precision foil stamping. Production Quality & Workflow

Every rigid box passes through 15 quality control checkpoints across our dedicated production line:

  1. Raw Material Inspection — Board caliper (±0.1mm), paper GSM verification, ribbon tensile strength test
  2. Die-Cutting QC — Edge precision ±0.5mm, crease depth calibration, waste stripping integrity
  3. Board Assembly — Greyboard lamination bond strength (≥2.5 N/cm² peel test), corner squareness (90° ±0.5°)
  4. Wrapping & Gluing — Surface bubble detection, seam overlap ≥12mm, adhesive coverage ≥95%
  5. Magnet Insertion — Polarity verification, pull-force test (≥3N for standard flap), recess depth consistency
  6. Foil Stamping — Heated brass die alignment (±0.3mm), foil adhesion rub-test (50 cycles minimum), color consistency under D65 lighting
  7. Embossing Registration — Multi-level depth measurement (laser profilometer), emboss-to-print register (±0.2mm)
  8. Ribbon Installation — Thread tension test (1.5-2.5N), bow symmetry check, color match against Pantone swatch
  9. Lid-Base Fit — Gap tolerance ±1mm, hinge smoothness (opens/closes without resistance), flat-close verification
  10. Print Colorimetry — X-Rite spectrophotometer measurement against brand Pantone references, ΔE ≤ 2.0
  11. Interior Fit — Insert alignment, foam/EVA density verification, flocking adhesion test
  12. Drop Test — 1.2m free-fall drop onto concrete (6 faces, 3 edges, 1 corner), box must protect contents
  13. Environmental — 48-hour humidity chamber (85% RH, 40°C), 24-hour cold cycle (-20°C), adhesive and board integrity verified
  14. Final Visual — Inspection under D65 (daylight), TL84 (store), and A (incandescent) lighting conditions
  15. Pack-Out Audit — Polybag seal, barcode scan, carton label verification, pallet stacking test

Production timeline: Dieline approval (24h) → Digital proof (48h) → Sample production (3-5 days) → Customer approval → Mass production (10-15 days) → QC & shipping.
