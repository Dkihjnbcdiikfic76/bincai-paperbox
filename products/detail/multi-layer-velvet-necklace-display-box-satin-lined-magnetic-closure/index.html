Multi-layer velvet necklace display box with satin-lined compartments and magnetic flap closure — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. Purpose-built for fine jewelry brands, artisan jewelers, and luxury retailers, this box presents necklaces, pendants, and chains in a layered display format that allows each piece to be viewed independently. The exterior is wrapped in premium flocked velvet with gold or silver hot foil branding, while the interior features satin-lined compartments with die-cut necklace holders. The magnetic flap closure provides a satisfying premium closing experience. Produced across our 18,000 sqm factory with 1.7M daily output capacity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What velvet colors do you offer? A: Standard velvet colors include black, navy, royal blue, burgundy, emerald green, ivory, blush pink, charcoal grey, and chocolate brown. Custom Pantone-matched velvet is available for orders of 3,000+ units with a dye-matching fee and 5-7 extra working days.

Q: Can you create custom compartment layouts for my specific jewelry pieces? A: Yes. We design custom die-cut inserts based on your jewelry dimensions — necklace chain lengths, pendant sizes, clasp types. Provide your jewelry samples or CAD drawings, and we’ll create a custom insert layout. The die charge is included in orders of 1,000+ units.

Q: How is the velvet applied — will it peel or shed? A: Our velvet flocking is electrostatically applied to a pre-glued paper wrap, then heat-cured. This creates a durable bond that passes 500+ open/close cycles without peeling. Unlike spray-on flocking, our process produces minimal shedding — we test with adhesive tape to ensure no loose fibers transfer.

Q: What are the MOQ and lead times? A: Standard MOQ is 300-500 units. Lead time is 15-20 working days for standard configurations, 20-25 working days with custom inserts. Rush orders available at 10-12 working days with surcharge.

Q: Can you include LED lighting inside the box? A: Yes. We offer battery-powered LED strip lighting that auto-illuminates when the box is opened. The LED module is embedded in the lid or base with a magnetic reed switch. Power is from CR2032 coin cells (replaceable) with 30+ hours of illumination. LED adds $1.50-3.00 per unit and 3-5 working days.

Q: Is the box gift-ready? A: Yes. Every box comes individually poly-bagged and can be optionally packaged in a kraft outer sleeve or gift bag. We also offer custom tissue paper, ribbon, and hang tags as add-on services.

Q: Can you hot foil stamp my logo on velvet? A: Yes. Hot foil stamping on velvet produces a luxurious, permanent imprint. We use heated brass dies (120-140°C) with metallic foil that bonds into the velvet fibers. Available in gold, silver, rose gold, copper, and holographic. We recommend die sizes up to 80×80mm for best results on velvet.
