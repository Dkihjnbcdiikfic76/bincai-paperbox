FAQ

Q: What’s the difference between E-flute, B-flute, and BC-flute for a gift box? A: E-flute (1.2mm, ~90 flutes/m) provides the finest printing surface — best for luxury cosmetics and retail shelf display. B-flute (2.5mm, ~50 flutes/m) offers crush resistance plus good print quality — ideal for e-commerce gift boxes that ship directly. BC double-wall (6mm, B+C combined) delivers maximum protection for heavy or fragile items like wine bottles and electronics. F-flute (0.8mm, ~125 flutes/m) is our ultra-fine option for compact premium packaging.

Q: Can embossing and spot UV be combined on the same box? A: Yes. We can register embossing to the printed design and apply spot UV in adjacent or overlapping areas. The Heidelberg 7+1 UV press applies UV coating inline with printing for perfect registration. Embossing runs as a separate post-print process on a dedicated Bobst foiling/embossing press.

Q: Is the box food-safe if I’m packaging chocolates or tea? A: Food-safe options are available. We use low-migration soy-based inks and water-based aqueous coatings compliant with EU 1935/2004 and FDA 21 CFR for indirect food contact. An inner food-grade PET/PLA liner or bag can be added for direct food contact.

Q: What’s the MOQ for custom sizes and custom print? A: MOQ is 500 units for standard sizes with custom CMYK print. Custom structural designs requiring new cutting dies have MOQ of 1,000+ with a one-time die fee of $80-200 depending on complexity.

Q: How do you ensure consistent branding across repeat orders? A: We maintain digital color profiles for every brand in our color management database. Each repeat order is matched to the original Pantone/CMYK reference with ΔE ≤ 2.0 tolerance. Printing plates are archived for 24 months for seamless reorders.
