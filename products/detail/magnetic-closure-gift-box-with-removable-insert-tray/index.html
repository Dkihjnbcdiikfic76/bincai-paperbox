Product Overview

A premium magnetic closure gift box featuring a removable insert tray — the ultimate packaging solution for multi-product gift sets, sampler collections, and curated brand experiences. The removable tray allows customers to lift out the entire product array in one elegant motion, often revealing a second layer beneath for dramatic unboxing. Manufactured at Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) in our 18,000 sqm dual-factory complex (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory), each box combines Heidelberg 7+1 UV press printing with precision hand assembly and rigorous quality control. With 23 years of rigid box expertise and 1.7 million boxes produced daily, Bincai serves luxury brands across 30+ countries.

Applications

  • Luxury Skincare Gift Sets: Multi-product serum, cream, and treatment collections with individual cavity inserts
  • Fragrance Discovery Sets: Curated mini fragrance collections with foam or velvet tray cavities

  • Corporate Gift Boxes: Executive gift sets with branded removable tray for pens, accessories, and premium items

  • Artisan Chocolate Assortments: Premium chocolate collections with food-safe tray cavities
  • Limited-Edition Brand Collaborations: Special release packaging with collectible removable tray

Production Specifications

  • Press: Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press — CMYK + spot color + UV coating in single pass
  • KBA Offset: KBA Rapida 105 4-color at 15,000 sheets/hour for high-volume wrap sheet production
  • Board Core: Rigid greyboard 1200–1600gsm, PVA cold-glued with precision-mitered corners (±0.3mm)
  • Tray Die-Cutting: Bobst flatbed die-cutter — cavity tolerance ±0.5mm, crease depth calibrated to board thickness
  • Tray Flocking: Electrostatic velvet flocking at 1.0–1.5mm fiber length, ≥95% coverage density
  • Foil Stamping: Bobst SP 102 BMA — temperature 110–130°C, dwell 0.5–1.0s, multi-pass for multi-color foil
  • Assembly: Semi-automatic rigid box wrapping line + manual tray insertion + magnet alignment QC
  • Daily Capacity: 220,000+ rigid magnetic closure boxes; 1.7M total across all product lines
  • Lead Time: 12–18 days new tooling; 8–12 days repeat order; 5–7 days rush with surcharge
