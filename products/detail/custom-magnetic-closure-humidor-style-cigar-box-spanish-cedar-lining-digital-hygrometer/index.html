Product Description

Quality Control — 15 Checkpoints

Every humidor-style box passes through this 15-stage QC gate system:

  1. Material Inspection — Greyboard caliper ±0.1mm, leatherette color consistency under D65 lighting, cedar FSC chain-of-custody verification
  2. Cedar Quality — 4mm thickness ±0.3mm, moisture content 8–10%, zero knots or cracks, 400-grit sanded surface
  3. Pre-Press Proof — Color bars, foil registration marks, leatherette grain direction verification
  4. Print/Foil — ΔE ≤ 2.0 spectrophotometer, foil adhesion tape test per ASTM D3359
  5. Leatherette Wrap — Zero wrinkles, edge fold ±0.5mm, grain alignment matched at corners
  6. Board Cutting — Dimensional tolerance ±0.3mm for all panels
  7. Grooving/Scoring — Channel depth within 0.1mm of target, zero fiber cracking
  8. Cedar Installation — Flush fit against greyboard, zero gaps, adhesive VOC-free certified
  9. Magnet Installation — Flush-mounted, N52 grade verified, pull force ≥12N total
  10. Silicone Gasket — Continuous seal, zero gaps, Shore A 40±5 durometer
  11. Hygrometer Calibration — ±2% RH verified against reference at 75% RH, display functional
  12. Airtight Seal Test — Vacuum drop test: ≤5% pressure loss over 60 seconds
  13. Hinge/Cycle — 2,000 open/close cycles without gasket wear or magnetic degradation
  14. AQL Sampling — ISO 2859-1, Level II, AQL 2.5 for major defects
  15. Pack-Out Audit — Final 100% visual, hygrometer functional, cedar aroma verified, silica gel pack inserted
