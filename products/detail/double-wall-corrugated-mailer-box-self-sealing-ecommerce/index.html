Product Overview

Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., this double-wall corrugated mailer box integrates a self-sealing adhesive strip with easy-tear perforation — eliminating tape, reducing fulfillment labor, and delivering a premium branded unboxing experience. The BC-flute (6.0mm) or EB-flute (4.5mm) double-wall construction provides 32–44 ECT crush resistance and 1,800+ kPa burst strength, tested to ISTA 1A transit standards. Suitable for apparel, cosmetics, electronics, food products, and subscription box services shipping via Amazon FBA, Shopify, or direct-to-consumer channels. Full-color flexo or offset litho printing on white-top kraft liner with water-based inks for eco compliance. With 23 years of manufacturing excellence, 18,000 sqm of factory space, and a daily output of 1.7 million boxes, Bincai delivers consistent quality at competitive prices.

Why This Double-Wall Self-Sealing Mailer

Standard single-wall corrugated mailers fail when products exceed 2kg or transit distances exceed 500km. This double-wall self-sealing mailer eliminates the two most common e-commerce fulfillment pain points: crush damage from inadequate wall strength, and packing-line bottlenecks from manual taping.
