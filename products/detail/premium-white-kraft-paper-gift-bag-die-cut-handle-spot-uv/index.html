Product Overview

Our premium white kraft paper gift bag combines eco-friendly materials with luxury finishing techniques. Crafted from 200gsm white kraft paper with reinforced side and bottom gussets, this bag supports up to 5kg of merchandise while maintaining a crisp, high-end appearance. The die-cut handle eliminates metal or plastic hardware for a clean, sustainable design. Full CMYK offset printing with spot UV gloss highlights brand logos and design elements, creating contrast between matte kraft and glossy accents. Manufactured in our ISO 9001 certified factory on KBA 1050 and Heidelberg presses.

Design Features

  • White Kraft Paper: 200gsm bleached kraft provides a clean, premium canvas for full-color printing
  • Die-Cut Handle: Integrated paper handle with no metal eyelets — fully recyclable and comfortable to carry
  • Reinforced Gusset: 80mm side and bottom gussets with reinforced folding for load-bearing capacity
  • Spot UV Gloss: Selective high-gloss UV varnish on logos and design accents for tactile luxury contrast

  • Flat-Fold Shipping: Bags ship flat-folded to reduce freight volume; self-standing when opened

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom paper bags? A: Standard MOQ is 1,000 units per design. Custom size tooling requires 5,000 units. For trial orders with standard sizes and existing dies, we accept quantities as low as 500 units with a small-run surcharge.

Q: Can the die-cut handle shape be customized? A: Yes — we manufacture custom steel-rule dies for unique handle shapes beyond the standard oval and rectangular cutouts. Custom handle dies add 3-5 days to tooling time and require a 5,000-unit minimum order.

Q: How strong are these bags? A: The 200gsm white kraft with reinforced 80mm gussets supports up to 5kg of merchandise. For heavier items (up to 8kg), we recommend upgrading to 250gsm kraft with a reinforced bottom board. Every bag passes a 6kg static load test for 24 hours before shipment.

Q: Can I get samples before placing a full order? A: Free pre-production samples ship within 5-7 business days of artwork approval. Samples include your custom printing, selected finish (spot UV, lamination), and handle style. Expedited 3-day sampling is available.

Q: Are these bags suitable for food packaging? A: Yes — our water-based PVA adhesive and soy-based offset inks are food-safe for indirect contact. For direct food contact, we offer FDA-compliant barrier coatings applied inline on the Heidelberg 7+1 UV press.
