Product Overview

Bincai’s premium necklace and pendant jewelry box is purpose-built for jewelry retailers, e-commerce brands, and boutique artisans who demand retail-ready presentation at wholesale economics. The hinged lid opens to a full 180° display angle — the box transforms from protective packaging into a countertop showcase in one motion. A plush velvet interior (2mm flocking, 0.8mm pile) cradles the necklace chain in anti-tangle channels, while the removable satin display cushion elevates the pendant for maximum visual impact.

Manufactured in our 18,000 sqm ISO 9001:2015 and FSC-certified facility in Foshan, Guangdong, every box passes through KBA 1050 4-color offset printing, Bobst automatic die-cutting, and hand-applied velvet flocking assembly. The 0.5mm greyboard tolerance ensures consistent lid-to-base alignment across full production runs. 23 years of luxury packaging expertise — since 2003, Bincai has manufactured over 500 million jewelry boxes for brands in 60+ countries.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color offset + Heidelberg 7+1 UV for premium foil and spot UV — ±0.15mm print registration

FAQ

Q: Can the velvet color be matched to my brand’s Pantone? A: Yes. Our in-house flocking line can match any Pantone color for the velvet interior. We stock 20+ popular colors (black, navy, burgundy, cream, charcoal, forest green) for immediate sampling. Custom Pantone-matched velvet adds 3-5 days to the sample timeline and requires minimum 2,000 units for the custom flocking batch.

Q: What’s the difference between your flocking velvet and genuine suede? A: Flocking velvet is electrostatically applied short fibers (0.8–1.2mm pile) onto adhesive-coated greyboard — it delivers the plush look and feel of velvet at a significantly lower cost. Microfiber suede (available as upgrade) provides superior abrasion resistance and a more premium hand feel. We recommend flocking for retail display and suede for high-end collector or luxury items that will be handled frequently.

Q: Can I get a physical sample before committing to production? A: Absolutely. We provide a free white-glue mockup sample within 5-7 working days — this includes your exact exterior print, foil stamping, velvet color, and cushion configuration. Digital color proof within 48 hours. Sample shipped via DHL/FedEx (arrives 3-5 days after dispatch). MOQ 500 applies to production orders only.

Q: What’s the lead time from order to delivery? A: Sample approval → 10-14 working days production → ocean freight 18-30 days to major ports (Los Angeles, Rotterdam, Dubai, Sydney) or air freight 5-7 days for urgent orders. Rush production available at 7 days with 15% premium. Total door-to-door: approximately 4-6 weeks standard, 2 weeks rush.

Q: Can the box hold both a necklace and matching earrings? A: Yes. We can configure the interior with a pendant cushion plus dual earring slots in the same compartment — or split into a two-tier design with the necklace on top and earrings below. The maximum recommended interior is 250×180×60mm for a single-level box; larger sets may require our double-deck or drawer-style boxes.
