Product Overview

The Bincai Kraft Paper Watch Box combines sustainability with premium presentation — proving that eco-friendly doesn’t mean sacrificing quality. The natural kraft paperboard exterior, cotton pull strap, and soy-based ink printing create a 100% biodegradable packaging solution for environmentally conscious watch brands. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. in our FSC Chain-of-Custody certified facility, each box uses FSC-certified kraft paperboard, vegetable-based adhesives, and unbleached cotton strapping. Our 18,000 sqm Foshan factory (10,000 sqm original + 8,000 sqm smart expansion, 2025) produces 220,000+ gift boxes daily, with a dedicated eco-packaging line running 100% renewable energy for select production runs. ISO 9001:2015 quality management ensures every box meets the same rigorous standards as our luxury non-eco lines.
