Product Overview

The Bincai Two-Piece Rigid Gift Box combines sculptural blind embossing with luxurious satin ribbon pulls to create a tactile and visual experience worthy of premium fashion brands. Each box features precision two-piece telescoping construction — lid and base wrapped separately — with soft-touch matte lamination, N52 neodymium magnetic closure, and fully lined satin or velvet interior. The embossed pattern is created with custom brass dies on our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press, achieving crisp, sculptural relief that catches light and invites touch. With 23 years of rigid box expertise, Bincai operates from an 18,000 sqm dual facility producing 220,000+ gift boxes daily under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC certification.
