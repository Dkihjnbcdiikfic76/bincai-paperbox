FAQ

Q: Can the ribbon color match my brand’s Pantone color? A: Yes. We stock 50+ satin ribbon colors and can custom dye any Pantone shade with a minimum 500m ribbon order. The dyeing adds 3–5 working days to lead time.

Q: Is the ribbon closure secure enough for shipping? A: Our ribbon tie boxes are designed with reinforced greyboard panels at the ribbon anchor points. Combined with a snug-fitting lid, they survive ISTA 1A drop testing. For heavy items we add a hidden magnetic backup flap beneath the ribbon for extra security.

Q: What ribbon widths are available? A: Standard widths are 10mm, 15mm, 20mm, and 25mm. Custom widths available on request. We recommend 15mm for small boxes (under 150mm), 20mm for medium (150-250mm), and 25mm for large format boxes.

Q: Can you produce ribbon-closure boxes without a lid (open-top presentation)? A: Yes, we produce ribbon-tie boxes in open-top and two-piece telescopic lid configurations. Open-top ribbon boxes are popular for wedding favors and retail counter displays.
