Product Overview

A magnetic-closure rigid gift box purpose-built for premium 3-wick candles. The deep die-cut EVA foam insert securely cradles a candle jar up to 120mm diameter × 100mm height, preventing glass-on-glass contact during transit. Spot UV coating on the exterior lid creates a luxurious contrast between matte and gloss surfaces, while the embossed logo adds dimensional branding. A satin ribbon pull tab makes opening effortless and elegant. Manufactured at Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. with precision die-cutting and hot-foil finishing on Heidelberg 7+1 UV.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Board: 2.0mm greyboard + 157gsm C1S art paper, matte lamination base
  • Coating: Spot UV (selective gloss) on logo and border pattern — tactile contrast
  • Foam insert: 25mm EVA foam, density 45kg/m³, CNC die-cut cavity for 120mm diameter jar
  • Closure: N35 neodymium magnets (4×) embedded in front flap — 2.2kg total holding force
  • Printing: CMYK offset on KBA 1050 4-color + spot UV register on Heidelberg 7+1 UV
  • Embossing: Blind or foil-registered embossing on lid, 0.8mm depth
  • Daily output: 1.7 million boxes total factory capacity

Production Workflow

  1. Design & Prepress — CAD structural design with candle jar dimensions, CTP plate output on Kodak Magnus VLF
  2. Board Preparation — 2.0mm greyboard cut to blank, 157gsm C1S art paper PVA cold-glued to greyboard, mitered corners ±0.3mm
  3. Offset Printing — CMYK on KBA 1050 4-color press, aqueous coating base
  4. Spot UV & Foil — Spot UV register + embossing on Heidelberg 7+1 UV press in a single pass
  5. Foam CNC Routing — 25mm EVA foam, density 45kg/m³, CNC die-cut cavity matching candle jar profile within ±0.3mm
  6. Magnet Embedding — 4× N35 neodymium magnets (20×10×2mm) embedded into front flap and base, polarity-matched
  7. Assembly — Foam insert glued into base, lid hinged, ribbon pull tab attached, satin ribbon feed-through
  8. QC Gate — Magnet pull-force test, foam fit gauge, spot UV registration check, embossing depth measurement, drop test sampling per ISTA 3A
  9. Packing & Shipping — Flat-packed in corrugated master cartons (50 units per outer), fumigation-free palletization, DHL/FedEx/Sea freight
