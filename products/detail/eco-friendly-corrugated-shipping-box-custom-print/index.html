Product Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. brings 23 years of paper packaging expertise (ISO 9001:2015 & FSC Chain-of-Custody certified) to the most fundamental shipping challenge: getting products to customers safely, sustainably, and beautifully. Our eco-friendly custom-printed corrugated shipping box is engineered for the modern e-commerce era — where packaging must protect contents across 8,000+ miles of global logistics, eliminate unnecessary plastic waste, and serve as a brand’s first physical touchpoint with the customer.

Manufactured in our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility (10,000 sqm primary + 8,000 sqm smart factory, 2025 expansion), each box begins as FSC certified paperboard on our in-house corrugator. Three flute constructions serve distinct purposes: E-Flute (1.5mm) for lightweight, retail-ready packaging that prints like folding carton stock; B-Flute (3mm) for standard e-commerce and subscription box applications; and BC-Flute (6mm double-wall) for heavy industrial goods requiring ECT 44+ crush resistance. All are printed with low-VOC soy-based or water-based inks, with closure via self-sealing adhesive strip — no plastic tape required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Which flute should I choose — E, B, or BC?

A: E-Flute (1.5mm) is best for lightweight retail packaging and cosmetics — it prints beautifully and has a refined, folding-carton-like appearance. B-Flute (3mm) is the workhorse for e-commerce, subscription boxes, and standard product shipping — excellent crush resistance for products under 5kg. BC-Flute (6mm double-wall, ECT 44+) handles heavy industrial goods (5–25kg), international export, and high-stack warehouse scenarios. For extremely heavy items (25–50kg), we offer triple-wall (9mm+). Our structural engineers evaluate your product weight, shipping method, and stacking conditions to recommend the optimal flute — typically saving 10–15% on material costs vs. over-specification.

Q: Is the self-sealing strip really strong enough to replace packing tape?

A: Yes. The self-sealing closure uses a hot-melt pressure-sensitive adhesive rated at ≥5N/25mm peel strength. It bonds instantly when the flap is pressed down and reaches full strength within 24 hours. In ISTA 1A drop testing (10 drops from 760mm), self-sealed boxes perform equivalently to tape-sealed boxes. Benefits: eliminates plastic tape from your supply chain, faster packing (one motion vs. tape gun), and cleaner presentation for customer unboxing. For heavy industrial shipments, we recommend the self-seal strip plus an optional reinforced gummed paper tape for secondary closure.

Q: Can you print full-color graphics on a brown kraft corrugated box?

A: Yes — through litho-lamination. We print your artwork in vibrant CMYK on coated art paper using our KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press (with optional Heidelberg 7+1 UV for spot coatings), then laminate it to the corrugated board. The result is photo-quality retail packaging with the structural strength of corrugated. For simpler designs on natural kraft, 1–3 color flexo printing with water-based inks delivers cost-effective branding. Full-color digital printing is available for short runs (100–500 units).
