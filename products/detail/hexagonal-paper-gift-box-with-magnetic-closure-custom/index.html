Product Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing produces custom hexagonal paper gift boxes featuring premium magnetic closure systems that combine distinctive geometry with a secure, satisfying open-and-close experience. Each box is built on a rigid greyboard core wrapped in your choice of art paper, specialty paper, or fabric, with neodymium magnets discreetly embedded in the lid and base for invisible closure. Our hexagonal shape stands out on retail shelves and creates a memorable unboxing moment for jewelry, luxury goods, corporate gifts, and wedding favors.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Rigid board core: 800-1600 GSM greyboard, precision die-cut into hexagonal panels with hand-assembled construction for sharp, clean edges
  • Magnet type: N35 to N52 grade neodymium disc magnets (6-12 mm diameter, 1-3 mm thickness), embedded flush within the board layers so they are completely invisible
  • Magnet configuration: 1-3 magnet pairs depending on box size; N52 magnets for lids exceeding 200 mm to ensure reliable closure
  • Wrapping material: 128-200 GSM coated art paper, uncoated textured paper, leatherette paper, linen fabric, velvet paper, or PU leather
  • Printing: KBA 1050 4-color offset for full-wrap graphics; Heidelberg 7+1 UV for metallic foil stamping, embossing, debossing, and spot UV accents
  • Interior finishing: Flocked velvet (nylon or rayon, 0.5-1.5 mm pile), satin-lined, custom-fit foam or molded pulp inserts, ribbon pull tabs
  • Hinge style: Book-style spine hinge (wrapped), piano hinge for heavy lids, or lid-lift design for slide-off presentation
  • Production capacity: 220,000+ gift boxes daily, with dedicated rigid-box assembly lines for specialty shapes

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom hexagonal magnetic boxes?

A: MOQ is 500 units per design for standard configurations. Custom shapes or multi-color printing require 1,000 units minimum. Free 3D digital proofs are provided within 24 hours of artwork approval, and pre-production samples ship in 5-7 working days. Lead time for mass production is 15-20 working days.

Q: Can I get different geometric shapes — octagonal, pentagonal, triangular — from the same production partner?

A: Absolutely. Bincai specializes in custom geometric rigid boxes — hexagonal, octagonal, pentagonal, triangular, and fully custom polygonal shapes. We maintain dedicated assembly jigs and die-cut templates for each shape family, ensuring consistent quality across your geometric packaging range. Multi-shape orders across a brand family share artwork setup and foil dies for cost efficiency.

Q: How strong are the neodymium magnets, and will they hold over time?

A: We use N35 to N52 grade neodymium disc magnets (6-12mm diameter, epoxy-sealed against corrosion). Depending on box size and weight, we configure 1-3 magnet pairs delivering 2.5-5.0N calibrated pull force. All magnets are tested at 40°C/90% RH accelerated aging for 72 hours and 15,000 open/close cycle endurance. N52 magnets are standard for lids exceeding 200mm to ensure reliable closure on larger boxes.

Q: What wrapping materials are available for the hexagonal box exterior?

A: We offer 128-200gsm coated art paper (gloss/matte), uncoated textured paper, leatherette paper, linen fabric, velvet paper, and PU leather. All materials are applied with water-based adhesives applied at controlled temperature and pressure on semi-automatic casing-in machines. Custom-printed wrapping paper with your brand pattern or logo is available.

Q: Can you add a ribbon pull tab or custom interior insert?

A: Yes. Color-matched grosgrain or satin ribbon pull tabs are standard. Interior customizations include die-cut foam inserts (for jewelry or electronics), satin-covered board trays, molded pulp inserts, or cardboard dividers — all custom-fit to your product dimensions with ±0.5mm tolerance.
