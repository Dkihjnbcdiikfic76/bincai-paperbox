FAQ

Q: Can you match the suede tray cavities to our specific cufflink dimensions?

A: Absolutely — this is a fully customized product. We request a sample pair of your cufflinks (or detailed CAD drawings with dimensions), and our die-board engineering team designs the tray cavity layout to ±0.3mm tolerance. The sampling process includes a physical tray sample for your approval before mass production. Standard lead time for new tray tooling is 7-10 days, included in the sample phase.

Q: What’s the difference between velvet flocking and suede for the interior?

A: Velvet flocking is applied via electrostatic process — short nylon/polyester fibers are embedded into adhesive on the board surface, creating a uniform soft texture at lower cost. Suede is a genuine microfiber fabric laminated onto the board, offering a more premium tactile feel with subtle nap variation. Velvet flocking is more durable and color-consistent across batches; suede provides a richer luxury hand-feel. For cufflink presentation, suede trays are the preferred choice among luxury brands.

Q: How do you ensure the magnetic closure stays aligned after repeated opening?

A: We use neodymium disc magnets press-fit into precision die-cut cavities in the greyboard. Unlike surface-mounted magnets that can shift over time, our embedded magnet design is mechanically locked into the board structure. Every box undergoes a 100-cycle open/close test during QC — we verify both magnetic alignment (no lateral shift) and pull force (≥4N). The magnetic snap mechanism is designed for 5,000+ cycles in normal use.

Q: What are your lead times, MOQ, and customization process?

A: Standard lead time is 15-20 days for existing configurations and in-stock materials. New tray tooling adds 7-10 days for die-board fabrication and sampling. MOQ is 500 units for standard configurations, 1000+ for custom tray design or specialty materials (genuine leather wrap, custom-mixed Pantone flocking). Our customization process: (1) Receive your cufflink samples or CAD, (2) Design tray layout and submit for approval, (3) Produce physical sample (5-7 days), (4) Your approval, (5) Mass production (15-20 days). ISO 9001 batch documentation and FSC chain-of-custody certificates provided with every order.
