FAQ

Q: Can the satin pillow accommodate different types of brooch pin backs — standard safety catch, trombone clasp, or double-pin?

A: Yes — our pillow design is customized to your specific brooch hardware. During sampling, we request one sample brooch to measure the pin type, length, and clasp mechanism. For safety-catch brooches, we use a standard horizontal slot; for trombone clasps, a wider reinforced slot; for double-pin brooches, dual parallel slots with spacing matched to your piece. The pillow foam density and satin wrap are calibrated so the pin inserts smoothly without tearing the fabric, even after repeated use.

Q: Is the velvet interior safe for antique and heirloom brooches — will it prevent tarnishing?

A: Our standard velvet flocking uses nylon/polyester fibers that are inert and non-reactive with silver, gold, and base metals. For heirloom-quality pieces requiring the highest preservation standard, we offer an acid-free upgrade: the greyboard is replaced with pH-neutral archival board (pH 7.0-7.5), the flocking adhesive is solvent-free water-based PVA, and the satin pillow fabric is 100% silk or acid-free polyester. This “museum-grade” configuration is used by several gallery shops and auction houses for their reproduction jewelry packaging.

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity if I need multiple brooch box sizes for different pin collections?

A: MOQ is 500 units per size/design. If you need 3 different sizes (e.g., small for lapel pins, medium for cameo brooches, large for statement pieces), the total order would be 1,500 units (500 × 3 sizes). We can combine multiple sizes in a single production batch to streamline logistics — all ship together. For very small initial orders, we offer a “sampling plus” program: 100 units per size at a slight premium (typically 15-20% above MOQ pricing), ideal for testing new designs in the market before committing to volume.

Q: What are the lead times and what’s your sampling process?

A: Standard lead time is 15-20 days for existing configurations. Custom pillow design with new tooling adds 7-10 days for sampling. Sampling process: (1) You send us one sample brooch or detailed CAD with clasp specifications, (2) We produce 3-5 physical samples for your approval (5-7 days), (3) Once approved, mass production begins (15-20 days). All orders include ISO 9001 batch documentation, FSC chain-of-custody certificates, and SGS inspection reports upon request. Air freight samples via DHL/FedEx (3-5 days international).
