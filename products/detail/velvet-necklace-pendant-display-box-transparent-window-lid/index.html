Product Overview

Elegant velvet necklace pendant display box featuring a crystal-clear PET transparent lid that allows full product visibility without opening — ideal for retail shelf display and e-commerce photography. The interior is precision-flocked with electrostatic black velvet and includes two necklace hooks (with silicone retention caps) plus a recessed foam cushion for pendants up to 50mm diameter. The magnetic snap closure keeps the box securely shut during transport while providing an effortless open-close experience. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) — 23 years elevating jewelry packaging across our 18,000 sqm integrated facility.

Applications

  • Jewelry retail counter and window display — transparent lid shows product without handling
  • E-commerce necklace and pendant fulfillment (Amazon, Etsy, brand DTC sites)
  • Bridal party gift packaging — bridesmaid necklace sets, wedding day jewelry
  • Valentine’s Day, Mother’s Day, Christmas seasonal gift packaging
  • Jewelry trade show and wholesale showroom sample presentation
  • Subscription box insert — monthly jewelry club premium packaging

Customization Options

  • Velvet Colors: Black (standard), navy, burgundy, ivory, blush pink, teal — full Pantone match available
  • Insert Configuration: Standard 2-hook + pendant cushion; customizable to 1-hook, 3-hook, ring slot combo, or earring card slot
  • Window Options: Clear PET (standard), frosted PET, tinted PET (smoke/amber), or anti-fog coated for refrigeration
  • Logo Placement: UV digital print on PET lid (white or color), hot foil stamp on base exterior, or debossed logo on velvet insert
  • Exterior: Wrapped art paper 157gsm in matte/gloss/soft-touch lamination; specialty papers: linen, leatherette, pearlescent
  • Closure: Magnetic snap (standard), ribbon tie, or elastic band with button
