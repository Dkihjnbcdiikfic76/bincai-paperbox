Product Overview

The velvet bracelet bangle display box with N52 magnetic closure is designed for premium jewelry brands that demand flawless presentation. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing (founded 2003, Foshan, ISO 9001:2015 & FSC-C147399 certified), this rigid box features ultra-soft flocked velvet wrap, a precision-cut bangle insert, and an N52 neodymium magnet closure that delivers a satisfying snap. Our 18,000 sqm factory (10,000 sqm + 8,000 sqm smart factory expansion 2025) produces 1.7 million boxes daily on KBA Rapida 105 and Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV presses with Bobst SP 102 BMA/E die-cutters.
