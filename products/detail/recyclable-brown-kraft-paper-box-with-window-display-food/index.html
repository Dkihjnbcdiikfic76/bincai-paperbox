Applications

Window kraft boxes give consumers the transparency they demand while meeting the sustainability standards modern food brands require:

  • Bakery & Pastry: Croissants, muffins, cookies, brownies, cupcakes, macarons — the window shows the product’s golden crust and craftsmanship while kraft communicates artisanal authenticity
  • Organic & Natural Foods: Granola bars, energy bites, dried fruit, nut mixes, trail mix — kraft’s natural aesthetic aligns perfectly with clean-label and organic brand identities
  • Chocolate & Confectionery: Artisanal chocolate bars, truffle collections, toffee, fudge — window reveals the product while kraft provides rustic luxury
  • Tea & Coffee: Loose-leaf tea sachets, specialty coffee beans, matcha powder, herbal tea bags — window displays leaf grade, bean roast, or powder color
  • Deli & Charcuterie: Artisan cheese, cured meats, olives, antipasti — window with kraft conveys farm-to-table freshness
  • Pet Treats: Natural dog treats, catnip toys, bird seed — window shows product quality; kraft reinforces natural and wholesome messaging
  • Farmers’ Market & Farm Stand: Produce boxes, honey jars, preserves, jam, pickles, spice blends — kraft aligns with farm-fresh and handcrafted positioning
  • Gift & Subscription Boxes: Monthly snack subscriptions, tea-of-the-month clubs, curated food gift sets — window builds anticipation; kraft elevates the unboxing experience

Customization Options

  • Kraft Board: Natural brown kraft (unbleached), white kraft (bleached, for cleaner look), or black kraft (premium, sophisticated) in 250–400gsm weights; FSC Mix, FSC 100%, or FSC Recycled certification options
  • Window Material: Clear compostable PLA film (plant-based, industrially compostable), recyclable rPET film (post-consumer recycled), standard PVC, or anti-fog treated film for refrigerated products; window thickness from 0.2–0.5mm based on product weight and protection needs
  • Window Shape & Size: Custom die-cut window — rectangle, oval, circle, heart, product-silhouette; partial window or full-face window; multiple windows for multi-pack visibility
  • Box Structure: Reverse tuck end (RTE), straight tuck end (STE), auto-lock bottom for heavier products, sleeve-and-tray for premium gifting, or gable-top with window for grab-and-go retail
  • Printing: Up to 4-color CMYK offset (KBA 1050) using soy-based inks on kraft surface; 1–2 color designs most popular for kraft aesthetic — black, white, metallic gold, or brand-specific Pantone
  • Foil Stamping: Gold, silver, copper, or rose gold foil on kraft for premium contrast; blind deboss for subtle, tactile logo treatment
  • Surface Finishes: Keep natural kraft texture (most popular for eco-authenticity), matte aqueous coating for scuff resistance, or soft-touch coating for premium hand-feel
  • Add-Ons: Tear-strip for tamper evidence, euro-slot for pegboard retail, QR code linking to sourcing/origin story, “Please Recycle” logo printed on base, FSC logo placement on box
  • Size: Fully custom — from 60×60×30mm for individual chocolates to 350×250×80mm for family-size bakery boxes

Production Specifications

  • Board: 250–400gsm FSC-certified kraft board; unbleached natural brown kraft as standard; bleached white kraft and black kraft available
  • Press: KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset — 15,000 sheets/hour, max sheet 1050×740mm; soy-based inks standard for food packaging
  • Coating: Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV — inline UV or aqueous coating for food-safe surface protection
  • Window Patching: Bobst window patching machine — automatic film cutting, adhesive application, and precision film placement; ±0.5mm film-to-window registration
  • Adhesives: Water-based emulsion adhesives — food-safe, low-VOC, compliant with FDA 21 CFR 175.105 and EU Framework Regulation (EC) No 1935/2004 for indirect food contact
  • Inks: Soy-based offset inks (20%+ soy content) — low-VOC, renewable resource, de-inkable for recycling stream compatibility
  • Die-Cutting: Bobst SP 102-SE Autoplaten — precision die-cutting for window aperture and structural shape
  • Lead Time: 7–10 working days for standard; 10–15 days for complex multi-process
  • Daily Capacity: 800,000 folding cartons per day across production lines

FAQ

Q: What certifications do Bincai kraft paper boxes carry for food-grade applications? A: Our kraft paper boxes use food-safe materials including soy-based inks, PLA window film (compostable), and FSC-certified paperboard. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified with FSC Chain-of-Custody documentation available. All food-contact materials comply with FDA 21 CFR and EU Regulation (EC) No 1935/2004 standards.

Q: Can Bincai produce completely plastic-free kraft packaging? A: Yes. We offer 100% plastic-free kraft packaging options using paper tape closures, cellulose windows (instead of PLA), and water-based adhesives. Our eco-friendly kraft mailer boxes with paper tape are 100% recyclable and biodegradable. We also offer seed-embedded tags and soy-ink printing as add-ons.

Q: What is the MOQ for custom window kraft boxes? A: Our standard MOQ is 500 pieces, with flexible trial orders available for new customers. Bincai produces 1.7M+ color boxes daily from our 18,000 sqm facility. We provide free pre-production samples with 3D digital proof within 24 hours. Lead time is 15-20 days for standard orders.

Q: How does Bincai ensure sustainability across its manufacturing process? A: Sustainability is built into our entire workflow: FSC-certified paperboard sourcing, soy-based inks with VOC-free printing on Heidelberg 7+1 UV and KBA 1050 presses, water-based adhesives, and energy-efficient production in our smart factory (8,000 sqm, 2025 expansion). We also offer carbon-offset shipping options and provide full chain-of-custody documentation.
