FAQ

Q: Can the pen slots be customized for different pen diameters? A: Yes. We can die-cut grooves to match any pen diameter (8-16mm typical). Elastic bands offer a universal-fit alternative for unknown dimensions.

Q: What is the minimum order for custom sizes and configurations? A: MOQ is 500-1,000 units. Tooling fee for custom die-cut trays is $80-200 (one-time).

Q: Can you include a matching gift bag or sleeve? A: Yes. We offer matching paper sleeves, gift bags with ribbon handles, and outer cardboard mailers as a complete packaging solution.

Q: Are the magnets strong enough to keep the box closed during shipping? A: Our dual N35-N52 neodymium magnets provide 500g+ holding force, tested for drop (1.2m) and vibration (ISTA 1A) to ensure secure closure during transit.

Q: What exterior materials give the most premium look for executive gifts? A: PU leatherette with gold foil debossing is our most popular premium choice — it delivers a genuine leather aesthetic at a paper-box price point. Textured linen with blind embossing offers a sophisticated minimal look favored by European luxury brands. Carbon-fiber pattern wrap gives a modern, technical feel ideal for high-end engineering and tech company corporate gifts.

Q: Can the box be designed to hold a pen set (fountain pen + rollerball + cartridge)? A: Yes. We can design multi-slot trays for 2, 3, or 4 writing instruments plus accessories. Common configurations include: fountain pen + rollerball, pen + mechanical pencil set, or pen + ink cartridge + converter. Each slot is individually die-cut to the specific instrument dimensions.

Q: What lead time should I expect for a custom corporate pen box order? A: Standard lead time is 15-20 working days from artwork approval. Sample production is 5-7 days. For large corporate orders (5,000+ units) during Q4 gifting season (Oct-Dec), we recommend ordering 30-45 days in advance to secure production capacity.

Sustainability & Materials Responsibility

Bincai luxury pen display boxes combine premium presentation with responsible manufacturing:

  • FSC Certified Greyboard: 1000-1400gsm greyboard core sourced from FSC Chain-of-Custody certified mills — full documentation provided for corporate ESG reporting
  • Water-Based Adhesives: All rigid box assembly uses PVA water-based adhesives (zero VOC emissions) — safe for workers and environment
  • Recyclable Design: Paper-dominant construction (≥90% paper by weight) — magnets are small and easily separated; satin lining is polyester (recyclable #1 stream when separated)
  • Leatherette Option: PU leatherette is a synthetic alternative to genuine leather — no animal products, lower environmental impact than chrome-tanned leather
  • Foil Recovery: Hot foil stamping waste (carrier film) is collected and sent to specialized foil recyclers — recovering aluminum content
  • Smart Factory Efficiency: 18,000 sqm facility with LED lighting retrofit (2025), automated production scheduling to minimize per-unit energy, and waste paper recycling (100% of paper trim recycled)
