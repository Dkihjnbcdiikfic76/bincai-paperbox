Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming material inspection — greyboard thickness, paper GSM, ribbon tensile strength
  2. Pre-press color verification — Pantone matching and registration check
  3. In-line print quality monitoring — density, dot gain, color consistency
  4. Post-forming structural inspection — cylinder roundness, lid fit tolerance
  5. Handle and eyelet installation verification — pull test and alignment
  6. Surface finish inspection — lamination coverage, foil adhesion, scratch resistance
  7. Final audit per AQL 2.5 Level II — random sampling of finished units
