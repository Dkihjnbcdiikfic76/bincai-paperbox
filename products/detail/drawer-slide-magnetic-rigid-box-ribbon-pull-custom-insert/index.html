Product Overview

The drawer-slide magnetic rigid box with satin ribbon pull and custom die-cut insert is the definitive premium unboxing format for high-value products. Engineered for luxury watches, fine jewelry, premium electronics, executive gifts, and limited-edition collectibles, this box delivers a multi-sensory reveal experience — the smooth glide of the drawer, the satisfying magnetic snap-lock, and the tactile ribbon pull. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003) in our ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified 18,000 sqm facility.

The drawer assembly uses precision-scored greyboard (1,200–1,800gsm) with finger-jointed corners and reinforced rail edges, achieving 5,000+ open/close cycles without degradation. N52 neodymium magnets — the strongest commercially available grade — engage at full insertion with an audible and tactile “click,” preventing accidental opening during handling or shipping. The 15mm/25mm satin ribbon pull-tab is heat-sealed between board layers and pull-strength tested to 5 kgf. Inside, a CNC knife-cut EVA/PU foam insert (45–70 Shore A density, ±0.2mm cavity tolerance) cradles the product with precision fit.

Available in single-drawer, double-drawer (slide from both ends), and stacked multi-drawer (2–4 levels) configurations with optional flip-lid top compartment. Outer wrap options span 128–200gsm art paper finishes (matte, gloss, soft-touch, linen, pearlescent, metallic), faux leather paper, textured woodgrain, or carbon fiber effect. Printing uses KBA Rapida 105 4-color and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses with hot foil stamping (gold/silver/rose gold/holographic), embossing/debossing, spot UV, and full-surface glitter UV via Bobst SP 102 BMA/E at ±0.1mm foil-to-print registration. 23 years, 1.7M boxes daily, MOQ 500.

FAQ

Q: Can the drawer be configured to slide from the short side instead of the long side? A: Yes. The drawer slide orientation is fully customizable — long-side pull (standard for retail display), short-side pull (compact packaging footprint), or dual-end slide (drawer accessible from both sides for symmetrical unboxing). Our structural engineers will optimize the finger-jointed rail design for your chosen orientation to maintain the same 5,000+ cycle durability.

Q: What’s the difference between EVA and PU foam inserts? A: EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate) foam is our standard — firm density 45–70 Shore A, excellent chemical resistance, and the most cost-effective option. PU (polyurethane) foam offers superior softness and rebound memory — preferred for highly polished surfaces (watch crystals, lacquered items) that are scratch-sensitive. PU foam is approximately 20–30% more expensive. Both are CNC knife-cut to ±0.2mm cavity tolerance with anti-yellowing UV-stabilized formulations.

Q: Can the magnetic lock strength be adjusted? A: Yes. We select magnet quantity (2–6 per drawer), size, and grade based on your drawer weight and security requirements. Standard configuration uses 2× N52 grade providing 0.5–3.0 kgf total pull force. For heavier contents (500g+) or applications requiring stronger retention (travel cases, shipping-ready boxes), we increase to 4–6 magnets or upgrade to N52SH (high-temperature stable) for tropical and Middle Eastern markets. We’ll recommend the optimal configuration during sampling.

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity for fully custom sizes? A: MOQ is 500 units for standard sizes and configurations. Custom dimensions (non-standard width/depth/height ratios), multi-drawer configurations (3+ levels), specialty materials (genuine leather, carbon fiber wrap, metal inlay), or complex foam cavity layouts require 1,000–2,000 units depending on complexity. Free pre-production samples with full decoration and insert in 7–10 working days.

Q: Can the foam insert be designed to hold multiple different products? A: Absolutely. Our CNC knife-cutting allows any cavity configuration — mixed product layouts with different shapes and depths in a single tray, multi-level foam inserts (stacked trays), removable foam modules for reconfigurable kits, and foam bases with elastic straps or satin-lined wells. Our CAD team provides 3D renderings with your exact product dimensions, and we produce physical fit-check samples before production tooling.

Q: Is this box suitable for drop-ship e-commerce? A: Yes. The magnetic lock prevents drawer opening during transit, and the rigid greyboard construction passes ISTA 1A drop testing. We recommend adding a clear OPP overwrap or belly band for additional shipping security. For high-volume e-commerce fulfillment, we can integrate the drawer-slide box into a custom corrugated mailer outer — creating a “box-in-box” system where the premium drawer box is the keep-sake inner packaging and a branded corrugated mailer is the shipping outer.

Q: What’s the lead time for repeat orders? A: Repeat orders with existing tooling and approved samples ship in 7–10 working days for quantities up to 5,000 units. For larger volumes (10,000+), lead time is 12–15 working days. Rush service (5 working days) is available for repeat orders with a 15% surcharge, subject to production line availability. First-time orders require 15–20 working days to allow for foam insert tooling, magnet placement engineering, and pre-production sample approval.
