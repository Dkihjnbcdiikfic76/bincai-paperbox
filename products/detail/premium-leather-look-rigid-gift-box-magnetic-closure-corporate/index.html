FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for this leather-look rigid box?

A: MOQ is 500 units. We also offer pre-production samples (5–7 days, free of charge) so you can evaluate the quality and tactile experience before committing to production.

Q: What leatherette colors and textures are available?

A: We stock 30+ standard colors including black, navy, burgundy, tan, grey, cream, forest green, and champagne. Textures include smooth grain, saffiano cross-hatch, crocodile, pebbled, and carbon-fiber. Custom colors can be matched from your Pantone reference.

Q: How durable is the magnetic closure?

A: We use N52-grade neodymium magnets that deliver ≥ 8 N pull force per pair. In our QA testing, the closure maintains its snap action through 2,000+ open-close cycles without loosening. This is industry-leading for luxury rigid box closures.

Q: Can the box be shipped flat to save freight?

A: Yes. We offer both flat-packed (unassembled) and pre-assembled shipping. Flat-packed reduces freight volume by 60–70% and is recommended for orders above 5,000 units. Pre-assembled is available for smaller orders where fulfillment speed is the priority.

Q: What is the production lead time?

A: Standard lead time is 15–25 days. Pre-production samples ship in 5–7 business days. For repeat orders with existing tooling and materials, rush production at 10–12 days is available.
