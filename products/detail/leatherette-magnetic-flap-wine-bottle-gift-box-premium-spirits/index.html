FAQ

Q: Can this box accommodate different bottle shapes — Bordeaux, Burgundy, Champagne? A: Yes — we can design the die-cut cavity to fit any standard bottle shape. Bordeaux bottles (straight shoulders, 750ml), Burgundy bottles (sloping shoulders), Champagne bottles (thicker glass, wider diameter), and even specialty shapes like square gin bottles or flask-style whiskey bottles. We maintain CAD templates for all major bottle profiles. For the most accurate fit, we recommend sending one sample bottle to our factory for precise cavity design.

Q: Is the leatherette material durable enough for retail shelf display? A: Our PU leatherette is specifically selected for retail durability. It passes the Martindale abrasion test at 50,000+ rub cycles, resists UV fading for 12+ months of indirect retail lighting, and is treated with an anti-fingerprint coating that prevents smudging during handling. For direct sunlight retail environments, we can upgrade to UV-stabilized leatherette that maintains color for 24+ months.

Q: Can I order this box without a bottle cavity — as a flat gift box? A: Yes — the same leatherette magnetic flap construction is available in flat gift box format without the bottle cavity. This configuration is suitable for gift sets containing multiple small items (chocolates, accessories, cosmetics) or for general luxury gift packaging. The flat configuration uses the same materials and production processes with a simpler die-cut tray or flat interior.

Q: What is the lead time for a fully custom wine box with our distillery’s branding? A: Standard lead time is 18–22 business days: 3–5 days for artwork and structural design, 5–7 days for pre-production samples, and 10–12 days for mass production after sample approval. For seasonal peaks (Q3 September–November for holiday wine gifting), we recommend placing orders 8–10 weeks in advance to secure production slots. Rush production (12 business days) is available for a 25% surcharge.
