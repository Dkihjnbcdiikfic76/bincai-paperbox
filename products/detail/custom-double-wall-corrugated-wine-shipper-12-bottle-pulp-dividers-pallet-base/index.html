Product Description

Custom double-wall corrugated wine shipper 12-bottle with die-cut pulp dividers and pallet base by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — a Diamond Member on Made-in-China.com with 23 years of manufacturing excellence (founded 2003). This heavy-duty wine shipping solution is engineered for international export logistics — protecting 12 standard 750ml wine bottles through ocean freight, air cargo, and last-mile delivery. The double-wall BC-flute construction provides exceptional stacking strength (ECT 44+), while the molded pulp dividers cradle each bottle individually with shock-absorbing protection. An integrated pallet base enables forklift handling without secondary palletization.

Produced on our BHS 2.5m Corrugator for board production, KBA Rapida 105 4-color for litho-laminate branding, Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV for specialty finishes, Bobst Visioncut 106 LER for precision die-cutting, and Bobst Expertfold 145 for heavy-duty folder-gluer assembly — all under one roof across our 18,000 sqm facility (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory, 2025 expansion). Daily output: 1.7 million color boxes + 220,000+ gift boxes. ISO 9001:2015, FSC-C147399, Sedex SMETA, and BSCI certified.

  • Wine Competitions: Competition and tasting event sample logistics requiring pristine bottle arrival

Customization Options

  • Double-Wall BC-Flute: 6mm thickness, ECT 44+ lb/in minimum, superior stacking for containerized shipping
  • Molded Pulp Dividers: 12 individual bottle cells, 100% recycled fiber, compostable, shock-absorbing — die-cut on Bobst Visioncut 106 LER for ±0.5mm cell precision
  • Integrated Pallet Base: Forklift access from all four sides, eliminates secondary palletization cost
  • Litho-Laminate Printing: KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset + Heidelberg 7+1 UV for full-color branding on B-flute outer liner
  • Box Style Options: Half-Slotted Container (HSC), Regular Slotted Container (RSC), or Full Telescope Design (FTD)
  • Bottle Configurations: 6-bottle, 12-bottle, 15-bottle, or mixed-bottle sizes; custom for magnum (1.5L), jeroboam (3L), and specialty formats
  • Consumer Features: Perforated tear strips for easy opening, self-locking bottom for rapid assembly, carry handles
  • Surface Treatments: Water-resistant coating for humid/cold-chain shipping, anti-slip coating for pallet stacking
  • Branding Options: Full CMYK litho-laminate, single-color flexo logo, kraft natural finish, foil stamping accents
  • Security Features: Tamper-evident tape, security seals, QR code tracking labels, serialized batch numbering
  • Assembly: Bobst Expertfold 145 folder-gluer for consistent, high-strength glue seams across production runs
