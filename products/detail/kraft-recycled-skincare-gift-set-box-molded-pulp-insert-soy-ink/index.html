The kraft recycled skincare gift set box is an eco-conscious rigid packaging solution designed for clean beauty and organic skincare brands. Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. manufactures this product in our 18,000 sqm dual-factory complex in Foshan, China, using KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses. Customization Options

  • Board Construction: 350gsm recycled kraft (standard), 400gsm for heavier product sets, or 250gsm for lighter kits — all FSC-certified recycled content
  • Exterior Finish: Natural kraft (uncoated), white kraft with clay coating for brighter printing, or kraft with matte aqueous coating for moisture resistance
  • Printing: Soy-based offset inks via KBA Rapida 105 — supports single-color (black, white, metallic), two-color, and full-color printing on kraft substrate; Heidelberg 7+1 UV available for high-coverage brand graphics
  • Surface Finishes: Blind embossing/debossing (no ink — pure texture), foil stamping in metallic or matte foil, or simple uncoated natural kraft for maximum eco-credentials
  • Molded Pulp Insert: Custom molded from post-industrial paper waste — available in natural grey, white, or kraft brown; 2mm–3mm wall thickness; holds 3–6 products with precision cavities
  • Closure: N52 neodymium magnetic snap (2× 8mm disc, 1.2kg pull) or natural jute twine tie for zero-metal option; magnetic closure magnets are removable for recycling
  • Window Option: PLA (polylactic acid) compostable window made from corn starch — fully industrially compostable per EN 13432
  • Dimensions: Standard 280×200×60mm; custom sizing from 180×140mm to 350×250mm

FAQ

Q: Is the kraft board truly 100% recycled?

A: Yes. Our standard kraft board is 100% post-consumer recycled content with FSC Chain-of-Custody certification. Each batch comes with mill certification documenting recycled fiber percentage. We also offer 70% recycled and FSC virgin fiber options if brand specifications require different fiber content.

Q: Can I use full-color printing on kraft board?

A: Yes, but with some limitations. Kraft’s natural brown base affects color rendition — whites and pastels appear muted compared to white-coated board. We recommend dark, bold brand colors and metallic inks for best results on kraft. Our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press can apply a white underprint layer to improve color vibrancy on kraft (adds approximately $0.15/unit for the extra station).

Q: Is the molded pulp insert sturdy enough for international shipping?

A: Yes. Our compression-molded pulp inserts have 2.5mm wall thickness and pass ISTA 1A drop testing (10 drops from 760mm) with target product weight simulators. For heavier product sets (500g+ total), we offer 3.0mm wall thickness as an upgrade.

Q: Can the magnets be removed for recycling?

A: Yes. The N52 magnets are housed in paper pockets behind the kraft board — end users can pry them out with a fingernail or small tool. We include a small “Recycle Me” instruction on the box interior. For brands wanting a fully magnet-free option, we offer jute twine tie closure.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for molded pulp inserts?

A: 500 units is our standard MOQ, which covers both the box and the custom molded pulp insert. The pulp mold tooling (one-time cost of $200–400 depending on cavity complexity) is included at no charge for first-time orders of 2,000+ units.
