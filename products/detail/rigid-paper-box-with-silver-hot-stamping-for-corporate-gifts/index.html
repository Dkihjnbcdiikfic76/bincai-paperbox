  • Foil Stamping: Silver, champagne silver, brushed silver, matte silver, holographic silver, or custom metallic Pantone; single or multi-level foil registration
  • Structure: Two-piece lid-and-base, magnetic flap, book-style hinge, or drawer-slide with ribbon pull

  • Add-Ons: Magnetic closure, satin ribbon pull-tab, gold/silver edge gilding, printed insert card, outer sleeve/obi band
  • Size Range: Compact 120×90×30mm (pen box) to 450×350×120mm (multi-item gift hamper)

Production Specifications

  • KBA Rapida 105 4-color: High-speed precision offset at 15,000 sheets/hour
  • Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV: Specialty metallic coatings and instant UV curing
  • Bobst SP 102 BMA: Hot foil stamping with ±0.15mm registration accuracy and multi-zone temperature control
  • Rigid Box Assembly: Fully automatic line — greyboard cutting (1000–2000gsm), corner taping, wrapping paper lamination, interior lining, and final assembly
  • Greyboard: 1000gsm–2000gsm FSC-certified recycled greyboard; double-ply lamination for structural integrity
  • Foil Film: High-grade polyester-based silver foil — 12μm thickness for crisp edge definition
