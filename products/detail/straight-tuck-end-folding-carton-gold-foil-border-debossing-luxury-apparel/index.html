Product Overview

Our straight tuck end folding carton elevates luxury apparel packaging with gold foil border detailing and custom debossed branding. Constructed from premium 18pt SBS (Solid Bleached Sulfate) board with soft-touch matte lamination, these cartons deliver the tactile and visual quality expected by high-end fashion brands. The straight tuck end closure with friction-lock tuck flap provides secure product retention while enabling elegant unboxing. Gold foil hot-stamped borders frame the debossed logo, creating depth and premium shelf presence. Manufactured on our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press in the 18,000m² ISO 9001 certified factory.

Design Features

  • Gold Foil Border: Precision hot foil stamping creates a luminous perimeter frame on the carton face
  • Custom Debossing: 1.5mm deep debossed logo or pattern — no ink, pure tactile elegance
  • Soft-Touch Lamination: Velvet matte finish provides a luxurious tactile experience and scratch resistance
  • Straight Tuck End: Clean closure design with reinforced friction-lock tuck flap
  • Premium Board: 18pt SBS C1S for bright white surface, excellent print fidelity, and folding precision
  • Flat Shipping: Cartons ship flat to minimize freight costs; simple manual or automated assembly

Applications

  • Luxury apparel and fashion clothing packaging
  • Premium shirt, tie, and accessory boxes
  • Designer brand e-commerce packaging
  • High-end underwear and lingerie cartons
  • Boutique fashion retail packaging
  • Corporate uniform and premium merchandise boxes
