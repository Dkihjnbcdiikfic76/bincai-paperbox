Product Overview

Bincai’s velvet jewelry roll travel organizer brings the elegance of a boutique display case into a portable format. Six individually designed compartments — elastic band loops for necklaces, a removable ring bar, zippered mesh pocket for earrings, bracelet snap strap, brooch panel, and watch cushion slot — keep every piece tangle-free and protected. The exterior is wrapped in premium velvet over 2mm rigid paperboard with a suede tie closure and full YKK zipper. Manufactured on Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses with precision die-cutting in our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility. 23 years of luxury packaging expertise.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: Heidelberg 7+1 UV offset — precise color registration on velvet substrate
  • Board: 2mm greyboard wrapped in 180gsm velvet-laminated art paper
  • Interior: Velvet-flocked rigid dividers, elastic band retention system
  • Zipper: YKK nylon coil zipper, gold-tone or silver-tone finish
  • Closure: Suede leather tie with reinforced eyelet
  • Compartments: 6 dedicated zones — necklace (×2 loops), bracelet snap, ring bar (removable), earring mesh pocket, brooch panel, watch cushion
  • Daily Output: 1.7 million color boxes (post-2025 smart factory expansion)
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody
