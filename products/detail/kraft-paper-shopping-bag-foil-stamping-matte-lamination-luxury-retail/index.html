Customization Options

  • Paper Stock: 150g, 180g, or 200g kraft paper — natural brown, bleached white, or black kraft. FSC-certified kraft available. Laminated (matte/gloss) or uncoated natural finish. Recycled content: 40%, 70%, or 100% post-consumer waste options.
  • Printing: Full-color offset printing on kraft stock (KBA 1050 4-color + Heidelberg 7+1 UV). CMYK + Pantone spot colors. One-side or both-side printing. Full-bleed graphics with no white borders. Consistent color on natural kraft surface (ΔE ≤ 2.5, adjusted for kraft base tone).

  • Base Construction: Flat base with cardboard insert (standard) — load capacity 3–5 kg depending on paper weight. Gusseted sides expand for bulky items. Reinforced bottom with additional paper layer for heavy loads. Square or rectangular footprint options.
  • Sizing: Standard sizes: Small (180×80×220mm), Medium (250×120×300mm), Large (320×160×380mm), Extra Large (400×200×450mm). Custom dimensions available — width 100–500mm, gusset 50–250mm, height 150–500mm.

Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — kraft paper grammage (±5%), rope handle tensile strength (≥ 80N), lamination film quality, cardboard insert caliper
  2. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer check every 500 sheets (ΔE ≤ 2.5 on kraft), Heidelberg Prinect registration monitoring
  3. Foil Stamping — registration ±0.15mm, foil adhesion tape test, stamping temperature and pressure verification per batch
  4. Lamination — bond strength peel test, visual inspection for bubbles/wrinkles/delamination, gloss/matte level verification
  5. Bag Forming & Handle Attachment — automatic bag-making machine with inline seam inspection, handle pull test (sampling every 500 units), gusset fold integrity
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.5 (critical) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4, load test (2× rated weight, 24h), visual inspection under D65
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — random sample pull Level II, flat-pack bundle integrity, barcode/label verification, moisture content check (≤ 8%)

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom printed kraft bags? A: Standard MOQ is 1,000–2,000 units per design depending on size. Smaller sizes (small/medium) start at 1,000 units. Large and extra-large bags start at 2,000 units. We can accommodate 500 units for repeat customers with existing printing plates.

Q: Can you match our brand’s specific Pantone color on kraft paper? A: Yes — we use Pantone spot color matching with X-Rite spectrophotometer verification. Due to the natural brown kraft base tone, the ΔE tolerance is adjusted to ≤ 2.5 (vs. ≤ 2.0 on white stock). We recommend providing a physical Pantone chip and reviewing a pre-production sample to confirm color appearance on kraft.

Q: How much weight can the bags hold? A: Load capacity depends on paper weight and bag size: 150g paper holds up to 3 kg, 180g paper up to 4 kg, 200g paper up to 5 kg. All bags include a reinforced cardboard base insert. For heavy or sharp-edged items, we recommend 200g paper with reinforced side seams. Load testing at 2× rated weight for 24 hours is standard QC protocol.

Q: Are the bags available without plastic lamination? A: Yes — we offer a 100% plastic-free option: uncoated kraft paper with embossed/debossed logos and paper rope handles. This option has a more natural, textured feel and is fully biodegradable. Note that uncoated kraft shows more surface texture variation and has slightly lower print vibrancy than laminated versions.

Q: What are the lead times and shipping options? A: Sample turnaround: 5–7 days with free pre-production samples. Production: 10–14 days for standard bags. Bags ship flat-packed in bundles of 25–50 units for maximum freight efficiency. Sea freight: 15–30 days depending on destination port. Air freight: 3–7 days for urgent orders.
