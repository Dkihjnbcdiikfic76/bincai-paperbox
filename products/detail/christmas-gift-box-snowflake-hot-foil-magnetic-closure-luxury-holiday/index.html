FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for Christmas snowflake foil boxes?

A: Standard MOQ is 500 pcs for single-color foil. Multi-color registered foil requires MOQ of 1000 pcs due to additional production steps. Free pre-production sample with foil design — 7–10 working days, freight collect.

Q: Can you match our brand’s specific Christmas color palette?

A: Yes. We color-match base paper to any Pantone reference and can produce snowflake foil in gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic. Multi-color foil (e.g., gold snowflakes with silver accent dots) is available for orders of 1000+ pcs. Send us your brand guidelines.

Q: When should we place our Christmas packaging order?

A: We recommend placing orders by August for September–October delivery to ensure holiday retail readiness. Our production schedule fills quickly for Q3/Q4 Christmas orders. Rush service available with 20% surcharge for late-season orders.

Q: Can the interior be customized for specific products?

A: Yes. We can create die-cut foam or flocked inserts for any product shape — jewelry, bottles, chocolates, accessories. Send us your product dimensions or samples and we will engineer a custom insert that protects during shipping and presents beautifully.
