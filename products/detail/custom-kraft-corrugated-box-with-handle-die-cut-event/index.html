Overview

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing’s custom kraft corrugated box with integrated die-cut handle is engineered for maximum portability and brand impact. The elegant single-piece design eliminates the need for separate bagging — the handle is precision die-cut into the box body during manufacturing. Ideal for event giveaways, trade show swag, retail carry-out, festival merchandise, and DTC (direct-to-consumer) subscription shipments. Manufactured in our ISO 9001:2015 & FSC certified 18,000 sqm factory (10,000+8,000 smart factory) with 23 years of expertise.

Applications

  • Trade Show Giveaways: Branded boxes with handle for brochures, samples, and promotional items — attendees carry your brand throughout the venue
  • Event & Festival Merchandise: Concert, conference, and festival swag boxes with easy carry for attendees
  • Retail Carry-Out: Premium alternative to plastic bags — apparel, cosmetics, specialty foods, bookstores
  • DTC Subscription Boxes: Monthly subscription shipments with branded unboxing + practical handle for reuse
  • Corporate Gift Kits: Multi-item gift sets with secure handle for client delivery
  • Food & Beverage Takeaway: Bakery boxes, meal kit packaging, gourmet food carry-out
  • E-commerce Shipping: Protective corrugated construction with built-in handle — no secondary packaging needed

Customization Options

  • Flute Profiles: E-flute (1.2mm, premium print surface) for lightweight items; B-flute (2.5mm, crush resistant) for heavier goods; EB-flute (3.8mm) for maximum protection
  • Handle Styles: Single die-cut slot handle, reinforced double-layer handle, twisted paper cord handle, polypropylene rope handle, grosgrain ribbon handle
  • Sizes: From 150×100×50mm (jewelry/gadgets) to 500×400×300mm (apparel/bulk goods) — fully custom
  • Printing: High-quality flexo or offset litho-laminated — CMYK + Pantone spot colors on kraft or white-top kraft liner
  • Finishes: Matte/gloss aqueous coating, spot UV, hot foil stamping, blind embossing on litho-laminated surface
  • Closure: Tuck-top, crash-lock auto-bottom, or adhesive strip for secure transit
  • Inserts: Die-cut corrugated dividers, foam cushioning, cardboard partitions for multi-item kits

For event giveaways, trade shows, and DTC subscription shipments, buyers typically choose between three packaging approaches. Here’s how Bincai’s die-cut handle corrugated box outperforms each:

The Subscription Box Advantage: For DTC brands shipping monthly boxes, every gram matters. A separate bag adds weight, volume, and a fulfillment step. Bincai’s integrated handle eliminates the bag entirely — saving ¥0.30–1.00 per shipment and removing one failure point. Over 10,000 monthly subscribers, that’s ¥36,000–120,000/year saved. The handle also transforms the box from disposable shipping material into a reusable container — extending brand visibility from 30 seconds (unboxing) to months (household reuse).
