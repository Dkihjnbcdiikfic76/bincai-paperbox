Customization Options

  • Board Construction: 2.5mm or 3.0mm greyboard with 157g C2S art paper wrap. FSC-certified board available. Gloss, matte, or soft-touch aqueous coating on exterior.
  • Printing: Full-color CMYK offset (KBA 1050) + UV spot (Heidelberg 7+1). Holiday patterns: snowflakes, stars, holly, Christmas trees, custom designs. Pantone spot color matching with ΔE ≤ 2.0.
  • Window Design: Die-cut PET window — 0.5mm clear PET film, custom shapes (rectangle, oval, heart, star, snowflake silhouette). Anti-fog coating option for cold-chain products. UV-blocking PET for light-sensitive items.
  • LED Lighting: Warm white LED (3000K) or cool white (6000K) spotlight module. Concealed CR2032 battery compartment (replaceable). Magnetic reed switch — activates on lid opening, deactivates on closing. 100+ hours battery life. Optional multi-color RGB LED with remote control.
  • Surface Finishes: Hot foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold, holographic) on lid borders and seasonal motifs. Deep embossing for snowflake/star patterns. Spot UV gloss on window border.
  • Closure: N52 neodymium magnets (4 per box) — hidden mounting with fabric cover. Strong audible click feedback. Optional ribbon tie for traditional holiday look.
  • Interior: Velvet flocking (0.8mm pile) in red, green, black, white, or custom color. Die-cut foam insert with product-specific cavities. Satin or grosgrain ribbon pull-tab.
  • Sizing: Custom — standard range 100×100×40mm (jewelry) to 300×200×80mm (confectionery sets). LED module size scales with box dimensions.

Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — greyboard grammage, PET film clarity/haze, LED module functional test (100% incoming), magnet grade verification
  2. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0, Prinect registration monitoring
  3. Die-Cutting & Window Insertion — dimensional check ±0.3mm, PET window bond test, clean edge inspection under 10× magnification
  4. Foil Stamping & Embossing — registration ±0.15mm, foil adhesion tape test, embossing depth verification
  5. LED Assembly & Electronics QC — 100% LED functional test, reed switch activation/deactivation test, battery compartment integrity, polarity check
  6. Finished Goods Audit — AQL 2.5 (major), AQL 1.5 (critical) per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4, full functional test (LED + lid + window), color match under D65

FAQ

Q: How long do the LED lights last? A: The warm white LED module (3000K) has a rated lifespan of 50,000+ hours. The CR2032 battery powers the LED for 100+ hours of continuous illumination. Since the reed switch activates the light only when the lid is open, battery life typically lasts through the entire holiday season. Batteries are user-replaceable.

Q: Can the window shape be customized beyond standard rectangles? A: Yes — we can die-cut windows in custom shapes including hearts, stars, snowflake silhouettes, ovals, and organic curves. Complex shapes may require a minimum 3mm radius on internal corners. The PET film follows the die-cut contour precisely.

Q: What if the LED stops working during transit? A: Each box undergoes 100% LED functional testing at QC Gate 5 before packaging. We ship with battery isolation tabs inserted — the LED is only activated when the end user removes the tab. If any LED fails, we replace the box free of charge under our standard quality guarantee.

Q: Is there a multi-color LED option? A: Yes — we offer RGB LED modules with a small remote control (IR or RF) that allows switching between 12 colors and 4 lighting modes (steady, slow fade, strobe, color cycle). This adds approximately $0.80–1.20 per unit and 2 days to production lead time.

Q: Can you produce these boxes for non-Christmas occasions? A: Absolutely — the die-cut window LED box design is adaptable to any occasion. We produce versions for Valentine’s Day (heart windows, red velvet), Mother’s Day, corporate events, product launches, and year-round luxury packaging. The holiday graphics are customizable per your design.
