Product Overview

This kraft paper gift box combines an FSC-certified kraft board shell with a molded bamboo fiber interior tray and organic cotton drawstring closure — engineered for corporate welcome kits and sustainable brand gifting where environmental credentials are central to the brand message. Every component is plastic-free: the kraft board is printed with soy-based inks, the interior tray is molded bamboo fiber (home-compostable per EN 13432), and the closure uses undyed organic cotton drawstring. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing on Heidelberg offset presses with dedicated eco-line production that prevents cross-contamination with conventional materials, every box supports brand sustainability claims with third-party certifications. With 23 years of packaging expertise, ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certification, and 220,000+ daily output across an 18,000 sqm integrated facility, Bincai delivers eco-packaging at industrial scale without compromising quality or aesthetics.
