FAQ

Q: Is this carton food-safe for direct contact with tea sachets? A: Yes — the SBS board, water-based printing inks, aqueous coating, and PET film adhesive are all FDA 21 CFR compliant for indirect food contact (packaging that contains individually wrapped food items). For direct food contact (unwrapped tea leaves), we can upgrade to a food-grade PE-coated interior liner that provides an additional barrier layer. All materials have full traceability documentation for your food safety audit requirements.

Q: Does the PET window fog up in refrigerated display cases? A: Our standard PET film may develop condensation fogging in refrigerated environments (below 5°C). For cold-chain or refrigerated retail display, we recommend upgrading to our anti-fog PET film, which uses a hydrophilic coating that spreads moisture into a transparent film instead of forming visible droplets. Anti-fog PET adds approximately $0.02–0.04 per unit and is validated down to -2°C ambient temperature.

Q: What window shape works best for tea sachet display? A: For tea sachet display, we recommend the leaf-shaped or elongated oval window — it provides the best balance of product visibility and structural integrity. The leaf shape is particularly effective for tea branding as it communicates the natural/organic positioning of the product. Rectangular windows maximize visibility but leave less printable surface for branding and regulatory information. We can produce samples of 2–3 window shapes for comparison before committing to mass production.

Q: Can this carton design be adapted for other food products besides tea? A: Absolutely — the same window folding carton construction works for: chocolate truffle boxes, macaron packaging, dried fruit and nut cartons, energy bar multipacks, spice blend boxes, coffee pod cartons, and gourmet cookie packaging. The only adjustments needed are the internal dimensions, window shape/size, and potentially the board caliper (thicker for heavier products). We maintain a library of 50+ standard carton styles that can be adapted to your product.
