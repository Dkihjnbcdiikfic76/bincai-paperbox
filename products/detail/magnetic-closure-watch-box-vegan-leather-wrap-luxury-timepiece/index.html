FAQ

Q: How does the vegan leather compare to genuine leather in durability and appearance?

A: Our 0.8mm high-density PU vegan leather closely matches genuine leather in appearance — available in matte, pebble, croc, and saffiano textures across 30+ colors. In durability testing, our vegan leather outperforms bonded leather (which peels within 1–2 years) and approaches the longevity of full-grain leather at a fraction of the environmental cost. It resists UV fading (Blue Wool Scale 5–6), passes 100+ Taber abrasion cycles without visible wear, and maintains flexibility from -20°C to 60°C. For brands wanting the look of leather without animal products, this is the premium choice.

Q: What watch sizes can the box accommodate — will it fit oversize diving watches?

A: The standard removable microsuede cushion accommodates watch dials 38–48mm with strap/bracelet fully closed. For oversize divers (48–52mm), we offer an extended cushion option with deeper cutout. For watches with deployment clasps, the cushion profile is adjusted to prevent pressure on the clasp mechanism. For extra-thick watches (>16mm case height), we increase the lid depth accordingly. Provide your watch specifications and we’ll confirm fit with pre-production samples.

Q: Can we brand the box with our logo on both the exterior and interior?

A: Yes. Exterior branding options include hot foil stamping (gold/silver/rose gold/holographic) on the lid top, spine, and interior flap. We also offer embossed/debossed logos directly into the vegan leather surface for a subtle, high-end look. Interior branding includes foil-stamped logos on the microsuede lining, printed certificate cards, and custom-printed inner lid panels. Our Bobst SP 102 BMA achieves ±0.15mm foil registration — critical for intricate watch brand wordmarks. MOQ for multi-location branding is 500 units.

Q: Is the box drop-tested for international e-commerce shipping?

A: Yes. Every new watch box design undergoes ISTA 1A simulated transit testing: 10 drops from 760mm on corners, edges, and faces, plus compression testing (200 lb top-load). Our magnetic closure is engineered to remain engaged through the drop sequence — the N42 neodymium magnets provide >3.5 kg pull force, sufficient to keep the lid closed even if the box is inverted during transit. For international shipments, we recommend pairing this box with a custom corrugated outer shipper (also available from Bincai as a matched set).
