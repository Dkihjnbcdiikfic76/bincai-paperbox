Product Overview

Bincai’s die-cut folding carton display tray combines structural integrity with retail-ready presentation. The auto-lock (crash-lock) bottom provides rapid assembly in under 3 seconds without glue or tape, while precision Bobst flatbed die-cutting enables custom window cutouts for product visibility. Available in CCNB (350gsm), white-lined chipboard (400gsm), SBS (300gsm), or FSC-certified kraft (400gsm). The PET window (0.2mm, food-safe grade available) lets customers see the product without opening the tray — proven to increase impulse purchases by 23% in retail studies. Manufactured on KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset (15,000 sheets/hour) with Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV for specialty coatings (spot UV, soft-touch, glitter) in our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility. Ideal for cosmetics, confectionery, consumer electronics accessories, and health/beauty counter displays serving 200+ global clients since 2003.

FAQ

Q: What board grades are available for food-contact applications? A: We offer food-safe boards: 300gsm SBS (solid bleached sulfate) and 400gsm FSC-certified kraft with water-based barrier coating. PET windows can be specified as food-grade (EU 10/2011, FDA 21 CFR compliant). All food-contact materials come with migration test documentation.

Q: How does the auto-lock bottom work — do we need to assemble with glue? A: No glue required. The auto-lock (crash-lock) bottom is a die-cut feature that locks into itself when the side panels are pushed inward. Assembly time is under 3 seconds per box by hand — no tools, no tape, no glue. This saves 30–40% labor time vs. glued-bottom trays for retail display assembly.

Q: Can the window be anti-fog for refrigerated products? A: Yes. We offer anti-fog PET film (0.2mm, passes ASTM D1003 haze testing at ≤5% after 24hrs at 4°C). This prevents condensation clouding in chilled retail displays for fresh food, dairy, and refrigerated cosmetics.

Q: What’s the turnaround time for a new custom die shape? A: Die tooling takes 3–5 working days. Pre-production samples ship in 7 days. Full production: 10–14 days after sample approval. Expedited sampling (5 days) available for rush projects.

Q: Can you print inside the tray as well as outside?\nA: Yes. KBA 1050 and Heidelberg 7+1 UV both support double-sided printing in a single pass. Interior printing adds branding visibility when the tray is viewed from above, effective for gift sets and subscription box unboxing experiences.
