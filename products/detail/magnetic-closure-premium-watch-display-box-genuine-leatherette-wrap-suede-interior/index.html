FAQ

Q: How does the leatherette compare to genuine leather in look and durability? A: Our premium PU leatherette is virtually indistinguishable from genuine leather in appearance and hand-feel — even experienced watch collectors often cannot tell the difference without close inspection. Key advantages over genuine leather: (1) consistent color and texture across every box in a production run (no natural leather variations), (2) superior resistance to humidity, UV fading, and temperature changes, (3) no animal products — suitable for vegan and cruelty-free brand positioning, (4) approximately 40-60% lower cost than equivalent genuine leather, and (5) easier to clean and maintain. We offer grades from standard to premium Italian-inspired PU with micro-perforated backing for breathability.

Q: Can the watch cushion be customized for different watch sizes and styles? A: Yes. The removable cushion can be produced in any size and shape to fit your specific watch model — from slim dress watches (6mm case height) to large dive watches (16mm+). We produce round cushions for traditional watch displays and rectangular cushions for modern designs. Cushion firmness can be customized (soft, medium, firm) based on how you want the watch to sit. Brand debossing or embroidery on the cushion is also available for an additional brand touchpoint.

Q: Will the magnetic closure interfere with mechanical watch movements? A: We use neodymium disc magnets (typically 8-10mm diameter) positioned in the flap edge and box rim, not in the watch cavity. The magnetic field strength at the watch position (6-8cm from the magnet pair) measures below 1 gauss — well below the threshold that could affect even vintage mechanical movements. For brands concerned about magnetism, we can use a mechanical snap closure or elastic band instead of magnets. We also offer anti-magnetic (mu-metal) shielding inside the box for extra protection at a small premium.

Q: What is the lead time and MOQ for a fully custom watch box? A: Standard MOQ is 300-500 units depending on material complexity. Lead time is 18-25 working days from artwork approval: 5-7 days for pre-production sample (free, express courier), 10-15 days for mass production, 3-5 days for shipping. The one-time setup costs include: foiling die ($80-150), embossing die ($80-120), and cutting die for the cushion ($50-100). These tools are yours to keep for reorders. Rush orders available in 10-12 working days.

Q: Can Bincai produce matching packaging for a watch collection — different sizes but same design language? A: Yes, this is a common request. Once your design language is established (leatherette color, texture, foil color, interior lining, cushion style), we can produce matching boxes in multiple sizes — single watch, double watch, watch + strap set, and watch + accessory box — all with identical materials and finishing. The setup cost per additional size is lower because the foil die and embossing die artwork is already created and only needs resizing. This creates a cohesive brand presentation across your entire product line.
