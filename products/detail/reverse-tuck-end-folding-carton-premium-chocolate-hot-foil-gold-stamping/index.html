Product Overview

Bincai’s reverse tuck end (RTE) folding carton delivers premium chocolate presentation at volume-friendly economics. The RTE structure — with top and bottom flaps tucking from opposite ends — provides secure closure while enabling flat-packed shipping for efficient logistics. Double-sided CMYK printing on 350gsm C2S (coated two sides) art board, enhanced with matte lamination and hot gold foil stamping. Manufactured on KBA 1050 4-color offset presses in our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility. 23 years of folding carton expertise.

Manufacturing Specifications

  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color offset — high-registration double-sided CMYK printing
  • UV Enhancement: Heidelberg 7+1 UV — selective spot UV on brand logo area

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom RTE chocolate cartons?

A: MOQ is 1,000 units for standard sizes and 3,000+ for custom dimensions requiring a new die. Pre-production samples ship within 5-7 working days. Lead time is 12-18 working days for production runs. Rush service (10-12 days) available for seasonal launches.

Q: Can I get the carton with a window so customers can see the chocolate inside?

A: Yes. Die-cut windows with crystal-clear PET film or compostable PLA bio-film are available in rectangular, circular, or custom-contour shapes. Windows are bonded with food-grade adhesive and tested for optical clarity (≥90% light transmission). For refrigerated chocolate products, anti-fog film prevents condensation clouding.

Q: Is the hot foil stamping durable enough for retail handling and shipping?

A: Yes. Bincai’s hot foil stamping on C2S art board achieves ≥2.0 N/cm² adhesion strength (tested per ASTM D3359 cross-hatch method) and withstands 10+ tape-pull tests without peeling. The matte lamination overlay provides additional scuff protection. All cartons pass ISTA 1A drop testing before shipment.

Q: Are the inks and materials safe for direct food contact?

A: All inks used in Bincai’s food-grade production zone are compliant with FDA 21 CFR for indirect food contact, EU Framework Regulation 1935/2004, and Nestlé Guidance Note on Packaging Inks. The carton is designed for indirect food contact (chocolate is wrapped or in a tray inside). For unwrapped chocolate, we offer an inner grease-resistant glassine liner. Full migration test reports from third-party labs available upon request.

Q: How do RTE cartons compare to auto-lock bottom cartons for chocolate packaging?

A: RTE (Reverse Tuck End) cartons are the most economical folding carton style — perfect for lightweight products like 100-200g chocolate bars. They ship flat and assemble in seconds. Auto-lock bottom cartons offer greater structural strength for heavier products (300g+ chocolate boxes, multi-piece gift sets) and assemble with zero tape. Bincai can produce either style on the same production line — we’ll recommend the optimal structure based on your product weight, retail display needs, and filling line setup.

Q: What if I need different sizes for a chocolate product range (e.g., 50g, 100g, 200g bars)?

A: Bincai excels at multi-size product families. We can create a cohesive brand look across 3-5 carton sizes — matching foil placement, color palette, and typography — while optimizing each size’s board weight and structural design for its specific product. All sizes share the same foil die (one-time tooling fee) for brand consistency. Minimum 1,000 units per size.
