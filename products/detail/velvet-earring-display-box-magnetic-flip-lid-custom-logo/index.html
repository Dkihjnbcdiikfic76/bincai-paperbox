FAQ

Q: What velvet colors are available for this earring box?

A: We stock 12+ standard velvet colors: black, navy, burgundy, cream, grey, champagne, royal blue, emerald green, blush pink, chocolate brown, white, and charcoal. For orders above 3,000 units, we can custom Pantone-match the velvet color to your brand palette. Interior satin lining is available in matching or contrasting colors.

Q: Is the N52 magnetic closure strong enough for shipping?

A: Yes. N52 is the highest grade of neodymium magnet commercially available, delivering ≥ 8 N pull force per pair. This is significantly stronger than the N35 magnets used in standard rigid boxes. Our 2,000-cycle automated hinge test and ISTA 1A drop testing confirm the closure remains secure throughout transit and years of customer use.

Q: Can you create custom foam inserts for my specific earring styles?

A: Absolutely. We use CAD knife cutting (±0.2mm tolerance) or laser cutting for intricate shapes. Send us your earring samples and we’ll design, prototype, and verify the insert fit before production. We handle studs (pre-cut slits), hoops (elasticized retainer strap), dangles (anti-tangle layered compartments), and multi-pair sets (color-coded cavities).

Q: What logo customization options do you offer?

A: We offer gold/silver/rose gold foil stamping on the lid exterior, interior base, or both; debossed or blind emboss for minimalist luxury branding; and full-color UV inkjet printing on velvet for photographic designs. Foil registration tolerance is ±0.15mm on our Bobst SP 102 BMA.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity and lead time?

A: MOQ is 500 units. Free pre-production samples ship in 5–7 working days with full decoration. Production lead time is 10–15 working days for new tooling and 5–7 working days for repeat orders. Rush production is available upon request.

Q: Is the velvet jewelry-safe? Will it cause tarnishing?

A: Yes. Our velvet undergoes VOC/residual solvent testing per GB/T standards and passes jewelry-safe certification — no tarnish reaction with silver, gold, platinum, or costume jewelry metals. The polyester velvet pile is chemically inert and moisture-resistant, protecting jewelry from environmental degradation.
