Product Overview

This custom magnetic closure wine bottle gift box is engineered specifically for 750ml standard wine and spirits bottles. The precision-cut EVA foam insert cradles the bottle securely, while the N35-grade magnetic closure provides a premium unboxing experience. Produced by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing — manufacturing premium packaging since 2003 — each box uses 2.5mm greyboard for structural integrity and 200gsm art paper wrap for a refined finish. Our 18,000 sqm facility with daily output of 1.7 million boxes ensures consistent quality at any order volume. Ideal for wineries, spirits brands, corporate gifting, and premium retail. The two-piece construction with removable lid creates a dramatic presentation that transforms a bottle into a luxurious gift — the recipient lifts the lid to reveal the bottle nestled in a precision-cut foam cradle, immediately communicating quality before the label is even seen. Bincai’s wine box line supports both single-bottle and dual-bottle configurations, with custom foam profiling for any bottle shape from standard Bordeaux to oversized Champagne magnums.

Features

  • Bottle-Specific Design: Fits standard 750ml Bordeaux, Burgundy, and spirits bottles; custom sizes available
