Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for custom folding carton boxes? A: Our standard MOQ is 500–1,000 pieces depending on carton size and complexity. For simple straight-tuck-end designs on standard 300–350gsm board, we can accommodate orders as low as 500 units. Complex structures with custom die-cuts, window patches, or special finishes may require 1,000+ pieces for cost efficiency. We provide free samples before mass production so you can verify quality.

Q2: How long does production take from artwork approval to shipment? A: A typical folding carton order follows this timeline: artwork preflight (4 hours) → digital color proof (24 hours) → die creation (48 hours) → physical sample approval (3–5 days) → mass production (7–12 working days) → QC and packing (1–2 days). Total lead time is approximately 15–22 working days from final artwork approval to FOB Foshan. Rush orders with a 30% surcharge can compress production to 7–10 days.

Q3: What printing and finishing options are available? A: Bincai offers full CMYK + Pantone spot color offset printing on our KBA Rapida 105 and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV presses. Finishing options include matte or gloss lamination, soft-touch coating, spot UV varnish, hot foil stamping (gold, silver, holographic, matte metallic), embossing, debossing, aqueous coating, and window patching (PVC, PET, or biodegradable PLA film). All processes are performed in-house at our 18,000 sqm Foshan factory.

Q4: Are your folding cartons food-safe and compliant with international regulations? A: Yes. We offer food-grade folding cartons printed with low-migration inks compliant with EU Regulation 10/2011 and FDA 21 CFR for indirect food contact. Our board stocks are sourced from FSC Chain-of-Custody certified mills. We also hold Sedex SMETA certification for ethical manufacturing practices. For pharmaceutical packaging, we provide tamper-evident seals, Braille embossing, and GMP-compliant production protocols.

Q5: Can Bincai handle large-volume orders and seasonal peaks? A: Absolutely. With a daily output capacity of 1.7 million boxes, 80+ workers operating across two shifts, and an equipment lineup that includes KBA Rapida 105, Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102, and Bobst Novacut 106-E, our 18,000 sqm facility is built for scale. We routinely handle seasonal surges for holiday packaging, promotional launches, and e-commerce peak seasons. Our BHS 2.5m corrugator line also allows integrated corrugated packaging production for brands that need both folding cartons and shipping boxes from a single supplier.
