FAQ

Q: Is the recycled kraft board food-safe for wine bottles? A: Yes — the PCR kraft board meets FDA 21 CFR 176.170 (components of paper and paperboard in contact with aqueous and fatty foods) and EU Framework Regulation (EC) No 1935/2004 for food contact materials. The box contacts the glass bottle exterior, not the beverage itself, so migration risk is negligible. Full compliance documentation available on request.

Q: Why only 1-2 color printing? Can I get full-color printing? A: Uncoated recycled kraft is highly absorbent — full-color CMYK offset would soak in and appear muddy. Water-based flexo with 1-2 solid Pantone colors produces crisp, vibrant results that suit eco-brand aesthetics. For full-color branding, consider our rigid gift box line with FSC Mix certified art paper wrap (still recyclable but requires lamination).

Q: Does the recycled content affect box strength? A: PCR kraft at 350-450gsm delivers comparable tear resistance and burst strength to virgin kraft of the same weight. The key difference is slightly higher porosity (absorbs moisture faster in high humidity). For export to tropical climates, we recommend the 450gsm option with molded pulp insert for additional bottle protection.

Q: Can you provide carbon-neutral shipping? A: Yes — we partner with Sea Green carbon offset program. For an additional ~0.03 USD/unit, we purchase verified carbon credits (Gold Standard / VCS) to offset ocean freight emissions from Foshan to your destination port. A carbon-neutral certificate with your order details is provided for your sustainability reporting.
