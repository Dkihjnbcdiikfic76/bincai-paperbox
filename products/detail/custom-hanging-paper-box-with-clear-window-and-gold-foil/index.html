Product Overview

The custom hanging paper box with clear window and gold foil stamping is purpose-built for retail pegboard display — combining a Euroslot hang tab with a transparent product window that lets shoppers see exactly what’s inside. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (founded 2003, Foshan, Guangdong), this folding carton is printed on our KBA 1050 4-color offset press with gold foil stamping for premium shelf appeal. Our 18,000 sqm factory (10,000+8,000 smart factory, expanded 2025) produces 1.7 million boxes daily, with full in-house printing, die-cutting, window-patching, and gluing under ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certifications.

The Euroslot hang tab is die-cut as part of the carton structure — no separate plastic hanger required — making the entire box 100% paper-recyclable. The clear PET or PVC window is machine-patched with ±0.5mm precision, and gold foil stamping on the front panel creates an eye-catching contrast that draws consumer attention on crowded retail walls. Suitable for toys, cosmetics, small electronics, stationery, and gift items sold in retail environments.

Quality Control

Bincai’s 6-stage QC for hanging window boxes:

  1. Material Inspection — Board GSM verification, moisture content 6–8%, window film clarity ≥ 92% transmittance
  2. Print Registration — Inline spectrophotometry, ΔE ≤ 2.0, 100% first-article approval
  3. Foil Stamp — 3M tape peel test for adhesion, foil coverage ≥ 98%, no edge lift
  4. Window Patch — Placement accuracy ±0.5mm, bond strength ≥ 15 N/cm, 72-hour cure verification
  5. Hang Tab Strength — Load test: 3× rated weight for 1 hour without deformation or tear
  6. Pre-Shipment — ISTA 1A drop test, AQL 2.5 sampling per ISO 2859-1, 100% visual for foil and window defects
