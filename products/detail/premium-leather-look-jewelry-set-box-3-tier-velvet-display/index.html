Product Overview

The Bincai Premium Leather-Look Jewelry Set Box is the ultimate all-in-one jewelry presentation and storage solution. Three independently-accessible tiers organize a complete jewelry collection: Tier 1 — necklace hooks with anti-tangle channel dividers; Tier 2 — ring rolls (12 slots) and earring cards (16 pairs); Tier 3 — bracelet cushion and watch pillow plus a hidden document compartment. The leather-look PU exterior with contrast stitching delivers the visual impact of genuine leather at a fraction of the cost, while the magnetic closure ensures all three tiers stay securely aligned. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — an ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified factory with 23 years of packaging expertise — each set box undergoes rigorous tier-alignment QC, hinge cycle testing, and velvet adhesion verification. Our dual-factory Foshan operation produces 1.7 million+ boxes daily.
