Production Workflow

Every magnetic closure watch display box follows our 11-stage luxury packaging workflow:

  1. Greyboard Cutting — 2mm rigid greyboard (1,200gsm) precision-cut on guillotine. Panel dimensions verified to ±0.3mm. Moisture content 7-9%.

  2. Magnet Embedding — N40 NdFeB magnets (8mm dia × 2) embedded into greyboard layers. Magnet pockets CNC-routed to 0.1mm clearance. Pull force calibrated to 12-18N for secure closure with one-hand opening.

  3. Board Lamination — Greyboard panels laminated with 157gsm C1S art paper (for printing) or kraft liner (for wrapped exterior). Water-based PVA adhesive, cold-pressed at 2.0 MPa for 4 hours.

  4. Exterior Wrap — Vegan Leather — 0.6-0.8mm premium PU vegan leather applied with spray adhesive. Wrap seams positioned on rear panel for invisible front appearance. Zero-bubble tolerance. Corner folding precision to ±0.5mm.

  5. Printing & Embellishment — Hot foil stamping (gold/silver/rose gold/copper on KURZ foil). Debossing for understated luxury. Optional full-color UV digital print on vegan leather surface.

  6. Interior Lining — Suede — 0.4mm ultra-suede microfiber (150gsm) applied to interior walls and lid. Adhesive: water-based contact cement, VOC-free. Zero-lift warranty. Color-matched or contrasting to exterior.

  7. LED Module Assembly — Custom LED strip (warm white 3000K or cool white 5000K) with magnetic reed switch. Battery housing (2× CR2032) or USB-C rechargeable module. Reed switch calibrated to activate at 15-30° lid opening angle.

  8. Watch Pillow Fabrication — Removable suede-covered PU foam pillow. Elastic retention strap for bracelet/watch band security. Custom dimensions matched to case diameter (28-48mm).

  9. Box Assembly — Lid-hinge alignment within 0.5mm. Magnetic closure auto-alignment verified. Interior lining seams inspected for straightness and adhesion. Functional LED test (activate/deactivate × 5 cycles).

  10. Quality Inspection — Seven-stage QC gate: board caliper → wrap bonding → hot foil adhesion → LED function → magnet pull force → assembly alignment → final cosmetic (AQL 1.0 Level II).

  11. Packaging & Export — Individual polybag with desiccant. Custom-printed rigid presentation sleeve optional. Master case: double-wall corrugated with EPE foam corner protectors. Fumigation-free ISPM 15 pallets.

  • End-of-Life Recyclability — While the magnetic closure and LED module must be removed before recycling, the rigid greyboard body and paper components are fully curbside recyclable. We provide labeling templates indicating “Remove electronics + magnets before recycling” for consumer guidance.

  • Master Case Optimization — Our packaging engineers optimize master case dimensions to maximize container utilization, reducing per-unit shipping emissions by 25-35% compared to standard master case sizing.

FAQ

Q: How long do the LED batteries last, and are they user-replaceable?

A: The standard CR2032 battery configuration (2× cells) provides 200+ hours of continuous illumination or approximately 3,000-5,000 open/close cycles (assuming 3-5 seconds of illumination per open). The battery compartment is accessible via a discreet rear panel with a small Phillips-head screw. Batteries are standard CR2032 coin cells available at any electronics or drug store. For retail display applications with frequent opening, we recommend the USB-C rechargeable module (500mAh Li-Po, 50+ hours per charge, recharges in 2 hours via standard USB-C).

Q: Will the magnetic closure damage automatic watch movements?

A: No. The N40 NdFeB magnets (8mm diameter × 2, positioned in the lid and base perimeter) generate a field strength of approximately 120-180 Gauss at 10mm distance — well below the threshold that affects modern watch movements. Most mechanical watches (including automatic calibers from ETA, Sellita, Miyota, Seiko, and in-house movements from Rolex, Omega, Patek Philippe) are anti-magnetic to at least 4,800 A/m (60 Gauss) per ISO 764, with many certified to 15,000+ A/m. The magnet-to-movement distance in our box design (minimum 35mm from magnet center to watch pillow center) ensures the field at the movement position is <10 Gauss — completely safe. For vintage watches (pre-1960s without anti-magnetic protection), we offer a non-magnetic ribbon-closure variant.

Q: Can the vegan leather exterior be printed with a full-color brand pattern?

A: Yes. We offer UV digital printing directly onto the vegan leather surface for full-color brand graphics, patterns, and photographic imagery. The UV ink bonds to the PU surface and cures instantly under UV LED lamps, creating a durable, scratch-resistant print. Resolution: 1,200 DPI. Color gamut: approximately 85% of Adobe RGB. For metallic effects, we combine digital printing with hot foil stamping in a two-pass process. Note: UV printing on dark vegan leather (black, navy, dark brown) requires a white ink underbase layer, which adds $0.15-0.30 per unit.

Q: What’s the difference between the vegan leather and genuine leather upgrade?

A: Vegan leather (PU) is our standard — consistent color, uniform texture, water-resistant, easy to clean, 70% lower carbon footprint. Genuine Nappa leather upgrade adds: authentic grain texture that varies box-to-box (each one unique), rich leather aroma, develops patina over time, higher perceived luxury value. Genuine leather adds $1.50-3.00 per unit and extends lead time by 5-7 days for LWG-certified tannery sourcing. Both options pass our 50,000-cycle abrasion test (Martindale) and 72-hour humidity resistance test.

Q: Can the box accommodate oversized watches (Panerai, G-Shock, 50mm+ dive watches)?

A: Yes. The standard internal dimensions accept watches up to 48mm case diameter and 55mm lug-to-lug. For oversized watches (up to 55mm diameter), we enlarge the box to approximately 140×105×90mm with a wider watch pillow. The LED illumination, magnetic closure, and material options remain identical — only the dimensions change. MOQ for custom size is 500 units. One-time structural design fee: included with order.

Q: Is this box suitable for e-commerce shipping — will it survive transit without damage?

We offer three protection levels: Standard (for Amazon FBA and general e-commerce), Premium (additional rigid outer gift box for luxury DTC brands), and Ultra (wooden crate for limited-edition timepieces over $5,000). Our 2025 shipping data shows 99.7% damage-free delivery rate across all e-commerce orders.
