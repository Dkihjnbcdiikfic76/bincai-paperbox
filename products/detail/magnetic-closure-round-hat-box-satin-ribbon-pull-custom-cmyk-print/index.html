Magnetic closure round cylindrical hat box with satin ribbon pull and custom CMYK printing, manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — a Diamond Member on Made-in-China.com with 23 years of manufacturing excellence (founded 2003). The round cylindrical design with N52 neodymium magnetic closure provides a premium unboxing experience — the lid snaps securely shut and opens smoothly with the satin ribbon pull. Ideal for luxury fashion accessories, premium gift sets, and boutique retail packaging. Produced on KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses across our 18,000 sqm facility (10,000+8,000 sqm smart factory, 2025 expansion). Daily output: 1.7 million color boxes + 220,000+ gift boxes.

Applications

  • Luxury hat and fashion accessory packaging with branded round presentation
  • Premium gift sets including beauty products, candles, and home fragrance
  • Bridal and wedding favor packaging for high-end events
  • Limited edition product launches requiring distinctive cylindrical packaging
  • Boutique retail packaging for scarves, gloves, and small leather goods

Customization Options

  • Full CMYK offset printing with custom artwork wrapping 360° around the cylinder
  • Gold, silver, or rose gold hot foil stamping for luxury branding elements
  • Satin ribbon color matched to brand palette with custom pull length
  • Interior lining: satin, velvet, suedette, or custom flocked finish
  • Custom ribbon pull with printed brand name or metallic thread detail
  • Optional foam insert cavity for product-specific fit and protection

Production Specifications

  • Material: 157gsm art paper wrapped over 2.5mm greyboard, N52 grade neodymium magnets
  • Printing: KBA 1050 4-color offset + Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV for 360° cylinder graphics
  • Finishing: Precision cylinder forming, magnet embedding, ribbon attachment, hand assembly
  • QC: Magnetic closure force test ≥ 3N, lid alignment ±0.5mm, color spectrophotometry
  • Certifications: ISO 9001:2015 quality management, FSC-C147399 chain of custody
