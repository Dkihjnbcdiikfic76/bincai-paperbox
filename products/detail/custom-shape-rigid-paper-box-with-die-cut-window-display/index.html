Rigid Box Quality Control — 15 Checkpoints

  1. Board Inspection: GSM verification, moisture content ≤12%
  2. Wrap Material Check: Color consistency, coating uniformity
  3. Print Registration: ±0.15mm across all plates
  4. Color Fidelity: ΔE ≤ 2.0 vs. Pantone reference
  5. Foil Transfer: 100% coverage, no flaking at edges
  6. Lamination Bond: ≥2.5 N/cm² peel test
  7. Die-Cut Precision: Window position ±0.2mm
  8. Corner Wrap: No wrinkles, no adhesive bleed
  9. PET Installation: Bubble-free, edge-sealed
  10. Hinge Function: Opens/closes 5000+ cycles without wear
  11. Lid Fit: Gap uniformity ±1mm around entire perimeter
  12. Magnet Strength: ≥5N holding force (if magnetic closure)
  13. Interior Fit: Insert snug to product, no movement
  14. AQL Sampling: ISO 2859-1, Level II, AQL 2.5
  15. Pack-Out Audit: Final box inspection before carton sealing

FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom shape rigid boxes?

A: Standard shapes (rectangular, square) start at 500 units. Custom die-cut contours require 1,000+ units to amortize tooling costs. Contact us for your specific shape — we’ll provide an exact MOQ with your quote.

Q: How precise are Bincai’s custom die-cut shapes?

A: ±0.2mm window positioning accuracy on Bobst flatbed die-cutters. Complex contours are laser-cut for prototype validation before steel-rule die production. Every shape is measured against the CAD file at QC checkpoint #7.

Q: Can Bincai do shapes other than rectangles — hexagons, trapezoids, hearts?

A: Absolutely. We’ve manufactured hexagons, octagons, trapezoids, hearts, triangles, ovals, and fully custom organic contours. Complex geometries may require hand-wrapping for premium corners — our 80+ skilled craftspeople handle these daily.

Q: What’s the lead time for a custom shape rigid box order?

A: Design and sampling: 5-10 working days. Production: 15-20 working days (standard), 20-25 working days (complex custom contours + specialty finishes). Rush service available for qualified projects.

Q: Can you match my brand’s Pantone colors exactly?

A: Yes. Our Heidelberg 7+1 UV press calibrates to ΔE ≤ 2.0 versus your Pantone reference. We maintain a spectral library of past client colors. G7 Master Color certified for consistent reproduction run-to-run.
