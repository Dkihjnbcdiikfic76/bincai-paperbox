FAQ

Q: How long does the LED light last, and can the battery be replaced by the customer?

A: The standard CR2032 battery configuration delivers 500+ hours of continuous illumination — sufficient for years of retail display or personal use. The battery compartment is designed for user-replaceable access with a small Phillips screwdriver (included). For high-volume retail display, we recommend the USB-rechargeable lithium battery option: 8-hour runtime per charge, micro-USB or USB-C charging port discreetly placed on the rear panel, rechargeable 500+ cycles.

Q: Can the velvet flocking color be custom-matched to my brand’s Pantone color?

A: Yes — custom-dyed velvet flocking fibers are available for orders of 1,000+ units with an approximate 7-10 day color-matching lead time (we produce a color swatch proof for approval before full production). Standard colors (black, burgundy, navy, royal blue, emerald, ivory, grey, chocolate) are available at 500-unit MOQ with no color-match delay. All flocking uses colorfast dyes tested to 50+ hours UV exposure without fading.

Q: Is the LED lighting safe for direct contact with fine jewelry — will it tarnish or discolor metals?

A: Our LED strips are fully enclosed behind a diffuser panel — there is zero electrical contact with the jewelry compartments. The 3000K warm white LEDs emit no UV radiation (unlike halogen or incandescent spotlights that can accelerate tarnishing of silver and certain gemstones). The 80+ CRI rating ensures accurate color rendering of gemstones, gold tones, and diamond brilliance without the color distortion common in cooler LED temperatures. For museums and archival-grade jewelry storage, we offer 2700K ultra-warm LEDs with UV filters.
