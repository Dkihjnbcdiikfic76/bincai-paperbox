Product Overview

Our custom half-slotted container (HSC) with die-cut partitions is engineered specifically for the safe transport of glass bottles — wine, spirits, olive oil, gourmet beverages, and pharmaceutical liquids. The HSC design features an open top with flaps on one face only, combined with precisely die-cut interlocking dividers that isolate each bottle and prevent glass-to-glass contact during transit. Manufactured from double-wall B/C flute corrugated board for superior crush resistance, each container is assembled with industrial hot-melt adhesive for structural integrity. Bincai’s 23 years of packaging expertise and ISO 9001-certified quality system ensure every shipper meets ISTA performance standards.

Design Features

  • Half-Slotted Container (HSC) Design: Open-top construction with flaps on one face for easy loading and unloading
  • Die-Cut Interlocking Partitions: Precision-cut dividers create individual cells for each bottle — no glass-to-glass contact
  • Double-Wall B/C Flute: Combined B-flute (3mm) and C-flute (4mm) for maximum stacking strength and puncture resistance
  • Kraft or White Top Liner: Natural kraft for industrial use or white-coated top liner for branded presentation
  • Hot Melt Adhesive Assembly: Industrial-grade hot melt bonding for structural rigidity during palletized transport
  • Custom Cell Configuration: 2-bottle, 3-bottle, 6-bottle, and 12-bottle partition layouts available
  • Flat-Pack Shipping: Ships flat to minimize freight volume; assembles on-site in seconds

Applications

  • Wine and champagne bottle shipping for wineries and distributors
  • Spirits and liquor bottle transport (whiskey, vodka, gin, rum)
  • Olive oil and gourmet food bottle distribution
  • Craft beer and cider bottle shipping
  • Pharmaceutical and laboratory glass bottle logistics
  • Cosmetic and fragrance bottle bulk transport

Customization Options

  • Box dimensions tailored to exact bottle height, diameter, and count
  • Board grades: single-wall C flute, double-wall B/C flute, or triple-wall for heavy bottles
  • Liner options: natural kraft, white top, or clay-coated for custom printing
  • Cell count: 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, or 12 bottle configurations
  • Custom flexographic printing on exterior panels: 1–3 colors
  • Perforated tear-strip for easy retail conversion
  • Hand holes for ergonomic carrying
  • Partition material: corrugated, chipboard, or molded pulp fiber
