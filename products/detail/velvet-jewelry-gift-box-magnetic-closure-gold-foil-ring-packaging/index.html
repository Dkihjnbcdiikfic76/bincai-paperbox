Product Overview

This luxurious velvet jewelry gift box combines a magnetic flap closure with gold hot foil stamping — the quintessential packaging for fine jewelry brands that demand an unforgettable unboxing experience. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (established 2003, Foshan, China), the rigid box is constructed from 800–2000gsm greyboard wrapped in premium art paper with 0.8–1.5mm velvet flocking interior. The hidden magnetic closure ensures the flap seats flush every time, while the satin ribbon pull tab provides an elegant opening gesture. Produced on KBA 1050 and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses across our 18,000 sqm factory with 1.7 million boxes daily capacity, Bincai serves jewelry brands from boutique artisans to international retailers with consistent luxury quality.
