Customization Options

  • Handle Options: Twisted kraft paper handles (standard — fully recyclable mono-material), cotton rope (natural or dyed), jute twine, or flat cotton tape. Handle color can match or contrast with bag. Die-cut handle slots with reinforced backing.
  • Printing & Ink: Soy-based ink (standard — low-VOC, renewable), water-based ink, or traditional offset ink. CMYK printing on kraft stock requires a white underbase for vibrant colors. Single-color or limited-palette designs work best with the kraft aesthetic.

Optional: matte lamination for durability and water resistance (note: lamination makes the bag non-recyclable in standard paper streams). Water-based coating option for light moisture protection while maintaining recyclability.
  • Size Range: Small (150×100×200mm), Medium (250×180×280mm), Large (350×250×350mm), Jumbo (450×350×400mm). Custom dimensions available. Tall-and-narrow wine bottle bag configuration also available.

Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Raw Material Inspection — Kraft paper grammage and fiber composition, FSC chain-of-custody verification, handle material tensile strength, soy ink certification
  2. Pre-Press Proofing — Color calibration for kraft substrate (white underbase where needed), foil die fit verification, trap adjustment for uncoated paper
  3. In-Process Printing — Color consistency on absorbent kraft surface, drying time verification (soy ink requires 24–48 hours on kraft), registration check

FAQ

Q: Is this bag truly recyclable even with the gold foil?

A: Yes. Hot foil stamping is applied at < 0.1 micron thickness and disperses into inert particles during the repulping process at paper recycling facilities. The bag has been certified Category I (recyclable with standard paper/cardboard) per PTS-RH 021. Unlike laminated bags or those with plastic handles that require separation, this entire bag can go directly into curbside paper recycling.

Q: Can I use my brand color instead of gold foil?

A: Yes. We offer silver, rose gold, copper, white, and holographic foil options. For specific brand colors, we can custom-order colored foil (lead time adds 5–7 days). Alternatively, soy-based ink printing in your brand color on the kraft bag creates a different aesthetic — more understated and fully recyclable without any foil.

Q: Will the kraft paper bag look premium enough for my luxury products?

A: The combination of natural kraft paper and gold foil stamping creates a “quiet luxury” aesthetic that’s highly desirable in today’s market — think Aesop, Le Labo, or Glossier. For additional premium cues, we recommend: white kraft instead of natural brown, a heavier 210gsm stock, double-sided foil stamping, and a coordinating tissue paper insert. Many of our luxury brand clients have switched from glossy laminated bags to this understated, sustainable look.

Q: How does soy-based ink compare to traditional ink on kraft paper?

A: Soy-based ink produces slightly softer, more matte colors on uncoated kraft paper compared to petroleum-based ink — this actually complements the natural kraft aesthetic. Saturation is excellent with a white underbase for multi-color designs. Soy ink requires 24–48 hours of drying time on kraft (vs. 6–12 hours for coated paper), so we factor this into our production schedule. The environmental benefit is significant: soy ink is renewable, low-VOC, and makes the bag easier to repulp during recycling.

Q: What’s the minimum order for a custom size kraft bag?

A: MOQ is 500 units per design and size. Custom dimensions require a new die (fabricated in-house, 3–5 days, no charge for production orders). For the most cost-effective approach, we recommend choosing from our standard size range (S/M/L/XL) with custom printing and foil — this avoids die charges and typically ships within 10 days."
