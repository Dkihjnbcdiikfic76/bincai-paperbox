FAQ

Q: How long does the LED mirror battery last — will it still work when the customer receives it? A: The CR2032 battery provides approximately 200–300 open/close cycles (roughly 6–12 months of normal retail use). For products with extended shelf life, we can install a battery isolation tab that the end customer removes before first use, ensuring full battery life upon delivery. We also offer a rechargeable lithium-polymer upgrade with USB-C charging for premium applications.

Q: Can the LED color temperature be customized? A: Yes. Standard is 4000K neutral white, but we offer 2700K warm white (flattering for gold jewelry), 5000K cool white (ideal for silver and diamond), and RGB addressable LED strips for color-changing effects. All LED modules are CE and RoHS certified.

Q: Is the velvet interior removable for cleaning? A: The velvet flocking is permanently bonded to the insert tray for durability. We recommend gentle lint-roller cleaning rather than wet cleaning. For applications requiring removable/washable interiors, we offer a removable satin pillow insert option instead of bonded flocking.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for LED mirror boxes? A: MOQ for LED mirror boxes starts at 500 units due to the electronic component procurement. For orders above 5,000 units, the per-unit LED cost drops significantly. Free pre-production samples (including working LED) are provided in 7–10 days.
