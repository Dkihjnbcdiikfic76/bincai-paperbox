Kraft Paper Tube Cylinder Box with Metal Lid — Eco-Conscious Cosmetic Packaging

Meet growing consumer demand for sustainable packaging with Bincai’s kraft paper tube cylinder box. Crafted from FSC-certified kraft paperboard with an elegant metal lid, this packaging solution combines environmental responsibility with premium presentation.

Key Features

  • 100% Recyclable Construction: Kraft paperboard body and base — fully recyclable in standard paper waste streams. Metal lid is separable for dedicated metal recycling.
  • Smooth Interior Finish: Natural kraft interior or optional PLA bio-plastic lining for products requiring moisture or oil barrier protection.
  • Precision Metal Lid: Seamless tinplate or aluminum slip lid with brushed, matte, or polished finish. Embossed logo option available.
  • Water-Based Ink Printing: Full CMYK printing with eco-certified water-based inks on our KBA 1050 4-color offset press — vibrant colors without petroleum solvents.
  • Customizable Dimensions: Diameter from 30mm to 150mm, height from 30mm to 300mm. Standard sizes for popular cosmetic formats.
