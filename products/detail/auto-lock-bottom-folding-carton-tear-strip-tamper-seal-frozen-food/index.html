Product Overview

The auto-lock bottom folding carton with integrated tear-strip tamper seal is engineered for frozen food packaging — from premium frozen meals and ice cream to frozen seafood and ready-to-bake goods. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing (founded 2003, Foshan, 18,000 sqm, ISO 9001:2015 & FSC-C147399 certified), this carton combines moisture-resistant PE-coated SBS board with a patented tear-strip opening that provides visible tamper evidence — consumers can see at a glance if the package has been opened. The auto-lock bottom pops open for high-speed filling lines, and the tear strip eliminates the need for scissors or knives at point of use. Daily output reaches 1.7 million boxes on KBA Rapida 105 and Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV presses with Bobst SP 102 BMA/E die-cutters, Bobst Visioncut 106 LER, and Bobst Expertfold 145 folder-gluer.
