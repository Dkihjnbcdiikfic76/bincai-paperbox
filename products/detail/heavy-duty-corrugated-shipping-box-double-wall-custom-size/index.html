FAQ

Q: What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for custom double wall corrugated boxes? A: Standard MOQ is 500 units for simple RSC (Regular Slotted Container) styles. Complex structures such as die-cut mailers with tear strips or multi-piece inserts start at 1,000 units. We offer free pre-production samples within 3–5 business days so you can validate fit and print before committing to a production run.

Q: How do I choose between BC flute and BE flute for my product? A: BC double wall (~6mm thick, 48–71 lb/in ECT) is ideal for heavy industrial items over 20 kg — auto parts, machinery components, or bulk liquids. BE double wall (~4.5mm thick, 40–55 lb/in ECT) suits medium-weight products like electronics, appliances, or multi-unit e-commerce bundles where you want strong protection with a slightly thinner profile that optimizes shipping cube. Our engineers can recommend the right flute combination based on your product weight, fragility, and distribution channel.

Q: Can Bincai produce FBA-compliant shipping boxes? A: Yes. We manufacture to Amazon FBA packaging requirements including maximum outer dimensions (63.5 cm on the longest side for standard-size, or up to 150 cm for oversize), scannable FNSKU barcodes (ANSI Grade C minimum), and suffocation warning labels where required. We keep a library of FBA size templates and can advise on the most cost-efficient tier for your product dimensions.

Q: What print options are available on corrugated boxes? A: We offer three tiers of print quality. Flexo direct print (1–4 spot colors) is the most economical for simple logos and shipping marks. Litho-laminated labels use our KBA Rapida 105 or Heidelberg CD 102 7+1 UV presses to produce full CMYK + spot color graphics on coated paper, which is then bonded to the corrugated board — ideal for retail-ready or branded unboxing experiences. Digital preprint is available for short runs or variable data. All options use food-safe, low-VOC inks.

Q: What is the typical lead time from artwork approval to delivery? A: Production lead time is 12–18 business days after artwork approval and sample sign-off. Rush orders (7–10 days) are available for standard styles with a small surcharge. Shipping from Foshan to major ports takes 2–5 days domestically or 18–30 days by sea freight to North America and Europe. We also support FOB Nansha/Shekou, CIF, and DDP Incoterms for international orders.
