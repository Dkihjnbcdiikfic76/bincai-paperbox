Product Overview

The Snap-Lock Bottom Folding Carton for Premium Loose Tea Packaging has become the default format for specialty tea brands worldwide — and for good reason. The automatic snap-lock base deploys instantly with a single push, forming a rigid-bottom container capable of holding 100g to 500g of loose leaf tea, herbal infusions, or specialty coffee without the bottom collapsing under product weight once filled. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. in our 18,000 sqm ISO 9001 & FSC certified facility, this carton brings 23 years of paper packaging expertise to the booming global specialty tea market.

The carton body uses heavy 350gsm C1S (coated one side) SBS (solid bleached sulfate) board, giving it a weight and tactile quality that communicates premium value at shelf level. The coated side receives full CMYK offset printing on our KBA Rapida 105 4-color press or Heidelberg Speedmaster XL 7+1 UV for jobs requiring inline spot UV — achieving food-safe, vibrant graphics that bring your tea brand’s story to life. The uncoated interior naturally resists grease migration and is compatible with all standard airtight inner pouches (stand-up zipper, flat heat-seal, or tin-tie kraft).

Why Snap-Lock Bottom for Tea?

The floor-standing retail environment for premium tea is competitive. The box must sit squarely on the shelf, withstand repeated customer handling, and survive the journey from factory → distributor → retailer → consumer without losing its shape. A standard tuck-end carton bottom can buckle when filled with 250g+ of dense loose tea — the snap-lock mechanism eliminates this failure mode entirely.

The lock is formed by a die-cut interlocking tab pattern on the bottom flaps. When the carton is squared up and the base is pushed flat, the tabs engage and lock, distributing the product weight across four reinforced corner seams rather than relying on frictional tuck. Load testing confirms the snap-lock base holds over 3kg of static weight — well beyond any realistic tea fill weight.

Quality Control — AQL 1.5 Throughout

Our 8-stage QC process covers every carton:

  1. Board Incoming QC: Caliper, moisture, brightness, and stiffness per TAPPI standards
  2. Print Color Check: Spectrophotometer verification at makeready and every 500 sheets
  3. Die-Cut Tolerance: ±0.2mm on snap-lock tab profiles using go/no-go gauges
  4. Glue Bond Test: Pull-strength verification on random samples
  5. Snap-Lock Deployment: 100% of cartons manually deployed before packing
  6. Load Test: Random samples filled with 3× rated weight for 24 hours — zero failures accepted
  7. Food Contact Migration: Certified compliant with EU 1935/2004 and FDA 21 CFR for food contact
  8. Final AQL 1.5 Visual: General appearance, print registration, surface defects
