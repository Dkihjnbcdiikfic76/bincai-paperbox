Product Overview

The two-tier drawer-slide velvet jewelry display box represents the pinnacle of luxury jewelry presentation — an heirloom-quality storage solution engineered for fine jewelry brands, luxury retailers, and discerning consumers. Manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. (Diamond Member on Made-in-China.com, Audited Supplier, founded 2003), this box combines old-world jewelry-box charm with modern precision manufacturing.

The upper tier features a hinged lift-up lid revealing a fully flocked interior with ring roll channels (accommodating 4–12 rings), earring stud strips, necklace hooks, and a velvet-lined pendant well. The lower drawer operates on precision ball-bearing slides — tested to 5,000+ open/close cycles with ≤2N consistent pull force — and reveals compartmented channels for bracelets, bangles, and larger pieces. An antique brass lock and key mechanism (100% pairing verified) provides both security and classic jewelry-box aesthetics, with hardware finish options including polished chrome, rose gold, and matte black.

Every interior surface — lid underside, drawer cavity, and compartment dividers — is covered in electrostatic velvet flocking at 1.0–1.5mm pile height, tested to ISO 105-X12 rub-fastness (>4.0 grey scale). The exterior is wrapped in premium PU leatherette (30+ colors and textures: smooth, crocodile, saffiano, pebbled) applied with ±0.2mm precision on our automatic rigid-box mounting line. Optional features include a beveled-edge lid mirror, LED-lit mirror with magnetic reed switch activation, and hot foil stamped or debossed branding.

Produced across our 18,000 sqm dual-factory campus (10,000 + 8,000 sqm smart factory) with Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV and KBA Rapida 105 4-color presses, Bobst SP 102 BMA/E precision die-cutting, and a dedicated luxury jewelry box assembly zone staffed by 50+ specialist craft workers. Each box passes 8 inspection gates before final packaging.

FAQ

Q: Can the lock be keyed alike across a production run? A: Yes. For retail display applications requiring uniform keying (all display cases open with a single master key), we offer keyed-alike configurations with brass, chrome, or rose gold finishes. The lock mechanism is sourced from an ISO 9001-certified hardware supplier with 100% pairing verification. Minimum 500 units for keyed-alike runs.

Q: What’s the difference between rayon velvet and cotton velvet flocking? A: Rayon velvet (standard) offers consistent 1.0–1.5mm pile height with excellent color saturation and rub-fastness >4.0 grey scale (ISO 105-X12). Premium cotton velvet (natural fiber) provides a softer hand feel and superior moisture absorption — preferred for heirloom-quality boxes and brands emphasizing natural materials. Both are applied via the same electrostatic flocking process and available in 20+ colors.

Q: Can the drawer be configured for watches instead of bracelets? A: Absolutely. The lower drawer tray insert is fully customizable — we can configure it with watch pillow slots (1–4 watches with removable pillows), sunglass channels, cufflink compartments, or mixed layouts accommodating rings + bracelets + watches in a single drawer. Our CAD team provides 3D renderings of your exact layout before production.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom configurations? A: MOQ is 300–500 units for standard configurations (antique brass hardware, rayon velvet, PU leatherette exterior). Custom sizes, premium cotton velvet, genuine leather wrap, or complex interior layouts (4+ compartment types) require 500–1,000 units. Free pre-production physical samples with full decoration in 7–10 working days.

Q: How is the LED mirror powered, and can batteries be replaced? A: The optional LED-lit mirror uses a magnetic reed switch embedded in the lid — lights activate automatically when the lid is opened. Powered by 2× CR2032 coin cell batteries (included, user-replaceable) housed in a concealed battery compartment with screw-secured access panel. Battery life: approximately 1,500 open/close cycles at 20 seconds per cycle. For high-volume retail applications, we offer rechargeable Li-ion modules with micro-USB charging.

Q: Is the box suitable for international air freight? A: Yes. The rigid greyboard construction (1,200–1,800gsm) with reinforced corner joints passes ISTA 1A drop testing. Individual boxes are packed in OPP bags with EPE foam corner protectors, then placed in master cartons with corrugated dividers. We recommend double-wall export cartons for LCL sea freight or single-wall for air freight. Palletization uses ISPM 15 heat-treated wooden pallets compliant with all international shipping regulations.

Q: Can I order the box without the lock mechanism? A: Yes. We offer a lock-free version where the drawer secures via magnetic closure (N48 neodymium magnets) instead of the lock mechanism. This reduces unit cost by approximately 15–20% and is popular for retail counter displays where security is handled at the store level. The magnet option maintains the same drawer slide smoothness and visual aesthetic.
