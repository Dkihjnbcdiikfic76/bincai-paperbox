FAQ

Q: Is the jute handle strong enough to carry 3-4 full-size soap bars? A: Yes. Our jute twine handles are 3-ply twisted natural jute with a tensile strength of approximately 25 kg (55 lb). Each handle attachment point uses a reinforced brass eyelet (inner diameter 6mm) that distributes the load across the kraft board. We validate handle strength with a 24-hour static load test at 2× the rated box weight. For a box designed to hold 4 soap bars (approximately 500g total), the handle is rated for 1 kg — more than adequate with a 25× safety margin. If you need even more strength, we can upgrade to 4-ply jute or cotton rope.

Q: Can the box be fully recycled or composted after use? A: The box body, recycled cardboard tray, and paper components are 100% recyclable in standard municipal paper waste streams. The jute twine handle is made from natural plant fiber and is biodegradable — it can be composted or recycled with the paper. We use water-based adhesives and soy-based inks that do not interfere with the recycling or composting process. The only component to separate is the optional PLA window film — PLA is industrially compostable where facilities exist (EN 13432 certified), but not accepted in most curbside recycling. We can provide a fully plastic-free version with no window for 100% curbside recyclability.

Q: Will soy-based ink print well on unbleached kraft board — won’t the colors look dull? A: Soy-based inks on kraft require specific formulation and press settings that differ from printing on white-coated board — and our press operators have mastered this over 23 years. We use higher pigment-load soy inks and adjust impression pressure to achieve vibrant, saturated colors on the kraft surface. Whites and light colors are printed as a base layer (like an undercoat) before the main color, and we use Heidelberg UV curing for crisp, sharp results. The result is a rich, organic color aesthetic that actually looks more premium on kraft than on white board — it conveys authenticity and craftsmanship that resonates with eco-conscious consumers.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for a custom kraft soap box? A: Standard MOQ is 500-1000 units depending on complexity. For artisan soap makers and small brands, we can produce as few as 300 units with a small production surcharge. Setup costs include: printing plate ($80-120), cutting die for tray ($60-100), and foiling die if hot stamping ($80-120). Lead time is 18-25 working days from artwork approval with free pre-production sample in 5-7 days.

Q: Can I get matching product packaging for a full line of bath products? A: Yes. Once your kraft and print design language is established, we can produce matching packaging across your entire product line: soap bar sleeves, bath bomb boxes, lotion cartons, candle boxes, gift set boxes in multiple sizes, and shipping mailers — all in consistent kraft aesthetic with matching print style, jute handles, and recycled tray inserts. This creates a cohesive, recognizable brand identity that consumers associate with your commitment to sustainability.

Sustainability

Bincai’s kraft eco packaging is designed from the ground up for environmental responsibility:

  • FSC Certified Kraft Paper — All kraft board is sourced from FSC-certified forests and controlled sources. Your packaging tells a responsible sourcing story.

  • 100% Recyclable — Kraft paperboard, recycled cardboard tray, and jute twine are all compatible with standard curbside paper recycling. Jute is a natural plant fiber that can be composted or recycled with paper.

  • Soy-Based Inks — Vegetable-based offset inks with lower VOC emissions and easier de-inking during the recycling process compared to petroleum-based inks.

  • Water-Based Adhesives — No solvent-based glues at any stage of production. Water-based adhesives do not interfere with recyclability or composting.

  • Flat-Ship Efficiency — Kraft boxes ship flat (knocked down), reducing transport volume and carbon footprint by up to 85% compared to pre-assembled boxes. Your warehouse team assembles the box in seconds: pop open the body, insert the tray, and the handle is pre-attached.
