Kraft paper seed-infused plantable gift box with biodegradable soy-ink printing — a packaging innovation from Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. that transforms from protective packaging into a garden. Each box is made from 100% post-consumer recycled kraft paperboard embedded with a curated blend of non-GMO wildflower seeds (typically a mix of poppy, daisy, cornflower, and black-eyed Susan). After the recipient enjoys the gift, they soak the box in water overnight, plant it in soil, and within 7-21 days the seeds germinate — leaving zero waste. Every element is biodegradable: the board decomposes within 90 days in soil, soy-based inks (heavy-metal-free, VOC-free) break down naturally, and starch-based adhesive dissolves without residue. No plastic windows, no synthetic lamination, no petroleum-based inks. Manufactured at our 18,000 sqm Foshan facility under ISO 9001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody, and seed quality certification — 23 years of Guangdong manufacturing since 2003.

The Plantable Packaging Story

Bincai developed this plantable packaging in response to growing demand from eco-conscious brands seeking packaging that aligns with their sustainability commitments. Traditional gift boxes — even “recyclable” ones — often end up in landfill due to lamination, foil stamping, or mixed-material construction that prevents recycling. Our plantable box solves this: the recipient is encouraged to plant it, and even if they don’t, the box fully biodegrades in composting conditions within 90 days.

The embedded seed blend is rigorously tested for germination rate (≥85%), seed purity (99.9% weed-free), and shelf life (2+ years when stored in cool, dry conditions). Seeds are sourced from certified non-GMO suppliers and are safe for planting in all USDA hardiness zones.

Customization Options

  • Seed Mix: Custom seed blend for your brand — wildflower meadow mix (poppy, daisy, cornflower, black-eyed Susan), herb garden mix (basil, thyme, oregano, chives), regional native species for specific geographies, or single-species for branded “signature flower” campaigns. Seed embedment density: 20-40 seeds per 100cm²
  • Board Construction: 280-350gsm recycled kraft paperboard, embedded with seeds between two kraft layers during the papermaking process. Available in natural kraft brown, unbleached cream, or post-consumer grey
  • Printing: Soy-based ink offset printing — full CMYK plus Pantone spot colors. The ink is heavy-metal-free (no cadmium, lead, mercury), VOC-free, and certified compostable. Coverage up to 60% recommended for optimal seed germination. Custom “Plant Me” instructions printed directly on the box with illustrated planting guide
  • Construction Styles: Two-piece rigid setup box (telescopic lid + base), tuck-top folding carton, pillow box, drawer box, or custom die-cut shapes. All with starch-based adhesive assembly
  • Interior: Seed-paper tissue interleaf (also plantable), unbleached crinkle paper fill, or cotton drawstring inner pouch. No petroleum-based foam or velvet — all-natural interior options
  • Finishing: Blind embossing (plastic-free branding), natural twine or jute tie, kraft paper belly band, or cotton ribbon. No foil stamping (aluminum foil is not biodegradable) — embossing achieves dimensional branding without synthetic materials
