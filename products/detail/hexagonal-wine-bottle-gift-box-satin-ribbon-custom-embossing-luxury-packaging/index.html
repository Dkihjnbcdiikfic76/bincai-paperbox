Product Overview

Bincai’s hexagonal wine bottle gift box combines architectural geometry with premium presentation — a six-sided design that commands attention on retail shelves and creates an unforgettable unboxing experience. Backed by 23 years of premium packaging expertise (founded 2003) and ISO 9001:2015 + FSC Chain-of-Custody certification, this hexagonal box is the definitive packaging solution for wineries, distilleries, corporate gifting, wedding favors, and luxury retail.

FAQ

Q: Can the hexagonal box accommodate different bottle shapes (Bordeaux, Burgundy, Champagne)? A: Yes. Bincai’s in-house die-making team can create custom EVA foam cradles based on your exact bottle profile — whether Bordeaux (tall/shoulder), Burgundy (sloping), Champagne (thick-walled), or proprietary bottle shapes. Simply provide your bottle CAD file or ship us a sample bottle for precision templating.

Q: What customization options are available for the exterior finish? A: Beyond brushed gold foil with matte lamination, we offer: full-color CMYK offset printing, Pantone spot colors, textured linen or leatherette wrapping, hot foil stamping in gold/silver/rose gold/copper/holographic, multi-level embossing/debossing, spot UV varnish, and soft-touch velvet coating. Combinations are fully supported.

Q: Can the box include compartments for accessories like glasses or corkscrews? A: Absolutely. The hexagonal interior can be configured with EVA foam cavities for a bottle plus companion glassware, corkscrew, wine pourer, or tasting notes booklet. Dual-compartment designs are popular for distillery gift sets and corporate gifting programs.

Q: What are the shipping advantages of the flat-pack design? A: The hexagonal outer shell ships flat (pre-assembly state) with the foam cradle and ribbon packed separately. This reduces shipping volume by approximately 60% vs. a pre-assembled rigid box, translating to significant freight cost savings — especially on international container shipments.

Q: Can I get a sample before committing to bulk production? A: Yes. Bincai provides free pre-production samples within 5–7 working days, including material swatches (foil, ribbon, velvet), print proofs, and a fully assembled structural sample. Custom tooling costs (new die, embossing plate, foil stamping plate) are typically credited against the first production order.
