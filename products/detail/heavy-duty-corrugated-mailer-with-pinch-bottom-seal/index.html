Product Overview

The heavy-duty corrugated mailer with pinch-bottom self-sealing closure is engineered for industrial ecommerce and bulk fulfillment where standard mailers fail. Produced by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. — an ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified manufacturer with 23 years of packaging expertise (founded 2003) — this triple-wall mailer withstands stacking, drops, and rough handling without interior damage. FAQ

Q: What’s the maximum weight this mailer can handle? A: The triple-wall BC-flute construction handles up to 8 kg for single-item shipments, depending on box dimensions and item shape. For heavier items (8-15 kg), we recommend upgrading to AB double-wall or adding interior corrugated partitions. Our engineering team can specify the optimal flute combination based on your product weight, dimensions, and shipping method.

Q: How does the pinch-bottom seal compare to tape in shipping durability? A: Pinch-bottom seals outperform tape in two critical ways. First, the acrylic adhesive penetrates the kraft fibers, creating a bond that actually strengthens over 24 hours. Second, temperature cycling (-20°C to 60°C) doesn’t degrade the bond — unlike packaging tape whose adhesive crystallizes in cold and oozes in heat. In ISTA 1A drop testing, our pinch-bottom seals maintain integrity through all 10 drops while taped boxes often fail at the tape seam by drop 6-7.

Q: Can the pinch-bottom mailer be reused for returns? A: Yes — we offer a dual-closure option: pinch-bottom for the outbound shipment plus a secondary adhesive strip with a fresh release liner for the return. The customer opens via the perforated tear strip (leaving the box structurally intact), places the return item inside, peels the second liner, and reseals. This is ideal for RMA, warranty exchanges, and subscription services with return logistics.

Q: Is the self-sealing adhesive food-safe? A: The standard hot melt EVA adhesive used in corrugated construction is not rated for direct food contact. For mailers that will contain unpackaged food items (coffee beans, loose tea, chocolate), we use food-grade hot melt (FDA 21 CFR 175.105 compliant) and specify a virgin kraft liner with no recycled content. The pinch-bottom adhesive strip is positioned on the exterior flap and does not contact contents.

Q: What’s the lead time for custom-printed mailers? A: Pre-production samples (printed, with pinch-bottom closure) ship in 5-7 days. Mass production: 15-20 working days for 30,000+ units. Smaller quantities (1,000-10,000) may ship in 10-12 days. Rush 7-day service is available for repeat orders with existing tooling.

Q: Can you do variable-depth scoring so one box handles multiple product SKUs? A: Yes — we can add multi-depth scoring (at 10mm increments) so a single box design compresses to fit different product heights. This is especially valuable for subscription fulfillment centers managing dozens of SKUs with varying thicknesses. The pinch-bottom closure works across the full depth range; the flap simply folds at the appropriate score line.
