Velvet double-layer ring and earring display jewelry box with built-in LED mirror and magnetic closure — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd. This elegant two-tier jewelry box features a mirror lid embedded with a warm-white LED strip that illuminates when opened, creating a boutique retail experience. The upper tray holds rings in plush velvet grooves while the removable lower layer stores earring pairs, small pendants, and other fine jewelry. Premium velvet flocking lines every surface, with a satisfying magnetic closure and gold foil logo on the exterior. Produced on KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses across our 18,000 sqm factory.

Customization Options

  • Velvet color: black, white, grey, navy, burgundy, emerald green, blush pink, or custom Pantone
  • LED options: warm white (3000K), cool white (6000K), or RGB color-changing with remote
  • Exterior wrap: art paper with matte/gloss lamination, leatherette, linen texture, or PU leather
  • Hot foil: gold, silver, rose gold, copper, or holographic on lid and spine

FAQ

Q: Can the LED light be customized for different colors or effects? A: Yes. We offer warm white (3000K), cool white (6000K), and RGB color-changing options with remote control. Battery type (CR2032/AAA) and auto-off timer can be customized.

Q: What is the minimum quantity for custom velvet colors? A: Standard colors (black, white, grey, navy) have no minimum. Custom Pantone-matched velvet requires 1,000+ units.

Q: How is the velvet applied and is it durable? A: Velvet is electrostatic flocked onto the greyboard with industrial-grade adhesive. It is rub-tested for 500+ cycles and does not shed fibers.

Q: Can you add RFID or NFC chips for brand authentication? A: Yes, we can embed RFID/NFC tags between the board layers for luxury brand authentication and customer engagement.

Q: What is the lead time for a custom jewelry box order? A: Sampling: 7-10 days (digital mockup 2-3 days + physical sample production). Bulk: 15-25 days depending on quantity.
