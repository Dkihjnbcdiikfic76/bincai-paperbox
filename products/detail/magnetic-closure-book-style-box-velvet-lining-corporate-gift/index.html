Product Overview

Premium magnetic closure book-style rigid box with plush velvet lining for luxury corporate gift packaging — manufactured by Guangdong Bincai Color Printing Co., Ltd., a full-chain packaging enterprise with 23 years of experience (founded 2003). The book-style design opens like a hardcover volume, revealing a velvet-lined interior with die-cut foam insert for secure product presentation. Manufactured across our 18,000 sqm integrated facility in Foshan, Guangdong, using KBA 1050 4-color and Heidelberg 7+1 UV presses. Daily output: 1.7 million color boxes + 220,000+ gift boxes.
