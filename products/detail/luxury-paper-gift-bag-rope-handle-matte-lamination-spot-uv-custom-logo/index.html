Seven-Stage QC Gates

  1. Incoming Material Inspection — paper grammage verification, handle material tensile strength, eyelet hardness, foil color match, lamination film quality
  2. Pre-Press Proofing — color calibration, trap settings, spot UV mask alignment check, foil die verification
  3. In-Process Printing — spectrophotometer color check every 500 sheets, ΔE ≤ 2.0 target, Heidelberg Prinect inline defect detection
  4. Bag Forming QC — fold precision (±0.5mm), glue bead consistency, gusset expansion test, seam burst test
  5. Handle Attachment Inspection — eyelet crimp quality, pull-force test (15kg static), handle twist uniformity, backing patch adhesion
  6. Finishing QC — lamination coverage (no peeling edges), spot UV alignment visual check, foil stamping centering, bag interior cleanliness
  7. Pre-Shipment Inspection — AQL random sample per ISO 2859-1, full load test, drop test per ISTA 1A, color match under D65 and TL84 lighting

FAQ

Q: What’s the weight capacity of your paper gift bags?

A: Standard 210gsm bags are rated for 5kg distributed load with reinforced bottom insert. With 250gsm paper and an additional cardboard base insert, capacity increases to 10kg — suitable for wine bottles, book sets, or heavy gift collections. Every production batch undergoes static load testing at 3× the rated capacity for 60 seconds. For exceptionally heavy items (12kg+), our 300gsm stock with dual-reinforced bottom is available.

Q: Will the rope handles hold if a customer carries the bag for an extended time?

A: Yes. Our handles are secured with reinforced backing patches that distribute the load across a 40mm diameter area behind each eyelet, preventing the common failure mode where handles tear through the paper. We pull-test every production batch to 15kg static load — equivalent to carrying a full bag for 30+ minutes. For bags over 350mm in height, we recommend grosgrain ribbon or flat cotton tape handles for additional comfort during extended carry.

Q: Can you match the bag color to our brand’s Pantone color?

A: Absolutely. We can print the bag in your exact Pantone color (ΔE ≤ 2.0 verified by X-Rite eXact spectrophotometer), or wrap the bag in colored paper stock. For solid-color bags, we offer over 50 stocked paper colors in matte and metallic finishes — saving you the cost of full-coverage printing. For two-color designs, your brand color can be combined with a contrasting interior print or handle color.

Q: What’s the minimum order quantity for custom printed gift bags?

A: MOQ is 500 units per size and design. For small boutiques or trial runs, we offer 300-unit minimums with a modest surcharge. Our flat-packed shipping means even 500 bags occupy only 2–3 cartons — manageable for small businesses. As your program scales, per-unit costs decrease significantly at 2,000+ and 5,000+ volumes.

Q: Can you provide matching tissue paper and accessories to complete the brand look?

A: Yes — this is one of our most requested services. We can produce matching tissue paper (custom printed with your logo in up to 4 colors, or solid color to match your bag), coordinating hang tags with string, thank-you postcards, and even magnetic closure gift boxes that nest perfectly inside the bag. Bundling everything from one supplier saves you coordination time, shipping costs, and ensures consistent color matching across all components."
