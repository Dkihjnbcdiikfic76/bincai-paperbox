Category 1: Rigid Gift Boxes (52 Products)

Best for: Luxury retail packaging, corporate gifts, premium electronics, high-end cosmetics, limited edition products.

What Is a Rigid Gift Box?

Rigid boxes (also called set-up boxes or presentation boxes) are constructed by wrapping 1.5mm–3.0mm greyboard with 128–157gsm art paper, creating a stiff, non-collapsible structure. Unlike folding cartons, rigid boxes do not fold flat after assembly — they maintain their shape and weight, conveying instant quality when held.

Key Manufacturing Specs

  • Board: 1200–1600gsm greyboard core, wrapped in art paper, specialty paper, or leatherette
  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color offset + Heidelberg 7+1 UV for specialty coatings
  • Finishing: Hot foil stamping, embossing/debossing, soft-touch lamination, matte/gloss lamination, spot UV, edge gilding
  • Structure: Hinged lid, telescopic lid, shoulder/neck boxes, book-style, double-door, drawer-style
  • Interior: Custom die-cut foam, velvet, satin, or flocked inserts
  • Tolerances: ±0.3mm on wrapping and corner alignment

Ideal Industries

  • Luxury cosmetics and skincare
  • Premium spirits and wine
  • High-end electronics (headphones, smartwatches)
  • Corporate gifting and awards
  • Limited-edition collectibles and sneakers

Bincai Advantage

Our rigid box line is fully automated, with precision wrap-and-turn machines that ensure consistent corner integrity — no splitting or delamination. Free pre-production samples in 5–7 days. MOQ starts at 500 units.

Category 2: Folding Cartons (48 Products)

Best for: Retail shelf packaging, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, consumer goods.

What Is a Folding Carton?

Folding cartons are manufactured from single-layer paperboard (typically 250–450gsm), die-cut with score lines, and shipped flat. They are the most cost-effective paper box category and dominate retail, food, pharma, and consumer goods packaging. They are assembled on-site or by automated filling lines.

Key Manufacturing Specs

  • Board: SBS (Solid Bleached Sulfate), CCNB (Clay-Coated News Back), or Duplex board, 250–450gsm
  • Press: KBA 1050 4-color offset + Heidelberg 7+1 UV
  • Structures: Auto-lock bottom, straight tuck, reverse tuck, crash-lock bottom, 4-corner/6-corner tray, sleeve, gable-top
  • Finishing: Aqueous coating, UV varnish, matte/gloss lamination, window patching with PET film
  • Food-grade: Available with PE/PET/PLA internal coating or liner for direct food contact
  • Tolerances: ±0.5mm on die-cutting and folding

Ideal Industries

  • Food and beverage (bakery, confectionery, tea, frozen foods)
  • Pharmaceuticals and healthcare (tablets, syrups, medical devices)
  • Cosmetics and personal care (soap, candles, creams)
  • Electronics accessories (cables, chargers, phone cases)
  • Retail FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods)

Bincai Advantage

Our auto-lock bottom and crash-lock designs reduce assembly time to under 3 seconds per box — critical for high-volume filling lines. We offer pharma-grade HEPA cleanroom production for medical packaging (AQL 1.5, cGMP compliant). Braille embossing available for pharmaceutical cartons. MOQ from 300 units.

Category 3: Corrugated Mailers (49 Products)

Best for: E-commerce shipping, subscription boxes, industrial parts, fragile item protection, direct-to-consumer fulfillment.

What Is a Corrugated Mailer?

Corrugated boxes are constructed from fluted kraft paper sandwiched between linerboards — providing exceptional crush resistance and impact protection. Unlike rigid boxes (which prioritize presentation), corrugated mailers prioritize structural protection during transit.

Key Manufacturing Specs

  • Flute types: B-flute (3mm, best for printing), E-flute (1.5mm, fine detail printing), BC-flute (6mm, heavy-duty), and EB-flute (4mm, balanced)
  • Wall construction: Single-wall, double-wall, or triple-wall for industrial applications
  • Board grades: Kraft (high ECT), Test (medium ECT), or White-top (printable surface)
  • Die-cut inserts: Custom foam (EPE/EVA), corrugated dividers, pulp trays
  • Closures: Self-seal adhesive strips, tuck-top, crash-lock, or tape-seal
  • Standards: ISTA 1A/3A transit tested, Amazon FBA Frustration-Free Packaging compliant

Ideal Industries

  • E-commerce DTC (Direct-to-Consumer) brands
  • Book and literature shipping (self-seal book mailers)
  • Wine and spirit bottle shipping (die-cut foam inserts)
  • Electronics shipping (laptops, monitors, TVs with EPE cushioning)
  • Industrial parts and automotive components
  • Subscription box services

Bincai Advantage

Our corrugated mailers are designed for the Amazon FBA ecosystem — optimized box dimensions reduce dimensional weight charges. Self-seal adhesive strips eliminate tape, speeding up fulfillment by 40%. Flat-shipping design reduces freight volume by 60–75%. MOQ from 300 units.

Category 4: Magnetic Closure Boxes (46 Products)

Best for: Premium unboxing experiences, luxury retail, jewelry presentation, corporate gifts, high-end electronics.

What Is a Magnetic Closure Box?

Magnetic closure boxes combine rigid box construction with concealed neodymium magnets in the flap or lid. The magnets create a satisfying snap-shut action that elevates the unboxing experience — making them the fastest-growing category in premium packaging.

Key Manufacturing Specs

  • Magnets: N35–N52 grade neodymium, 0.8–1.2kg closure force per magnet
  • Configurations: 2-magnet standard, 4-magnet heavy-duty, full-length magnetic strips
  • Board: 1200–1600gsm greyboard wrapped in 128–157gsm art paper
  • Structures: Magnetic flap lid, magnetic book-style, magnetic double-door, magnetic drawer, magnetic side-panel
  • Finishing: Soft-touch lamination, matte lamination, spot UV, hot foil stamping, embossing, edge gilding, velvet lining
  • Testing: 100-cycle open/close test, inverted shake test with full 1.5kg payload

Ideal Industries

  • Jewelry and watches (velvet/suede-lined magnetic boxes)
  • Premium cosmetics and fragrance
  • Corporate gifts and executive presentation kits
  • Luxury confectionery and gourmet food
  • High-end electronics (magnetic presentation cases)
  • Wedding and bridal party favors

Bincai Advantage

Every magnetic closure box undergoes individual inspection — magnets sit flush with zero protrusion, closure force is tested at 100 open/close cycles, and the lid holds closed when inverted. We use N52-grade neodymium for maximum strength in minimal thickness. MOQ from 500 units. Free samples in 5–7 days.

Category 5: Kraft Eco Boxes (47 Products)

Best for: Sustainable brands, organic products, eco-conscious consumers, food packaging, minimalist branding.

What Is a Kraft Eco Box?

Kraft eco boxes use unbleached or recycled kraft paperboard with water-based inks, soy-based inks, and compostable PLA windows — meeting the growing demand for plastic-free, biodegradable packaging. The natural brown kraft aesthetic communicates sustainability without sacrificing structural integrity.

Key Manufacturing Specs

  • Board: 100% recycled kraft, FSC-certified virgin kraft, or unbleached kraft — 250–450gsm
  • Inks: Soy-based (SGS certified) or water-based, VOC-free
  • Windows: Compostable PLA (polylactic acid) film — plant-based, industrial compost certified
  • Adhesives: Water-based, solvent-free, REACH and RoHS compliant
  • Finishing: Natural uncoated, aqueous matte, or minimal foil stamping for brand marks
  • Customization: Jute/hemp handles, cotton ribbon closures, seed-infused paper tags

Ideal Industries

  • Organic food and beverage (tea, coffee, granola, supplements)
  • Natural cosmetics and skincare
  • Sustainable fashion and accessories
  • Eco-friendly subscription boxes
  • Zero-waste and plastic-free brands
  • Plantable packaging (seed-infused paperboard)

Bincai Advantage

We offer seed-infused plantable paperboard — after use, the box can be planted to grow wildflowers or herbs. This innovation has driven 3× higher social media sharing rates for our clients’ unboxing content. All kraft materials are FSC certified with full chain-of-custody documentation. MOQ from 300 units.

Category 6: Jewelry Boxes (43 Products)

Best for: Jewelry brands, watchmakers, accessory designers, luxury retail, e-commerce jewelry.

What Is a Jewelry Box?

Jewelry boxes are a specialized sub-category of rigid boxes, engineered for precision presentation of rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and watches. They feature die-cut velvet, satin, or leatherette inserts with exact-fit cavities for each piece, often including LED lighting, rotating displays, or multi-tier organization.

Key Manufacturing Specs

  • Construction: Rigid greyboard, leatherette-wrapped, or paper-over-board
  • Inserts: Die-cut velvet (rayon or cotton), flocked PET, satin, or suede in custom shapes
  • Lighting: Battery-powered LED strips with magnetic reed switch (lights on when opened)
  • Display mechanisms: Rotating platforms with 360° motorized rotation, pull-out drawers, flip trays
  • Finishing: Hot foil stamping, debossing, edge gilding, ribbon pull tabs, magnetic snap closures
  • Sizes: Ring boxes (30×30×30mm) to multi-tier watch cases (400×300×120mm)

Ideal Industries

  • Fine jewelry (rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings)
  • Luxury watches and timepieces
  • Fashion accessories (cufflinks, tie pins, brooches)
  • Bridal and wedding jewelry sets
  • E-commerce jewelry brands (unboxing-optimized)

Our jewelry boxes feature die-cut insert trays custom-molded to your exact product dimensions — no generic foam slots, no movement in transit.
