23 Years of Paper Box Manufacturing — The Bincai Story

May 20, 2026

Founded in 2003 in Foshan, Guangdong Bincai Color Printing has grown from a small print shop to an 18,000 sqm full-chain packaging enterprise (10,000+8,000 smart factory, expanded 2025 in Shunde Daliang, Foshan).

From Print Shop to Full-Chain Manufacturer

In 2003, Bincai started as a small offset print shop in Foshan’s Shunde district — the heart of China’s printing and packaging industry. Over two decades, we invested in the most advanced equipment available: the KBA Rapida 1050 4-color offset press (15,000 sheets/hour), Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press, BHS 2.5m corrugator, and Bobst SP 102 BMA hot foil stamper and die-cutter. Today, our production lines run three shifts daily, producing over 1.7 million boxes per day for clients across 50+ countries.

Our Certifications & Quality Commitment

We hold ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody (FSC-C147358) certifications, with REACH, RoHS, Prop 65, and EN 71-3 compliance. Every order undergoes AQL 2.5 Level II inspection per ISO 2859-1 with X-Rite ΔE ≤2.0 color accuracy. This commitment to quality has earned us ⭐ Diamond Member status on Made-in-China.com and the trust of 200+ global brands.

What We Manufacture

Bincai specializes in premium paper box packaging across 8 product categories:

  • Rigid Gift Boxes — Luxury two-piece boxes with magnetic closures, velvet flocking, and multi-level foil stamping
  • Folding Cartons — CMYK offset printed cartons for retail, food, and consumer goods
  • Corrugated Mailers — E-commerce shipping boxes and retail display packaging
  • Magnetic Closure Boxes — Premium unboxing experiences with N52 neodymium magnets
  • Kraft Eco Boxes — Sustainable, FSC-certified kraft packaging
  • Paper Bags — Custom branded paper bags for retail and gifting

We serve industries including cosmetics, electronics, food packaging, healthcare, luxury goods, and gift packaging.

Why 23 Years Matters

Two decades in packaging means we’ve seen every challenge: rush seasonal orders, complex multi-SKU launches, tight budgets, and demanding brand standards. Our 80+ skilled workers, in-house die-making workshop, and rapid prototyping (free samples in 5-7 days, digital 3D proofs in 24 hours) mean we can handle any project — from 500 custom gift boxes to 1 million folding cartons.

Whether you are a startup launching your first product or an established brand expanding your packaging lineup, Bincai delivers quality, consistency, and competitive pricing directly from our factory floor.
