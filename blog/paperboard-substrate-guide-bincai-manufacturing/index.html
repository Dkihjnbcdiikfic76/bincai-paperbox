Paperboard Selection Guide for Custom Packaging: SBS, CCNB, Kraft, and Duplex — How Bincai Engineers the Right Substrate for Every Box

June 11, 2026

The difference between a box that arrives crushed and one that protects a $200 product through 45 days of ocean freight often comes down to a single decision: which paperboard you choose. At Guangdong Bincai Color Printing, where we manufacture 1.7 million boxes daily across six product categories, material selection is where engineering meets aesthetics. Here’s the guide our own project managers use.

The Four Core Paperboard Substrates

1. SBS (Solid Bleached Sulfate) — The Premium Standard

SBS is the workhorse of luxury packaging. Made from chemically pulped virgin fiber and bleached to a bright white, it offers:

PropertyValueWhy It Matters
Brightness88–92 ISOSuperior print reproduction for brand colors
Caliper range0.3–0.8mm (10–24 pt)Versatility across box sizes
Smoothness<1.0 μm (PPS)Clean foil stamping and spot UV registration
StiffnessTaber 4–20 mN·mStructural integrity for rigid box wrapping

Best for: Rigid gift boxes, magnetic closure boxes, luxury jewelry packaging, cosmetic folding cartons
Bincai use: Primary substrate for rigid gift boxes and premium folding cartons. Run on both Heidelberg 7+1 UV and KBA 1050 4-color lines.

2. CCNB (Clay-Coated News Back) — The Economical Performer

CCNB uses recycled fiber with a clay-coated top layer for print quality. It’s the cost-effective alternative when SBS is overkill:

PropertyValueWhy It Matters
Caliper range0.35–0.8mmGood thickness at lower grammage
Brightness70–78 ISOAcceptable print for non-premium applications
Burst strength250–400 kPaAdequate for lightweight products
Cost vs SBS25–35% lessBudget optimization without sacrificing function

Best for: Economy folding cartons, e-commerce inner packaging, promotional boxes, non-luxury retail
Bincai use: Often recommended for high-volume folding carton runs where budget is the priority and the box is a functional container, not a brand statement.

3. Kraft Paperboard — The Sustainable Workhorse

Unbleached or lightly bleached Kraft delivers the “natural” look that’s dominating eco-conscious markets:

PropertyValueWhy It Matters
Tear resistance600–1,200 mNHighest durability of all substrates
Natural colorBrown (unbleached) / light brown (bleached)Zero optical brighteners, fully recyclable
Moisture resistanceBetter than SBSSuitable for products with slight humidity exposure
FSC availabilityWidely certifiedSupports sustainability claims on-pack

Best for: Eco-branded packaging, food-contact boxes, kraft mailer boxes, industrial packaging, subscription boxes
Bincai use: Core substrate for our kraft-eco-boxes category. All kraft stock is FSC-certified, sourced from audited mills, and processed in our Shunde factory with full chain-of-custody documentation.

4. Duplex Board (Grey Back / White Back) — The Structural Specialist

Duplex board has a white-coated top layer on a grey or white recycled-fiber back. It’s the go-to for boxes that need thickness without weight:

PropertyValueWhy It Matters
Caliper range0.4–1.2mmMaximum thickness for rigid-feel boxes
DensityLower than SBSLighter shipping weight saves freight costs
Ply adhesion>200 J/m²Won’t delaminate under folding stress
Recycled content70–90%High post-consumer recycled (PCR) content

Best for: Shoe boxes, apparel boxes, electronics packaging, shelf-ready retail packaging
Bincai use: Frequently specified for rigid box base construction where the grey back is hidden by wrapping paper and the white-coated top provides a clean printing surface.

Quality Assurance on Raw Materials

Every paperboard shipment arriving at Bincai’s 18,000 sqm Shunde facility undergoes incoming QC before it reaches production:

  • Grammage verification per ISO 536 — tolerance ±3%
  • Caliper measurement at 5 points per sheet — reject if variation exceeds ±5%
  • Brightness check against supplier certificate — any deviation triggers batch hold
  • Moisture content — maintained at 6–8% for optimal press performance in Foshan’s subtropical climate
  • FSC chain-of-custody documentation verified digitally against purchase order before material release

Rejected stock is quarantined and returned to supplier — it never reaches our Heidelberg or KBA press lines.

Why This Matters for Your Order

The right paperboard:

  • Cuts shipping damage by matching strength to transit conditions
  • Saves 15–30% on material costs by avoiding over-specification
  • Ensures print consistency across 100,000-unit runs
  • Supports sustainability claims with verifiable FSC documentation
  • Reduces lead time by using stock we maintain in our 3,000 sqm material warehouse
