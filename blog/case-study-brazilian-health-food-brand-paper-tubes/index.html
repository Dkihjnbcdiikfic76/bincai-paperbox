Case Study: Brazilian Health Food Brand Consolidated 3 Packaging SKUs with Bincai Paper Tubes

June 17, 2026

The Challenge

A mid-sized health food brand in Brazil — selling protein powder, whey supplements, and seasoned spice blends across Mercado Livre and regional health stores — was sourcing packaging from two local suppliers. The problems: inconsistent aluminum foil seal quality caused moisture ingress during Amazon-region transit (60%+ humidity), minimum order quantities from Brazilian converters were 3× their batch sizes, and they couldn’t match packaging formats across their three product lines — protein came in plastic tubs, whey in composite cans, and spices in glass jars. Their brand looked like a reseller, not a manufacturer.

The Bincai Solution

Bincai proposed a unified paper tube platform: 99mm aluminum-lined kraft tubes for protein and whey powder, and 73mm tubes with shaker-fit lids for spice blends. All tubes use the same FSC-certified kraft exterior, the same food-grade aluminum foil peel-off seal, and the same CMYK offset printing for brand consistency across the three SKUs. The client ordered a semi-automatic sealer machine with their first shipment to bring in-house can-sealing capability — eliminating dependence on pre-sealed inventory.

Specifications:

  • Material: FSC-certified kraft paperboard + food-grade aluminum foil inner liner
  • Printing: CMYK offset (brand colors: green + white)
  • Sizes: 99mm dia × 150mm (protein/whey), 73mm dia × 120mm (spice)
  • MOQ: 5,000 units per SKU
  • Accessories: Semi-automatic aluminum can sealer machine
