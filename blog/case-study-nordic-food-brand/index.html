Case Study: Nordic Food Brand Reduced Packaging Cost 22% with Bincai Cylinder Tubes

June 16, 2026

The Challenge

A growing organic food brand in Scandinavia needed packaging for their new line of dried berry snacks. Their existing supplier charged premium prices with 45-day lead times. They needed: food-grade certification (FSSC 22000), moisture barrier for shelf life, and packaging that communicated “premium organic.”

The Bincai Solution

Our team proposed a custom kraft paper cylinder tube with aluminum foil inner liner — combining food safety with natural aesthetics. We provided 3 free sample variations within 7 days, allowing the client to test with their target market before committing.

Specifications:

  • Material: FSC-certified kraft paper + food-grade aluminum liner
  • Printing: CMYK offset with matte finish
  • Size: 73mm diameter × 150mm height (custom)
  • MOQ: 5,000 units
