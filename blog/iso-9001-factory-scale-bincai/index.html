ISO 9001 Certified, 18,000 sqm Factory: Scale & Quality at Bincai

June 10, 2026

When you source custom paper boxes from Guangdong Bincai Color Printing, you’re not dealing with a small workshop — you’re partnering with an 18,000 sqm (10,000+8,000 smart factory), ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing facility that produces over 1.7 million boxes per day. With 23 years of continuous operation since 2003, Bincai has the scale, quality systems, and expertise to handle orders from 500 units to 500,000+ units with consistent results.

The Scale Advantage: 18,000 sqm Under One Roof

Scale matters in paper box manufacturing. Here’s what our 18,000 sqm dual-factory campus delivers:

  • 18,000 sqm total floor area — 10,000 sqm original facility + 8,000 sqm smart factory (expanded 2025), purpose-built for high-volume paper box production with dedicated zones for printing, die-cutting, gluing, assembly, and quality inspection
  • 1.7 million boxes daily output — powered by our KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset press and Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV press running in tandem across both facilities
  • 220,000+ rigid gift boxes daily — a dedicated rigid box production line with automated case-making, wrapping, and magnetic closure assembly
  • 23 years of continuous operation — since 2003, we’ve served brands across North America, Europe, Middle East, and Asia-Pacific, building deep expertise in international packaging requirements
  • Climate-controlled material storage — 30-day raw material buffer ensures production continuity even during supply chain disruptions

ISO 9001:2015 — The Quality Backbone

Our ISO 9001 certification isn’t just a certificate on the wall — it defines every process from raw material receiving to final shipment:

Quality StageWhat We CheckStandard
Incoming Material InspectionPaperboard caliper, moisture content, whiteness; adhesive viscosity; ink color densityBatch-level testing before production release
Pre-Press VerificationPlate registration, color proof matching, die-cut sample approvalDigital proof sign-off required
In-Process QC GatesPrint registration (±0.1mm), colorimetry Delta E ≤ 2.0, glue bond strength, folding precision5 inspection points per production line
Assembly InspectionMagnetic closure force, ribbon attachment strength, insert fit, overall appearance100% visual inspection for premium boxes
Final QC SamplingAQL 2.5 (Major) / AQL 4.0 (Minor) — random sampling per ISO 2859-1Pre-shipment inspection report provided

This 7-stage quality system means fewer than 0.3% of shipped boxes have quality issues — a rate that consistently meets the standards of Fortune 500 brands and boutique luxury labels alike.

FSC Certified Materials

Beyond scale and quality, Bincai is FSC Chain-of-Custody certified (FSC-C147399). Every sheet of paperboard can be traced from responsibly managed forests to your finished box. For brands selling in the EU, UK, and North America, FSC certification supports regulatory compliance and consumer sustainability expectations.
