Case Study: Swiss Tea Brand Unifies Gift Packaging & Bulk Storage with Bincai Dual-Format Solution

June 17, 2026

The Challenge

A premium tea brand in Switzerland was sourcing from two separate suppliers — one for retail gift boxes and another for bulk tea powder canisters. This created inconsistent branding, double the logistics overhead, and mismatched lead times. The gift box supplier had a 45-day minimum lead time, while the canister supplier could not match the client’s eco-friendly FSC-certified material requirements. With EU Single-Use Plastics Directive compliance approaching, the brand needed a single partner who could deliver both formats with sustainable materials and unified quality control.

The Bincai Solution

Bincai proposed a dual-format packaging program: custom folding gift boxes with satin ribbon closure for retail gift sets, plus food-grade rigid lid-base boxes with aluminum foil inner liner for bulk tea powder. Both formats shared the same CMYK offset printing, matte lamination, and brand color profile — ensuring visual consistency across channels.

We shipped 3 free sample variations within 7 days. The client tested each format with their Swiss retail partners and approved within 2 weeks.
