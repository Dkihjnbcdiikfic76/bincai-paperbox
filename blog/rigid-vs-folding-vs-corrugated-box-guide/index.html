Rigid Gift Boxes — The Premium Choice

Rigid boxes (also called set-up boxes) are the gold standard of luxury packaging. They are constructed from 2-3mm greyboard wrapped in premium art paper, specialty paper, or fabric. Unlike folding cartons, rigid boxes arrive fully assembled — they cannot be collapsed flat, which gives them unmatched structural integrity and an unmistakable luxury feel the moment a customer touches them.

When to Choose Rigid Boxes

Rigid boxes are the right choice when:

  • Brand perception matters. If your product competes on premium positioning, the tactile experience of a rigid box directly impacts perceived value. Studies show luxury packaging can increase willingness-to-pay by 20-40%.
  • The product is heavy or fragile. Rigid boxes protect items weighing up to 5 kg — ideal for glass bottles, ceramic items, multi-piece gift sets, and electronics.
  • You need custom interior engineering. Foam inserts, flocked trays, satin linings, magnetic closures, and ribbon pulls are all standard on rigid boxes.

Folding Cartons — The Cost-Effective Workhorse

Folding cartons are made from 200-400gsm paperboard (SBS, FBB, CCNB, or kraft), die-cut to precise dimensions, and scored with crease lines for flat folding. They ship flat and are erected at the filling line — making them the most economical packaging format for retail products.

When to Choose Folding Cartons

Folding cartons excel when:

  • Volume is high and cost sensitivity matters. At 500,000+ units, the savings from flat shipping alone (60-75% less freight volume) justify folding cartons over rigid boxes.
  • The product is lightweight. Items under 2 kg — cosmetics tubes, food bars, pharmaceutical blister packs, small electronics — are perfectly served by folding cartons.
  • Retail shelf impact is the goal. Folding cartons offer full CMYK offset printing, window cutouts for product visibility, and hang tabs for pegboard display.

Corrugated Mailers — Protection-First Packaging

Corrugated boxes are engineered for strength. They consist of a fluted medium sandwiched between linerboards — the flutes create air channels that absorb impact and provide stacking strength. They are the standard for e-commerce shipping, subscription boxes, and protective product mailers.

For most B2C e-commerce brands, E Flute or F Flute offers the best balance of protection, print quality, and unboxing experience. B Flute is the standard for B2B and heavy-item shipping.

When to Choose Corrugated Mailers

  • E-commerce shipping is your primary channel — corrugated survives the carrier journey
  • Product protection is non-negotiable — electronics, glass, ceramics, multi-piece kits
  • You want a branded unboxing experience — custom-printed corrugated mailers with branded tape create a cohesive brand moment from doorstep to opening
  • Self-locking design eliminates the need for additional tape or glue
