Custom Electronics Paper Box Packaging

Bincai manufactures premium packaging for electronics and tech products. Our rigid boxes with custom CNC-milled foam inserts provide both military-grade protection and a luxury unboxing experience. With 23 years of precision manufacturing expertise and an 18,000 sqm dual factory featuring KBA 1050 4-color offset, Heidelberg 7+1 UV, and Bobst SP 102 BMA foil stamping, we deliver packaging that electronics brands trust.

Electronics Packaging Solutions

Rigid Gift Boxes

Premium two-piece rigid boxes with precision die-cut EVA foam inserts (CNC-milled, 80-100 kg/m³ density, Shore A 45 hardness, cavity tolerance ±0.5mm) for headphones, wireless earbuds, smartwatches, speakers, and smart devices. Available with N52 neodymium magnetic closure (≥2.5 kgF pull force, polarity sensor verified), soft-touch lamination, gold foil stamping, and velvet flocking interiors. Book-style, clamshell, drawer-slide, and two-piece configurations.

Folding Cartons

Cost-effective CMYK offset printed cartons on KBA 1050 4-color press for charging cables, adapters, phone cases, screen protectors, and small accessories. Reverse tuck end, straight tuck end, auto-lock bottom styles. Euro hang-tab for retail pegboard display. Anti-static coating available for sensitive components.

Corrugated Mailers

ISTA 1A drop-test validated corrugated shipping boxes with custom print for e-commerce electronics delivery. B-flute (3mm, crush-resistant), E-flute (1.5mm, fine print surface), or BC double-wall (6mm, heavy cargo). Self-locking auto-bottom, tear strip, and custom die-cut interior partitions. Anti-static and ESD-safe options available for circuit boards, semiconductors, and sensitive electronics.
