Custom Cosmetic Paper Box Packaging

Guangdong Bincai Color Printing has been manufacturing premium cosmetic packaging since 2003. Our 18,000 sqm dual factory (10,000 + 8,000 sqm smart factory, commissioned 2025) operates KBA Rapida 105 4-color offset (15,000 sheets/hour), Heidelberg Speedmaster CD 102 7+1 UV, and Bobst SP 102 BMA hot foil/embossing presses, producing 1.7 million color boxes and 220,000+ rigid gift boxes daily. ISO 9001:2015 and FSC Chain-of-Custody certified. We’ve delivered over 150 million boxes to cosmetic brands across 40+ countries.

Cosmetic Packaging Solutions

Rigid Gift Boxes

Luxury two-piece rigid boxes with N52 neodymium magnetic closure, velvet flocking interiors (18 colors), and multi-level gold foil stamping on Bobst SP 102 BMA (±0.15mm registration). Perfect for high-end skincare sets, perfume gift collections, lipstick duo boxes, and seasonal makeup palettes. Double-layer greyboard 1200-2000gsm with art paper wrap (128-250gsm) — available in matte, gloss, soft-touch, linen-textured, pearlescent, or metallic finishes.

Folding Cartons

Custom CMYK offset printed cartons on KBA 1050 4-color press with window cutouts (PVC/PET/acrylic), spot UV gloss ±0.2mm registration, and soft-touch matte lamination (fingerprint-resistant, velvet-feel). Ideal for retail cosmetic products including creams, serums, masks, and individual makeup items. Reverse tuck end, straight tuck end, auto-lock bottom, and sleeve-tray configurations.

Magnetic Closure Boxes

Premium magnetic closure boxes with N52 neodymium magnets (6-12mm, triple-layer nickel-copper-nickel plating), satin ribbon pulls (5 kgF tensile tested), and velvet flocking interiors. CNC-routed magnet cavities to ±0.3mm tolerance. 100% polarity sensor and flush-closure inspection. Perfect for luxury cosmetics, fragrance gift sets, beauty advent calendars, and limited-edition brand collaborations.
